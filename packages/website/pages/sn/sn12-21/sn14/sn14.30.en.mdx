At one time, the Blessed One was dwelling in Sāvatthī, in Jeta's Grove, Anāthapiṇḍika's Park...

"Bhikkhus, there are these four elements. What four? The \{earth element} (solid state [pathavīdhātu]), the \{water element} (liquid state, element of cohesion [āpodhātu]), the \{fire element} (fiery state [tejodhātu]), and the \{air element} (gaseous state, element of motion [vāyodhātu]). These are the four elements."
