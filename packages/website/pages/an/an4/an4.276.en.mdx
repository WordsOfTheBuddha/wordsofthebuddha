Bhikkhus, for the full understanding of passion, desire, and attachment, four qualities should be developed. What are these four? Here, bhikkhus, a bhikkhu develops the base of psychic ability endowed with collectedness based on aspiration (a goal/an objective/an interest) and accompanied by an intention of continuous effort; the base of psychic ability endowed with collectedness based on determination and accompanied by an intentino of continuous effort; the base of psychic ability endowed with collectedness based on [purification of] mind and accompanied by an intention of continuous effort; and the base of psychic ability endowed with collectedness based on investigation (reflection and close examination) and accompanied by an intention of continuous effort, for the full understanding of passion, desire, and attachment, these four qualities should be developed.
