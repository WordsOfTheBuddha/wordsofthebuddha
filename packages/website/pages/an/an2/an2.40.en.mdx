Discourse on Right and Wrong Practice

"Bhikkhus, I do not praise two kinds of wrong practice, whether by a householder or a renunciant. A householder or a renunciant who practices wrongly due to wrong practice does not accomplish the proper, skillful Dhamma.

Bhikkhus, I praise two kinds of right practice, whether by a householder or a renunciant. A householder or a renunciant who practices rightly due to right practice accomplishes the proper, skillful Dhamma."
