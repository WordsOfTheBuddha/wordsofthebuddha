"Bhikkhus, any bhikkhu or bhikkhunī who develops and frequently practices these five qualities can expect one of two results: either final knowledge (enlightenment) in this very life or, if there is a trace of clinging left, the state of non-returning.

What are these five?

Here, bhikkhus, a bhikkhu develops the base of psychic ability that is endowed with collectedness arising from aspiration and accompanied by an intention of continuous effort,

develops the base of psychic ability that is endowed with collectedness due to determination and accompanied by an intention of continuous effort,

develops the base of psychic ability that is endowed with collectedness due to [purification of] mind and active effort and accompanied by an intention of continuous effort,

develops the base of Psychic ability that is endowed with collectedness due to investigation (reflection and close examination) and accompanied by an intention of continuous effort,

enthusiasm and active involvement itself makes the fifth.

Bhikkhus, any bhikkhu or bhikkhunī who develops and frequently practices these five qualities can expect one of two results: either final knowledge in this very life or, if there is a trace of clinging left, the state of non-returning."
