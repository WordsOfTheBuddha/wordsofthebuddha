At Sāvatthi.

“Bhikkhus, beings come together and associate |according to disposition::in line with their mental qualities or inclinations [dhātuso]|. Those with |wrong views::false beliefs, false concepts, incorrect opinions [micchādiṭṭhika]| come together and associate with those with wrong views; those with |wrong intentions::wrong notions, ideas, concepts [micchāsaṅkappa]| come together and associate with those with wrong intentions; those with |wrong speech::incorrect speech [micchāvācā]| come together and associate with those with wrong speech; those with |wrong actions::wrong behavior [micchākammanta]| come together and associate with those with wrong actions; those with |wrong livelihood::wrong mode of living [micchāājīva]| come together and associate with those with wrong livelihood; those with |wrong effort::incorrect endeavor [micchāvāyāma]| come together and associate with those with wrong effort; those with |wrong mindfulness::muddle-mindedness, having a forgetful nature [micchāsati]| come together and associate with those with wrong mindfulness; those with |wrong collectedness::imperfect stability of mind, incorrect mental composure [micchāsamādhi]| come together and associate with those with wrong collectedness; those with |wrong wisdom::wrong discernment, incorrect understanding [micchāñāṇī]| come together and associate with those with wrong wisdom; those with |wrong liberation::wrong release, wrong freedom [micchāvimuttī]| come together and associate with those with wrong liberation.

Those with |right views::correct beliefs, correct concepts, correct opinions [sammādiṭṭhikā]| come together and associate with those with right views; those with |right intentions::right notions, ideas, concepts [sammāsaṅkappā]| come together and associate with those with right intentions; those with |right speech::correct speech [sammāvācā]| come together and associate with those with right speech; those with |right actions::right behavior [sammākammanta]| come together and associate with those with right actions; those with |right livelihood::right mode of living [sammāājīva]| come together and associate with those with right livelihood; those with |right effort::correct endeavor [sammāvāyāma]| come together and associate with those with right effort; those with |right mindfulness::correct active presence of mind and lucid retention; accurately remembering the Dhamma to anchor attention on the body, felt experience, mind, and mental qualities [sammāsati]| come together and associate with those with right mindfulness; those |with right collectedness::with perfect stability of mind, with correct mental composure [sammāsamādhi]| come together and associate with those with right collectedness; those with |right wisdom::right discernment, correct understanding [sammāñāṇī]| come together and associate with those with right wisdom; those with |right liberation::right release, right freedom [sammāvimuttī]| come together and associate with those with right liberation.”
