“The |appearance::manifestation; lit. being in front [pātubhāva]| of six things is rare in the world|, bhikkhus.::::.| What six?

1.) The appearance of a |Tathāgata::one who has arrived at the truth, an epithet of a perfectly Awakened One [tathāgata]|, an Arahant, a perfectly Awakened One is rare in the world.

2.) A person who can teach the |Dhamma::teachings of the Buddha that point to the nature of reality, the ultimate truth [dhamma]| and |Vinaya::code of monastic discipline rules, training [vinaya]| proclaimed by a Tathāgata is rare in the world.

3.) Rebirth in the |realm of the Noble Ones::region from where it is possible to access the Dhamma of the Noble Ones [ariyāyatana]| is rare in the world.

4.) Having no deformity in the [sense] faculties is rare in the world.

5.) Being |intelligent::lit. not stupid state [ajaḷatā]| and |astute::lit. not drool or dumb state [aneḷamūgatā]| is rare in the world.

6.) The desire for |wholesome qualities::skillful actions, good habits [kusale dhamme]| is rare in the world.

The appearance of these six things|, bhikkhus,::::| is rare in the world.”
