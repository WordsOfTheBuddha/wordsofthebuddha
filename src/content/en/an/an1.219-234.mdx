#### 1.219

“Bhikkhus, the |foremost::chief, best [agga]| of my bhikkhu disciples among those who are |very learned::well educated, knowledgeable [bahussuta]| is Ānanda.

#### 1.220

<collapse>Bhikkhus, the foremost of my bhikkhu disciples</collapse> among those who are |mindful::who has recollection, is aware, present [satimant]| is Ānanda.

#### 1.221

<collapse>Bhikkhus, the foremost of my bhikkhu disciples</collapse> among those |with excellent retention::having ability to store [gatimant]| is Ānanda.

#### 1.222

<collapse>Bhikkhus, the foremost of my bhikkhu disciples</collapse> among those |with total recall::who are resolute, who are determined [dhitimant]| is Ānanda.

#### 1.223

<collapse>Bhikkhus, the foremost of my bhikkhu disciples</collapse> among personal attendants is Ānanda.

#### 1.224

<collapse>Bhikkhus, the foremost of my bhikkhu disciples</collapse> among those having a large following is Kassapa from Uruvelā.

#### 1.225

<collapse>Bhikkhus, the foremost of my bhikkhu disciples</collapse> among those who inspire confidence in families is Kāludāyi.

#### 1.226

<collapse>Bhikkhus, the foremost of my bhikkhu disciples</collapse> among those who are seldom sick is Bākula.

#### 1.227

<collapse>Bhikkhus, the foremost of my bhikkhu disciples</collapse> among those who recollect past lives is Sobhita.

#### 1.228

<collapse>Bhikkhus, the foremost of my bhikkhu disciples</collapse> among those who are |experts in Vinaya::keepers of the monastic law [vinayadhara]| is Upāli.

#### 1.229

<collapse>Bhikkhus, the foremost of my bhikkhu disciples</collapse> among those who are instructors of nuns is Nandaka.

#### 1.230

<collapse>Bhikkhus, the foremost of my bhikkhu disciples</collapse> among those who guard the doors of the sense faculties is Nanda.

#### 1.231

<collapse>Bhikkhus, the foremost of my bhikkhu disciples</collapse> among those who instruct the bhikkhus is Mahākappina.

#### 1.232

<collapse>Bhikkhus, the foremost of my bhikkhu disciples</collapse> among those who are skilled in the fire element is Sāgata.

#### 1.233

<collapse>Bhikkhus, the foremost of my bhikkhu disciples</collapse> among those inspiring eloquence is Rādha.

#### 1.234

<collapse>Bhikkhus, the foremost of my bhikkhu disciples</collapse> among those who wear coarse robes is Mogharāja.”
