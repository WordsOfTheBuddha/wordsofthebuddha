#### 1.209

“Bhikkhus, the |foremost::chief, best [agga]| of my bhikkhu disciples among those |desiring to train::wanting to practice [sikkhākāma]| is Rāhula.

#### 1.210

<collapse>Bhikkhus, the foremost of my bhikkhu disciples</collapse> among those who ordained out of |faith::confidence, conviction, trust [saddha]| is Raṭṭhapāla.

#### 1.211

<collapse>Bhikkhus, the foremost of my bhikkhu disciples</collapse> among those who are the first to take meal tickets is Kuṇḍadhāna.

#### 1.212

<collapse>Bhikkhus, the foremost of my bhikkhu disciples</collapse> among those who are |eloquent::astute, intelligent, quick-witted [paṭibhānavant]| is Vaṅgīsa.

#### 1.213

<collapse>Bhikkhus, the foremost of my bhikkhu disciples</collapse> among those who inspire confidence in every way is Upasena Vaṅgantaputta.

#### 1.214

<collapse>Bhikkhus, the foremost of my bhikkhu disciples</collapse> among those who assign dwellings is Dabba Mallaputta.

#### 1.215

<collapse>Bhikkhus, the foremost of my bhikkhu disciples</collapse> among those pleasing and agreeable to deities is Piḷindavaccha.

#### 1.216

<collapse>Bhikkhus, the foremost of my bhikkhu disciples</collapse> among those with quick realization is Bāhiya of the |Bark Cloth::dressed in bark strips [dārucīriya]|.

#### 1.217

<collapse>Bhikkhus, the foremost of my bhikkhu disciples</collapse> among those who speak articulately is Kumārakassapa.

#### 1.218

<collapse>Bhikkhus, the foremost of my bhikkhu disciples</collapse> among those who have attained penetrative insight is Mahākoṭṭhita.”
