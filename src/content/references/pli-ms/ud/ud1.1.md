<!-- @segment ud1.1:1.1 -->

Evaṁ me sutaṁ—

<!-- @segment ud1.1:1.2 -->

ekaṁ samayaṁ bhagavā uruvelāyaṁ viharati najjā nerañjarāya tīre bodhirukkhamūle paṭhamābhisambuddho.

<!-- @segment ud1.1:1.3 -->

Tena kho pana samayena bhagavā sattāhaṁ ekapallaṅkena nisinno hoti vimuttisukhapaṭisaṁvedī.

<!-- @segment ud1.1:1.4 -->

Atha kho bhagavā tassa sattāhassa accayena tamhā samādhimhā vuṭṭhahitvā rattiyā paṭhamaṁ yāmaṁ paṭiccasamuppādaṁ anulomaṁ sādhukaṁ manasākāsi:

<!-- @segment ud1.1:2.1 -->

“Iti imasmiṁ sati idaṁ hoti, imassuppādā idaṁ uppajjati, yadidaṁ—

<!-- @segment ud1.1:2.2 -->

avijjāpaccayā saṅkhārā,

<!-- @segment ud1.1:2.3 -->

saṅkhārapaccayā viññāṇaṁ,

<!-- @segment ud1.1:2.4 -->

viññāṇapaccayā nāmarūpaṁ,

<!-- @segment ud1.1:2.5 -->

nāmarūpapaccayā saḷāyatanaṁ,

<!-- @segment ud1.1:2.6 -->

saḷāyatanapaccayā phasso,

<!-- @segment ud1.1:2.7 -->

phassapaccayā vedanā,

<!-- @segment ud1.1:2.8 -->

vedanāpaccayā taṇhā,

<!-- @segment ud1.1:2.9 -->

taṇhāpaccayā upādānaṁ,

<!-- @segment ud1.1:2.10 -->

upādānapaccayā bhavo,

<!-- @segment ud1.1:2.11 -->

bhavapaccayā jāti,

<!-- @segment ud1.1:2.12 -->

jātipaccayā jarāmaraṇaṁ sokaparidevadukkhadomanassupāyāsā sambhavanti.

<!-- @segment ud1.1:2.13 -->

Evametassa kevalassa dukkhakkhandhassa samudayo hotī”ti.

<!-- @segment ud1.1:3.1 -->

Atha kho bhagavā etamatthaṁ viditvā tāyaṁ velāyaṁ imaṁ udānaṁ udānesi:

<!-- @segment ud1.1:4.1 -->

“Yadā have pātubhavanti dhammā,

<!-- @segment ud1.1:4.2 -->

Ātāpino jhāyato brāhmaṇassa;

<!-- @segment ud1.1:4.3 -->

Athassa kaṅkhā vapayanti sabbā,

<!-- @segment ud1.1:4.4 -->

Yato pajānāti sahetudhamman”ti.

<!-- @segment ud1.1:4.5 -->

Paṭhamaṁ.
