<!-- @segment iti13:1.1 -->

Vuttañhetaṁ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṁ:

<!-- @segment iti13:2.1 -->

“Makkhaṁ, bhikkhave, anabhijānaṁ aparijānaṁ tattha cittaṁ avirājayaṁ appajahaṁ abhabbo dukkhakkhayāya.

<!-- @segment iti13:2.2 -->

Makkhañca kho, bhikkhave, abhijānaṁ parijānaṁ tattha cittaṁ virājayaṁ pajahaṁ bhabbo dukkhakkhayāyā”ti.

<!-- @segment iti13:2.3 -->

Etamatthaṁ bhagavā avoca.

<!-- @segment iti13:2.4 -->

Tatthetaṁ iti vuccati:

<!-- @segment iti13:3.1 -->

“Yena makkhena makkhāse,

<!-- @segment iti13:3.2 -->

sattā gacchanti duggatiṁ;

<!-- @segment iti13:3.3 -->

Taṁ makkhaṁ sammadaññāya,

<!-- @segment iti13:3.4 -->

pajahanti vipassino;

<!-- @segment iti13:3.5 -->

Pahāya na punāyanti,

<!-- @segment iti13:3.6 -->

imaṁ lokaṁ kudācanan”ti.

<!-- @segment iti13:4.1 -->

Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.

<!-- @segment iti13:4.2 -->

Tatiyaṁ.
