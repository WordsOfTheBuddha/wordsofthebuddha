<!-- @segment iti36:1.1 -->

Vuttañhetaṁ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṁ:

<!-- @segment iti36:2.1 -->

“Nayidaṁ, bhikkhave, brahmacariyaṁ vussati janakuhanatthaṁ, na janalapanatthaṁ, na lābhasakkārasilokānisaṁsatthaṁ, na ‘iti maṁ jano jānātū’ti.

<!-- @segment iti36:2.2 -->

Atha kho idaṁ, bhikkhave, brahmacariyaṁ vussati abhiññatthañceva pariññatthañcā”ti.

<!-- @segment iti36:2.3 -->

Etamatthaṁ bhagavā avoca.

<!-- @segment iti36:2.4 -->

Tatthetaṁ iti vuccati:

<!-- @segment iti36:3.1 -->

“Abhiññatthaṁ pariññatthaṁ,

<!-- @segment iti36:3.2 -->

brahmacariyaṁ anītihaṁ;

<!-- @segment iti36:3.3 -->

Adesayi so bhagavā,

<!-- @segment iti36:3.4 -->

nibbānogadhagāminaṁ.

<!-- @segment iti36:4.1 -->

Esa maggo mahattehi,

<!-- @segment iti36:4.2 -->

anuyāto mahesibhi;

<!-- @segment iti36:4.3 -->

Ye ye taṁ paṭipajjanti,

<!-- @segment iti36:4.4 -->

yathā buddhena desitaṁ;

<!-- @segment iti36:4.5 -->

Dukkhassantaṁ karissanti,

<!-- @segment iti36:4.6 -->

satthusāsanakārino”ti.

<!-- @segment iti36:5.1 -->

Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.

<!-- @segment iti36:5.2 -->

Navamaṁ.
