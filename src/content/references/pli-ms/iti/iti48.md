<!-- @segment iti48:1.1 -->

Vuttañhetaṁ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṁ:

<!-- @segment iti48:2.1 -->

“Dveme, bhikkhave, āpāyikā nerayikā idamappahāya.

<!-- @segment iti48:2.2 -->

Katame dve?

<!-- @segment iti48:2.3 -->

Yo ca abrahmacārī brahmacāripaṭiñño, yo ca paripuṇṇaṁ parisuddhaṁ brahmacariyaṁ carantaṁ amūlakena abrahmacariyena anuddhaṁseti.

<!-- @segment iti48:2.4 -->

Ime kho, bhikkhave, dve āpāyikā nerayikā idamappahāyā”ti.

<!-- @segment iti48:2.5 -->

Etamatthaṁ bhagavā avoca.

<!-- @segment iti48:2.6 -->

Tatthetaṁ iti vuccati:

<!-- @segment iti48:3.1 -->

“Abhūtavādī nirayaṁ upeti,

<!-- @segment iti48:3.2 -->

Yo vāpi katvā na karomi cāha;

<!-- @segment iti48:3.3 -->

Ubhopi te pecca samā bhavanti,

<!-- @segment iti48:3.4 -->

Nihīnakammā manujā parattha.

<!-- @segment iti48:4.1 -->

Kāsāvakaṇṭhā bahavo,

<!-- @segment iti48:4.2 -->

pāpadhammā asaññatā;

<!-- @segment iti48:4.3 -->

Pāpā pāpehi kammehi,

<!-- @segment iti48:4.4 -->

nirayaṁ te upapajjare.

<!-- @segment iti48:5.1 -->

Seyyo ayoguḷo bhutto,

<!-- @segment iti48:5.2 -->

tatto aggisikhūpamo;

<!-- @segment iti48:5.3 -->

Yañce bhuñjeyya dussīlo,

<!-- @segment iti48:5.4 -->

raṭṭhapiṇḍamasaññato”ti.

<!-- @segment iti48:6.1 -->

Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.

<!-- @segment iti48:6.2 -->

Ekādasamaṁ.
