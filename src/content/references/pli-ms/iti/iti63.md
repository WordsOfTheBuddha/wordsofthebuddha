<!-- @segment iti63:1.1 -->

Vuttañhetaṁ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṁ:

<!-- @segment iti63:2.1 -->

“Tayome, bhikkhave, addhā.

<!-- @segment iti63:2.2 -->

Katame tayo?

<!-- @segment iti63:2.3 -->

Atīto addhā, anāgato addhā, paccuppanno addhā—

<!-- @segment iti63:2.4 -->

ime kho, bhikkhave, tayo addhā”ti.

<!-- @segment iti63:2.5 -->

Etamatthaṁ bhagavā avoca.

<!-- @segment iti63:2.6 -->

Tatthetaṁ iti vuccati:

<!-- @segment iti63:3.1 -->

“Akkheyyasaññino sattā,

<!-- @segment iti63:3.2 -->

akkheyyasmiṁ patiṭṭhitā;

<!-- @segment iti63:3.3 -->

Akkheyyaṁ apariññāya,

<!-- @segment iti63:3.4 -->

yogamāyanti maccuno.

<!-- @segment iti63:4.1 -->

Akkheyyañca pariññāya,

<!-- @segment iti63:4.2 -->

akkhātāraṁ na maññati;

<!-- @segment iti63:4.3 -->

Phuṭṭho vimokkho manasā,

<!-- @segment iti63:4.4 -->

santipadamanuttaraṁ.

<!-- @segment iti63:5.1 -->

Sa ve akkheyyasampanno,

<!-- @segment iti63:5.2 -->

santo santipade rato;

<!-- @segment iti63:5.3 -->

Saṅkhāya sevī dhammaṭṭho,

<!-- @segment iti63:5.4 -->

saṅkhyaṁ nopeti vedagū”ti.

<!-- @segment iti63:6.1 -->

Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.

<!-- @segment iti63:6.2 -->

Catutthaṁ.
