<!-- @segment iti6:1.1 -->

Vuttañhetaṁ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṁ:

<!-- @segment iti6:2.1 -->

“Ekadhammaṁ, bhikkhave, pajahatha;

<!-- @segment iti6:2.2 -->

ahaṁ vo pāṭibhogo anāgāmitāya.

<!-- @segment iti6:2.3 -->

Katamaṁ ekadhammaṁ?

<!-- @segment iti6:2.4 -->

Mānaṁ, bhikkhave, ekadhammaṁ pajahatha;

<!-- @segment iti6:2.5 -->

ahaṁ vo pāṭibhogo anāgāmitāyā”ti.

<!-- @segment iti6:2.6 -->

Etamatthaṁ bhagavā avoca.

<!-- @segment iti6:2.7 -->

Tatthetaṁ iti vuccati:

<!-- @segment iti6:3.1 -->

“Yena mānena mattāse,

<!-- @segment iti6:3.2 -->

sattā gacchanti duggatiṁ;

<!-- @segment iti6:3.3 -->

Taṁ mānaṁ sammadaññāya,

<!-- @segment iti6:3.4 -->

pajahanti vipassino;

<!-- @segment iti6:3.5 -->

Pahāya na punāyanti,

<!-- @segment iti6:3.6 -->

imaṁ lokaṁ kudācanan”ti.

<!-- @segment iti6:4.1 -->

Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.

<!-- @segment iti6:4.2 -->

Chaṭṭhaṁ.
