<!-- @segment iti33:1.1 -->

Vuttañhetaṁ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṁ:

<!-- @segment iti33:2.1 -->

“Dvīhi, bhikkhave, dhammehi samannāgato puggalo yathābhataṁ nikkhitto evaṁ sagge.

<!-- @segment iti33:2.2 -->

Katamehi dvīhi?

<!-- @segment iti33:2.3 -->

Bhaddakena ca sīlena, bhaddikāya ca diṭṭhiyā.

<!-- @segment iti33:2.4 -->

Imehi kho, bhikkhave, dvīhi dhammehi samannāgato puggalo yathābhataṁ nikkhitto evaṁ sagge”ti.

<!-- @segment iti33:2.5 -->

Etamatthaṁ bhagavā avoca.

<!-- @segment iti33:2.6 -->

Tatthetaṁ iti vuccati:

<!-- @segment iti33:3.1 -->

“Bhaddakena ca sīlena,

<!-- @segment iti33:3.2 -->

bhaddikāya ca diṭṭhiyā;

<!-- @segment iti33:3.3 -->

Etehi dvīhi dhammehi,

<!-- @segment iti33:3.4 -->

yo samannāgato naro;

<!-- @segment iti33:3.5 -->

Kāyassa bhedā sappañño,

<!-- @segment iti33:3.6 -->

saggaṁ so upapajjatī”ti.

<!-- @segment iti33:4.1 -->

Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.

<!-- @segment iti33:4.2 -->

Chaṭṭhaṁ.
