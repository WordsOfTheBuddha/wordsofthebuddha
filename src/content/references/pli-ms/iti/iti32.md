<!-- @segment iti32:1.1 -->

Vuttañhetaṁ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṁ:

<!-- @segment iti32:2.1 -->

“Dvīhi, bhikkhave, dhammehi samannāgato puggalo yathābhataṁ nikkhitto evaṁ niraye.

<!-- @segment iti32:2.2 -->

Katamehi dvīhi?

<!-- @segment iti32:2.3 -->

Pāpakena ca sīlena, pāpikāya ca diṭṭhiyā.

<!-- @segment iti32:2.4 -->

Imehi kho, bhikkhave, dvīhi dhammehi samannāgato puggalo yathābhataṁ nikkhitto evaṁ niraye”ti.

<!-- @segment iti32:2.5 -->

Etamatthaṁ bhagavā avoca.

<!-- @segment iti32:2.6 -->

Tatthetaṁ iti vuccati:

<!-- @segment iti32:3.1 -->

“Pāpakena ca sīlena,

<!-- @segment iti32:3.2 -->

pāpikāya ca diṭṭhiyā;

<!-- @segment iti32:3.3 -->

Etehi dvīhi dhammehi,

<!-- @segment iti32:3.4 -->

yo samannāgato naro;

<!-- @segment iti32:3.5 -->

Kāyassa bhedā duppañño,

<!-- @segment iti32:3.6 -->

nirayaṁ sopapajjatī”ti.

<!-- @segment iti32:4.1 -->

Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.

<!-- @segment iti32:4.2 -->

Pañcamaṁ.
