<!-- @segment iti80:1.1 -->

Vuttañhetaṁ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṁ:

<!-- @segment iti80:2.1 -->

“Tayome, bhikkhave, akusalavitakkā.

<!-- @segment iti80:2.2 -->

Katame tayo?

<!-- @segment iti80:2.3 -->

Anavaññattipaṭisaṁyutto vitakko, lābhasakkārasilokapaṭisaṁyutto vitakko, parānuddayatāpaṭisaṁyutto vitakko.

<!-- @segment iti80:2.4 -->

Ime kho, bhikkhave, tayo akusalavitakkā”ti.

<!-- @segment iti80:2.5 -->

Etamatthaṁ bhagavā avoca.

<!-- @segment iti80:2.6 -->

Tatthetaṁ iti vuccati:

<!-- @segment iti80:3.1 -->

“Anavaññattisaṁyutto,

<!-- @segment iti80:3.2 -->

lābhasakkāragāravo;

<!-- @segment iti80:3.3 -->

Sahanandī amaccehi,

<!-- @segment iti80:3.4 -->

ārā saṁyojanakkhayā.

<!-- @segment iti80:4.1 -->

Yo ca puttapasuṁ hitvā,

<!-- @segment iti80:4.2 -->

Vivāhe saṁharāni ca;

<!-- @segment iti80:4.3 -->

Bhabbo so tādiso bhikkhu,

<!-- @segment iti80:4.4 -->

Phuṭṭhuṁ sambodhimuttaman”ti.

<!-- @segment iti80:5.1 -->

Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.

<!-- @segment iti80:5.2 -->

Paṭhamaṁ.
