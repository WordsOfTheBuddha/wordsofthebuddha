<!-- @segment iti40:1.1 -->

Vuttañhetaṁ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṁ:

<!-- @segment iti40:2.1 -->

“Avijjā, bhikkhave, pubbaṅgamā akusalānaṁ dhammānaṁ samāpattiyā anvadeva ahirikaṁ anottappaṁ;

<!-- @segment iti40:2.2 -->

vijjā ca kho, bhikkhave, pubbaṅgamā kusalānaṁ dhammānaṁ samāpattiyā anvadeva hirottappan”ti.

<!-- @segment iti40:2.3 -->

Etamatthaṁ bhagavā avoca.

<!-- @segment iti40:2.4 -->

Tatthetaṁ iti vuccati:

<!-- @segment iti40:3.1 -->

“Yā kācimā duggatiyo,

<!-- @segment iti40:3.2 -->

asmiṁ loke paramhi ca;

<!-- @segment iti40:3.3 -->

Avijjāmūlikā sabbā,

<!-- @segment iti40:3.4 -->

icchālobhasamussayā.

<!-- @segment iti40:4.1 -->

Yato ca hoti pāpiccho,

<!-- @segment iti40:4.2 -->

ahirīko anādaro;

<!-- @segment iti40:4.3 -->

Tato pāpaṁ pasavati,

<!-- @segment iti40:4.4 -->

apāyaṁ tena gacchati.

<!-- @segment iti40:5.1 -->

Tasmā chandañca lobhañca,

<!-- @segment iti40:5.2 -->

avijjañca virājayaṁ;

<!-- @segment iti40:5.3 -->

Vijjaṁ uppādayaṁ bhikkhu,

<!-- @segment iti40:5.4 -->

sabbā duggatiyo jahe”ti.

<!-- @segment iti40:6.1 -->

Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.

<!-- @segment iti40:6.2 -->

Tatiyaṁ.

<!-- @segment iti40:6.3 -->

Paṭhamabhāṇavāro.
