<!-- @segment iti61:1.1 -->

Vuttañhetaṁ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṁ:

<!-- @segment iti61:2.1 -->

“Tīṇimāni, bhikkhave, cakkhūni.

<!-- @segment iti61:2.2 -->

Katamāni tīṇi?

<!-- @segment iti61:2.3 -->

Maṁsacakkhu, dibbacakkhu, paññācakkhu—

<!-- @segment iti61:2.4 -->

imāni kho, bhikkhave, tīṇi cakkhūnī”ti.

<!-- @segment iti61:2.5 -->

Etamatthaṁ bhagavā avoca.

<!-- @segment iti61:2.6 -->

Tatthetaṁ iti vuccati:

<!-- @segment iti61:3.1 -->

“Maṁsacakkhu dibbacakkhu,

<!-- @segment iti61:3.2 -->

paññācakkhu anuttaraṁ;

<!-- @segment iti61:3.3 -->

Etāni tīṇi cakkhūni,

<!-- @segment iti61:3.4 -->

akkhāsi purisuttamo.

<!-- @segment iti61:4.1 -->

Maṁsacakkhussa uppādo,

<!-- @segment iti61:4.2 -->

maggo dibbassa cakkhuno;

<!-- @segment iti61:4.3 -->

Yato ñāṇaṁ udapādi,

<!-- @segment iti61:4.4 -->

paññācakkhu anuttaraṁ;

<!-- @segment iti61:4.5 -->

Yassa cakkhussa paṭilābhā,

<!-- @segment iti61:4.6 -->

sabbadukkhā pamuccatī”ti.

<!-- @segment iti61:5.1 -->

Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.

<!-- @segment iti61:5.2 -->

Dutiyaṁ.
