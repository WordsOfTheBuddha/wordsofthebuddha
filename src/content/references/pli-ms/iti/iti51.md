<!-- @segment iti51:1.1 -->

Vuttañhetaṁ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṁ:

<!-- @segment iti51:2.1 -->

“Tisso imā, bhikkhave, dhātuyo.

<!-- @segment iti51:2.2 -->

Katamā tisso?

<!-- @segment iti51:2.3 -->

Rūpadhātu, arūpadhātu, nirodhadhātu—

<!-- @segment iti51:2.4 -->

imā kho, bhikkhave, tisso dhātuyo”ti.

<!-- @segment iti51:2.5 -->

Etamatthaṁ bhagavā avoca.

<!-- @segment iti51:2.6 -->

Tatthetaṁ iti vuccati:

<!-- @segment iti51:3.1 -->

“Rūpadhātuṁ pariññāya,

<!-- @segment iti51:3.2 -->

āruppesu asaṇṭhitā;

<!-- @segment iti51:3.3 -->

Nirodhe ye vimuccanti,

<!-- @segment iti51:3.4 -->

te janā maccuhāyino.

<!-- @segment iti51:4.1 -->

Kāyena amataṁ dhātuṁ,

<!-- @segment iti51:4.2 -->

phusayitvā nirūpadhiṁ;

<!-- @segment iti51:4.3 -->

Upadhippaṭinissaggaṁ,

<!-- @segment iti51:4.4 -->

sacchikatvā anāsavo;

<!-- @segment iti51:4.5 -->

Deseti sammāsambuddho,

<!-- @segment iti51:4.6 -->

asokaṁ virajaṁ padan”ti.

<!-- @segment iti51:5.1 -->

Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.

<!-- @segment iti51:5.2 -->

Dutiyaṁ.
