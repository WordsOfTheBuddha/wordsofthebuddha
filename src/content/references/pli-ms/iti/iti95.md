<!-- @segment iti95:1.1 -->

Vuttañhetaṁ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṁ:

<!-- @segment iti95:2.1 -->

“Tisso imā, bhikkhave, kāmūpapattiyo.

<!-- @segment iti95:2.2 -->

Katamā tisso?

<!-- @segment iti95:2.3 -->

Paccupaṭṭhitakāmā, nimmānaratino, paranimmitavasavattino—

<!-- @segment iti95:2.4 -->

imā kho, bhikkhave, tisso kāmūpapattiyo”ti.

<!-- @segment iti95:2.5 -->

Etamatthaṁ bhagavā avoca.

<!-- @segment iti95:2.6 -->

Tatthetaṁ iti vuccati:

<!-- @segment iti95:3.1 -->

“Paccupaṭṭhitakāmā ca,

<!-- @segment iti95:3.2 -->

ye devā vasavattino;

<!-- @segment iti95:3.3 -->

Nimmānaratino devā,

<!-- @segment iti95:3.4 -->

ye caññe kāmabhogino;

<!-- @segment iti95:3.5 -->

Itthabhāvaññathābhāvaṁ,

<!-- @segment iti95:3.6 -->

saṁsāraṁ nātivattare.

<!-- @segment iti95:4.1 -->

Etamādīnavaṁ ñatvā,

<!-- @segment iti95:4.2 -->

kāmabhogesu paṇḍito;

<!-- @segment iti95:4.3 -->

Sabbe pariccaje kāme,

<!-- @segment iti95:4.4 -->

ye dibbā ye ca mānusā.

<!-- @segment iti95:5.1 -->

Piyarūpasātagadhitaṁ,

<!-- @segment iti95:5.2 -->

chetvā sotaṁ duraccayaṁ;

<!-- @segment iti95:5.3 -->

Asesaṁ parinibbanti,

<!-- @segment iti95:5.4 -->

asesaṁ dukkhamaccaguṁ.

<!-- @segment iti95:6.1 -->

Ariyaddasā vedaguno,

<!-- @segment iti95:6.2 -->

sammadaññāya paṇḍitā;

<!-- @segment iti95:6.3 -->

Jātikkhayamabhiññāya,

<!-- @segment iti95:6.4 -->

nāgacchanti punabbhavan”ti.

<!-- @segment iti95:7.1 -->

Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.

<!-- @segment iti95:7.2 -->

Chaṭṭhaṁ.
