<!-- @segment iti89:1.1 -->

Vuttañhetaṁ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṁ:

<!-- @segment iti89:2.1 -->

“Tīhi, bhikkhave, asaddhammehi abhibhūto pariyādinnacitto devadatto āpāyiko nerayiko kappaṭṭho atekiccho.

<!-- @segment iti89:2.2 -->

Katamehi tīhi?

<!-- @segment iti89:2.3 -->

Pāpicchatāya, bhikkhave, abhibhūto pariyādinnacitto devadatto āpāyiko nerayiko kappaṭṭho atekiccho.

<!-- @segment iti89:2.4 -->

Pāpamittatāya, bhikkhave, abhibhūto pariyādinnacitto devadatto āpāyiko nerayiko kappaṭṭho atekiccho.

<!-- @segment iti89:2.5 -->

Sati kho pana uttarikaraṇīye oramattakena visesādhigamena antarā vosānaṁ āpādi.

<!-- @segment iti89:2.6 -->

Imehi kho, bhikkhave, tīhi asaddhammehi abhibhūto pariyādinnacitto devadatto āpāyiko nerayiko kappaṭṭho atekiccho”ti.

<!-- @segment iti89:2.7 -->

Etamatthaṁ bhagavā avoca.

<!-- @segment iti89:2.8 -->

Tatthetaṁ iti vuccati:

<!-- @segment iti89:3.1 -->

“Mā jātu koci lokasmiṁ,

<!-- @segment iti89:3.2 -->

pāpiccho udapajjatha;

<!-- @segment iti89:3.3 -->

Tadamināpi jānātha,

<!-- @segment iti89:3.4 -->

pāpicchānaṁ yathā gati.

<!-- @segment iti89:4.1 -->

Paṇḍitoti samaññāto,

<!-- @segment iti89:4.2 -->

bhāvitattoti sammato;

<!-- @segment iti89:4.3 -->

Jalaṁva yasasā aṭṭhā,

<!-- @segment iti89:4.4 -->

devadattoti vissuto.

<!-- @segment iti89:5.1 -->

So samānamanuciṇṇo,

<!-- @segment iti89:5.2 -->

āsajja naṁ tathāgataṁ;

<!-- @segment iti89:5.3 -->

Avīcinirayaṁ patto,

<!-- @segment iti89:5.4 -->

catudvāraṁ bhayānakaṁ.

<!-- @segment iti89:6.1 -->

Aduṭṭhassa hi yo dubbhe,

<!-- @segment iti89:6.2 -->

pāpakammaṁ akubbato;

<!-- @segment iti89:6.3 -->

Tameva pāpaṁ phusati,

<!-- @segment iti89:6.4 -->

duṭṭhacittaṁ anādaraṁ.

<!-- @segment iti89:7.1 -->

Samuddaṁ visakumbhena,

<!-- @segment iti89:7.2 -->

yo maññeyya padūsituṁ;

<!-- @segment iti89:7.3 -->

Na so tena padūseyya,

<!-- @segment iti89:7.4 -->

bhesmā hi udadhi mahā.

<!-- @segment iti89:8.1 -->

Evamevaṁ tathāgataṁ,

<!-- @segment iti89:8.2 -->

yo vādena vihiṁsati;

<!-- @segment iti89:8.3 -->

Sammaggataṁ santacittaṁ,

<!-- @segment iti89:8.4 -->

vādo tamhi na rūhati.

<!-- @segment iti89:9.1 -->

Tādisaṁ mittaṁ kubbetha,

<!-- @segment iti89:9.2 -->

tañca seveyya paṇḍito;

<!-- @segment iti89:9.3 -->

Yassa maggānugo bhikkhu,

<!-- @segment iti89:9.4 -->

khayaṁ dukkhassa pāpuṇe”ti.

<!-- @segment iti89:10.1 -->

Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.

<!-- @segment iti89:10.2 -->

Dasamaṁ.

<!-- @segment iti89:10.3 -->

Catuttho vaggo.

<!-- @segment iti89:11.0 -->

Tassuddānaṁ

<!-- @segment iti89:11.1 -->

Vitakkāsakkārasadda,

<!-- @segment iti89:11.2 -->

cavanaloke asubhaṁ;

<!-- @segment iti89:11.3 -->

Dhammaandhakāramalaṁ,

<!-- @segment iti89:11.4 -->

devadattena te dasāti.
