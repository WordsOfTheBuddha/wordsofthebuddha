<!-- @segment iti73:1.1 -->

Vuttañhetaṁ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṁ:

<!-- @segment iti73:2.1 -->

“Rūpehi, bhikkhave, arūpā santatarā, arūpehi nirodho santataro”ti.

<!-- @segment iti73:2.2 -->

Etamatthaṁ bhagavā avoca.

<!-- @segment iti73:2.3 -->

Tatthetaṁ iti vuccati:

<!-- @segment iti73:3.1 -->

“Ye ca rūpūpagā sattā,

<!-- @segment iti73:3.2 -->

ye ca arūpaṭṭhāyino;

<!-- @segment iti73:3.3 -->

Nirodhaṁ appajānantā,

<!-- @segment iti73:3.4 -->

āgantāro punabbhavaṁ.

<!-- @segment iti73:4.1 -->

Ye ca rūpe pariññāya,

<!-- @segment iti73:4.2 -->

arūpesu asaṇṭhitā;

<!-- @segment iti73:4.3 -->

Nirodhe ye vimuccanti,

<!-- @segment iti73:4.4 -->

te janā maccuhāyino.

<!-- @segment iti73:5.1 -->

Kāyena amatadhātuṁ,

<!-- @segment iti73:5.2 -->

phusayitvā nirūpadhiṁ;

<!-- @segment iti73:5.3 -->

Upadhippaṭinissaggaṁ,

<!-- @segment iti73:5.4 -->

sacchikatvā anāsavo;

<!-- @segment iti73:5.5 -->

Deseti sammāsambuddho,

<!-- @segment iti73:5.6 -->

asokaṁ virajaṁ padan”ti.

<!-- @segment iti73:6.1 -->

Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.

<!-- @segment iti73:6.2 -->

Catutthaṁ.
