<!-- @segment iti62:1.1 -->

Vuttañhetaṁ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṁ:

<!-- @segment iti62:2.1 -->

“Tīṇimāni, bhikkhave, indriyāni.

<!-- @segment iti62:2.2 -->

Katamāni tīṇi?

<!-- @segment iti62:2.3 -->

Anaññātaññassāmītindriyaṁ, aññindriyaṁ, aññātāvindriyaṁ—

<!-- @segment iti62:2.4 -->

imāni kho, bhikkhave, tīṇi indriyānī”ti.

<!-- @segment iti62:2.5 -->

Etamatthaṁ bhagavā avoca.

<!-- @segment iti62:2.6 -->

Tatthetaṁ iti vuccati:

<!-- @segment iti62:3.1 -->

“Sekhassa sikkhamānassa,

<!-- @segment iti62:3.2 -->

ujumaggānusārino;

<!-- @segment iti62:3.3 -->

Khayasmiṁ paṭhamaṁ ñāṇaṁ,

<!-- @segment iti62:3.4 -->

tato aññā anantarā.

<!-- @segment iti62:4.1 -->

Tato aññāvimuttassa,

<!-- @segment iti62:4.2 -->

ñāṇaṁ ve hoti tādino;

<!-- @segment iti62:4.3 -->

Akuppā me vimuttīti,

<!-- @segment iti62:4.4 -->

bhavasaṁyojanakkhayā.

<!-- @segment iti62:5.1 -->

Sa ve indriyasampanno,

<!-- @segment iti62:5.2 -->

santo santipade rato;

<!-- @segment iti62:5.3 -->

Dhāreti antimaṁ dehaṁ,

<!-- @segment iti62:5.4 -->

jetvā māraṁ savāhinin”ti.

<!-- @segment iti62:6.1 -->

Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.

<!-- @segment iti62:6.2 -->

Tatiyaṁ.
