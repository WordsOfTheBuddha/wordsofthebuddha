<!-- @segment iti77:1.1 -->

Vuttañhetaṁ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṁ:

<!-- @segment iti77:2.1 -->

“Bhidurāyaṁ, bhikkhave, kāyo, viññāṇaṁ virāgadhammaṁ, sabbe upadhī aniccā dukkhā vipariṇāmadhammā”ti.

<!-- @segment iti77:2.2 -->

Etamatthaṁ bhagavā avoca.

<!-- @segment iti77:2.3 -->

Tatthetaṁ iti vuccati:

<!-- @segment iti77:3.1 -->

“Kāyañca bhiduraṁ ñatvā,

<!-- @segment iti77:3.2 -->

Viññāṇañca virāgunaṁ;

<!-- @segment iti77:3.3 -->

Upadhīsu bhayaṁ disvā,

<!-- @segment iti77:3.4 -->

Jātimaraṇamaccagā;

<!-- @segment iti77:3.5 -->

Sampatvā paramaṁ santiṁ,

<!-- @segment iti77:3.6 -->

Kālaṁ kaṅkhati bhāvitatto”ti.

<!-- @segment iti77:4.1 -->

Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.

<!-- @segment iti77:4.2 -->

Aṭṭhamaṁ.
