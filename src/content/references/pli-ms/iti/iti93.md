<!-- @segment iti93:1.1 -->

Vuttañhetaṁ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṁ:

<!-- @segment iti93:2.1 -->

“Tayome, bhikkhave, aggī.

<!-- @segment iti93:2.2 -->

Katame tayo?

<!-- @segment iti93:2.3 -->

Rāgaggi, dosaggi, mohaggi—

<!-- @segment iti93:2.4 -->

ime kho, bhikkhave, tayo aggī”ti.

<!-- @segment iti93:2.5 -->

Etamatthaṁ bhagavā avoca.

<!-- @segment iti93:2.6 -->

Tatthetaṁ iti vuccati:

<!-- @segment iti93:3.1 -->

“Rāgaggi dahati macce,

<!-- @segment iti93:3.2 -->

ratte kāmesu mucchite;

<!-- @segment iti93:3.3 -->

Dosaggi pana byāpanne,

<!-- @segment iti93:3.4 -->

nare pāṇātipātino.

<!-- @segment iti93:4.1 -->

Mohaggi pana sammūḷhe,

<!-- @segment iti93:4.2 -->

ariyadhamme akovide;

<!-- @segment iti93:4.3 -->

Ete aggī ajānantā,

<!-- @segment iti93:4.4 -->

sakkāyābhiratā pajā.

<!-- @segment iti93:5.1 -->

Te vaḍḍhayanti nirayaṁ,

<!-- @segment iti93:5.2 -->

tiracchānañca yoniyo;

<!-- @segment iti93:5.3 -->

Asuraṁ pettivisayaṁ,

<!-- @segment iti93:5.4 -->

amuttā mārabandhanā.

<!-- @segment iti93:6.1 -->

Ye ca rattindivā yuttā,

<!-- @segment iti93:6.2 -->

sammāsambuddhasāsane;

<!-- @segment iti93:6.3 -->

Te nibbāpenti rāgaggiṁ,

<!-- @segment iti93:6.4 -->

niccaṁ asubhasaññino.

<!-- @segment iti93:7.1 -->

Dosaggiṁ pana mettāya,

<!-- @segment iti93:7.2 -->

nibbāpenti naruttamā;

<!-- @segment iti93:7.3 -->

Mohaggiṁ pana paññāya,

<!-- @segment iti93:7.4 -->

yāyaṁ nibbedhagāminī.

<!-- @segment iti93:8.1 -->

Te nibbāpetvā nipakā,

<!-- @segment iti93:8.2 -->

rattindivamatanditā;

<!-- @segment iti93:8.3 -->

Asesaṁ parinibbanti,

<!-- @segment iti93:8.4 -->

asesaṁ dukkhamaccaguṁ.

<!-- @segment iti93:9.1 -->

Ariyaddasā vedaguno,

<!-- @segment iti93:9.2 -->

sammadaññāya paṇḍitā;

<!-- @segment iti93:9.3 -->

Jātikkhayamabhiññāya,

<!-- @segment iti93:9.4 -->

nāgacchanti punabbhavan”ti.

<!-- @segment iti93:10.1 -->

Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.

<!-- @segment iti93:10.2 -->

Catutthaṁ.
