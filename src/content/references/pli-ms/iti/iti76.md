<!-- @segment iti76:1.1 -->

Vuttañhetaṁ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṁ:

<!-- @segment iti76:2.1 -->

“Tīṇimāni, bhikkhave, sukhāni patthayamāno sīlaṁ rakkheyya paṇḍito.

<!-- @segment iti76:2.2 -->

Katamāni tīṇi?

<!-- @segment iti76:2.3 -->

Pasaṁsā me āgacchatūti sīlaṁ rakkheyya paṇḍito, bhogā me uppajjantūti sīlaṁ rakkheyya paṇḍito, kāyassa bhedā paraṁ maraṇā sugatiṁ saggaṁ lokaṁ upapajjissāmīti sīlaṁ rakkheyya paṇḍito.

<!-- @segment iti76:2.4 -->

Imāni kho, bhikkhave, tīṇi sukhāni patthayamāno sīlaṁ rakkheyya paṇḍito”ti.

<!-- @segment iti76:2.5 -->

Etamatthaṁ bhagavā avoca.

<!-- @segment iti76:2.6 -->

Tatthetaṁ iti vuccati:

<!-- @segment iti76:3.1 -->

“Sīlaṁ rakkheyya medhāvī,

<!-- @segment iti76:3.2 -->

patthayāno tayo sukhe;

<!-- @segment iti76:3.3 -->

Pasaṁsaṁ vittalābhañca,

<!-- @segment iti76:3.4 -->

pecca sagge pamodanaṁ.

<!-- @segment iti76:4.1 -->

Akarontopi ce pāpaṁ,

<!-- @segment iti76:4.2 -->

karontamupasevati;

<!-- @segment iti76:4.3 -->

Saṅkiyo hoti pāpasmiṁ,

<!-- @segment iti76:4.4 -->

avaṇṇo cassa rūhati.

<!-- @segment iti76:5.1 -->

Yādisaṁ kurute mittaṁ,

<!-- @segment iti76:5.2 -->

yādisaṁ cūpasevati;

<!-- @segment iti76:5.3 -->

Sa ve tādisako hoti,

<!-- @segment iti76:5.4 -->

sahavāso hi tādiso.

<!-- @segment iti76:6.1 -->

Sevamāno sevamānaṁ,

<!-- @segment iti76:6.2 -->

samphuṭṭho samphusaṁ paraṁ;

<!-- @segment iti76:6.3 -->

Saro diddho kalāpaṁva,

<!-- @segment iti76:6.4 -->

alittamupalimpati;

<!-- @segment iti76:6.5 -->

Upalepabhayā dhīro,

<!-- @segment iti76:6.6 -->

neva pāpasakhā siyā.

<!-- @segment iti76:7.1 -->

Pūtimacchaṁ kusaggena,

<!-- @segment iti76:7.2 -->

yo naro upanayhati;

<!-- @segment iti76:7.3 -->

Kusāpi pūti vāyanti,

<!-- @segment iti76:7.4 -->

evaṁ bālūpasevanā.

<!-- @segment iti76:8.1 -->

Tagarañca palāsena,

<!-- @segment iti76:8.2 -->

yo naro upanayhati;

<!-- @segment iti76:8.3 -->

Pattāpi surabhi vāyanti,

<!-- @segment iti76:8.4 -->

evaṁ dhīrūpasevanā.

<!-- @segment iti76:9.1 -->

Tasmā pattapuṭasseva,

<!-- @segment iti76:9.2 -->

ñatvā sampākamattano;

<!-- @segment iti76:9.3 -->

Asante nupaseveyya,

<!-- @segment iti76:9.4 -->

sante seveyya paṇḍito;

<!-- @segment iti76:9.5 -->

Asanto nirayaṁ nenti,

<!-- @segment iti76:9.6 -->

santo pāpenti suggatin”ti.

<!-- @segment iti76:10.1 -->

Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.

<!-- @segment iti76:10.2 -->

Sattamaṁ.
