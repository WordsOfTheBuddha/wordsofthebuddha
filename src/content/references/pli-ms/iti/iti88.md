<!-- @segment iti88:1.1 -->

Vuttañhetaṁ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṁ:

<!-- @segment iti88:2.1 -->

“Tayome, bhikkhave, antarāmalā antarāamittā antarāsapattā antarāvadhakā antarāpaccatthikā.

<!-- @segment iti88:2.2 -->

Katame tayo?

<!-- @segment iti88:2.3 -->

Lobho, bhikkhave, antarāmalo antarāamitto antarāsapatto antarāvadhako antarāpaccatthiko.

<!-- @segment iti88:2.4 -->

Doso, bhikkhave, antarāmalo antarāamitto antarāsapatto antarāvadhako antarāpaccatthiko.

<!-- @segment iti88:2.5 -->

Moho, bhikkhave, antarāmalo antarāamitto antarāsapatto antarāvadhako antarāpaccatthiko.

<!-- @segment iti88:2.6 -->

Ime kho, bhikkhave, tayo antarāmalā antarāamittā antarāsapattā antarāvadhakā antarāpaccatthikā”ti.

<!-- @segment iti88:2.7 -->

Etamatthaṁ bhagavā avoca.

<!-- @segment iti88:2.8 -->

Tatthetaṁ iti vuccati:

<!-- @segment iti88:3.1 -->

“Anatthajanano lobho,

<!-- @segment iti88:3.2 -->

lobho cittappakopano;

<!-- @segment iti88:3.3 -->

Bhayamantarato jātaṁ,

<!-- @segment iti88:3.4 -->

taṁ jano nāvabujjhati.

<!-- @segment iti88:4.1 -->

Luddho atthaṁ na jānāti,

<!-- @segment iti88:4.2 -->

luddho dhammaṁ na passati;

<!-- @segment iti88:4.3 -->

Andhatamaṁ tadā hoti,

<!-- @segment iti88:4.4 -->

yaṁ lobho sahate naraṁ.

<!-- @segment iti88:5.1 -->

Yo ca lobhaṁ pahantvāna,

<!-- @segment iti88:5.2 -->

lobhaneyye na lubbhati;

<!-- @segment iti88:5.3 -->

Lobho pahīyate tamhā,

<!-- @segment iti88:5.4 -->

udabindūva pokkharā.

<!-- @segment iti88:6.1 -->

Anatthajanano doso,

<!-- @segment iti88:6.2 -->

doso cittappakopano;

<!-- @segment iti88:6.3 -->

Bhayamantarato jātaṁ,

<!-- @segment iti88:6.4 -->

taṁ jano nāvabujjhati.

<!-- @segment iti88:7.1 -->

Duṭṭho atthaṁ na jānāti,

<!-- @segment iti88:7.2 -->

duṭṭho dhammaṁ na passati;

<!-- @segment iti88:7.3 -->

Andhatamaṁ tadā hoti,

<!-- @segment iti88:7.4 -->

yaṁ doso sahate naraṁ.

<!-- @segment iti88:8.1 -->

Yo ca dosaṁ pahantvāna,

<!-- @segment iti88:8.2 -->

dosaneyye na dussati;

<!-- @segment iti88:8.3 -->

Doso pahīyate tamhā,

<!-- @segment iti88:8.4 -->

tālapakkaṁva bandhanā.

<!-- @segment iti88:9.1 -->

Anatthajanano moho,

<!-- @segment iti88:9.2 -->

moho cittappakopano;

<!-- @segment iti88:9.3 -->

Bhayamantarato jātaṁ,

<!-- @segment iti88:9.4 -->

taṁ jano nāvabujjhati.

<!-- @segment iti88:10.1 -->

Mūḷho atthaṁ na jānāti,

<!-- @segment iti88:10.2 -->

mūḷho dhammaṁ na passati;

<!-- @segment iti88:10.3 -->

Andhatamaṁ tadā hoti,

<!-- @segment iti88:10.4 -->

yaṁ moho sahate naraṁ.

<!-- @segment iti88:11.1 -->

Yo ca mohaṁ pahantvāna,

<!-- @segment iti88:11.2 -->

mohaneyye na muyhati;

<!-- @segment iti88:11.3 -->

Mohaṁ vihanti so sabbaṁ,

<!-- @segment iti88:11.4 -->

ādiccovudayaṁ taman”ti.

<!-- @segment iti88:12.1 -->

Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.

<!-- @segment iti88:12.2 -->

Navamaṁ.
