<!-- @segment iti10:1.1 -->

Vuttañhetaṁ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṁ:

<!-- @segment iti10:2.1 -->

“Dosaṁ, bhikkhave, anabhijānaṁ aparijānaṁ tattha cittaṁ avirājayaṁ appajahaṁ abhabbo dukkhakkhayāya.

<!-- @segment iti10:2.2 -->

Dosañca kho, bhikkhave, abhijānaṁ parijānaṁ tattha cittaṁ virājayaṁ pajahaṁ bhabbo dukkhakkhayāyā”ti.

<!-- @segment iti10:2.3 -->

Etamatthaṁ bhagavā avoca.

<!-- @segment iti10:2.4 -->

Tatthetaṁ iti vuccati:

<!-- @segment iti10:3.1 -->

“Yena dosena duṭṭhāse,

<!-- @segment iti10:3.2 -->

sattā gacchanti duggatiṁ;

<!-- @segment iti10:3.3 -->

Taṁ dosaṁ sammadaññāya,

<!-- @segment iti10:3.4 -->

pajahanti vipassino;

<!-- @segment iti10:3.5 -->

Pahāya na punāyanti,

<!-- @segment iti10:3.6 -->

imaṁ lokaṁ kudācanan”ti.

<!-- @segment iti10:4.1 -->

Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.

<!-- @segment iti10:4.2 -->

Dasamaṁ.

<!-- @segment iti10:4.3 -->

Pāṭibhogavaggo paṭhamo.

<!-- @segment iti10:5.0 -->

Tassuddānaṁ

<!-- @segment iti10:5.1 -->

Rāgadosā atha moho,

<!-- @segment iti10:5.2 -->

Kodhamakkhā mānaṁ sabbaṁ;

<!-- @segment iti10:5.3 -->

Mānato rāgadosā puna dve,

<!-- @segment iti10:5.4 -->

Pakāsitā vaggamāhu paṭhamanti.
