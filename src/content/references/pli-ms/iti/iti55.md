<!-- @segment iti55:1.1 -->

Vuttañhetaṁ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṁ:

<!-- @segment iti55:2.1 -->

“Tisso imā, bhikkhave, esanā.

<!-- @segment iti55:2.2 -->

Katamā tisso?

<!-- @segment iti55:2.3 -->

Kāmesanā, bhavesanā, brahmacariyesanā—

<!-- @segment iti55:2.4 -->

imā kho, bhikkhave, tisso esanā”ti.

<!-- @segment iti55:2.5 -->

Etamatthaṁ bhagavā avoca.

<!-- @segment iti55:2.6 -->

Tatthetaṁ iti vuccati:

<!-- @segment iti55:3.1 -->

“Kāmesanā bhavesanā,

<!-- @segment iti55:3.2 -->

brahmacariyesanā saha;

<!-- @segment iti55:3.3 -->

Iti saccaparāmāso,

<!-- @segment iti55:3.4 -->

diṭṭhiṭṭhānā samussayā.

<!-- @segment iti55:4.1 -->

Sabbarāgavirattassa,

<!-- @segment iti55:4.2 -->

taṇhakkhayavimuttino;

<!-- @segment iti55:4.3 -->

Esanā paṭinissaṭṭhā,

<!-- @segment iti55:4.4 -->

diṭṭhiṭṭhānā samūhatā;

<!-- @segment iti55:4.5 -->

Esanānaṁ khayā bhikkhu,

<!-- @segment iti55:4.6 -->

nirāso akathaṅkathī”ti.

<!-- @segment iti55:5.1 -->

Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.

<!-- @segment iti55:5.2 -->

Chaṭṭhaṁ.
