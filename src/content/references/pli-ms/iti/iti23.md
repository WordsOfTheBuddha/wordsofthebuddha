<!-- @segment iti23:1.1 -->

Vuttañhetaṁ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṁ:

<!-- @segment iti23:2.1 -->

“Ekadhammo, bhikkhave, bhāvito bahulīkato ubho atthe samadhigayha tiṭṭhati—

<!-- @segment iti23:2.2 -->

diṭṭhadhammikañceva atthaṁ samparāyikañca.

<!-- @segment iti23:2.3 -->

Katamo ekadhammo?

<!-- @segment iti23:2.4 -->

Appamādo kusalesu dhammesu.

<!-- @segment iti23:2.5 -->

Ayaṁ kho, bhikkhave, ekadhammo bhāvito bahulīkato ubho atthe samadhigayha tiṭṭhati—

<!-- @segment iti23:2.6 -->

diṭṭhadhammikañceva atthaṁ samparāyikañcā”ti.

<!-- @segment iti23:2.7 -->

Etamatthaṁ bhagavā avoca.

<!-- @segment iti23:2.8 -->

Tatthetaṁ iti vuccati:

<!-- @segment iti23:3.1 -->

“Appamādaṁ pasaṁsanti,

<!-- @segment iti23:3.2 -->

puññakiriyāsu paṇḍitā;

<!-- @segment iti23:3.3 -->

Appamatto ubho atthe,

<!-- @segment iti23:3.4 -->

adhigaṇhāti paṇḍito.

<!-- @segment iti23:4.1 -->

Diṭṭhe dhamme ca yo attho,

<!-- @segment iti23:4.2 -->

yo cattho samparāyiko;

<!-- @segment iti23:4.3 -->

Atthābhisamayā dhīro,

<!-- @segment iti23:4.4 -->

paṇḍitoti pavuccatī”ti.

<!-- @segment iti23:5.1 -->

Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.

<!-- @segment iti23:5.2 -->

Tatiyaṁ.
