<!-- @segment snp2.4:1.1 -->

Evaṁ me sutaṁ—

<!-- @segment snp2.4:1.2 -->

ekaṁ samayaṁ bhagavā sāvatthiyaṁ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme.

<!-- @segment snp2.4:1.3 -->

Atha kho aññatarā devatā abhikkantāya rattiyā abhikkantavaṇṇā kevalakappaṁ jetavanaṁ obhāsetvā yena bhagavā tenupasaṅkami; upasaṅkamitvā bhagavantaṁ abhivādetvā ekamantaṁ aṭṭhāsi.

<!-- @segment snp2.4:1.4 -->

Ekamantaṁ ṭhitā kho sā devatā bhagavantaṁ gāthāya ajjhabhāsi:

<!-- @segment snp2.4:2.1 -->

“Bahū devā manussā ca,

<!-- @segment snp2.4:2.2 -->

maṅgalāni acintayuṁ;

<!-- @segment snp2.4:2.3 -->

Ākaṅkhamānā sotthānaṁ,

<!-- @segment snp2.4:2.4 -->

brūhi maṅgalamuttamaṁ”.

<!-- @segment snp2.4:3.1 -->

“Asevanā ca bālānaṁ,

<!-- @segment snp2.4:3.2 -->

paṇḍitānañca sevanā;

<!-- @segment snp2.4:3.3 -->

Pūjā ca pūjaneyyānaṁ,

<!-- @segment snp2.4:3.4 -->

etaṁ maṅgalamuttamaṁ.

<!-- @segment snp2.4:4.1 -->

Patirūpadesavāso ca,

<!-- @segment snp2.4:4.2 -->

pubbe ca katapuññatā;

<!-- @segment snp2.4:4.3 -->

Attasammāpaṇidhi ca,

<!-- @segment snp2.4:4.4 -->

etaṁ maṅgalamuttamaṁ.

<!-- @segment snp2.4:5.1 -->

Bāhusaccañca sippañca,

<!-- @segment snp2.4:5.2 -->

vinayo ca susikkhito;

<!-- @segment snp2.4:5.3 -->

Subhāsitā ca yā vācā,

<!-- @segment snp2.4:5.4 -->

etaṁ maṅgalamuttamaṁ.

<!-- @segment snp2.4:6.1 -->

Mātāpitu upaṭṭhānaṁ,

<!-- @segment snp2.4:6.2 -->

puttadārassa saṅgaho;

<!-- @segment snp2.4:6.3 -->

Anākulā ca kammantā,

<!-- @segment snp2.4:6.4 -->

etaṁ maṅgalamuttamaṁ.

<!-- @segment snp2.4:7.1 -->

Dānañca dhammacariyā ca,

<!-- @segment snp2.4:7.2 -->

ñātakānañca saṅgaho;

<!-- @segment snp2.4:7.3 -->

Anavajjāni kammāni,

<!-- @segment snp2.4:7.4 -->

etaṁ maṅgalamuttamaṁ.

<!-- @segment snp2.4:8.1 -->

Āratī viratī pāpā,

<!-- @segment snp2.4:8.2 -->

majjapānā ca saṁyamo;

<!-- @segment snp2.4:8.3 -->

Appamādo ca dhammesu,

<!-- @segment snp2.4:8.4 -->

etaṁ maṅgalamuttamaṁ.

<!-- @segment snp2.4:9.1 -->

Gāravo ca nivāto ca,

<!-- @segment snp2.4:9.2 -->

santuṭṭhi ca kataññutā;

<!-- @segment snp2.4:9.3 -->

Kālena dhammassavanaṁ,

<!-- @segment snp2.4:9.4 -->

etaṁ maṅgalamuttamaṁ.

<!-- @segment snp2.4:10.1 -->

Khantī ca sovacassatā,

<!-- @segment snp2.4:10.2 -->

samaṇānañca dassanaṁ;

<!-- @segment snp2.4:10.3 -->

Kālena dhammasākacchā,

<!-- @segment snp2.4:10.4 -->

etaṁ maṅgalamuttamaṁ.

<!-- @segment snp2.4:11.1 -->

Tapo ca brahmacariyañca,

<!-- @segment snp2.4:11.2 -->

Ariyasaccāna dassanaṁ;

<!-- @segment snp2.4:11.3 -->

Nibbānasacchikiriyā ca,

<!-- @segment snp2.4:11.4 -->

Etaṁ maṅgalamuttamaṁ.

<!-- @segment snp2.4:12.1 -->

Phuṭṭhassa lokadhammehi,

<!-- @segment snp2.4:12.2 -->

Cittaṁ yassa na kampati;

<!-- @segment snp2.4:12.3 -->

Asokaṁ virajaṁ khemaṁ,

<!-- @segment snp2.4:12.4 -->

Etaṁ maṅgalamuttamaṁ.

<!-- @segment snp2.4:13.1 -->

Etādisāni katvāna,

<!-- @segment snp2.4:13.2 -->

Sabbattha maparājitā;

<!-- @segment snp2.4:13.3 -->

Sabbattha sotthiṁ gacchanti,

<!-- @segment snp2.4:13.4 -->

Taṁ tesaṁ maṅgalamuttaman”ti.

<!-- @segment snp2.4:13.5 -->

Maṅgalasuttaṁ catutthaṁ.
