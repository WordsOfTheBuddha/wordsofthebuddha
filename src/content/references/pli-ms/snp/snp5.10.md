<!-- @segment snp5.10:1.1 -->

“Yasmiṁ kāmā na vasanti,

<!-- @segment snp5.10:1.2 -->

(iccāyasmā todeyyo)

<!-- @segment snp5.10:1.3 -->

Taṇhā yassa na vijjati;

<!-- @segment snp5.10:1.4 -->

Kathaṅkathā ca yo tiṇṇo,

<!-- @segment snp5.10:1.5 -->

Vimokkho tassa kīdiso”.

<!-- @segment snp5.10:2.1 -->

“Yasmiṁ kāmā na vasanti,

<!-- @segment snp5.10:2.2 -->

(todeyyāti bhagavā)

<!-- @segment snp5.10:2.3 -->

Taṇhā yassa na vijjati;

<!-- @segment snp5.10:2.4 -->

Kathaṅkathā ca yo tiṇṇo,

<!-- @segment snp5.10:2.5 -->

Vimokkho tassa nāparo”.

<!-- @segment snp5.10:3.1 -->

“Nirāsaso so uda āsasāno,

<!-- @segment snp5.10:3.2 -->

Paññāṇavā so uda paññakappī;

<!-- @segment snp5.10:3.3 -->

Muniṁ ahaṁ sakka yathā vijaññaṁ,

<!-- @segment snp5.10:3.4 -->

Taṁ me viyācikkha samantacakkhu”.

<!-- @segment snp5.10:4.1 -->

“Nirāsaso so na ca āsasāno,

<!-- @segment snp5.10:4.2 -->

Paññāṇavā so na ca paññakappī;

<!-- @segment snp5.10:4.3 -->

Evampi todeyya muniṁ vijāna,

<!-- @segment snp5.10:4.4 -->

Akiñcanaṁ kāmabhave asattan”ti.

<!-- @segment snp5.10:4.5 -->

Todeyyamāṇavapucchā navamā.
