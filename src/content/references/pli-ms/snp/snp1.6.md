<!-- @segment snp1.6:1.1 -->

Evaṁ me sutaṁ—

<!-- @segment snp1.6:1.2 -->

ekaṁ samayaṁ bhagavā sāvatthiyaṁ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme.

<!-- @segment snp1.6:1.3 -->

Atha kho aññatarā devatā abhikkantāya rattiyā abhikkantavaṇṇā kevalakappaṁ jetavanaṁ obhāsetvā yena bhagavā tenupasaṅkami; upasaṅkamitvā bhagavantaṁ abhivādetvā ekamantaṁ aṭṭhāsi.

<!-- @segment snp1.6:1.4 -->

Ekamantaṁ ṭhitā kho sā devatā bhagavantaṁ gāthāya ajjhabhāsi:

<!-- @segment snp1.6:2.1 -->

“Parābhavantaṁ purisaṁ,

<!-- @segment snp1.6:2.2 -->

mayaṁ pucchāma gotama;

<!-- @segment snp1.6:2.3 -->

Bhavantaṁ puṭṭhumāgamma,

<!-- @segment snp1.6:2.4 -->

kiṁ parābhavato mukhaṁ”.

<!-- @segment snp1.6:3.1 -->

“Suvijāno bhavaṁ hoti,

<!-- @segment snp1.6:3.2 -->

suvijāno parābhavo;

<!-- @segment snp1.6:3.3 -->

Dhammakāmo bhavaṁ hoti,

<!-- @segment snp1.6:3.4 -->

dhammadessī parābhavo”.

<!-- @segment snp1.6:4.1 -->

“Iti hetaṁ vijānāma,

<!-- @segment snp1.6:4.2 -->

paṭhamo so parābhavo;

<!-- @segment snp1.6:4.3 -->

Dutiyaṁ bhagavā brūhi,

<!-- @segment snp1.6:4.4 -->

kiṁ parābhavato mukhaṁ”.

<!-- @segment snp1.6:5.1 -->

“Asantassa piyā honti,

<!-- @segment snp1.6:5.2 -->

sante na kurute piyaṁ;

<!-- @segment snp1.6:5.3 -->

Asataṁ dhammaṁ roceti,

<!-- @segment snp1.6:5.4 -->

taṁ parābhavato mukhaṁ”.

<!-- @segment snp1.6:6.1 -->

“Iti hetaṁ vijānāma,

<!-- @segment snp1.6:6.2 -->

dutiyo so parābhavo;

<!-- @segment snp1.6:6.3 -->

Tatiyaṁ bhagavā brūhi,

<!-- @segment snp1.6:6.4 -->

kiṁ parābhavato mukhaṁ”.

<!-- @segment snp1.6:7.1 -->

“Niddāsīlī sabhāsīlī,

<!-- @segment snp1.6:7.2 -->

anuṭṭhātā ca yo naro;

<!-- @segment snp1.6:7.3 -->

Alaso kodhapaññāṇo,

<!-- @segment snp1.6:7.4 -->

taṁ parābhavato mukhaṁ”.

<!-- @segment snp1.6:8.1 -->

“Iti hetaṁ vijānāma,

<!-- @segment snp1.6:8.2 -->

tatiyo so parābhavo;

<!-- @segment snp1.6:8.3 -->

Catutthaṁ bhagavā brūhi,

<!-- @segment snp1.6:8.4 -->

kiṁ parābhavato mukhaṁ”.

<!-- @segment snp1.6:9.1 -->

“Yo mātaraṁ pitaraṁ vā,

<!-- @segment snp1.6:9.2 -->

jiṇṇakaṁ gatayobbanaṁ;

<!-- @segment snp1.6:9.3 -->

Pahu santo na bharati,

<!-- @segment snp1.6:9.4 -->

taṁ parābhavato mukhaṁ”.

<!-- @segment snp1.6:10.1 -->

“Iti hetaṁ vijānāma,

<!-- @segment snp1.6:10.2 -->

catuttho so parābhavo;

<!-- @segment snp1.6:10.3 -->

Pañcamaṁ bhagavā brūhi,

<!-- @segment snp1.6:10.4 -->

kiṁ parābhavato mukhaṁ”.

<!-- @segment snp1.6:11.1 -->

“Yo brāhmaṇaṁ samaṇaṁ vā,

<!-- @segment snp1.6:11.2 -->

Aññaṁ vāpi vanibbakaṁ;

<!-- @segment snp1.6:11.3 -->

Musāvādena vañceti,

<!-- @segment snp1.6:11.4 -->

Taṁ parābhavato mukhaṁ”.

<!-- @segment snp1.6:12.1 -->

“Iti hetaṁ vijānāma,

<!-- @segment snp1.6:12.2 -->

Pañcamo so parābhavo;

<!-- @segment snp1.6:12.3 -->

Chaṭṭhamaṁ bhagavā brūhi,

<!-- @segment snp1.6:12.4 -->

Kiṁ parābhavato mukhaṁ”.

<!-- @segment snp1.6:13.1 -->

“Pahūtavitto puriso,

<!-- @segment snp1.6:13.2 -->

Sahirañño sabhojano;

<!-- @segment snp1.6:13.3 -->

Eko bhuñjati sādūni,

<!-- @segment snp1.6:13.4 -->

Taṁ parābhavato mukhaṁ”.

<!-- @segment snp1.6:14.1 -->

“Iti hetaṁ vijānāma,

<!-- @segment snp1.6:14.2 -->

Chaṭṭhamo so parābhavo;

<!-- @segment snp1.6:14.3 -->

Sattamaṁ bhagavā brūhi,

<!-- @segment snp1.6:14.4 -->

Kiṁ parābhavato mukhaṁ”.

<!-- @segment snp1.6:15.1 -->

“Jātitthaddho dhanatthaddho,

<!-- @segment snp1.6:15.2 -->

Gottatthaddho ca yo naro;

<!-- @segment snp1.6:15.3 -->

Saññātiṁ atimaññeti,

<!-- @segment snp1.6:15.4 -->

Taṁ parābhavato mukhaṁ”.

<!-- @segment snp1.6:16.1 -->

“Iti hetaṁ vijānāma,

<!-- @segment snp1.6:16.2 -->

Sattamo so parābhavo;

<!-- @segment snp1.6:16.3 -->

Aṭṭhamaṁ bhagavā brūhi,

<!-- @segment snp1.6:16.4 -->

Kiṁ parābhavato mukhaṁ”.

<!-- @segment snp1.6:17.1 -->

“Itthidhutto surādhutto,

<!-- @segment snp1.6:17.2 -->

Akkhadhutto ca yo naro;

<!-- @segment snp1.6:17.3 -->

Laddhaṁ laddhaṁ vināseti,

<!-- @segment snp1.6:17.4 -->

Taṁ parābhavato mukhaṁ”.

<!-- @segment snp1.6:18.1 -->

“Iti hetaṁ vijānāma,

<!-- @segment snp1.6:18.2 -->

Aṭṭhamo so parābhavo;

<!-- @segment snp1.6:18.3 -->

Navamaṁ bhagavā brūhi,

<!-- @segment snp1.6:18.4 -->

Kiṁ parābhavato mukhaṁ”.

<!-- @segment snp1.6:19.1 -->

“Sehi dārehi asantuṭṭho,

<!-- @segment snp1.6:19.2 -->

Vesiyāsu padussati;

<!-- @segment snp1.6:19.3 -->

Dussati paradāresu,

<!-- @segment snp1.6:19.4 -->

Taṁ parābhavato mukhaṁ”.

<!-- @segment snp1.6:20.1 -->

“Iti hetaṁ vijānāma,

<!-- @segment snp1.6:20.2 -->

Navamo so parābhavo;

<!-- @segment snp1.6:20.3 -->

Dasamaṁ bhagavā brūhi,

<!-- @segment snp1.6:20.4 -->

Kiṁ parābhavato mukhaṁ”.

<!-- @segment snp1.6:21.1 -->

“Atītayobbano poso,

<!-- @segment snp1.6:21.2 -->

Āneti timbarutthaniṁ;

<!-- @segment snp1.6:21.3 -->

Tassā issā na supati,

<!-- @segment snp1.6:21.4 -->

Taṁ parābhavato mukhaṁ”.

<!-- @segment snp1.6:22.1 -->

“Iti hetaṁ vijānāma,

<!-- @segment snp1.6:22.2 -->

Dasamo so parābhavo;

<!-- @segment snp1.6:22.3 -->

Ekādasamaṁ bhagavā brūhi,

<!-- @segment snp1.6:22.4 -->

Kiṁ parābhavato mukhaṁ”.

<!-- @segment snp1.6:23.1 -->

“Itthiṁ soṇḍiṁ vikiraṇiṁ,

<!-- @segment snp1.6:23.2 -->

Purisaṁ vāpi tādisaṁ;

<!-- @segment snp1.6:23.3 -->

Issariyasmiṁ ṭhapeti,

<!-- @segment snp1.6:23.4 -->

Taṁ parābhavato mukhaṁ”.

<!-- @segment snp1.6:24.1 -->

“Iti hetaṁ vijānāma,

<!-- @segment snp1.6:24.2 -->

Ekādasamo so parābhavo;

<!-- @segment snp1.6:24.3 -->

Dvādasamaṁ bhagavā brūhi,

<!-- @segment snp1.6:24.4 -->

Kiṁ parābhavato mukhaṁ”.

<!-- @segment snp1.6:25.1 -->

“Appabhogo mahātaṇho,

<!-- @segment snp1.6:25.2 -->

Khattiye jāyate kule;

<!-- @segment snp1.6:25.3 -->

So ca rajjaṁ patthayati,

<!-- @segment snp1.6:25.4 -->

Taṁ parābhavato mukhaṁ.

<!-- @segment snp1.6:26.1 -->

Ete parābhave loke,

<!-- @segment snp1.6:26.2 -->

Paṇḍito samavekkhiya;

<!-- @segment snp1.6:26.3 -->

Ariyo dassanasampanno,

<!-- @segment snp1.6:26.4 -->

Sa lokaṁ bhajate sivan”ti.

<!-- @segment snp1.6:26.5 -->

Parābhavasuttaṁ chaṭṭhaṁ.
