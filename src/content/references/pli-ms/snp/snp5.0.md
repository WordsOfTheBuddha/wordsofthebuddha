Sambuddhoti vaco sutvā,

Udaggo bāvarī ahu;

Sokassa tanuko āsi,

<!-- @segment snp5.1:20.4 -->

<!-- @segment snp5.1:20.5 -->

Sambuddhaṁ dvipaduttamaṁ”.
