<!-- @segment snp4.9:1.1 -->

“Disvāna taṇhaṁ aratiṁ ragañca,

<!-- @segment snp4.9:1.2 -->

Nāhosi chando api methunasmiṁ;

<!-- @segment snp4.9:1.3 -->

Kimevidaṁ muttakarīsapuṇṇaṁ,

<!-- @segment snp4.9:1.4 -->

Pādāpi naṁ samphusituṁ na icche”.

<!-- @segment snp4.9:2.1 -->

“Etādisañce ratanaṁ na icchasi,

<!-- @segment snp4.9:2.2 -->

Nāriṁ narindehi bahūhi patthitaṁ;

<!-- @segment snp4.9:2.3 -->

Diṭṭhigataṁ sīlavataṁ nu jīvitaṁ,

<!-- @segment snp4.9:2.4 -->

Bhavūpapattiñca vadesi kīdisaṁ”.

<!-- @segment snp4.9:3.1 -->

“Idaṁ vadāmīti na tassa hoti,

<!-- @segment snp4.9:3.2 -->

(māgaṇḍiyāti bhagavā)

<!-- @segment snp4.9:3.3 -->

Dhammesu niccheyya samuggahītaṁ;

<!-- @segment snp4.9:3.4 -->

Passañca diṭṭhīsu anuggahāya,

<!-- @segment snp4.9:3.5 -->

Ajjhattasantiṁ pacinaṁ adassaṁ”.

<!-- @segment snp4.9:4.1 -->

“Vinicchayā yāni pakappitāni,

<!-- @segment snp4.9:4.2 -->

(iti māgaṇḍiyo)

<!-- @segment snp4.9:4.3 -->

Te ve munī brūsi anuggahāya;

<!-- @segment snp4.9:4.4 -->

Ajjhattasantīti yametamatthaṁ,

<!-- @segment snp4.9:4.5 -->

Kathaṁ nu dhīrehi paveditaṁ taṁ”.

<!-- @segment snp4.9:5.1 -->

“Na diṭṭhiyā na sutiyā na ñāṇena,

<!-- @segment snp4.9:5.2 -->

(māgaṇḍiyāti bhagavā)

<!-- @segment snp4.9:5.3 -->

Sīlabbatenāpi na suddhimāha;

<!-- @segment snp4.9:5.4 -->

Adiṭṭhiyā assutiyā añāṇā,

<!-- @segment snp4.9:5.5 -->

Asīlatā abbatā nopi tena;

<!-- @segment snp4.9:5.6 -->

Ete ca nissajja anuggahāya,

<!-- @segment snp4.9:5.7 -->

Santo anissāya bhavaṁ na jappe”.

<!-- @segment snp4.9:6.1 -->

“No ce kira diṭṭhiyā na sutiyā na ñāṇena,

<!-- @segment snp4.9:6.2 -->

(iti māgaṇḍiyo)

<!-- @segment snp4.9:6.3 -->

Sīlabbatenāpi na suddhimāha;

<!-- @segment snp4.9:6.4 -->

Adiṭṭhiyā assutiyā añāṇā,

<!-- @segment snp4.9:6.5 -->

Asīlatā abbatā nopi tena;

<!-- @segment snp4.9:6.6 -->

Maññāmahaṁ momuhameva dhammaṁ,

<!-- @segment snp4.9:6.7 -->

Diṭṭhiyā eke paccenti suddhiṁ”.

<!-- @segment snp4.9:7.1 -->

“Diṭṭhañca nissāya anupucchamāno,

<!-- @segment snp4.9:7.2 -->

(māgaṇḍiyāti bhagavā)

<!-- @segment snp4.9:7.3 -->

Samuggahītesu pamohamāgā;

<!-- @segment snp4.9:7.4 -->

Ito ca nāddakkhi aṇumpi saññaṁ,

<!-- @segment snp4.9:7.5 -->

Tasmā tuvaṁ momuhato dahāsi.

<!-- @segment snp4.9:8.1 -->

Samo visesī uda vā nihīno,

<!-- @segment snp4.9:8.2 -->

Yo maññati so vivadetha tena;

<!-- @segment snp4.9:8.3 -->

Tīsu vidhāsu avikampamāno,

<!-- @segment snp4.9:8.4 -->

Samo visesīti na tassa hoti.

<!-- @segment snp4.9:9.1 -->

Saccanti so brāhmaṇo kiṁ vadeyya,

<!-- @segment snp4.9:9.2 -->

Musāti vā so vivadetha kena;

<!-- @segment snp4.9:9.3 -->

Yasmiṁ samaṁ visamaṁ vāpi natthi,

<!-- @segment snp4.9:9.4 -->

Sa kena vādaṁ paṭisaṁyujeyya.

<!-- @segment snp4.9:10.1 -->

Okaṁ pahāya aniketasārī,

<!-- @segment snp4.9:10.2 -->

Gāme akubbaṁ muni santhavāni;

<!-- @segment snp4.9:10.3 -->

Kāmehi ritto apurakkharāno,

<!-- @segment snp4.9:10.4 -->

Kathaṁ na viggayha janena kayirā.

<!-- @segment snp4.9:11.1 -->

Yehi vivitto vicareyya loke,

<!-- @segment snp4.9:11.2 -->

Na tāni uggayha vadeyya nāgo;

<!-- @segment snp4.9:11.3 -->

Jalambujaṁ kaṇḍakavārijaṁ yathā,

<!-- @segment snp4.9:11.4 -->

Jalena paṅkena canūpalittaṁ;

<!-- @segment snp4.9:11.5 -->

Evaṁ munī santivādo agiddho,

<!-- @segment snp4.9:11.6 -->

Kāme ca loke ca anūpalitto.

<!-- @segment snp4.9:12.1 -->

Na vedagū diṭṭhiyāyako na mutiyā,

<!-- @segment snp4.9:12.2 -->

Sa mānameti na hi tammayo so;

<!-- @segment snp4.9:12.3 -->

Na kammunā nopi sutena neyyo,

<!-- @segment snp4.9:12.4 -->

Anūpanīto sa nivesanesu.

<!-- @segment snp4.9:13.1 -->

Saññāvirattassa na santi ganthā,

<!-- @segment snp4.9:13.2 -->

Paññāvimuttassa na santi mohā;

<!-- @segment snp4.9:13.3 -->

Saññañca diṭṭhiñca ye aggahesuṁ,

<!-- @segment snp4.9:13.4 -->

Te ghaṭṭayantā vicaranti loke”ti.

<!-- @segment snp4.9:13.5 -->

Māgaṇḍiyasuttaṁ navamaṁ.
