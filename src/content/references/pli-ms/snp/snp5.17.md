<!-- @segment snp5.17:1.1 -->

“Jiṇṇohamasmi abalo vītavaṇṇo,

<!-- @segment snp5.17:1.2 -->

(iccāyasmā piṅgiyo)

<!-- @segment snp5.17:1.3 -->

Nettā na suddhā savanaṁ na phāsu;

<!-- @segment snp5.17:1.4 -->

Māhaṁ nassaṁ momuho antarāva,

<!-- @segment snp5.17:1.5 -->

Ācikkha dhammaṁ yamahaṁ vijaññaṁ;

<!-- @segment snp5.17:1.6 -->

Jātijarāya idha vippahānaṁ”.

<!-- @segment snp5.17:2.1 -->

“Disvāna rūpesu vihaññamāne,

<!-- @segment snp5.17:2.2 -->

(piṅgiyāti bhagavā)

<!-- @segment snp5.17:2.3 -->

Ruppanti rūpesu janā pamattā;

<!-- @segment snp5.17:2.4 -->

Tasmā tuvaṁ piṅgiya appamatto,

<!-- @segment snp5.17:2.5 -->

Jahassu rūpaṁ apunabbhavāya”.

<!-- @segment snp5.17:3.1 -->

“Disā catasso vidisā catasso,

<!-- @segment snp5.17:3.2 -->

Uddhaṁ adho dasa disā imāyo;

<!-- @segment snp5.17:3.3 -->

Na tuyhaṁ adiṭṭhaṁ asutaṁ amutaṁ,

<!-- @segment snp5.17:3.4 -->

Atho aviññātaṁ kiñcanamatthi loke;

<!-- @segment snp5.17:3.5 -->

Ācikkha dhammaṁ yamahaṁ vijaññaṁ,

<!-- @segment snp5.17:3.6 -->

Jātijarāya idha vippahānaṁ”.

<!-- @segment snp5.17:4.1 -->

“Taṇhādhipanne manuje pekkhamāno,

<!-- @segment snp5.17:4.2 -->

(piṅgiyāti bhagavā)

<!-- @segment snp5.17:4.3 -->

Santāpajāte jarasā parete;

<!-- @segment snp5.17:4.4 -->

Tasmā tuvaṁ piṅgiya appamatto,

<!-- @segment snp5.17:4.5 -->

Jahassu taṇhaṁ apunabbhavāyā”ti.

<!-- @segment snp5.17:4.6 -->

Piṅgiyamāṇavapucchā soḷasamā.
