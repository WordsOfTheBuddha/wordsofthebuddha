<!-- @segment snp1.7:1.1 -->

Evaṁ me sutaṁ—

<!-- @segment snp1.7:1.2 -->

ekaṁ samayaṁ bhagavā sāvatthiyaṁ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme.

<!-- @segment snp1.7:1.3 -->

Atha kho bhagavā pubbaṇhasamayaṁ nivāsetvā pattacīvaramādāya sāvatthiṁ piṇḍāya pāvisi.

<!-- @segment snp1.7:1.4 -->

Tena kho pana samayena aggikabhāradvājassa brāhmaṇassa nivesane aggi pajjalito hoti āhuti paggahitā.

<!-- @segment snp1.7:1.5 -->

Atha kho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṁ sapadānaṁ piṇḍāya caramāno yena aggikabhāradvājassa brāhmaṇassa nivesanaṁ tenupasaṅkami.

<!-- @segment snp1.7:2.1 -->

Addasā kho aggikabhāradvājo brāhmaṇo bhagavantaṁ dūratova āgacchantaṁ.

<!-- @segment snp1.7:2.2 -->

Disvāna bhagavantaṁ etadavoca:

<!-- @segment snp1.7:2.3 -->

“tatreva, muṇḍaka;

<!-- @segment snp1.7:2.4 -->

tatreva, samaṇaka;

<!-- @segment snp1.7:2.5 -->

tatreva, vasalaka, tiṭṭhāhī”ti.

<!-- @segment snp1.7:3.1 -->

Evaṁ vutte, bhagavā aggikabhāradvājaṁ brāhmaṇaṁ etadavoca:

<!-- @segment snp1.7:3.2 -->

“jānāsi pana tvaṁ, brāhmaṇa, vasalaṁ vā vasalakaraṇe vā dhamme”ti?

<!-- @segment snp1.7:3.3 -->

“Na khvāhaṁ, bho gotama, jānāmi vasalaṁ vā vasalakaraṇe vā dhamme;

<!-- @segment snp1.7:3.4 -->

sādhu me bhavaṁ gotamo tathā dhammaṁ desetu, yathāhaṁ jāneyyaṁ vasalaṁ vā vasalakaraṇe vā dhamme”ti.

<!-- @segment snp1.7:3.5 -->

“Tena hi, brāhmaṇa, suṇāhi, sādhukaṁ manasi karohi; bhāsissāmī”ti.

<!-- @segment snp1.7:3.6 -->

“Evaṁ, bho”ti kho aggikabhāradvājo brāhmaṇo bhagavato paccassosi.

<!-- @segment snp1.7:3.7 -->

Bhagavā etadavoca:

<!-- @segment snp1.7:4.1 -->

“Kodhano upanāhī ca,

<!-- @segment snp1.7:4.2 -->

pāpamakkhī ca yo naro;

<!-- @segment snp1.7:4.3 -->

Vipannadiṭṭhi māyāvī,

<!-- @segment snp1.7:4.4 -->

taṁ jaññā vasalo iti.

<!-- @segment snp1.7:5.1 -->

Ekajaṁ vā dvijaṁ vāpi,

<!-- @segment snp1.7:5.2 -->

yodha pāṇaṁ vihiṁsati;

<!-- @segment snp1.7:5.3 -->

Yassa pāṇe dayā natthi,

<!-- @segment snp1.7:5.4 -->

taṁ jaññā vasalo iti.

<!-- @segment snp1.7:6.1 -->

Yo hanti parirundhati,

<!-- @segment snp1.7:6.2 -->

gāmāni nigamāni ca;

<!-- @segment snp1.7:6.3 -->

Niggāhako samaññāto,

<!-- @segment snp1.7:6.4 -->

taṁ jaññā vasalo iti.

<!-- @segment snp1.7:7.1 -->

Gāme vā yadi vā raññe,

<!-- @segment snp1.7:7.2 -->

yaṁ paresaṁ mamāyitaṁ;

<!-- @segment snp1.7:7.3 -->

Theyyā adinnamādeti,

<!-- @segment snp1.7:7.4 -->

taṁ jaññā vasalo iti.

<!-- @segment snp1.7:8.1 -->

Yo have iṇamādāya,

<!-- @segment snp1.7:8.2 -->

cujjamāno palāyati;

<!-- @segment snp1.7:8.3 -->

Na hi te iṇamatthīti,

<!-- @segment snp1.7:8.4 -->

taṁ jaññā vasalo iti.

<!-- @segment snp1.7:9.1 -->

Yo ve kiñcikkhakamyatā,

<!-- @segment snp1.7:9.2 -->

panthasmiṁ vajantaṁ janaṁ;

<!-- @segment snp1.7:9.3 -->

Hantvā kiñcikkhamādeti,

<!-- @segment snp1.7:9.4 -->

taṁ jaññā vasalo iti.

<!-- @segment snp1.7:10.1 -->

Attahetu parahetu,

<!-- @segment snp1.7:10.2 -->

dhanahetu ca yo naro;

<!-- @segment snp1.7:10.3 -->

Sakkhipuṭṭho musā brūti,

<!-- @segment snp1.7:10.4 -->

taṁ jaññā vasalo iti.

<!-- @segment snp1.7:11.1 -->

Yo ñātīnaṁ sakhīnaṁ vā,

<!-- @segment snp1.7:11.2 -->

dāresu paṭidissati;

<!-- @segment snp1.7:11.3 -->

Sāhasā sampiyena vā,

<!-- @segment snp1.7:11.4 -->

taṁ jaññā vasalo iti.

<!-- @segment snp1.7:12.1 -->

Yo mātaraṁ pitaraṁ vā,

<!-- @segment snp1.7:12.2 -->

jiṇṇakaṁ gatayobbanaṁ;

<!-- @segment snp1.7:12.3 -->

Pahu santo na bharati,

<!-- @segment snp1.7:12.4 -->

taṁ jaññā vasalo iti.

<!-- @segment snp1.7:13.1 -->

Yo mātaraṁ pitaraṁ vā,

<!-- @segment snp1.7:13.2 -->

Bhātaraṁ bhaginiṁ sasuṁ;

<!-- @segment snp1.7:13.3 -->

Hanti roseti vācāya,

<!-- @segment snp1.7:13.4 -->

Taṁ jaññā vasalo iti.

<!-- @segment snp1.7:14.1 -->

Yo atthaṁ pucchito santo,

<!-- @segment snp1.7:14.2 -->

Anatthamanusāsati;

<!-- @segment snp1.7:14.3 -->

Paṭicchannena manteti,

<!-- @segment snp1.7:14.4 -->

Taṁ jaññā vasalo iti.

<!-- @segment snp1.7:15.1 -->

Yo katvā pāpakaṁ kammaṁ,

<!-- @segment snp1.7:15.2 -->

Mā maṁ jaññāti icchati;

<!-- @segment snp1.7:15.3 -->

Yo paṭicchannakammanto,

<!-- @segment snp1.7:15.4 -->

Taṁ jaññā vasalo iti.

<!-- @segment snp1.7:16.1 -->

Yo ve parakulaṁ gantvā,

<!-- @segment snp1.7:16.2 -->

Bhutvāna sucibhojanaṁ;

<!-- @segment snp1.7:16.3 -->

Āgataṁ nappaṭipūjeti,

<!-- @segment snp1.7:16.4 -->

Taṁ jaññā vasalo iti.

<!-- @segment snp1.7:17.1 -->

Yo brāhmaṇaṁ samaṇaṁ vā,

<!-- @segment snp1.7:17.2 -->

Aññaṁ vāpi vanibbakaṁ;

<!-- @segment snp1.7:17.3 -->

Musāvādena vañceti,

<!-- @segment snp1.7:17.4 -->

Taṁ jaññā vasalo iti.

<!-- @segment snp1.7:18.1 -->

Yo brāhmaṇaṁ samaṇaṁ vā,

<!-- @segment snp1.7:18.2 -->

Bhattakāle upaṭṭhite;

<!-- @segment snp1.7:18.3 -->

Roseti vācā na ca deti,

<!-- @segment snp1.7:18.4 -->

Taṁ jaññā vasalo iti.

<!-- @segment snp1.7:19.1 -->

Asataṁ yodha pabrūti,

<!-- @segment snp1.7:19.2 -->

Mohena paliguṇṭhito;

<!-- @segment snp1.7:19.3 -->

Kiñcikkhaṁ nijigīsāno,

<!-- @segment snp1.7:19.4 -->

Taṁ jaññā vasalo iti.

<!-- @segment snp1.7:20.1 -->

Yo cattānaṁ samukkaṁse,

<!-- @segment snp1.7:20.2 -->

Pare ca mavajānāti;

<!-- @segment snp1.7:20.3 -->

Nihīno sena mānena,

<!-- @segment snp1.7:20.4 -->

Taṁ jaññā vasalo iti.

<!-- @segment snp1.7:21.1 -->

Rosako kadariyo ca,

<!-- @segment snp1.7:21.2 -->

Pāpiccho maccharī saṭho;

<!-- @segment snp1.7:21.3 -->

Ahiriko anottappī,

<!-- @segment snp1.7:21.4 -->

Taṁ jaññā vasalo iti.

<!-- @segment snp1.7:22.1 -->

Yo buddhaṁ paribhāsati,

<!-- @segment snp1.7:22.2 -->

Atha vā tassa sāvakaṁ;

<!-- @segment snp1.7:22.3 -->

Paribbājaṁ gahaṭṭhaṁ vā,

<!-- @segment snp1.7:22.4 -->

Taṁ jaññā vasalo iti.

<!-- @segment snp1.7:23.1 -->

Yo ve anarahaṁ santo,

<!-- @segment snp1.7:23.2 -->

Arahaṁ paṭijānāti;

<!-- @segment snp1.7:23.3 -->

Coro sabrahmake loke,

<!-- @segment snp1.7:23.4 -->

Eso kho vasalādhamo;

<!-- @segment snp1.7:23.5 -->

Ete kho vasalā vuttā,

<!-- @segment snp1.7:23.6 -->

Mayā yete pakāsitā.

<!-- @segment snp1.7:24.1 -->

Na jaccā vasalo hoti,

<!-- @segment snp1.7:24.2 -->

Na jaccā hoti brāhmaṇo;

<!-- @segment snp1.7:24.3 -->

Kammunā vasalo hoti,

<!-- @segment snp1.7:24.4 -->

Kammunā hoti brāhmaṇo.

<!-- @segment snp1.7:25.1 -->

Tadamināpi jānātha,

<!-- @segment snp1.7:25.2 -->

Yathāmedaṁ nidassanaṁ;

<!-- @segment snp1.7:25.3 -->

Caṇḍālaputto sopāko,

<!-- @segment snp1.7:25.4 -->

Mātaṅgo iti vissuto.

<!-- @segment snp1.7:26.1 -->

So yasaṁ paramaṁ patto,

<!-- @segment snp1.7:26.2 -->

Mātaṅgo yaṁ sudullabhaṁ;

<!-- @segment snp1.7:26.3 -->

Āgacchuṁ tassupaṭṭhānaṁ,

<!-- @segment snp1.7:26.4 -->

Khattiyā brāhmaṇā bahū.

<!-- @segment snp1.7:27.1 -->

Devayānaṁ abhiruyha,

<!-- @segment snp1.7:27.2 -->

Virajaṁ so mahāpathaṁ;

<!-- @segment snp1.7:27.3 -->

Kāmarāgaṁ virājetvā,

<!-- @segment snp1.7:27.4 -->

Brahmalokūpago ahu;

<!-- @segment snp1.7:27.5 -->

Na naṁ jāti nivāresi,

<!-- @segment snp1.7:27.6 -->

Brahmalokūpapattiyā.

<!-- @segment snp1.7:28.1 -->

Ajjhāyakakule jātā,

<!-- @segment snp1.7:28.2 -->

Brāhmaṇā mantabandhavā;

<!-- @segment snp1.7:28.3 -->

Te ca pāpesu kammesu,

<!-- @segment snp1.7:28.4 -->

Abhiṇhamupadissare.

<!-- @segment snp1.7:29.1 -->

Diṭṭheva dhamme gārayhā,

<!-- @segment snp1.7:29.2 -->

Samparāye ca duggati;

<!-- @segment snp1.7:29.3 -->

Na ne jāti nivāreti,

<!-- @segment snp1.7:29.4 -->

Duggatyā garahāya vā.

<!-- @segment snp1.7:30.1 -->

Na jaccā vasalo hoti,

<!-- @segment snp1.7:30.2 -->

Na jaccā hoti brāhmaṇo;

<!-- @segment snp1.7:30.3 -->

Kammunā vasalo hoti,

<!-- @segment snp1.7:30.4 -->

Kammunā hoti brāhmaṇo”ti.

<!-- @segment snp1.7:31.1 -->

Evaṁ vutte, aggikabhāradvājo brāhmaṇo bhagavantaṁ etadavoca:

<!-- @segment snp1.7:31.2 -->

“abhikkantaṁ, bho gotama …pe…

<!-- @segment snp1.7:31.3 -->

upāsakaṁ maṁ bhavaṁ gotamo dhāretu ajjatagge pāṇupetaṁ saraṇaṁ gatan”ti.

<!-- @segment snp1.7:31.4 -->

Vasalasuttaṁ sattamaṁ.
