<!-- @segment snp2.7:1.1 -->

Evaṁ me sutaṁ—

<!-- @segment snp2.7:1.2 -->

ekaṁ samayaṁ bhagavā sāvatthiyaṁ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme.

<!-- @segment snp2.7:1.3 -->

Atha kho sambahulā kosalakā brāhmaṇamahāsālā jiṇṇā vuḍḍhā mahallakā addhagatā vayoanuppattā yena bhagavā tenupasaṅkamiṁsu; upasaṅkamitvā bhagavatā saddhiṁ sammodiṁsu.

<!-- @segment snp2.7:1.4 -->

Sammodanīyaṁ kathaṁ sāraṇīyaṁ vītisāretvā ekamantaṁ nisīdiṁsu. Ekamantaṁ nisinnā kho te brāhmaṇamahāsālā bhagavantaṁ etadavocuṁ:

<!-- @segment snp2.7:1.5 -->

“sandissanti nu kho, bho gotama, etarahi brāhmaṇā porāṇānaṁ brāhmaṇānaṁ brāhmaṇadhamme”ti?

<!-- @segment snp2.7:1.6 -->

“Na kho, brāhmaṇā, sandissanti etarahi brāhmaṇā porāṇānaṁ brāhmaṇānaṁ brāhmaṇadhamme”ti.

<!-- @segment snp2.7:1.7 -->

“Sādhu no bhavaṁ gotamo porāṇānaṁ brāhmaṇānaṁ brāhmaṇadhammaṁ bhāsatu, sace bhoto gotamassa agarū”ti.

<!-- @segment snp2.7:1.8 -->

“Tena hi, brāhmaṇā, suṇātha, sādhukaṁ manasi karotha, bhāsissāmī”ti.

<!-- @segment snp2.7:1.9 -->

“Evaṁ, bho”ti kho te brāhmaṇamahāsālā bhagavato paccassosuṁ.

<!-- @segment snp2.7:1.10 -->

Bhagavā etadavoca:

<!-- @segment snp2.7:2.1 -->

“Isayo pubbakā āsuṁ,

<!-- @segment snp2.7:2.2 -->

saññatattā tapassino;

<!-- @segment snp2.7:2.3 -->

Pañca kāmaguṇe hitvā,

<!-- @segment snp2.7:2.4 -->

attadatthamacārisuṁ.

<!-- @segment snp2.7:3.1 -->

Na pasū brāhmaṇānāsuṁ,

<!-- @segment snp2.7:3.2 -->

na hiraññaṁ na dhāniyaṁ;

<!-- @segment snp2.7:3.3 -->

Sajjhāyadhanadhaññāsuṁ,

<!-- @segment snp2.7:3.4 -->

brahmaṁ nidhimapālayuṁ.

<!-- @segment snp2.7:4.1 -->

Yaṁ nesaṁ pakataṁ āsi,

<!-- @segment snp2.7:4.2 -->

dvārabhattaṁ upaṭṭhitaṁ;

<!-- @segment snp2.7:4.3 -->

Saddhāpakatamesānaṁ,

<!-- @segment snp2.7:4.4 -->

dātave tadamaññisuṁ.

<!-- @segment snp2.7:5.1 -->

Nānārattehi vatthehi,

<!-- @segment snp2.7:5.2 -->

sayanehāvasathehi ca;

<!-- @segment snp2.7:5.3 -->

Phītā janapadā raṭṭhā,

<!-- @segment snp2.7:5.4 -->

te namassiṁsu brāhmaṇe.

<!-- @segment snp2.7:6.1 -->

Avajjhā brāhmaṇā āsuṁ,

<!-- @segment snp2.7:6.2 -->

ajeyyā dhammarakkhitā;

<!-- @segment snp2.7:6.3 -->

Na ne koci nivāresi,

<!-- @segment snp2.7:6.4 -->

kuladvāresu sabbaso.

<!-- @segment snp2.7:7.1 -->

Aṭṭhacattālīsaṁ vassāni,

<!-- @segment snp2.7:7.2 -->

(komāra) Brahmacariyaṁ cariṁsu te;

<!-- @segment snp2.7:7.3 -->

Vijjācaraṇapariyeṭṭhiṁ,

<!-- @segment snp2.7:7.4 -->

Acaruṁ brāhmaṇā pure.

<!-- @segment snp2.7:8.1 -->

Na brāhmaṇā aññamagamuṁ,

<!-- @segment snp2.7:8.2 -->

napi bhariyaṁ kiṇiṁsu te;

<!-- @segment snp2.7:8.3 -->

Sampiyeneva saṁvāsaṁ,

<!-- @segment snp2.7:8.4 -->

saṅgantvā samarocayuṁ.

<!-- @segment snp2.7:9.1 -->

Aññatra tamhā samayā,

<!-- @segment snp2.7:9.2 -->

utuveramaṇiṁ pati;

<!-- @segment snp2.7:9.3 -->

Antarā methunaṁ dhammaṁ,

<!-- @segment snp2.7:9.4 -->

nāssu gacchanti brāhmaṇā.

<!-- @segment snp2.7:10.1 -->

Brahmacariyañca sīlañca,

<!-- @segment snp2.7:10.2 -->

ajjavaṁ maddavaṁ tapaṁ;

<!-- @segment snp2.7:10.3 -->

Soraccaṁ avihiṁsañca,

<!-- @segment snp2.7:10.4 -->

khantiñcāpi avaṇṇayuṁ.

<!-- @segment snp2.7:11.1 -->

Yo nesaṁ paramo āsi,

<!-- @segment snp2.7:11.2 -->

Brahmā daḷhaparakkamo;

<!-- @segment snp2.7:11.3 -->

Sa vāpi methunaṁ dhammaṁ,

<!-- @segment snp2.7:11.4 -->

Supinantepi nāgamā.

<!-- @segment snp2.7:12.1 -->

Tassa vattamanusikkhantā,

<!-- @segment snp2.7:12.2 -->

Idheke viññujātikā;

<!-- @segment snp2.7:12.3 -->

Brahmacariyañca sīlañca,

<!-- @segment snp2.7:12.4 -->

Khantiñcāpi avaṇṇayuṁ.

<!-- @segment snp2.7:13.1 -->

Taṇḍulaṁ sayanaṁ vatthaṁ,

<!-- @segment snp2.7:13.2 -->

Sappitelañca yāciya;

<!-- @segment snp2.7:13.3 -->

Dhammena samodhānetvā,

<!-- @segment snp2.7:13.4 -->

Tato yaññamakappayuṁ.

<!-- @segment snp2.7:14.1 -->

Upaṭṭhitasmiṁ yaññasmiṁ,

<!-- @segment snp2.7:14.2 -->

Nāssu gāvo haniṁsu te;

<!-- @segment snp2.7:14.3 -->

Yathā mātā pitā bhātā,

<!-- @segment snp2.7:14.4 -->

Aññe vāpi ca ñātakā;

<!-- @segment snp2.7:14.5 -->

Gāvo no paramā mittā,

<!-- @segment snp2.7:14.6 -->

Yāsu jāyanti osadhā.

<!-- @segment snp2.7:15.1 -->

Annadā baladā cetā,

<!-- @segment snp2.7:15.2 -->

Vaṇṇadā sukhadā tathā;

<!-- @segment snp2.7:15.3 -->

Etamatthavasaṁ ñatvā,

<!-- @segment snp2.7:15.4 -->

Nāssu gāvo haniṁsu te.

<!-- @segment snp2.7:16.1 -->

Sukhumālā mahākāyā,

<!-- @segment snp2.7:16.2 -->

Vaṇṇavanto yasassino;

<!-- @segment snp2.7:16.3 -->

Brāhmaṇā sehi dhammehi,

<!-- @segment snp2.7:16.4 -->

Kiccākiccesu ussukā;

<!-- @segment snp2.7:16.5 -->

Yāva loke avattiṁsu,

<!-- @segment snp2.7:16.6 -->

Sukhamedhitthayaṁ pajā.

<!-- @segment snp2.7:17.1 -->

Tesaṁ āsi vipallāso,

<!-- @segment snp2.7:17.2 -->

Disvāna aṇuto aṇuṁ;

<!-- @segment snp2.7:17.3 -->

Rājino ca viyākāraṁ,

<!-- @segment snp2.7:17.4 -->

Nāriyo samalaṅkatā.

<!-- @segment snp2.7:18.1 -->

Rathe cājaññasaṁyutte,

<!-- @segment snp2.7:18.2 -->

Sukate cittasibbane;

<!-- @segment snp2.7:18.3 -->

Nivesane nivese ca,

<!-- @segment snp2.7:18.4 -->

Vibhatte bhāgaso mite.

<!-- @segment snp2.7:19.1 -->

Gomaṇḍalaparibyūḷhaṁ,

<!-- @segment snp2.7:19.2 -->

Nārīvaragaṇāyutaṁ;

<!-- @segment snp2.7:19.3 -->

Uḷāraṁ mānusaṁ bhogaṁ,

<!-- @segment snp2.7:19.4 -->

Abhijjhāyiṁsu brāhmaṇā.

<!-- @segment snp2.7:20.1 -->

Te tattha mante ganthetvā,

<!-- @segment snp2.7:20.2 -->

Okkākaṁ tadupāgamuṁ;

<!-- @segment snp2.7:20.3 -->

Pahūtadhanadhaññosi,

<!-- @segment snp2.7:20.4 -->

Yajassu bahu te vittaṁ;

<!-- @segment snp2.7:20.5 -->

Yajassu bahu te dhanaṁ.

<!-- @segment snp2.7:21.1 -->

Tato ca rājā saññatto,

<!-- @segment snp2.7:21.2 -->

Brāhmaṇehi rathesabho;

<!-- @segment snp2.7:21.3 -->

Assamedhaṁ purisamedhaṁ,

<!-- @segment snp2.7:21.4 -->

Sammāpāsaṁ vājapeyyaṁ niraggaḷaṁ;

<!-- @segment snp2.7:21.5 -->

Ete yāge yajitvāna,

<!-- @segment snp2.7:21.6 -->

Brāhmaṇānamadā dhanaṁ.

<!-- @segment snp2.7:22.1 -->

Gāvo sayanañca vatthañca,

<!-- @segment snp2.7:22.2 -->

Nāriyo samalaṅkatā;

<!-- @segment snp2.7:22.3 -->

Rathe cājaññasaṁyutte,

<!-- @segment snp2.7:22.4 -->

Sukate cittasibbane.

<!-- @segment snp2.7:23.1 -->

Nivesanāni rammāni,

<!-- @segment snp2.7:23.2 -->

Suvibhattāni bhāgaso;

<!-- @segment snp2.7:23.3 -->

Nānādhaññassa pūretvā,

<!-- @segment snp2.7:23.4 -->

Brāhmaṇānamadā dhanaṁ.

<!-- @segment snp2.7:24.1 -->

Te ca tattha dhanaṁ laddhā,

<!-- @segment snp2.7:24.2 -->

Sannidhiṁ samarocayuṁ;

<!-- @segment snp2.7:24.3 -->

Tesaṁ icchāvatiṇṇānaṁ,

<!-- @segment snp2.7:24.4 -->

Bhiyyo taṇhā pavaḍḍhatha;

<!-- @segment snp2.7:24.5 -->

Te tattha mante ganthetvā,

<!-- @segment snp2.7:24.6 -->

Okkākaṁ puna mupāgamuṁ.

<!-- @segment snp2.7:25.1 -->

Yathā āpo ca pathavī ca,

<!-- @segment snp2.7:25.2 -->

Hiraññaṁ dhanadhāniyaṁ;

<!-- @segment snp2.7:25.3 -->

Evaṁ gāvo manussānaṁ,

<!-- @segment snp2.7:25.4 -->

Parikkhāro so hi pāṇinaṁ;

<!-- @segment snp2.7:25.5 -->

Yajassu bahu te vittaṁ,

<!-- @segment snp2.7:25.6 -->

Yajassu bahu te dhanaṁ.

<!-- @segment snp2.7:26.1 -->

Tato ca rājā saññatto,

<!-- @segment snp2.7:26.2 -->

Brāhmaṇehi rathesabho;

<!-- @segment snp2.7:26.3 -->

Nekā satasahassiyo,

<!-- @segment snp2.7:26.4 -->

Gāvo yaññe aghātayi.

<!-- @segment snp2.7:27.1 -->

Na pādā na visāṇena,

<!-- @segment snp2.7:27.2 -->

Nāssu hiṁsanti kenaci;

<!-- @segment snp2.7:27.3 -->

Gāvo eḷakasamānā,

<!-- @segment snp2.7:27.4 -->

Soratā kumbhadūhanā;

<!-- @segment snp2.7:27.5 -->

Tā visāṇe gahetvāna,

<!-- @segment snp2.7:27.6 -->

Rājā satthena ghātayi.

<!-- @segment snp2.7:28.1 -->

Tato devā pitaro ca,

<!-- @segment snp2.7:28.2 -->

Indo asurarakkhasā;

<!-- @segment snp2.7:28.3 -->

Adhammo iti pakkanduṁ,

<!-- @segment snp2.7:28.4 -->

Yaṁ satthaṁ nipatī gave.

<!-- @segment snp2.7:29.1 -->

Tayo rogā pure āsuṁ,

<!-- @segment snp2.7:29.2 -->

Icchā anasanaṁ jarā;

<!-- @segment snp2.7:29.3 -->

Pasūnañca samārambhā,

<!-- @segment snp2.7:29.4 -->

Aṭṭhānavutimāgamuṁ.

<!-- @segment snp2.7:30.1 -->

Eso adhammo daṇḍānaṁ,

<!-- @segment snp2.7:30.2 -->

Okkanto purāṇo ahu;

<!-- @segment snp2.7:30.3 -->

Adūsikāyo haññanti,

<!-- @segment snp2.7:30.4 -->

Dhammā dhaṁsanti yājakā.

<!-- @segment snp2.7:31.1 -->

Evameso aṇudhammo,

<!-- @segment snp2.7:31.2 -->

Porāṇo viññugarahito;

<!-- @segment snp2.7:31.3 -->

Yattha edisakaṁ passati,

<!-- @segment snp2.7:31.4 -->

Yājakaṁ garahatī jano.

<!-- @segment snp2.7:32.1 -->

Evaṁ dhamme viyāpanne,

<!-- @segment snp2.7:32.2 -->

Vibhinnā suddavessikā;

<!-- @segment snp2.7:32.3 -->

Puthū vibhinnā khattiyā,

<!-- @segment snp2.7:32.4 -->

Patiṁ bhariyāvamaññatha.

<!-- @segment snp2.7:33.1 -->

Khattiyā brahmabandhū ca,

<!-- @segment snp2.7:33.2 -->

Ye caññe gottarakkhitā;

<!-- @segment snp2.7:33.3 -->

Jātivādaṁ nirākatvā,

<!-- @segment snp2.7:33.4 -->

Kāmānaṁ vasamanvagun”ti.

<!-- @segment snp2.7:34.1 -->

Evaṁ vutte, te brāhmaṇamahāsālā bhagavantaṁ etadavocuṁ:

<!-- @segment snp2.7:34.2 -->

“abhikkantaṁ, bho gotama …pe…

<!-- @segment snp2.7:34.3 -->

upāsake no bhavaṁ gotamo dhāretu ajjatagge pāṇupete saraṇaṁ gate”ti.

<!-- @segment snp2.7:34.4 -->

Brāhmaṇadhammikasuttaṁ sattamaṁ.
