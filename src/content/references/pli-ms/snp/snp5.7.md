<!-- @segment snp5.7:1.1 -->

“Eko ahaṁ sakka mahantamoghaṁ,

<!-- @segment snp5.7:1.2 -->

(iccāyasmā upasīvo)

<!-- @segment snp5.7:1.3 -->

Anissito no visahāmi tārituṁ;

<!-- @segment snp5.7:1.4 -->

Ārammaṇaṁ brūhi samantacakkhu,

<!-- @segment snp5.7:1.5 -->

Yaṁ nissito oghamimaṁ tareyyaṁ”.

<!-- @segment snp5.7:2.1 -->

“Ākiñcaññaṁ pekkhamāno satimā,

<!-- @segment snp5.7:2.2 -->

(upasīvāti bhagavā)

<!-- @segment snp5.7:2.3 -->

Natthīti nissāya tarassu oghaṁ;

<!-- @segment snp5.7:2.4 -->

Kāme pahāya virato kathāhi,

<!-- @segment snp5.7:2.5 -->

Taṇhakkhayaṁ nattamahābhipassa”.

<!-- @segment snp5.7:3.1 -->

“Sabbesu kāmesu yo vītarāgo,

<!-- @segment snp5.7:3.2 -->

(iccāyasmā upasīvo)

<!-- @segment snp5.7:3.3 -->

Ākiñcaññaṁ nissito hitvā maññaṁ;

<!-- @segment snp5.7:3.4 -->

Saññāvimokkhe parame vimutto,

<!-- @segment snp5.7:3.5 -->

Tiṭṭhe nu so tattha anānuyāyī”.

<!-- @segment snp5.7:4.1 -->

“Sabbesu kāmesu yo vītarāgo,

<!-- @segment snp5.7:4.2 -->

(upasīvāti bhagavā)

<!-- @segment snp5.7:4.3 -->

Ākiñcaññaṁ nissito hitvā maññaṁ;

<!-- @segment snp5.7:4.4 -->

Saññāvimokkhe parame vimutto,

<!-- @segment snp5.7:4.5 -->

Tiṭṭheyya so tattha anānuyāyī”.

<!-- @segment snp5.7:5.1 -->

“Tiṭṭhe ce so tattha anānuyāyī,

<!-- @segment snp5.7:5.2 -->

Pūgampi vassānaṁ samantacakkhu;

<!-- @segment snp5.7:5.3 -->

Tattheva so sītisiyā vimutto,

<!-- @segment snp5.7:5.4 -->

Cavetha viññāṇaṁ tathāvidhassa”.

<!-- @segment snp5.7:6.1 -->

“Accī yathā vātavegena khittā,

<!-- @segment snp5.7:6.2 -->

(upasīvāti bhagavā)

<!-- @segment snp5.7:6.3 -->

Atthaṁ paleti na upeti saṅkhaṁ;

<!-- @segment snp5.7:6.4 -->

Evaṁ munī nāmakāyā vimutto,

<!-- @segment snp5.7:6.5 -->

Atthaṁ paleti na upeti saṅkhaṁ”.

<!-- @segment snp5.7:7.1 -->

“Atthaṅgato so uda vā so natthi,

<!-- @segment snp5.7:7.2 -->

Udāhu ve sassatiyā arogo;

<!-- @segment snp5.7:7.3 -->

Taṁ me munī sādhu viyākarohi,

<!-- @segment snp5.7:7.4 -->

Tathā hi te vidito esa dhammo”.

<!-- @segment snp5.7:8.1 -->

“Atthaṅgatassa na pamāṇamatthi,

<!-- @segment snp5.7:8.2 -->

(upasīvāti bhagavā)

<!-- @segment snp5.7:8.3 -->

Yena naṁ vajjuṁ taṁ tassa natthi;

<!-- @segment snp5.7:8.4 -->

Sabbesu dhammesu samohatesu,

<!-- @segment snp5.7:8.5 -->

Samūhatā vādapathāpi sabbe”ti.

<!-- @segment snp5.7:8.6 -->

Upasīvamāṇavapucchā chaṭṭhī.
