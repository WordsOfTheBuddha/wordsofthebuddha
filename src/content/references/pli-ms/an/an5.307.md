<!-- @segment an5.307:1.1 -->

“Rāgassa, bhikkhave, abhiññāya pañca dhammā bhāvetabbā.

<!-- @segment an5.307:1.2 -->

Katame pañca?

<!-- @segment an5.307:1.3 -->

Saddhābalaṁ, vīriyabalaṁ, satibalaṁ, samādhibalaṁ, paññābalaṁ—

<!-- @segment an5.307:1.4 -->

rāgassa, bhikkhave, abhiññāya ime pañca dhammā bhāvetabbā”ti.
