<!-- @segment an6.35:1.1 -->

“Chayime, bhikkhave, dhammā vijjābhāgiyā.

<!-- @segment an6.35:1.2 -->

Katame cha?

<!-- @segment an6.35:1.3 -->

Aniccasaññā, anicce dukkhasaññā, dukkhe anattasaññā, pahānasaññā, virāgasaññā, nirodhasaññā—

<!-- @segment an6.35:1.4 -->

ime kho, bhikkhave, cha dhammā vijjābhāgiyā”ti.

<!-- @segment an6.35:1.5 -->

Pañcamaṁ.
