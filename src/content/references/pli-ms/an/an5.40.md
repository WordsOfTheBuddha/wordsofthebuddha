<!-- @segment an5.40:1.1 -->

“Himavantaṁ, bhikkhave, pabbatarājaṁ nissāya mahāsālā pañcahi vaḍḍhīhi vaḍḍhanti.

<!-- @segment an5.40:1.2 -->

Katamāhi pañcahi?

<!-- @segment an5.40:1.3 -->

Sākhāpattapalāsena vaḍḍhanti;

<!-- @segment an5.40:1.4 -->

tacena vaḍḍhanti;

<!-- @segment an5.40:1.5 -->

papaṭikāya vaḍḍhanti;

<!-- @segment an5.40:1.6 -->

pheggunā vaḍḍhanti;

<!-- @segment an5.40:1.7 -->

sārena vaḍḍhanti.

<!-- @segment an5.40:1.8 -->

Himavantaṁ, bhikkhave, pabbatarājaṁ nissāya mahāsālā imāhi pañcahi vaḍḍhīhi vaḍḍhanti.

<!-- @segment an5.40:1.9 -->

Evamevaṁ kho, bhikkhave, saddhaṁ kulaputtaṁ nissāya antojano pañcahi vaḍḍhīhi vaḍḍhati.

<!-- @segment an5.40:1.10 -->

Katamāhi pañcahi?

<!-- @segment an5.40:1.11 -->

Saddhāya vaḍḍhati;

<!-- @segment an5.40:1.12 -->

sīlena vaḍḍhati;

<!-- @segment an5.40:1.13 -->

sutena vaḍḍhati;

<!-- @segment an5.40:1.14 -->

cāgena vaḍḍhati;

<!-- @segment an5.40:1.15 -->

paññāya vaḍḍhati.

<!-- @segment an5.40:1.16 -->

Saddhaṁ, bhikkhave, kulaputtaṁ nissāya antojano imāhi pañcahi vaḍḍhīhi vaḍḍhatīti.

<!-- @segment an5.40:2.1 -->

Yathā hi pabbato selo,

<!-- @segment an5.40:2.2 -->

araññasmiṁ brahāvane;

<!-- @segment an5.40:2.3 -->

Taṁ rukkhā upanissāya,

<!-- @segment an5.40:2.4 -->

vaḍḍhante te vanappatī.

<!-- @segment an5.40:3.1 -->

Tatheva sīlasampannaṁ,

<!-- @segment an5.40:3.2 -->

Saddhaṁ kulaputtaṁ imaṁ;

<!-- @segment an5.40:3.3 -->

Upanissāya vaḍḍhanti,

<!-- @segment an5.40:3.4 -->

Puttadārā ca bandhavā;

<!-- @segment an5.40:3.5 -->

Amaccā ñātisaṅghā ca,

<!-- @segment an5.40:3.6 -->

Ye cassa anujīvino.

<!-- @segment an5.40:4.1 -->

Tyassa sīlavato sīlaṁ,

<!-- @segment an5.40:4.2 -->

cāgaṁ sucaritāni ca;

<!-- @segment an5.40:4.3 -->

Passamānānukubbanti,

<!-- @segment an5.40:4.4 -->

ye bhavanti vicakkhaṇā.

<!-- @segment an5.40:5.1 -->

Idha dhammaṁ caritvāna,

<!-- @segment an5.40:5.2 -->

maggaṁ sugatigāminaṁ;

<!-- @segment an5.40:5.3 -->

Nandino devalokasmiṁ,

<!-- @segment an5.40:5.4 -->

modanti kāmakāmino”ti.

<!-- @segment an5.40:5.5 -->

Dasamaṁ.

<!-- @segment an5.40:5.6 -->

Sumanavaggo catuttho.

<!-- @segment an5.40:6.0 -->

Tassuddānaṁ

<!-- @segment an5.40:6.1 -->

Sumanā cundī uggaho,

<!-- @segment an5.40:6.2 -->

sīho dānānisaṁsako;

<!-- @segment an5.40:6.3 -->

Kālabhojanasaddhā ca,

<!-- @segment an5.40:6.4 -->

puttasālehi te dasāti.
