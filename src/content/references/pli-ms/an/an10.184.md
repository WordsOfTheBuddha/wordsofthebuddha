<!-- @segment an10.184:1.1 -->

“Sāvajjañca vo, bhikkhave, dhammaṁ desessāmi anavajjañca.

<!-- @segment an10.184:1.2 -->

Taṁ suṇātha …pe…

<!-- @segment an10.184:1.3 -->

katamo ca, bhikkhave, sāvajjo dhammo?

<!-- @segment an10.184:1.4 -->

Pāṇātipāto …pe…

<!-- @segment an10.184:1.5 -->

micchādiṭṭhi—

<!-- @segment an10.184:1.6 -->

ayaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, sāvajjo dhammo.

<!-- @segment an10.184:2.1 -->

Katamo ca, bhikkhave, anavajjo dhammo?

<!-- @segment an10.184:2.2 -->

Pāṇātipātā veramaṇī …pe…

<!-- @segment an10.184:2.3 -->

sammādiṭṭhi—

<!-- @segment an10.184:2.4 -->

ayaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, anavajjo dhammo”ti.

<!-- @segment an10.184:2.5 -->

Sattamaṁ.
