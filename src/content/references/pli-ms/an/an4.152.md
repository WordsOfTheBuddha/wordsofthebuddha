<!-- @segment an4.152:1.1 -->

“Cattārimāni, bhikkhave, balāni.

<!-- @segment an4.152:1.2 -->

Katamāni cattāri?

<!-- @segment an4.152:1.3 -->

Saddhābalaṁ, vīriyabalaṁ, satibalaṁ, samādhibalaṁ—

<!-- @segment an4.152:1.4 -->

Imāni kho, bhikkhave, cattāri balānī”ti.

<!-- @segment an4.152:1.5 -->

Dutiyaṁ.
