<!-- @segment an10.185:1.1 -->

“Tapanīyañca vo, bhikkhave, dhammaṁ desessāmi atapanīyañca.

<!-- @segment an10.185:1.2 -->

Taṁ suṇātha …pe…

<!-- @segment an10.185:1.3 -->

katamo ca, bhikkhave, tapanīyo dhammo?

<!-- @segment an10.185:1.4 -->

Pāṇātipāto …pe…

<!-- @segment an10.185:1.5 -->

micchādiṭṭhi—

<!-- @segment an10.185:1.6 -->

ayaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, tapanīyo dhammo.

<!-- @segment an10.185:2.1 -->

Katamo ca, bhikkhave, atapanīyo dhammo?

<!-- @segment an10.185:2.2 -->

Pāṇātipātā veramaṇī …pe…

<!-- @segment an10.185:2.3 -->

sammādiṭṭhi—

<!-- @segment an10.185:2.4 -->

ayaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, atapanīyo dhammo”ti.

<!-- @segment an10.185:2.5 -->

Aṭṭhamaṁ.
