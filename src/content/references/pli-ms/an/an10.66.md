<!-- @segment an10.66:1.1 -->

Ekaṁ samayaṁ āyasmā sāriputto magadhesu viharati nālakagāmake.

<!-- @segment an10.66:1.2 -->

Atha kho sāmaṇḍakāni paribbājako yenāyasmā sāriputto tenupasaṅkami; upasaṅkamitvā āyasmatā sāriputtena saddhiṁ sammodi.

<!-- @segment an10.66:1.3 -->

Sammodanīyaṁ kathaṁ sāraṇīyaṁ vītisāretvā ekamantaṁ nisīdi. Ekamantaṁ nisinno kho sāmaṇḍakāni paribbājako āyasmantaṁ sāriputtaṁ etadavoca:

<!-- @segment an10.66:2.1 -->

“Kiṁ nu kho, āvuso, sāriputta, imasmiṁ dhammavinaye sukhaṁ, kiṁ dukkhan”ti?

<!-- @segment an10.66:2.2 -->

“Anabhirati kho, āvuso, imasmiṁ dhammavinaye dukkhā, abhirati sukhā.

<!-- @segment an10.66:2.3 -->

Anabhiratiyā, āvuso, sati idaṁ dukkhaṁ pāṭikaṅkhaṁ—

<!-- @segment an10.66:2.4 -->

gacchantopi sukhaṁ sātaṁ nādhigacchati,

<!-- @segment an10.66:2.5 -->

ṭhitopi …

<!-- @segment an10.66:2.6 -->

nisinnopi …

<!-- @segment an10.66:2.7 -->

sayānopi …

<!-- @segment an10.66:2.8 -->

gāmagatopi …

<!-- @segment an10.66:2.9 -->

araññagatopi …

<!-- @segment an10.66:2.10 -->

rukkhamūlagatopi …

<!-- @segment an10.66:2.11 -->

suññāgāragatopi …

<!-- @segment an10.66:2.12 -->

abbhokāsagatopi …

<!-- @segment an10.66:2.13 -->

bhikkhumajjhagatopi sukhaṁ sātaṁ nādhigacchati.

<!-- @segment an10.66:2.14 -->

Anabhiratiyā, āvuso, sati idaṁ dukkhaṁ pāṭikaṅkhaṁ.

<!-- @segment an10.66:3.1 -->

Abhiratiyā, āvuso, sati idaṁ sukhaṁ pāṭikaṅkhaṁ—

<!-- @segment an10.66:3.2 -->

gacchantopi sukhaṁ sātaṁ adhigacchati,

<!-- @segment an10.66:3.3 -->

ṭhitopi …

<!-- @segment an10.66:3.4 -->

nisinnopi …

<!-- @segment an10.66:3.5 -->

sayānopi …

<!-- @segment an10.66:3.6 -->

gāmagatopi …

<!-- @segment an10.66:3.7 -->

araññagatopi …

<!-- @segment an10.66:3.8 -->

rukkhamūlagatopi …

<!-- @segment an10.66:3.9 -->

suññāgāragatopi …

<!-- @segment an10.66:3.10 -->

abbhokāsagatopi …

<!-- @segment an10.66:3.11 -->

bhikkhumajjhagatopi sukhaṁ sātaṁ adhigacchati.

<!-- @segment an10.66:3.12 -->

Abhiratiyā, āvuso, sati idaṁ sukhaṁ pāṭikaṅkhan”ti.

<!-- @segment an10.66:3.13 -->

Chaṭṭhaṁ.
