<!-- @segment an10.192:1.1 -->

“Sappurisadhammañca vo, bhikkhave, desessāmi asappurisadhammañca.

<!-- @segment an10.192:1.2 -->

Taṁ suṇātha …pe…

<!-- @segment an10.192:1.3 -->

katamo ca, bhikkhave, asappurisadhammo?

<!-- @segment an10.192:1.4 -->

Pāṇātipāto …pe…

<!-- @segment an10.192:1.5 -->

micchādiṭṭhi—

<!-- @segment an10.192:1.6 -->

ayaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, asappurisadhammo.

<!-- @segment an10.192:2.1 -->

Katamo ca, bhikkhave, sappurisadhammo?

<!-- @segment an10.192:2.2 -->

Pāṇātipātā veramaṇī …pe…

<!-- @segment an10.192:2.3 -->

sammādiṭṭhi—

<!-- @segment an10.192:2.4 -->

ayaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, sappurisadhammo”ti.

<!-- @segment an10.192:2.5 -->

Catutthaṁ.
