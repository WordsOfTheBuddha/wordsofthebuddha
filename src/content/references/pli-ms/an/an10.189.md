<!-- @segment an10.189:1.1 -->

“Ariyamaggañca vo, bhikkhave, desessāmi anariyamaggañca.

<!-- @segment an10.189:1.2 -->

Taṁ suṇātha …pe…

<!-- @segment an10.189:1.3 -->

katamo ca, bhikkhave, anariyo maggo?

<!-- @segment an10.189:1.4 -->

Pāṇātipāto …pe…

<!-- @segment an10.189:1.5 -->

micchādiṭṭhi—

<!-- @segment an10.189:1.6 -->

ayaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, anariyo maggo.

<!-- @segment an10.189:2.1 -->

Katamo ca, bhikkhave, ariyo maggo?

<!-- @segment an10.189:2.2 -->

Pāṇātipātā veramaṇī …pe…

<!-- @segment an10.189:2.3 -->

sammādiṭṭhi—

<!-- @segment an10.189:2.4 -->

ayaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, ariyo maggo”ti.

<!-- @segment an10.189:2.5 -->

Paṭhamaṁ.
