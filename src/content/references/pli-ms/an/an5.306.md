<!-- @segment an5.306:1.1 -->

“Rāgassa, bhikkhave, abhiññāya pañca dhammā bhāvetabbā.

<!-- @segment an5.306:1.2 -->

Katame pañca?

<!-- @segment an5.306:1.3 -->

Saddhindriyaṁ, vīriyindriyaṁ, satindriyaṁ, samādhindriyaṁ, paññindriyaṁ—

<!-- @segment an5.306:1.4 -->

rāgassa, bhikkhave, abhiññāya ime pañca dhammā bhāvetabbā”ti.
