<!-- @segment an6.8:1.1 -->

“Chayimāni, bhikkhave, anuttariyāni.

<!-- @segment an6.8:1.2 -->

Katamāni cha?

<!-- @segment an6.8:1.3 -->

Dassanānuttariyaṁ, savanānuttariyaṁ, lābhānuttariyaṁ, sikkhānuttariyaṁ, pāricariyānuttariyaṁ, anussatānuttariyaṁ.

<!-- @segment an6.8:1.4 -->

Imāni kho, bhikkhave, cha anuttariyānī”ti.

<!-- @segment an6.8:1.5 -->

Aṭṭhamaṁ.
