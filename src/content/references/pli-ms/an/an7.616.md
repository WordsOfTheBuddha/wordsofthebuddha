<!-- @segment an7.616:1.1 -->

“Rāgassa, bhikkhave, abhiññāya satta dhammā bhāvetabbā.

<!-- @segment an7.616:1.2 -->

Katame satta?

<!-- @segment an7.616:1.3 -->

Aniccasaññā, anattasaññā, asubhasaññā, ādīnavasaññā, pahānasaññā, virāgasaññā, nirodhasaññā—

<!-- @segment an7.616:1.4 -->

rāgassa, bhikkhave, abhiññāya ime satta dhammā bhāvetabbā”ti.
