<!-- @segment an10.188:1.1 -->

“Dukkhavipākañca vo, bhikkhave, dhammaṁ desessāmi sukhavipākañca.

<!-- @segment an10.188:1.2 -->

Taṁ suṇātha …pe…

<!-- @segment an10.188:1.3 -->

katamo ca, bhikkhave, dukkhavipāko dhammo?

<!-- @segment an10.188:1.4 -->

Pāṇātipāto …pe…

<!-- @segment an10.188:1.5 -->

micchādiṭṭhi—

<!-- @segment an10.188:1.6 -->

ayaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, dukkhavipāko dhammo.

<!-- @segment an10.188:2.1 -->

Katamo ca, bhikkhave, sukhavipāko dhammo?

<!-- @segment an10.188:2.2 -->

Pāṇātipātā veramaṇī …pe…

<!-- @segment an10.188:2.3 -->

sammādiṭṭhi—

<!-- @segment an10.188:2.4 -->

ayaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, sukhavipāko dhammo”ti.

<!-- @segment an10.188:2.5 -->

Ekādasamaṁ.

<!-- @segment an10.188:2.6 -->

Sādhuvaggo tatiyo.
