<!-- @segment an4.32:1.1 -->

“Cattārimāni, bhikkhave, saṅgahavatthūni.

<!-- @segment an4.32:1.2 -->

Katamāni cattāri?

<!-- @segment an4.32:1.3 -->

Dānaṁ, peyyavajjaṁ, atthacariyā, samānattatā—

<!-- @segment an4.32:1.4 -->

Imāni kho, bhikkhave, cattāri saṅgahavatthūnīti.

<!-- @segment an4.32:2.1 -->

Dānañca peyyavajjañca,

<!-- @segment an4.32:2.2 -->

atthacariyā ca yā idha;

<!-- @segment an4.32:2.3 -->

Samānattatā ca dhammesu,

<!-- @segment an4.32:2.4 -->

tattha tattha yathārahaṁ;

<!-- @segment an4.32:2.5 -->

Ete kho saṅgahā loke,

<!-- @segment an4.32:2.6 -->

rathassāṇīva yāyato.

<!-- @segment an4.32:3.1 -->

Ete ca saṅgahā nāssu,

<!-- @segment an4.32:3.2 -->

na mātā puttakāraṇā;

<!-- @segment an4.32:3.3 -->

Labhetha mānaṁ pūjaṁ vā,

<!-- @segment an4.32:3.4 -->

pitā vā puttakāraṇā.

<!-- @segment an4.32:4.1 -->

Yasmā ca saṅgahā ete,

<!-- @segment an4.32:4.2 -->

samavekkhanti paṇḍitā;

<!-- @segment an4.32:4.3 -->

Tasmā mahattaṁ papponti,

<!-- @segment an4.32:4.4 -->

pāsaṁsā ca bhavanti te”ti.

<!-- @segment an4.32:4.5 -->

Dutiyaṁ.
