<!-- @segment an4.151:1.1 -->

“Cattārimāni, bhikkhave, indriyāni.

<!-- @segment an4.151:1.2 -->

Katamāni cattāri?

<!-- @segment an4.151:1.3 -->

Saddhindriyaṁ, vīriyindriyaṁ, satindriyaṁ, samādhindriyaṁ—

<!-- @segment an4.151:1.4 -->

Imāni kho, bhikkhave, cattāri indriyānī”ti.

<!-- @segment an4.151:1.5 -->

Paṭhamaṁ.
