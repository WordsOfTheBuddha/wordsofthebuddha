<!-- @segment an4.153:1.1 -->

“Cattārimāni, bhikkhave, balāni.

<!-- @segment an4.153:1.2 -->

Katamāni cattāri?

<!-- @segment an4.153:1.3 -->

Paññābalaṁ, vīriyabalaṁ, anavajjabalaṁ, saṅgahabalaṁ—

<!-- @segment an4.153:1.4 -->

Imāni kho, bhikkhave, cattāri balānī”ti.

<!-- @segment an4.153:1.5 -->

Tatiyaṁ.
