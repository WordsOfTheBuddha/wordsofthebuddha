<!-- @segment an4.119:1.1 -->

“Cattārimāni, bhikkhave, bhayāni.

<!-- @segment an4.119:1.2 -->

Katamāni cattāri?

<!-- @segment an4.119:1.3 -->

Jātibhayaṁ, jarābhayaṁ, byādhibhayaṁ, maraṇabhayaṁ—

<!-- @segment an4.119:1.4 -->

Imāni kho, bhikkhave, cattāri bhayānī”ti.

<!-- @segment an4.119:1.5 -->

Navamaṁ.
