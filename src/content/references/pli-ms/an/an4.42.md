<!-- @segment an4.42:1.1 -->

“Cattārimāni, bhikkhave, pañhabyākaraṇāni.

<!-- @segment an4.42:1.2 -->

Katamāni cattāri?

<!-- @segment an4.42:1.3 -->

Atthi, bhikkhave, pañho ekaṁsabyākaraṇīyo;

<!-- @segment an4.42:1.4 -->

atthi, bhikkhave, pañho vibhajjabyākaraṇīyo;

<!-- @segment an4.42:1.5 -->

atthi, bhikkhave, pañho paṭipucchābyākaraṇīyo;

<!-- @segment an4.42:1.6 -->

atthi, bhikkhave, pañho ṭhapanīyo.

<!-- @segment an4.42:1.7 -->

Imāni kho, bhikkhave, cattāri pañhabyākaraṇānīti.

<!-- @segment an4.42:2.1 -->

Ekaṁsavacanaṁ ekaṁ,

<!-- @segment an4.42:2.2 -->

vibhajjavacanāparaṁ;

<!-- @segment an4.42:2.3 -->

Tatiyaṁ paṭipuccheyya,

<!-- @segment an4.42:2.4 -->

catutthaṁ pana ṭhāpaye.

<!-- @segment an4.42:3.1 -->

Yo ca tesaṁ tattha tattha,

<!-- @segment an4.42:3.2 -->

jānāti anudhammataṁ;

<!-- @segment an4.42:3.3 -->

Catupañhassa kusalo,

<!-- @segment an4.42:3.4 -->

āhu bhikkhuṁ tathāvidhaṁ.

<!-- @segment an4.42:4.1 -->

Durāsado duppasaho,

<!-- @segment an4.42:4.2 -->

gambhīro duppadhaṁsiyo;

<!-- @segment an4.42:4.3 -->

Atho atthe anatthe ca,

<!-- @segment an4.42:4.4 -->

ubhayassa hoti kovido.

<!-- @segment an4.42:5.1 -->

Anatthaṁ parivajjeti,

<!-- @segment an4.42:5.2 -->

atthaṁ gaṇhāti paṇḍito;

<!-- @segment an4.42:5.3 -->

Atthābhisamayā dhīro,

<!-- @segment an4.42:5.4 -->

paṇḍitoti pavuccatī”ti.

<!-- @segment an4.42:5.5 -->

Dutiyaṁ.
