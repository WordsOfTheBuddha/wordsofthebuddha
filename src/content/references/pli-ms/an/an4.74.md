<!-- @segment an4.74:1.1 -->

“Cattārimāni, bhikkhave, aggāni.

<!-- @segment an4.74:1.2 -->

Katamāni cattāri?

<!-- @segment an4.74:1.3 -->

Sīlaggaṁ, samādhiggaṁ, paññāggaṁ, vimuttaggaṁ—

<!-- @segment an4.74:1.4 -->

Imāni kho, bhikkhave, cattāri aggānī”ti.

<!-- @segment an4.74:1.5 -->

Catutthaṁ.
