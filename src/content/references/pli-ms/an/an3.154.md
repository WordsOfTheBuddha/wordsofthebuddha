<!-- @segment an3.154:1.1 -->

“Tisso imā, bhikkhave, vandanā.

<!-- @segment an3.154:1.2 -->

Katamā tisso?

<!-- @segment an3.154:1.3 -->

Kāyena, vācāya, manasā—

<!-- @segment an3.154:1.4 -->

imā kho, bhikkhave, tisso vandanā”ti.

<!-- @segment an3.154:1.5 -->

Navamaṁ.
