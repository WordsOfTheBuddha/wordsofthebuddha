<!-- @segment an10.120:1.1 -->

“Ariyaṁ vo, bhikkhave, paccorohaṇiṁ desessāmi.

<!-- @segment an10.120:1.2 -->

Taṁ suṇātha …

<!-- @segment an10.120:1.3 -->

katamā ca, bhikkhave, ariyā paccorohaṇī?

<!-- @segment an10.120:1.4 -->

Idha, bhikkhave, ariyasāvako iti paṭisañcikkhati:

<!-- @segment an10.120:1.5 -->

‘micchādiṭṭhiyā kho pāpako vipāko—

<!-- @segment an10.120:1.6 -->

diṭṭhe ceva dhamme abhisamparāyañcā’ti.

<!-- @segment an10.120:1.7 -->

So iti paṭisaṅkhāya micchādiṭṭhiṁ pajahati;

<!-- @segment an10.120:1.8 -->

micchādiṭṭhiyā paccorohati.

<!-- @segment an10.120:1.9 -->

Micchāsaṅkappassa kho pāpako vipāko …

<!-- @segment an10.120:1.10 -->

micchāvācāya kho …

<!-- @segment an10.120:1.11 -->

micchākammantassa kho …

<!-- @segment an10.120:1.12 -->

micchāājīvassa kho …

<!-- @segment an10.120:1.13 -->

micchāvāyāmassa kho …

<!-- @segment an10.120:1.14 -->

micchāsatiyā kho …

<!-- @segment an10.120:1.15 -->

micchāsamādhissa kho …

<!-- @segment an10.120:1.16 -->

micchāñāṇassa kho …

<!-- @segment an10.120:1.17 -->

micchāvimuttiyā kho pāpako vipāko—

<!-- @segment an10.120:1.18 -->

diṭṭhe ceva dhamme abhisamparāyañcāti.

<!-- @segment an10.120:1.19 -->

So iti paṭisaṅkhāya micchāvimuttiṁ pajahati;

<!-- @segment an10.120:1.20 -->

micchāvimuttiyā paccorohati.

<!-- @segment an10.120:1.21 -->

Ayaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, ariyā paccorohaṇī”ti.

<!-- @segment an10.120:1.22 -->

Aṭṭhamaṁ.
