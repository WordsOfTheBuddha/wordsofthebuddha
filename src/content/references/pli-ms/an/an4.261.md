<!-- @segment an4.261:1.1 -->

“Cattārimāni, bhikkhave, balāni.

<!-- @segment an4.261:1.2 -->

Katamāni cattāri?

<!-- @segment an4.261:1.3 -->

Vīriyabalaṁ, satibalaṁ, samādhibalaṁ, paññābalaṁ—

<!-- @segment an4.261:1.4 -->

Imāni kho, bhikkhave, cattāri balānī”ti.

<!-- @segment an4.261:1.5 -->

Aṭṭhamaṁ.
