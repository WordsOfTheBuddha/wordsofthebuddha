<!-- @segment an10.196:1.1 -->

“Bahulīkātabbañca vo, bhikkhave, dhammaṁ desessāmi na bahulīkātabbañca.

<!-- @segment an10.196:1.2 -->

Taṁ suṇātha …pe…

<!-- @segment an10.196:1.3 -->

katamo ca, bhikkhave, na bahulīkātabbo dhammo?

<!-- @segment an10.196:1.4 -->

Pāṇātipāto …pe…

<!-- @segment an10.196:1.5 -->

micchādiṭṭhi—

<!-- @segment an10.196:1.6 -->

ayaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, na bahulīkātabbo dhammo.

<!-- @segment an10.196:2.1 -->

Katamo ca, bhikkhave, bahulīkātabbo dhammo?

<!-- @segment an10.196:2.2 -->

Pāṇātipātā veramaṇī …pe…

<!-- @segment an10.196:2.3 -->

sammādiṭṭhi—

<!-- @segment an10.196:2.4 -->

ayaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, bahulīkātabbo dhammo”ti.

<!-- @segment an10.196:2.5 -->

Aṭṭhamaṁ.
