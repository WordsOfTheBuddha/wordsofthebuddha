<!-- @segment an5.36:1.1 -->

“Pañcimāni, bhikkhave, kāladānāni.

<!-- @segment an5.36:1.2 -->

Katamāni pañca?

<!-- @segment an5.36:1.3 -->

Āgantukassa dānaṁ deti;

<!-- @segment an5.36:1.4 -->

gamikassa dānaṁ deti;

<!-- @segment an5.36:1.5 -->

gilānassa dānaṁ deti;

<!-- @segment an5.36:1.6 -->

dubbhikkhe dānaṁ deti;

<!-- @segment an5.36:1.7 -->

yāni tāni navasassāni navaphalāni tāni paṭhamaṁ sīlavantesu patiṭṭhāpeti.

<!-- @segment an5.36:1.8 -->

Imāni kho, bhikkhave, pañca kāladānānīti.

<!-- @segment an5.36:2.1 -->

Kāle dadanti sappaññā,

<!-- @segment an5.36:2.2 -->

vadaññū vītamaccharā;

<!-- @segment an5.36:2.3 -->

Kālena dinnaṁ ariyesu,

<!-- @segment an5.36:2.4 -->

ujubhūtesu tādisu.

<!-- @segment an5.36:3.1 -->

Vippasannamanā tassa,

<!-- @segment an5.36:3.2 -->

vipulā hoti dakkhiṇā;

<!-- @segment an5.36:3.3 -->

Ye tattha anumodanti,

<!-- @segment an5.36:3.4 -->

veyyāvaccaṁ karonti vā;

<!-- @segment an5.36:3.5 -->

Na tena dakkhiṇā ūnā,

<!-- @segment an5.36:3.6 -->

tepi puññassa bhāgino.

<!-- @segment an5.36:4.1 -->

Tasmā dade appaṭivānacitto,

<!-- @segment an5.36:4.2 -->

Yattha dinnaṁ mahapphalaṁ;

<!-- @segment an5.36:4.3 -->

Puññāni paralokasmiṁ,

<!-- @segment an5.36:4.4 -->

Patiṭṭhā honti pāṇinan”ti.

<!-- @segment an5.36:4.5 -->

Chaṭṭhaṁ.
