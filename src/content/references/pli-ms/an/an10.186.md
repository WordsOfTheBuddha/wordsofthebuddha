<!-- @segment an10.186:1.1 -->

“Ācayagāmiñca vo, bhikkhave, dhammaṁ desessāmi apacayagāmiñca.

<!-- @segment an10.186:1.2 -->

Taṁ suṇātha …pe…

<!-- @segment an10.186:1.3 -->

katamo ca, bhikkhave, ācayagāmī dhammo?

<!-- @segment an10.186:1.4 -->

Pāṇātipāto …pe…

<!-- @segment an10.186:1.5 -->

micchādiṭṭhi—

<!-- @segment an10.186:1.6 -->

ayaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, ācayagāmī dhammo.

<!-- @segment an10.186:2.1 -->

Katamo ca, bhikkhave, apacayagāmī dhammo?

<!-- @segment an10.186:2.2 -->

Pāṇātipātā veramaṇī …pe…

<!-- @segment an10.186:2.3 -->

sammādiṭṭhi—

<!-- @segment an10.186:2.4 -->

ayaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, apacayagāmī dhammo”ti.

<!-- @segment an10.186:2.5 -->

Navamaṁ.
