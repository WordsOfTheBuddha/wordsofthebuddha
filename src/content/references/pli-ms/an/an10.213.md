<!-- @segment an10.213:1.1 -->

“Dasahi, bhikkhave, dhammehi samannāgato mātugāmo yathābhataṁ nikkhitto evaṁ niraye.

<!-- @segment an10.213:1.2 -->

Katamehi dasahi?

<!-- @segment an10.213:1.3 -->

Pāṇātipātī hoti …pe…

<!-- @segment an10.213:1.4 -->

adinnādāyī hoti …

<!-- @segment an10.213:1.5 -->

kāmesumicchācārī hoti …

<!-- @segment an10.213:1.6 -->

musāvādī hoti …

<!-- @segment an10.213:1.7 -->

pisuṇavāco hoti …

<!-- @segment an10.213:1.8 -->

pharusavāco hoti …

<!-- @segment an10.213:1.9 -->

samphappalāpī hoti …

<!-- @segment an10.213:1.10 -->

abhijjhālu hoti …

<!-- @segment an10.213:1.11 -->

byāpannacitto hoti …

<!-- @segment an10.213:1.12 -->

micchādiṭṭhiko hoti ….

<!-- @segment an10.213:1.13 -->

Imehi kho, bhikkhave, dasahi dhammehi samannāgato mātugāmo yathābhataṁ nikkhitto evaṁ niraye.

<!-- @segment an10.213:2.1 -->

Dasahi, bhikkhave, dhammehi samannāgato mātugāmo yathābhataṁ nikkhitto evaṁ sagge.

<!-- @segment an10.213:2.2 -->

Katamehi dasahi?

<!-- @segment an10.213:2.3 -->

Pāṇātipātā paṭivirato hoti …pe…

<!-- @segment an10.213:2.4 -->

adinnādānā paṭivirato hoti …

<!-- @segment an10.213:2.5 -->

kāmesumicchācārā paṭivirato hoti …

<!-- @segment an10.213:2.6 -->

musāvādā paṭivirato hoti …

<!-- @segment an10.213:2.7 -->

pisuṇāya vācāya paṭivirato hoti …

<!-- @segment an10.213:2.8 -->

pharusāya vācāya paṭivirato hoti …

<!-- @segment an10.213:2.9 -->

samphappalāpā paṭivirato hoti …

<!-- @segment an10.213:2.10 -->

anabhijjhālu hoti …

<!-- @segment an10.213:2.11 -->

abyāpannacitto hoti …

<!-- @segment an10.213:2.12 -->

sammādiṭṭhiko hoti ….

<!-- @segment an10.213:2.13 -->

Imehi kho, bhikkhave, dasahi dhammehi samannāgato mātugāmo yathābhataṁ nikkhitto evaṁ sagge”ti.

<!-- @segment an10.213:2.14 -->

Tatiyaṁ.
