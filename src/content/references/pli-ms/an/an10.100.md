<!-- @segment an10.100:1.1 -->

“Dasayime, bhikkhave, dhamme appahāya abhabbo arahattaṁ sacchikātuṁ.

<!-- @segment an10.100:1.2 -->

Katame dasa?

<!-- @segment an10.100:1.3 -->

Rāgaṁ, dosaṁ, mohaṁ, kodhaṁ, upanāhaṁ, makkhaṁ, paḷāsaṁ, issaṁ, macchariyaṁ, mānaṁ—

<!-- @segment an10.100:1.4 -->

ime kho, bhikkhave, dasa dhamme appahāya abhabbo arahattaṁ sacchikātuṁ.

<!-- @segment an10.100:2.1 -->

Dasayime, bhikkhave, dhamme pahāya bhabbo arahattaṁ sacchikātuṁ.

<!-- @segment an10.100:2.2 -->

Katame dasa?

<!-- @segment an10.100:2.3 -->

Rāgaṁ, dosaṁ, mohaṁ, kodhaṁ, upanāhaṁ, makkhaṁ, paḷāsaṁ, issaṁ, macchariyaṁ, mānaṁ—

<!-- @segment an10.100:2.4 -->

ime kho, bhikkhave, dasa dhamme pahāya bhabbo arahattaṁ sacchikātun”ti.

<!-- @segment an10.100:2.5 -->

Dasamaṁ.

<!-- @segment an10.100:2.6 -->

Upālivaggo pañcamo.

<!-- @segment an10.100:3.0 -->

Tassuddānaṁ

<!-- @segment an10.100:3.1 -->

Kāmabhogī bhayaṁ diṭṭhi,

<!-- @segment an10.100:3.2 -->

vajjiyamāhituttiyā;

<!-- @segment an10.100:3.3 -->

Kokanudo āhuneyyo,

<!-- @segment an10.100:3.4 -->

thero upāli abhabboti.

<!-- @segment an10.100:3.5 -->

Dutiyo paṇṇāsako samatto.
