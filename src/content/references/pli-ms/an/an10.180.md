<!-- @segment an10.180:1.1 -->

“Kusalañca vo, bhikkhave, desessāmi akusalañca.

<!-- @segment an10.180:1.2 -->

Taṁ suṇātha …pe…

<!-- @segment an10.180:1.3 -->

katamañca, bhikkhave, akusalaṁ?

<!-- @segment an10.180:1.4 -->

Pāṇātipāto …pe…

<!-- @segment an10.180:1.5 -->

micchādiṭṭhi—

<!-- @segment an10.180:1.6 -->

idaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, akusalaṁ.

<!-- @segment an10.180:2.1 -->

Katamañca, bhikkhave, kusalaṁ?

<!-- @segment an10.180:2.2 -->

Pāṇātipātā veramaṇī …pe…

<!-- @segment an10.180:2.3 -->

sammādiṭṭhi—

<!-- @segment an10.180:2.4 -->

idaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, kusalan”ti.

<!-- @segment an10.180:2.5 -->

Tatiyaṁ.
