<!-- @segment an7.29:1.1 -->

“Sattime, bhikkhave, dhammā upāsakassa parihānāya saṁvattanti.

<!-- @segment an7.29:1.2 -->

Katame satta?

<!-- @segment an7.29:1.3 -->

Bhikkhudassanaṁ hāpeti,

<!-- @segment an7.29:1.4 -->

saddhammassavanaṁ pamajjati,

<!-- @segment an7.29:1.5 -->

adhisīle na sikkhati,

<!-- @segment an7.29:1.6 -->

appasādabahulo hoti,

<!-- @segment an7.29:1.7 -->

bhikkhūsu theresu ceva navesu ca majjhimesu ca upārambhacitto dhammaṁ suṇāti randhagavesī,

<!-- @segment an7.29:1.8 -->

ito bahiddhā dakkhiṇeyyaṁ gavesati,

<!-- @segment an7.29:1.9 -->

tattha ca pubbakāraṁ karoti.

<!-- @segment an7.29:1.10 -->

Ime kho, bhikkhave, satta dhammā upāsakassa parihānāya saṁvattanti.

<!-- @segment an7.29:2.1 -->

Sattime, bhikkhave, dhammā upāsakassa aparihānāya saṁvattanti.

<!-- @segment an7.29:2.2 -->

Katame satta?

<!-- @segment an7.29:2.3 -->

Bhikkhudassanaṁ na hāpeti,

<!-- @segment an7.29:2.4 -->

saddhammassavanaṁ nappamajjati,

<!-- @segment an7.29:2.5 -->

adhisīle sikkhati,

<!-- @segment an7.29:2.6 -->

pasādabahulo hoti,

<!-- @segment an7.29:2.7 -->

bhikkhūsu theresu ceva navesu ca majjhimesu ca anupārambhacitto dhammaṁ suṇāti na randhagavesī,

<!-- @segment an7.29:2.8 -->

na ito bahiddhā dakkhiṇeyyaṁ gavesati,

<!-- @segment an7.29:2.9 -->

idha ca pubbakāraṁ karoti.

<!-- @segment an7.29:2.10 -->

Ime kho, bhikkhave, satta dhammā upāsakassa aparihānāya saṁvattantī”ti.

<!-- @segment an7.29:2.11 -->

Idamavoca bhagavā.

<!-- @segment an7.29:2.12 -->

Idaṁ vatvāna sugato athāparaṁ etadavoca satthā:

<!-- @segment an7.29:3.1 -->

“Dassanaṁ bhāvitattānaṁ,

<!-- @segment an7.29:3.2 -->

Yo hāpeti upāsako;

<!-- @segment an7.29:3.3 -->

Savanañca ariyadhammānaṁ,

<!-- @segment an7.29:3.4 -->

Adhisīle na sikkhati.

<!-- @segment an7.29:4.1 -->

Appasādo ca bhikkhūsu,

<!-- @segment an7.29:4.2 -->

bhiyyo bhiyyo pavaḍḍhati;

<!-- @segment an7.29:4.3 -->

Upārambhakacitto ca,

<!-- @segment an7.29:4.4 -->

saddhammaṁ sotumicchati.

<!-- @segment an7.29:5.1 -->

Ito ca bahiddhā aññaṁ,

<!-- @segment an7.29:5.2 -->

dakkhiṇeyyaṁ gavesati;

<!-- @segment an7.29:5.3 -->

Tattheva ca pubbakāraṁ,

<!-- @segment an7.29:5.4 -->

yo karoti upāsako.

<!-- @segment an7.29:6.1 -->

Ete kho parihāniye,

<!-- @segment an7.29:6.2 -->

satta dhamme sudesite;

<!-- @segment an7.29:6.3 -->

Upāsako sevamāno,

<!-- @segment an7.29:6.4 -->

saddhammā parihāyati.

<!-- @segment an7.29:7.1 -->

Dassanaṁ bhāvitattānaṁ,

<!-- @segment an7.29:7.2 -->

Yo na hāpeti upāsako;

<!-- @segment an7.29:7.3 -->

Savanañca ariyadhammānaṁ,

<!-- @segment an7.29:7.4 -->

Adhisīle ca sikkhati.

<!-- @segment an7.29:8.1 -->

Pasādo cassa bhikkhūsu,

<!-- @segment an7.29:8.2 -->

bhiyyo bhiyyo pavaḍḍhati;

<!-- @segment an7.29:8.3 -->

Anupārambhacitto ca,

<!-- @segment an7.29:8.4 -->

saddhammaṁ sotumicchati.

<!-- @segment an7.29:9.1 -->

Na ito bahiddhā aññaṁ,

<!-- @segment an7.29:9.2 -->

dakkhiṇeyyaṁ gavesati;

<!-- @segment an7.29:9.3 -->

Idheva ca pubbakāraṁ,

<!-- @segment an7.29:9.4 -->

yo karoti upāsako.

<!-- @segment an7.29:10.1 -->

Ete kho aparihāniye,

<!-- @segment an7.29:10.2 -->

Satta dhamme sudesite;

<!-- @segment an7.29:10.3 -->

Upāsako sevamāno,

<!-- @segment an7.29:10.4 -->

Saddhammā na parihāyatī”ti.

<!-- @segment an7.29:10.5 -->

Navamaṁ.
