<!-- @segment an10.181:1.1 -->

“Atthañca vo, bhikkhave, desessāmi anatthañca.

<!-- @segment an10.181:1.2 -->

Taṁ suṇātha …pe…

<!-- @segment an10.181:1.3 -->

katamo ca, bhikkhave, anattho?

<!-- @segment an10.181:1.4 -->

Pāṇātipāto …pe…

<!-- @segment an10.181:1.5 -->

micchādiṭṭhi—

<!-- @segment an10.181:1.6 -->

ayaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, anattho.

<!-- @segment an10.181:2.1 -->

Katamo ca, bhikkhave, attho?

<!-- @segment an10.181:2.2 -->

Pāṇātipātā veramaṇī …pe…

<!-- @segment an10.181:2.3 -->

sammādiṭṭhi—

<!-- @segment an10.181:2.4 -->

ayaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, attho”ti.

<!-- @segment an10.181:2.5 -->

Catutthaṁ.
