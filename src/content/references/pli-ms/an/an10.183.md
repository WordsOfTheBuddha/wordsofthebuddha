<!-- @segment an10.183:1.1 -->

“Sāsavañca vo, bhikkhave, dhammaṁ desessāmi anāsavañca.

<!-- @segment an10.183:1.2 -->

Taṁ suṇātha …pe…

<!-- @segment an10.183:1.3 -->

katamo ca, bhikkhave, sāsavo dhammo?

<!-- @segment an10.183:1.4 -->

Pāṇātipāto …pe…

<!-- @segment an10.183:1.5 -->

micchādiṭṭhi—

<!-- @segment an10.183:1.6 -->

ayaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, sāsavo dhammo.

<!-- @segment an10.183:2.1 -->

Katamo ca, bhikkhave, anāsavo dhammo?

<!-- @segment an10.183:2.2 -->

Pāṇātipātā veramaṇī …pe…

<!-- @segment an10.183:2.3 -->

sammādiṭṭhi—

<!-- @segment an10.183:2.4 -->

ayaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, anāsavo dhammo”ti.

<!-- @segment an10.183:2.5 -->

Chaṭṭhaṁ.
