<!-- @segment an4.75:1.1 -->

“Cattārimāni, bhikkhave, aggāni.

<!-- @segment an4.75:1.2 -->

Katamāni cattāri?

<!-- @segment an4.75:1.3 -->

Rūpaggaṁ, vedanāggaṁ, saññāggaṁ, bhavaggaṁ—

<!-- @segment an4.75:1.4 -->

Imāni kho, bhikkhave, cattāri aggānī”ti.

<!-- @segment an4.75:1.5 -->

Pañcamaṁ.
