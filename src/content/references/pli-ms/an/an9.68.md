<!-- @segment an9.68:1.1 -->

“Pañcimā, bhikkhave, gatiyo.

<!-- @segment an9.68:1.2 -->

Katamā pañca?

<!-- @segment an9.68:1.3 -->

Nirayo, tiracchānayoni, pettivisayo, manussā, devā—

<!-- @segment an9.68:1.4 -->

imā kho, bhikkhave, pañca gatiyo.

<!-- @segment an9.68:2.1 -->

Imāsaṁ kho, bhikkhave, pañcannaṁ gatīnaṁ pahānāya …pe… ime cattāro satipaṭṭhānā bhāvetabbā”ti.

<!-- @segment an9.68:2.2 -->

Chaṭṭhaṁ.
