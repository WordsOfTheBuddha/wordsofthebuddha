<!-- @segment an10.182:1.1 -->

“Dhammañca vo, bhikkhave, desessāmi adhammañca.

<!-- @segment an10.182:1.2 -->

Taṁ suṇātha …pe…

<!-- @segment an10.182:1.3 -->

katamo ca, bhikkhave, adhammo?

<!-- @segment an10.182:1.4 -->

Pāṇātipāto …pe…

<!-- @segment an10.182:1.5 -->

micchādiṭṭhi—

<!-- @segment an10.182:1.6 -->

ayaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, adhammo.

<!-- @segment an10.182:2.1 -->

Katamo ca, bhikkhave, dhammo?

<!-- @segment an10.182:2.2 -->

Pāṇātipātā veramaṇī …pe…

<!-- @segment an10.182:2.3 -->

sammādiṭṭhi—

<!-- @segment an10.182:2.4 -->

ayaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, dhammo”ti.

<!-- @segment an10.182:2.5 -->

Pañcamaṁ.
