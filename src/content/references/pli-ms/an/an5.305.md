<!-- @segment an5.305:1.1 -->

“Rāgassa, bhikkhave, abhiññāya pañca dhammā bhāvetabbā.

<!-- @segment an5.305:1.2 -->

Katame pañca?

<!-- @segment an5.305:1.3 -->

Aniccasaññā, anicce dukkhasaññā, dukkhe anattasaññā, pahānasaññā, virāgasaññā—

<!-- @segment an5.305:1.4 -->

rāgassa, bhikkhave, abhiññāya ime pañca dhammā bhāvetabbā”ti.
