<!-- @segment an4.120:1.1 -->

“Cattārimāni, bhikkhave, bhayāni.

<!-- @segment an4.120:1.2 -->

Katamāni cattāri?

<!-- @segment an4.120:1.3 -->

Aggibhayaṁ, udakabhayaṁ, rājabhayaṁ, corabhayaṁ—

<!-- @segment an4.120:1.4 -->

Imāni kho, bhikkhave, cattāri bhayānī”ti.

<!-- @segment an4.120:1.5 -->

Dasamaṁ.

<!-- @segment an4.120:1.6 -->

Kesivaggo dutiyo.

<!-- @segment an4.120:2.0 -->

Tassuddānaṁ

<!-- @segment an4.120:2.1 -->

Kesi javo patodo ca,

<!-- @segment an4.120:2.2 -->

Nāgo ṭhānena pañcamaṁ;

<!-- @segment an4.120:2.3 -->

Appamādo ca ārakkho,

<!-- @segment an4.120:2.4 -->

Saṁvejanīyañca dve bhayāti.
