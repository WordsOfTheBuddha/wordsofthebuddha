<!-- @segment an5.207:1.1 -->

“Pañcime, bhikkhave, ānisaṁsā yāguyā.

<!-- @segment an5.207:1.2 -->

Katame pañca?

<!-- @segment an5.207:1.3 -->

Khuddaṁ paṭihanati, pipāsaṁ paṭivineti, vātaṁ anulometi, vatthiṁ sodheti, āmāvasesaṁ pāceti.

<!-- @segment an5.207:1.4 -->

Ime kho, bhikkhave, pañca ānisaṁsā yāguyā”ti.

<!-- @segment an5.207:1.5 -->

Sattamaṁ.
