<!-- @segment an4.141:1.1 -->

“Catasso imā, bhikkhave, ābhā.

<!-- @segment an4.141:1.2 -->

Katamā catasso?

<!-- @segment an4.141:1.3 -->

Candābhā, sūriyābhā, aggābhā, paññābhā—

<!-- @segment an4.141:1.4 -->

imā kho, bhikkhave, catasso ābhā.

<!-- @segment an4.141:1.5 -->

Etadaggaṁ, bhikkhave, imāsaṁ catunnaṁ ābhānaṁ yadidaṁ paññābhā”ti.

<!-- @segment an4.141:1.6 -->

Paṭhamaṁ.
