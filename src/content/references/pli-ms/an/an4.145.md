<!-- @segment an4.145:1.1 -->

“Cattārome, bhikkhave, pajjotā.

<!-- @segment an4.145:1.2 -->

Katame cattāro?

<!-- @segment an4.145:1.3 -->

Candapajjoto, sūriyapajjoto, aggipajjoto, paññāpajjoto—

<!-- @segment an4.145:1.4 -->

Ime kho, bhikkhave, cattāro pajjotā.

<!-- @segment an4.145:1.5 -->

Etadaggaṁ, bhikkhave, imesaṁ catunnaṁ pajjotānaṁ yadidaṁ paññāpajjoto”ti.

<!-- @segment an4.145:1.6 -->

Pañcamaṁ.
