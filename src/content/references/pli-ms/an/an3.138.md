<!-- @segment an3.138:1.1 -->

“Tisso imā, bhikkhave, sampadā.

<!-- @segment an3.138:1.2 -->

Katamā tisso?

<!-- @segment an3.138:1.3 -->

Saddhāsampadā, sīlasampadā, paññāsampadā—

<!-- @segment an3.138:1.4 -->

imā kho, bhikkhave, tisso sampadā”ti.

<!-- @segment an3.138:1.5 -->

Chaṭṭhaṁ.
