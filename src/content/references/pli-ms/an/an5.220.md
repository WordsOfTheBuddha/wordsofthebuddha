<!-- @segment an5.220:1.1 -->

“Pañcime, bhikkhave, ādīnavā madhurāyaṁ.

<!-- @segment an5.220:1.2 -->

Katame pañca?

<!-- @segment an5.220:1.3 -->

Visamā, bahurajā, caṇḍasunakhā, vāḷayakkhā, dullabhapiṇḍā.

<!-- @segment an5.220:1.4 -->

Ime kho, bhikkhave, pañca ādīnavā madhurāyan”ti.

<!-- @segment an5.220:1.5 -->

Dasamaṁ.

<!-- @segment an5.220:1.6 -->

Akkosakavaggo dutiyo.

<!-- @segment an5.220:2.0 -->

Tassuddānaṁ

<!-- @segment an5.220:2.1 -->

Akkosabhaṇḍanasīlaṁ,

<!-- @segment an5.220:2.2 -->

bahubhāṇī dve akhantiyo;

<!-- @segment an5.220:2.3 -->

Apāsādikā dve vuttā,

<!-- @segment an5.220:2.4 -->

aggismiṁ madhurena cāti.
