<!-- @segment an5.204:1.1 -->

“Pañcimāni, bhikkhave, balāni.

<!-- @segment an5.204:1.2 -->

Katamāni pañca?

<!-- @segment an5.204:1.3 -->

Saddhābalaṁ, hiribalaṁ, ottappabalaṁ, vīriyabalaṁ, paññābalaṁ—

<!-- @segment an5.204:1.4 -->

imāni kho, bhikkhave, pañca balānī”ti.

<!-- @segment an5.204:1.5 -->

Catutthaṁ.
