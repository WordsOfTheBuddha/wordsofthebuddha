<!-- @segment an4.142:1.1 -->

“Catasso imā, bhikkhave, pabhā.

<!-- @segment an4.142:1.2 -->

Katamā catasso?

<!-- @segment an4.142:1.3 -->

Candappabhā, sūriyappabhā, aggippabhā, paññāpabhā—

<!-- @segment an4.142:1.4 -->

imā kho, bhikkhave, catasso pabhā.

<!-- @segment an4.142:1.5 -->

Etadaggaṁ, bhikkhave, imāsaṁ catunnaṁ pabhānaṁ yadidaṁ paññāpabhā”ti.

<!-- @segment an4.142:1.6 -->

Dutiyaṁ.
