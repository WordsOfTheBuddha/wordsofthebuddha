<!-- @segment an7.46:1.1 -->

“Sattime, bhikkhave, aggī.

<!-- @segment an7.46:1.2 -->

Katame satta?

<!-- @segment an7.46:1.3 -->

Rāgaggi, dosaggi, mohaggi, āhuneyyaggi, gahapataggi, dakkhiṇeyyaggi, kaṭṭhaggi—

<!-- @segment an7.46:1.4 -->

ime kho, bhikkhave, satta aggī”ti.

<!-- @segment an7.46:1.5 -->

Tatiyaṁ.
