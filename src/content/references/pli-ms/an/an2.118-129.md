118

<!-- @segment an2.118:1.1 -->

“Dvemā, bhikkhave, āsā duppajahā.

<!-- @segment an2.118:1.2 -->

Katamā dve?

<!-- @segment an2.118:1.3 -->

Lābhāsā ca jīvitāsā ca.

<!-- @segment an2.118:1.4 -->

Imā kho, bhikkhave, dve āsā duppajahā”ti.

<!-- @segment an2.119:1.0 -->

119

<!-- @segment an2.119:1.1 -->

“Dveme, bhikkhave, puggalā dullabhā lokasmiṁ.

<!-- @segment an2.119:1.2 -->

Katame dve?

<!-- @segment an2.119:1.3 -->

Yo ca pubbakārī, yo ca kataññū katavedī.

<!-- @segment an2.119:1.4 -->

Ime kho, bhikkhave, dve puggalā dullabhā lokasmin”ti.

120

<!-- @segment an2.120:1.1 -->

“Dveme, bhikkhave, puggalā dullabhā lokasmiṁ.

<!-- @segment an2.120:1.2 -->

Katame dve?

<!-- @segment an2.120:1.3 -->

Titto ca tappetā ca.

<!-- @segment an2.120:1.4 -->

Ime kho, bhikkhave, dve puggalā dullabhā lokasmin”ti.

<!-- @segment an2.121:1.0 -->

121

<!-- @segment an2.121:1.1 -->

“Dveme, bhikkhave, puggalā duttappayā.

<!-- @segment an2.121:1.2 -->

Katame dve?

<!-- @segment an2.121:1.3 -->

Yo ca laddhaṁ laddhaṁ nikkhipati, yo ca laddhaṁ laddhaṁ vissajjeti.

<!-- @segment an2.121:1.4 -->

Ime kho, bhikkhave, dve puggalā duttappayā”ti.

<!-- @segment an2.122:1.0 -->

122

<!-- @segment an2.122:1.1 -->

“Dveme, bhikkhave, puggalā sutappayā.

<!-- @segment an2.122:1.2 -->

Katame dve?

<!-- @segment an2.122:1.3 -->

Yo ca laddhaṁ laddhaṁ na nikkhipati, yo ca laddhaṁ laddhaṁ na vissajjeti.

<!-- @segment an2.122:1.4 -->

Ime kho, bhikkhave, dve puggalā sutappayā”ti.

123

<!-- @segment an2.123:1.1 -->

<!-- @segment an2.123:1.2 -->

Katame dve?

<!-- @segment an2.123:1.3 -->

Subhanimittañca ayoniso ca manasikāro.

<!-- @segment an2.123:1.4 -->

Ime kho, bhikkhave, dve paccayā rāgassa uppādāyā”ti.

124

<!-- @segment an2.124:1.1 -->

<!-- @segment an2.124:1.2 -->

Katame dve?

<!-- @segment an2.124:1.3 -->

Paṭighanimittañca ayoniso ca manasikāro.

<!-- @segment an2.124:1.4 -->

Ime kho, bhikkhave, dve paccayā dosassa uppādāyā”ti.

125

<!-- @segment an2.125:1.1 -->

<!-- @segment an2.125:1.2 -->

<!-- @segment an2.125:1.4 -->

Ime kho, bhikkhave, dve paccayā micchādiṭṭhiyā uppādāyā”ti.
