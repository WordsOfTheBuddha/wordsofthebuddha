<!-- @segment an10.191:1.1 -->

“Saddhammañca vo, bhikkhave, desessāmi asaddhammañca.

<!-- @segment an10.191:1.2 -->

Taṁ suṇātha …pe…

<!-- @segment an10.191:1.3 -->

katamo ca, bhikkhave, asaddhammo?

<!-- @segment an10.191:1.4 -->

Pāṇātipāto …pe…

<!-- @segment an10.191:1.5 -->

micchādiṭṭhi—

<!-- @segment an10.191:1.6 -->

ayaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, asaddhammo.

<!-- @segment an10.191:2.1 -->

Katamo ca, bhikkhave, saddhammo?

<!-- @segment an10.191:2.2 -->

Pāṇātipātā veramaṇī …pe…

<!-- @segment an10.191:2.3 -->

sammādiṭṭhi—

<!-- @segment an10.191:2.4 -->

ayaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, saddhammo”ti.

<!-- @segment an10.191:2.5 -->

Tatiyaṁ.
