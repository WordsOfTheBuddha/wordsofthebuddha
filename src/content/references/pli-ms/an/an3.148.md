<!-- @segment an3.148:1.1 -->

“Tīhi, bhikkhave …pe…

<!-- @segment an3.148:1.2 -->

visamena kāyakammena, visamena vacīkammena, visamena manokammena—

<!-- @segment an3.148:1.3 -->

imehi kho …pe… evaṁ niraye.

<!-- @segment an3.148:2.1 -->

Tīhi, bhikkhave, dhammehi …pe…

<!-- @segment an3.148:2.2 -->

samena kāyakammena, samena vacīkammena, samena manokammena—

<!-- @segment an3.148:2.3 -->

imehi kho …pe… evaṁ sagge”ti.

<!-- @segment an3.148:2.4 -->

Tatiyaṁ.
