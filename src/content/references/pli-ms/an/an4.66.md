<!-- @segment an4.66:1.1 -->

“Cattārome, bhikkhave, puggalā santo saṁvijjamānā lokasmiṁ.

<!-- @segment an4.66:1.2 -->

Katame cattāro?

<!-- @segment an4.66:1.3 -->

Sarāgo, sadoso, samoho, samāno—

<!-- @segment an4.66:1.4 -->

Ime kho, bhikkhave, cattāro puggalā santo saṁvijjamānā lokasminti.

<!-- @segment an4.66:2.1 -->

Sārattā rajanīyesu,

<!-- @segment an4.66:2.2 -->

Piyarūpābhinandino;

<!-- @segment an4.66:2.3 -->

Mohena āvutā sattā,

<!-- @segment an4.66:2.4 -->

Baddhā vaḍḍhenti bandhanaṁ.

<!-- @segment an4.66:3.1 -->

Rāgajaṁ dosajañcāpi,

<!-- @segment an4.66:3.2 -->

mohajaṁ cāpaviddasū;

<!-- @segment an4.66:3.3 -->

Karontākusalaṁ kammaṁ,

<!-- @segment an4.66:3.4 -->

savighātaṁ dukhudrayaṁ.

<!-- @segment an4.66:4.1 -->

Avijjānivutā posā,

<!-- @segment an4.66:4.2 -->

andhabhūtā acakkhukā;

<!-- @segment an4.66:4.3 -->

Yathā dhammā tathā santā,

<!-- @segment an4.66:4.4 -->

na tassevanti maññare”ti.

<!-- @segment an4.66:4.5 -->

Chaṭṭhaṁ.
