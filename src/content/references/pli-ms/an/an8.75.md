<!-- @segment an8.75:1.1 -->

“Aṭṭhimā, bhikkhave, sampadā.

<!-- @segment an8.75:1.2 -->

Katamā aṭṭha?

<!-- @segment an8.75:1.3 -->

Uṭṭhānasampadā, ārakkhasampadā, kalyāṇamittatā, samajīvitā, saddhāsampadā, sīlasampadā, cāgasampadā, paññāsampadā—

<!-- @segment an8.75:1.4 -->

imā kho, bhikkhave, aṭṭha sampadāti.

<!-- @segment an8.75:2.1 -->

Uṭṭhātā kammadheyyesu,

<!-- @segment an8.75:2.2 -->

appamatto vidhānavā;

<!-- @segment an8.75:2.3 -->

Samaṁ kappeti jīvikaṁ,

<!-- @segment an8.75:2.4 -->

sambhataṁ anurakkhati.

<!-- @segment an8.75:3.1 -->

Saddho sīlena sampanno,

<!-- @segment an8.75:3.2 -->

vadaññū vītamaccharo;

<!-- @segment an8.75:3.3 -->

Niccaṁ maggaṁ visodheti,

<!-- @segment an8.75:3.4 -->

sotthānaṁ samparāyikaṁ.

<!-- @segment an8.75:4.1 -->

Iccete aṭṭha dhammā ca,

<!-- @segment an8.75:4.2 -->

saddhassa gharamesino;

<!-- @segment an8.75:4.3 -->

Akkhātā saccanāmena,

<!-- @segment an8.75:4.4 -->

ubhayattha sukhāvahā.

<!-- @segment an8.75:5.1 -->

Diṭṭhadhammahitatthāya,

<!-- @segment an8.75:5.2 -->

samparāyasukhāya ca;

<!-- @segment an8.75:5.3 -->

Evametaṁ gahaṭṭhānaṁ,

<!-- @segment an8.75:5.4 -->

cāgo puññaṁ pavaḍḍhatī”ti.

<!-- @segment an8.75:5.5 -->

Pañcamaṁ.
