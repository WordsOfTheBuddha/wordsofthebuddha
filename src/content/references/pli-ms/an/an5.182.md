<!-- @segment an5.182:1.1 -->

“Pañcime, bhikkhave, paṁsukūlikā.

<!-- @segment an5.182:1.2 -->

Katame pañca?

<!-- @segment an5.182:1.3 -->

Mandattā momūhattā paṁsukūliko hoti …pe… idamatthitaṁyeva nissāya paṁsukūliko hoti.

<!-- @segment an5.182:1.4 -->

Ime kho, bhikkhave, pañca paṁsukūlikā”ti.

<!-- @segment an5.182:1.5 -->

Dutiyaṁ.
