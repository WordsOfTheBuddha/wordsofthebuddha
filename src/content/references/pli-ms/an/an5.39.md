<!-- @segment an5.39:1.1 -->

“Pañcimāni, bhikkhave, ṭhānāni sampassantā mātāpitaro puttaṁ icchanti kule jāyamānaṁ.

<!-- @segment an5.39:1.2 -->

Katamāni pañca?

<!-- @segment an5.39:1.3 -->

Bhato vā no bharissati;

<!-- @segment an5.39:1.4 -->

kiccaṁ vā no karissati;

<!-- @segment an5.39:1.5 -->

kulavaṁso ciraṁ ṭhassati;

<!-- @segment an5.39:1.6 -->

dāyajjaṁ paṭipajjissati;

<!-- @segment an5.39:1.7 -->

atha vā pana petānaṁ kālaṅkatānaṁ dakkhiṇaṁ anuppadassatīti.

<!-- @segment an5.39:1.8 -->

Imāni kho, bhikkhave, pañca ṭhānāni sampassantā mātāpitaro puttaṁ icchanti kule jāyamānanti.

<!-- @segment an5.39:2.1 -->

Pañca ṭhānāni sampassaṁ,

<!-- @segment an5.39:2.2 -->

puttaṁ icchanti paṇḍitā;

<!-- @segment an5.39:2.3 -->

Bhato vā no bharissati,

<!-- @segment an5.39:2.4 -->

kiccaṁ vā no karissati.

<!-- @segment an5.39:3.1 -->

Kulavaṁso ciraṁ tiṭṭhe,

<!-- @segment an5.39:3.2 -->

dāyajjaṁ paṭipajjati;

<!-- @segment an5.39:3.3 -->

Atha vā pana petānaṁ,

<!-- @segment an5.39:3.4 -->

dakkhiṇaṁ anuppadassati.

<!-- @segment an5.39:4.1 -->

Ṭhānānetāni sampassaṁ,

<!-- @segment an5.39:4.2 -->

puttaṁ icchanti paṇḍitā;

<!-- @segment an5.39:4.3 -->

Tasmā santo sappurisā,

<!-- @segment an5.39:4.4 -->

kataññū katavedino.

<!-- @segment an5.39:5.1 -->

Bharanti mātāpitaro,

<!-- @segment an5.39:5.2 -->

pubbe katamanussaraṁ;

<!-- @segment an5.39:5.3 -->

Karonti nesaṁ kiccāni,

<!-- @segment an5.39:5.4 -->

yathā taṁ pubbakārinaṁ.

<!-- @segment an5.39:6.1 -->

Ovādakārī bhataposī,

<!-- @segment an5.39:6.2 -->

kulavaṁsaṁ ahāpayaṁ;

<!-- @segment an5.39:6.3 -->

Saddho sīlena sampanno,

<!-- @segment an5.39:6.4 -->

putto hoti pasaṁsiyo”ti.

<!-- @segment an5.39:6.5 -->

Navamaṁ.
