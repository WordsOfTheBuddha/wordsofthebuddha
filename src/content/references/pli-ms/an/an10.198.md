<!-- @segment an10.198:1.1 -->

“Sacchikātabbañca vo, bhikkhave, dhammaṁ desessāmi na sacchikātabbañca.

<!-- @segment an10.198:1.2 -->

Taṁ suṇātha …pe…

<!-- @segment an10.198:1.3 -->

katamo ca, bhikkhave, na sacchikātabbo dhammo?

<!-- @segment an10.198:1.4 -->

Pāṇātipāto …pe…

<!-- @segment an10.198:1.5 -->

micchādiṭṭhi—

<!-- @segment an10.198:1.6 -->

ayaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, na sacchikātabbo dhammo.

<!-- @segment an10.198:2.1 -->

Katamo ca, bhikkhave, sacchikātabbo dhammo?

<!-- @segment an10.198:2.2 -->

Pāṇātipātā veramaṇī …pe…

<!-- @segment an10.198:2.3 -->

sammādiṭṭhi—

<!-- @segment an10.198:2.4 -->

ayaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, sacchikātabbo dhammo”ti.

<!-- @segment an10.198:2.5 -->

Dasamaṁ.

<!-- @segment an10.198:2.6 -->

Ariyamaggavaggo catuttho.
