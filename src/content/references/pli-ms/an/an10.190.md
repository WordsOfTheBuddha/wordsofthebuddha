<!-- @segment an10.190:1.1 -->

“Kaṇhamaggañca vo, bhikkhave, desessāmi sukkamaggañca.

<!-- @segment an10.190:1.2 -->

Taṁ suṇātha …pe…

<!-- @segment an10.190:1.3 -->

katamo ca, bhikkhave, kaṇho maggo?

<!-- @segment an10.190:1.4 -->

Pāṇātipāto …pe…

<!-- @segment an10.190:1.5 -->

micchādiṭṭhi—

<!-- @segment an10.190:1.6 -->

ayaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, kaṇho maggo.

<!-- @segment an10.190:2.1 -->

Katamo ca, bhikkhave, sukko maggo?

<!-- @segment an10.190:2.2 -->

Pāṇātipātā veramaṇī …pe…

<!-- @segment an10.190:2.3 -->

sammādiṭṭhi—

<!-- @segment an10.190:2.4 -->

ayaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, sukko maggo”ti.

<!-- @segment an10.190:2.5 -->

Dutiyaṁ.
