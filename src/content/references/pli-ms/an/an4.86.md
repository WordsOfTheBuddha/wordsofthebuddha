<!-- @segment an4.86:1.1 -->

“Cattārome, bhikkhave, puggalā santo saṁvijjamānā lokasmiṁ.

<!-- @segment an4.86:1.2 -->

Katame cattāro?

<!-- @segment an4.86:1.3 -->

Oṇatoṇato,

<!-- @segment an4.86:1.4 -->

oṇatuṇṇato,

<!-- @segment an4.86:1.5 -->

uṇṇatoṇato,

<!-- @segment an4.86:1.6 -->

uṇṇatuṇṇato.

<!-- @segment an4.86:1.7 -->

… Ime kho, bhikkhave, cattāro puggalā santo saṁvijjamānā lokasmin”ti.

<!-- @segment an4.86:1.8 -->

Chaṭṭhaṁ.
