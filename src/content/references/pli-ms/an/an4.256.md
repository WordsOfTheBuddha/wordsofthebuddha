<!-- @segment an4.256:1.1 -->

“Cattārimāni, bhikkhave, saṅgahavatthūni.

<!-- @segment an4.256:1.2 -->

Katamāni cattāri?

<!-- @segment an4.256:1.3 -->

Dānaṁ, peyyavajjaṁ, atthacariyā, samānattatā—

<!-- @segment an4.256:1.4 -->

Imāni kho, bhikkhave, cattāri saṅgahavatthūnī”ti.

<!-- @segment an4.256:1.5 -->

Tatiyaṁ.
