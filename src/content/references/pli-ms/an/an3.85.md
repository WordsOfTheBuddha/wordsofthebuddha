<!-- @segment an3.85:1.1 -->

Atha kho aññataro bhikkhu yena bhagavā tenupasaṅkami; upasaṅkamitvā bhagavantaṁ abhivādetvā ekamantaṁ nisīdi. Ekamantaṁ nisinno kho so bhikkhu bhagavantaṁ etadavoca:

<!-- @segment an3.85:2.1 -->

“‘Sekho, sekho’ti, bhante, vuccati.

<!-- @segment an3.85:2.2 -->

Kittāvatā nu kho, bhante, sekho hotī”ti?

<!-- @segment an3.85:2.3 -->

“Sikkhatīti kho, bhikkhu, tasmā sekhoti vuccati.

<!-- @segment an3.85:2.4 -->

Kiñca sikkhati?

<!-- @segment an3.85:2.5 -->

Adhisīlampi sikkhati, adhicittampi sikkhati, adhipaññampi sikkhati.

<!-- @segment an3.85:2.6 -->

Sikkhatīti kho, bhikkhu, tasmā sekhoti vuccatīti.

<!-- @segment an3.85:3.1 -->

Sekhassa sikkhamānassa,

<!-- @segment an3.85:3.2 -->

ujumaggānusārino;

<!-- @segment an3.85:3.3 -->

Khayasmiṁ paṭhamaṁ ñāṇaṁ,

<!-- @segment an3.85:3.4 -->

tato aññā anantarā.

<!-- @segment an3.85:4.1 -->

Tato aññāvimuttassa,

<!-- @segment an3.85:4.2 -->

ñāṇaṁ ve hoti tādino;

<!-- @segment an3.85:4.3 -->

Akuppā me vimuttīti,

<!-- @segment an3.85:4.4 -->

bhavasaṁyojanakkhaye”ti.

<!-- @segment an3.85:4.5 -->

Pañcamaṁ.
