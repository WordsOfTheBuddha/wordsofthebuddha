<!-- @segment an2.151:1.1 -->

“Dveme, bhikkhave, santhārā.

<!-- @segment an2.151:1.2 -->

Katame dve?

<!-- @segment an2.151:1.4 -->

<!-- @segment an2.151:1.5 -->
