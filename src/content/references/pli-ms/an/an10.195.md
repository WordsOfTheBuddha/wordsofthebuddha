<!-- @segment an10.195:1.1 -->

“Bhāvetabbañca vo, bhikkhave, dhammaṁ desessāmi na bhāvetabbañca.

<!-- @segment an10.195:1.2 -->

Taṁ suṇātha …pe…

<!-- @segment an10.195:1.3 -->

katamo ca, bhikkhave, na bhāvetabbo dhammo?

<!-- @segment an10.195:1.4 -->

Pāṇātipāto …pe…

<!-- @segment an10.195:1.5 -->

micchādiṭṭhi—

<!-- @segment an10.195:1.6 -->

ayaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, na bhāvetabbo dhammo.

<!-- @segment an10.195:2.1 -->

Katamo ca, bhikkhave, bhāvetabbo dhammo?

<!-- @segment an10.195:2.2 -->

Pāṇātipātā veramaṇī …pe…

<!-- @segment an10.195:2.3 -->

sammādiṭṭhi—

<!-- @segment an10.195:2.4 -->

ayaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, bhāvetabbo dhammo”ti.

<!-- @segment an10.195:2.5 -->

Sattamaṁ.
