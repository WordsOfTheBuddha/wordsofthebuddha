<!-- @segment an4.15:1.1 -->

“Catasso imā, bhikkhave, aggapaññattiyo.

<!-- @segment an4.15:1.2 -->

Katamā catasso?

<!-- @segment an4.15:1.3 -->

Etadaggaṁ, bhikkhave, attabhāvīnaṁ yadidaṁ—

<!-- @segment an4.15:1.4 -->

rāhu asurindo.

<!-- @segment an4.15:1.5 -->

Etadaggaṁ, bhikkhave, kāmabhogīnaṁ yadidaṁ—

<!-- @segment an4.15:1.6 -->

rājā mandhātā.

<!-- @segment an4.15:1.7 -->

Etadaggaṁ, bhikkhave, ādhipateyyānaṁ yadidaṁ—

<!-- @segment an4.15:1.8 -->

māro pāpimā.

<!-- @segment an4.15:1.9 -->

Sadevake, bhikkhave, loke samārake sabrahmake sassamaṇabrāhmaṇiyā pajāya sadevamanussāya tathāgato aggamakkhāyati arahaṁ sammāsambuddho.

<!-- @segment an4.15:1.10 -->

Imā kho, bhikkhave, catasso aggapaññattiyoti.

<!-- @segment an4.15:2.1 -->

Rāhuggaṁ attabhāvīnaṁ,

<!-- @segment an4.15:2.2 -->

mandhātā kāmabhoginaṁ;

<!-- @segment an4.15:2.3 -->

Māro ādhipateyyānaṁ,

<!-- @segment an4.15:2.4 -->

iddhiyā yasasā jalaṁ.

<!-- @segment an4.15:3.1 -->

Uddhaṁ tiriyaṁ apācīnaṁ,

<!-- @segment an4.15:3.2 -->

yāvatājagatogati;

<!-- @segment an4.15:3.3 -->

Sadevakassa lokassa,

<!-- @segment an4.15:3.4 -->

buddho aggo pavuccatī”ti.

<!-- @segment an4.15:3.5 -->

Pañcamaṁ.
