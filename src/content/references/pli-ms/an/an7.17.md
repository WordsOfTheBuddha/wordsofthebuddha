<!-- @segment an7.17:1.1 -->

Sattime, bhikkhave, puggalā āhuneyyā …pe… anuttaraṁ puññakkhettaṁ lokassa.

<!-- @segment an7.17:1.2 -->

Katame satta?

<!-- @segment an7.17:1.3 -->

Idha, bhikkhave, ekacco puggalo sabbasaṅkhāresu dukkhānupassī viharati …pe….

<!-- @segment an7.17:1.4 -->

Sattamaṁ.
