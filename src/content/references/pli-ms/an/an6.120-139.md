<!-- @segment an6.120-139:1.1 -->

“Chahi, bhikkhave, dhammehi samannāgato bhalliko gahapati …pe…

<!-- @segment an6.120-139:1.2 -->

sudatto gahapati anāthapiṇḍiko …

<!-- @segment an6.120-139:1.3 -->

citto gahapati macchikāsaṇḍiko …

<!-- @segment an6.120-139:1.4 -->

hatthako āḷavako …

<!-- @segment an6.120-139:1.5 -->

mahānāmo sakko …

<!-- @segment an6.120-139:1.6 -->

uggo gahapati vesāliko …

<!-- @segment an6.120-139:1.7 -->

uggato gahapati …

<!-- @segment an6.120-139:1.8 -->

sūrambaṭṭho …

<!-- @segment an6.120-139:1.9 -->

jīvako komārabhacco …

<!-- @segment an6.120-139:1.10 -->

nakulapitā gahapati …

<!-- @segment an6.120-139:1.11 -->

tavakaṇṇiko gahapati …

<!-- @segment an6.120-139:1.12 -->

pūraṇo gahapati …

<!-- @segment an6.120-139:1.13 -->

isidatto gahapati …

<!-- @segment an6.120-139:1.14 -->

sandhāno gahapati …

<!-- @segment an6.120-139:1.15 -->

vicayo gahapati …

<!-- @segment an6.120-139:1.16 -->

vijayamāhiko gahapati …

<!-- @segment an6.120-139:1.17 -->

meṇḍako gahapati …

<!-- @segment an6.120-139:1.18 -->

vāseṭṭho upāsako …

<!-- @segment an6.120-139:1.19 -->

ariṭṭho upāsako …

<!-- @segment an6.120-139:1.20 -->

sāraggo upāsako tathāgate niṭṭhaṅgato amataddaso amataṁ sacchikatvā iriyati.

<!-- @segment an6.120-139:1.21 -->

Katamehi chahi?

<!-- @segment an6.120-139:1.22 -->

Buddhe aveccappasādena, dhamme aveccappasādena, saṅghe aveccappasādena, ariyena sīlena, ariyena ñāṇena, ariyāya vimuttiyā.

<!-- @segment an6.120-139:1.23 -->

Imehi kho, bhikkhave, chahi dhammehi samannāgato sāraggo upāsako tathāgate niṭṭhaṅgato amataddaso amataṁ sacchikatvā iriyatī”ti.

<!-- @segment an6.120-139:1.24 -->

Tevīsatimaṁ.

<!-- @segment an6.120-139:1.25 -->

Sāmaññavaggo dvādasamo.
