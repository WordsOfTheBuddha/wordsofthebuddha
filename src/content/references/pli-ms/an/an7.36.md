<!-- @segment an7.36:1.1 -->

“Sattahi, bhikkhave, aṅgehi samannāgato mitto sevitabbo.

<!-- @segment an7.36:1.2 -->

Katamehi sattahi?

<!-- @segment an7.36:1.3 -->

Duddadaṁ dadāti, dukkaraṁ karoti, dukkhamaṁ khamati, guyhamassa āvi karoti, guyhamassa pariguhati, āpadāsu na jahati, khīṇena nātimaññati.

<!-- @segment an7.36:1.4 -->

Imehi kho, bhikkhave, sattahi aṅgehi samannāgato mitto sevitabboti.

<!-- @segment an7.36:2.1 -->

Duddadaṁ dadāti mitto,

<!-- @segment an7.36:2.2 -->

dukkarañcāpi kubbati;

<!-- @segment an7.36:2.3 -->

Athopissa duruttāni,

<!-- @segment an7.36:2.4 -->

khamati dukkhamāni ca.

<!-- @segment an7.36:3.1 -->

Guyhañca tassa akkhāti,

<!-- @segment an7.36:3.2 -->

guyhassa parigūhati;

<!-- @segment an7.36:3.3 -->

Āpadāsu na jahāti,

<!-- @segment an7.36:3.4 -->

khīṇena nātimaññati.

<!-- @segment an7.36:4.1 -->

Yamhi etāni ṭhānāni,

<!-- @segment an7.36:4.2 -->

saṁvijjantīdha puggale;

<!-- @segment an7.36:4.3 -->

So mitto mittakāmena,

<!-- @segment an7.36:4.4 -->

bhajitabbo tathāvidho”ti.

<!-- @segment an7.36:4.5 -->

Pañcamaṁ.
