<!-- @segment an5.303:1.1 -->

“Rāgassa, bhikkhave, abhiññāya pañca dhammā bhāvetabbā.

<!-- @segment an5.303:1.2 -->

Katame pañca?

<!-- @segment an5.303:1.3 -->

Asubhasaññā, maraṇasaññā, ādīnavasaññā, āhāre paṭikūlasaññā, sabbaloke anabhiratasaññā—

<!-- @segment an5.303:1.4 -->

rāgassa, bhikkhave, abhiññāya ime pañca dhammā bhāvetabbā”ti.
