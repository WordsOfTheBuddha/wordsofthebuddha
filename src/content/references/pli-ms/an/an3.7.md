<!-- @segment an3.7:1.1 -->

“Tīhi, bhikkhave, dhammehi samannāgato bālo veditabbo.

<!-- @segment an3.7:1.2 -->

Katamehi tīhi?

<!-- @segment an3.7:1.3 -->

Sāvajjena kāyakammena, sāvajjena vacīkammena, sāvajjena manokammena …pe… anavajjena kāyakammena, anavajjena vacīkammena, anavajjena manokammena …pe….

<!-- @segment an3.7:1.4 -->

Sattamaṁ.
