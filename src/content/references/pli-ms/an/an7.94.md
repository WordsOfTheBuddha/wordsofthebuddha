<!-- @segment an7.94:1.1 -->

“Sattime, bhikkhave, saddhammā.

<!-- @segment an7.94:1.2 -->

Katame satta?

<!-- @segment an7.94:1.3 -->

Saddho hoti, hirīmā hoti, ottappī hoti, bahussuto hoti, āraddhavīriyo hoti, satimā hoti, paññavā hoti.

<!-- @segment an7.94:1.4 -->

Ime kho, bhikkhave, satta saddhammā”ti.

<!-- @segment an7.94:1.5 -->

Dasamaṁ.

<!-- @segment an7.94:1.6 -->

Samaṇavaggo navamo.

<!-- @segment an7.94:2.0 -->

Tassuddānaṁ

<!-- @segment an7.94:2.1 -->

Bhikkhuṁ samaṇo brāhmaṇo,

<!-- @segment an7.94:2.2 -->

sottiyo ceva nhātako;

<!-- @segment an7.94:2.3 -->

Vedagū ariyo arahā,

<!-- @segment an7.94:2.4 -->

asaddhammā ca saddhammāti.
