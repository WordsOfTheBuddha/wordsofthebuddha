<!-- @segment an6.141:1.1 -->

“Rāgassa, bhikkhave, abhiññāya cha dhammā bhāvetabbā.

<!-- @segment an6.141:1.2 -->

Katame cha?

<!-- @segment an6.141:1.3 -->

Buddhānussati, dhammānussati, saṅghānussati, sīlānussati, cāgānussati, devatānussati.

<!-- @segment an6.141:1.4 -->

Rāgassa, bhikkhave, abhiññāya ime cha dhammā bhāvetabbā”ti.
