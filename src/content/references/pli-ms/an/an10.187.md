<!-- @segment an10.187:1.1 -->

“Dukkhudrayañca vo, bhikkhave, dhammaṁ desessāmi sukhudrayañca.

<!-- @segment an10.187:1.2 -->

Taṁ suṇātha …pe…

<!-- @segment an10.187:1.3 -->

katamo ca, bhikkhave, dukkhudrayo dhammo?

<!-- @segment an10.187:1.4 -->

Pāṇātipāto …pe…

<!-- @segment an10.187:1.5 -->

micchādiṭṭhi—

<!-- @segment an10.187:1.6 -->

ayaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, dukkhudrayo dhammo.

<!-- @segment an10.187:2.1 -->

Katamo ca, bhikkhave, sukhudrayo dhammo?

<!-- @segment an10.187:2.2 -->

Pāṇātipātā veramaṇī …pe…

<!-- @segment an10.187:2.3 -->

sammādiṭṭhi—

<!-- @segment an10.187:2.4 -->

ayaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, sukhudrayo dhammo”ti.

<!-- @segment an10.187:2.5 -->

Dasamaṁ.
