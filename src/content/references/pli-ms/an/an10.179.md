<!-- @segment an10.179:1.1 -->

“Ariyadhammañca vo, bhikkhave, desessāmi anariyadhammañca.

<!-- @segment an10.179:1.2 -->

Taṁ suṇātha …pe…

<!-- @segment an10.179:1.3 -->

katamo ca, bhikkhave, anariyo dhammo?

<!-- @segment an10.179:1.4 -->

Pāṇātipāto …pe…

<!-- @segment an10.179:1.5 -->

micchādiṭṭhi—

<!-- @segment an10.179:1.6 -->

ayaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, anariyo dhammo.

<!-- @segment an10.179:2.1 -->

Katamo ca, bhikkhave, ariyo dhammo?

<!-- @segment an10.179:2.2 -->

Pāṇātipātā veramaṇī …pe…

<!-- @segment an10.179:2.3 -->

sammādiṭṭhi—

<!-- @segment an10.179:2.4 -->

ayaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, ariyo dhammo”ti.

<!-- @segment an10.179:2.5 -->

Dutiyaṁ.
