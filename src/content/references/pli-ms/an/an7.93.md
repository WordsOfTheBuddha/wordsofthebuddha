<!-- @segment an7.93:1.1 -->

“Sattime, bhikkhave, asaddhammā.

<!-- @segment an7.93:1.2 -->

Katame satta?

<!-- @segment an7.93:1.3 -->

Assaddho hoti, ahiriko hoti, anottappī hoti, appassuto hoti, kusīto hoti, muṭṭhassati hoti, duppañño hoti.

<!-- @segment an7.93:1.4 -->

Ime kho, bhikkhave, satta asaddhammā”ti.

<!-- @segment an7.93:1.5 -->

Navamaṁ.
