<!-- @segment an3.135:1.1 -->

“Tīhi, bhikkhave, aṅgehi samannāgato mitto sevitabbo.

<!-- @segment an3.135:1.2 -->

Katamehi tīhi? (…)

<!-- @segment an3.135:1.3 -->

Duddadaṁ dadāti, dukkaraṁ karoti, dukkhamaṁ khamati—

<!-- @segment an3.135:1.4 -->

imehi kho, bhikkhave, tīhi aṅgehi samannāgato mitto sevitabbo”ti.

<!-- @segment an3.135:1.5 -->

Tatiyaṁ.
