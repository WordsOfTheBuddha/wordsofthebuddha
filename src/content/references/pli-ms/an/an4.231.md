<!-- @segment an4.231:1.1 -->

“Cattārome, bhikkhave, kavī.

<!-- @segment an4.231:1.2 -->

Katame cattāro?

<!-- @segment an4.231:1.3 -->

Cintākavi, sutakavi, atthakavi, paṭibhānakavi—

<!-- @segment an4.231:1.4 -->

Ime kho, bhikkhave, cattāro kavī”ti.

<!-- @segment an4.231:1.5 -->

Ekādasamaṁ.

<!-- @segment an4.231:1.6 -->

Duccaritavaggo tatiyo.

<!-- @segment an4.231:2.0 -->

Tassuddānaṁ

<!-- @segment an4.231:2.1 -->

Duccaritaṁ diṭṭhi akataññū ca,

<!-- @segment an4.231:2.2 -->

Pāṇātipātāpi dve maggā;

<!-- @segment an4.231:2.3 -->

Dve vohārapathā vuttā,

<!-- @segment an4.231:2.4 -->

Ahirikaṁ duppaññakavinā cāti.
