<!-- @segment an10.193:1.1 -->

“Uppādetabbañca vo, bhikkhave, dhammaṁ desessāmi na uppādetabbañca.

<!-- @segment an10.193:1.2 -->

Taṁ suṇātha …pe…

<!-- @segment an10.193:1.3 -->

katamo ca, bhikkhave, na uppādetabbo dhammo?

<!-- @segment an10.193:1.4 -->

Pāṇātipāto …pe…

<!-- @segment an10.193:1.5 -->

micchādiṭṭhi—

<!-- @segment an10.193:1.6 -->

ayaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, na uppādetabbo dhammo.

<!-- @segment an10.193:2.1 -->

Katamo ca, bhikkhave, uppādetabbo dhammo?

<!-- @segment an10.193:2.2 -->

Pāṇātipātā veramaṇī …pe…

<!-- @segment an10.193:2.3 -->

sammādiṭṭhi—

<!-- @segment an10.193:2.4 -->

ayaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, uppādetabbo dhammo”ti.

<!-- @segment an10.193:2.5 -->

Pañcamaṁ.
