<!-- @segment an3.139:1.1 -->

“Tisso imā, bhikkhave, vuddhiyo.

<!-- @segment an3.139:1.2 -->

Katamā tisso?

<!-- @segment an3.139:1.3 -->

Saddhāvuddhi, sīlavuddhi, paññāvuddhi—

<!-- @segment an3.139:1.4 -->

imā kho, bhikkhave, tisso vuddhiyo”ti.

<!-- @segment an3.139:1.5 -->

Sattamaṁ.
