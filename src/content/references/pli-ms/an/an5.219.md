<!-- @segment an5.219:1.1 -->

“Pañcime, bhikkhave, ādīnavā aggismiṁ.

<!-- @segment an5.219:1.2 -->

Katame pañca?

<!-- @segment an5.219:1.3 -->

Acakkhusso, dubbaṇṇakaraṇo, dubbalakaraṇo, saṅgaṇikāpavaḍḍhano, tiracchānakathāpavattaniko hoti.

<!-- @segment an5.219:1.4 -->

Ime kho, bhikkhave, pañca ādīnavā aggismin”ti.

<!-- @segment an5.219:1.5 -->

Navamaṁ.
