<!-- @segment sn22.120:1.1 -->

Sāvatthinidānaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn22.120:1.2 -->

“Saṁyojaniye ca, bhikkhave, dhamme desessāmi saṁyojanañca.

<!-- @segment sn22.120:1.3 -->

Taṁ suṇātha.

<!-- @segment sn22.120:1.4 -->

Katame ca, bhikkhave, saṁyojaniyā dhammā, katamaṁ saṁyojanaṁ?

<!-- @segment sn22.120:1.5 -->

Rūpaṁ, bhikkhave, saṁyojaniyo dhammo;

<!-- @segment sn22.120:1.6 -->

yo tattha chandarāgo, taṁ tattha saṁyojanaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn22.120:1.7 -->

Vedanā …pe…

<!-- @segment sn22.120:1.8 -->

saññā …

<!-- @segment sn22.120:1.9 -->

saṅkhārā …

<!-- @segment sn22.120:1.10 -->

viññāṇaṁ saṁyojaniyo dhammo;

<!-- @segment sn22.120:1.11 -->

yo tattha chandarāgo, taṁ tattha saṁyojanaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn22.120:1.12 -->

Ime vuccanti, bhikkhave, saṁyojaniyā dhammā, idaṁ saṁyojanan”ti.

<!-- @segment sn22.120:1.13 -->

Aṭṭhamaṁ.
