<!-- @segment sn22.40:1.1 -->

Sāvatthinidānaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn22.40:1.2 -->

“Dhammānudhammappaṭipannassa, bhikkhave, bhikkhuno ayamanudhammo hoti yaṁ rūpe aniccānupassī vihareyya …pe…

<!-- @segment sn22.40:1.3 -->

‘parimuccati dukkhasmā’ti vadāmī”ti.

<!-- @segment sn22.40:1.4 -->

Aṭṭhamaṁ.
