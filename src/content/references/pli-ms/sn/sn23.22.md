<!-- @segment sn23.22:1.1 -->

Sāvatthinidānaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn23.22:1.2 -->

Ekamantaṁ nisinno kho āyasmā rādho bhagavantaṁ etadavoca:

<!-- @segment sn23.22:1.3 -->

“‘nirodhadhammo, nirodhadhammo’ti, bhante, vuccati.

<!-- @segment sn23.22:1.4 -->

Katamo nu kho, bhante, nirodhadhammo”ti?

<!-- @segment sn23.22:1.5 -->

“Rūpaṁ kho, rādha, nirodhadhammo, vedanā nirodhadhammo, saññā nirodhadhammo, saṅkhārā nirodhadhammo, viññāṇaṁ nirodhadhammo.

<!-- @segment sn23.22:1.6 -->

Evaṁ passaṁ …pe…

<!-- @segment sn23.22:1.7 -->

nāparaṁ itthattāyāti pajānātī”ti.

<!-- @segment sn23.22:1.8 -->

Dvādasamaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn23.22:1.9 -->

Dutiyamāravaggo.

<!-- @segment sn23.22:2.0 -->

Tassuddānaṁ

<!-- @segment sn23.22:2.1 -->

Māro ca māradhammo ca,

<!-- @segment sn23.22:2.2 -->

Aniccena apare duve;

<!-- @segment sn23.22:2.3 -->

Dukkhena ca duve vuttā,

<!-- @segment sn23.22:2.4 -->

Anattena tatheva ca;

<!-- @segment sn23.22:2.5 -->

Khayavayasamudayaṁ,

<!-- @segment sn23.22:2.6 -->

Nirodhadhammena dvādasāti.
