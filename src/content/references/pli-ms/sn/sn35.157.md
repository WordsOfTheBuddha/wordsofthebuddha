<!-- @segment sn35.157:1.1 -->

“Anicceyeva, bhikkhave, bhikkhu rūpe aniccāti passati, sāssa hoti sammādiṭṭhi.

<!-- @segment sn35.157:1.2 -->

Sammā passaṁ nibbindati.

<!-- @segment sn35.157:1.3 -->

Nandikkhayā rāgakkhayo;

<!-- @segment sn35.157:1.4 -->

rāgakkhayā nandikkhayo.

<!-- @segment sn35.157:1.5 -->

Nandirāgakkhayā cittaṁ suvimuttanti vuccati.

<!-- @segment sn35.157:1.6 -->

Anicceyeva, bhikkhave, bhikkhu sadde …

<!-- @segment sn35.157:1.7 -->

gandhe …

<!-- @segment sn35.157:1.8 -->

rase …

<!-- @segment sn35.157:1.9 -->

phoṭṭhabbe …

<!-- @segment sn35.157:1.10 -->

dhamme aniccāti passati, sāssa hoti sammādiṭṭhi.

<!-- @segment sn35.157:1.11 -->

Sammā passaṁ nibbindati.

<!-- @segment sn35.157:1.12 -->

Nandikkhayā rāgakkhayo;

<!-- @segment sn35.157:1.13 -->

rāgakkhayā nandikkhayo.

<!-- @segment sn35.157:1.14 -->

Nandirāgakkhayā cittaṁ suvimuttanti vuccatī”ti.

<!-- @segment sn35.157:1.15 -->

Dutiyaṁ.
