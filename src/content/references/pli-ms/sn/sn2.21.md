<!-- @segment sn2.21:1.1 -->

Evaṁ me sutaṁ—

<!-- @segment sn2.21:1.2 -->

ekaṁ samayaṁ bhagavā sāvatthiyaṁ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme.

<!-- @segment sn2.21:1.3 -->

Atha kho sivo devaputto abhikkantāya rattiyā abhikkantavaṇṇo kevalakappaṁ jetavanaṁ obhāsetvā yena bhagavā tenupasaṅkami; upasaṅkamitvā bhagavantaṁ abhivādetvā ekamantaṁ aṭṭhāsi.

<!-- @segment sn2.21:1.4 -->

Ekamantaṁ ṭhito kho sivo devaputto bhagavato santike imā gāthāyo abhāsi:

<!-- @segment sn2.21:2.1 -->

“Sabbhireva samāsetha,

<!-- @segment sn2.21:2.2 -->

sabbhi kubbetha santhavaṁ;

<!-- @segment sn2.21:2.3 -->

Sataṁ saddhammamaññāya,

<!-- @segment sn2.21:2.4 -->

seyyo hoti na pāpiyo.

<!-- @segment sn2.21:3.1 -->

Sabbhireva samāsetha,

<!-- @segment sn2.21:3.2 -->

sabbhi kubbetha santhavaṁ;

<!-- @segment sn2.21:3.3 -->

Sataṁ saddhammamaññāya,

<!-- @segment sn2.21:3.4 -->

paññā labbhati nāññato.

<!-- @segment sn2.21:4.1 -->

Sabbhireva samāsetha,

<!-- @segment sn2.21:4.2 -->

sabbhi kubbetha santhavaṁ;

<!-- @segment sn2.21:4.3 -->

Sataṁ saddhammamaññāya,

<!-- @segment sn2.21:4.4 -->

sokamajjhe na socati.

<!-- @segment sn2.21:5.1 -->

Sabbhireva samāsetha,

<!-- @segment sn2.21:5.2 -->

sabbhi kubbetha santhavaṁ;

<!-- @segment sn2.21:5.3 -->

Sataṁ saddhammamaññāya,

<!-- @segment sn2.21:5.4 -->

ñātimajjhe virocati.

<!-- @segment sn2.21:6.1 -->

Sabbhireva samāsetha,

<!-- @segment sn2.21:6.2 -->

sabbhi kubbetha santhavaṁ;

<!-- @segment sn2.21:6.3 -->

Sataṁ saddhammamaññāya,

<!-- @segment sn2.21:6.4 -->

sattā gacchanti suggatiṁ.

<!-- @segment sn2.21:7.1 -->

Sabbhireva samāsetha,

<!-- @segment sn2.21:7.2 -->

sabbhi kubbetha santhavaṁ;

<!-- @segment sn2.21:7.3 -->

Sataṁ saddhammamaññāya,

<!-- @segment sn2.21:7.4 -->

sattā tiṭṭhanti sātatan”ti.

<!-- @segment sn2.21:8.1 -->

Atha kho bhagavā sivaṁ devaputtaṁ gāthāya paccabhāsi:

<!-- @segment sn2.21:9.1 -->

“Sabbhireva samāsetha,

<!-- @segment sn2.21:9.2 -->

sabbhi kubbetha santhavaṁ;

<!-- @segment sn2.21:9.3 -->

Sataṁ saddhammamaññāya,

<!-- @segment sn2.21:9.4 -->

sabbadukkhā pamuccatī”ti.
