<!-- @segment sn46.111-120:1.1 -->

“Tisso imā, bhikkhave, esanā.

<!-- @segment sn46.111-120:1.2 -->

Katamā tisso?

<!-- @segment sn46.111-120:1.3 -->

Kāmesanā, bhavesanā, brahmacariyesanā”ti vitthāretabbaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn46.111-120:1.4 -->

Esanāvaggo dvādasamo.

<!-- @segment sn46.111-120:2.0 -->

Tassuddānaṁ

<!-- @segment sn46.111-120:2.1 -->

Esanā vidhā āsavo,

<!-- @segment sn46.111-120:2.2 -->

Bhavo ca dukkhatā tisso;

<!-- @segment sn46.111-120:2.3 -->

Khilaṁ malañca nīgho ca,

<!-- @segment sn46.111-120:2.4 -->

Vedanā taṇhā tasināya cāti.

<!-- @segment sn46.111-120:3.1 -->

(Bojjhaṅgasaṁyuttassa esanāpeyyālaṁ vivekanissitato vitthāretabbaṁ.)
