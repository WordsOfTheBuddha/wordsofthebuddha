<!-- @segment sn35.6:1.1 -->

“Rūpā, bhikkhave, anattā.

<!-- @segment sn35.6:1.2 -->

Yadanattā taṁ ‘netaṁ mama, nesohamasmi, na meso attā’ti evametaṁ yathābhūtaṁ sammappaññāya daṭṭhabbaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn35.6:1.3 -->

Saddā …pe…

<!-- @segment sn35.6:1.4 -->

gandhā …

<!-- @segment sn35.6:1.5 -->

rasā …

<!-- @segment sn35.6:1.6 -->

phoṭṭhabbā …

<!-- @segment sn35.6:1.7 -->

dhammā anattā.

<!-- @segment sn35.6:1.8 -->

Yadanattā taṁ ‘netaṁ mama, nesohamasmi, na meso attā’ti evametaṁ yathābhūtaṁ sammappaññāya daṭṭhabbaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn35.6:1.9 -->

Evaṁ passaṁ …pe…

<!-- @segment sn35.6:1.10 -->

nāparaṁ itthattāyāti pajānātī”ti.

<!-- @segment sn35.6:1.11 -->

Chaṭṭhaṁ.
