<!-- @segment sn47.41:1.1 -->

Sāvatthinidānaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn47.41:1.2 -->

“Catūsu, bhikkhave, satipaṭṭhānesu suppatiṭṭhitacittā viharatha.

<!-- @segment sn47.41:1.3 -->

Mā vo amataṁ panassa.

<!-- @segment sn47.41:1.4 -->

Katamesu catūsu?

<!-- @segment sn47.41:1.5 -->

Idha, bhikkhave, bhikkhu kāye kāyānupassī viharati ātāpī sampajāno satimā, vineyya loke abhijjhādomanassaṁ;

<!-- @segment sn47.41:1.6 -->

vedanāsu …pe…

<!-- @segment sn47.41:1.7 -->

citte …pe…

<!-- @segment sn47.41:1.8 -->

dhammesu dhammānupassī viharati ātāpī sampajāno satimā, vineyya loke abhijjhādomanassaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn47.41:1.9 -->

Imesu, bhikkhave, catūsu satipaṭṭhānesu suppatiṭṭhitacittā viharatha.

<!-- @segment sn47.41:1.10 -->

Mā vo amataṁ panassā”ti.

<!-- @segment sn47.41:1.11 -->

Paṭhamaṁ.
