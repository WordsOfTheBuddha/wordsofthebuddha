<!-- @segment sn46.121-129:1.1 -->

“Cattārome, bhikkhave, oghā.

<!-- @segment sn46.121-129:1.2 -->

Katame cattāro?

<!-- @segment sn46.121-129:1.3 -->

Kāmogho, bhavogho, diṭṭhogho, avijjogho”ti.

<!-- @segment sn46.121-129:1.4 -->

vitthāretabbaṁ.
