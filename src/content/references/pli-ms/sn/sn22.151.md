<!-- @segment sn22.151:1.1 -->

Sāvatthinidānaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn22.151:1.2 -->

“Kismiṁ nu kho, bhikkhave, sati, kiṁ upādāya, kiṁ abhinivissa:

<!-- @segment sn22.151:1.3 -->

‘etaṁ mama, esohamasmi, eso me attā’ti samanupassatī”ti?

<!-- @segment sn22.151:1.4 -->

Bhagavaṁmūlakā no, bhante, dhammā …pe…

<!-- @segment sn22.151:1.5 -->

“rūpe kho, bhikkhave, sati, rūpaṁ upādāya, rūpaṁ abhinivissa …pe…

<!-- @segment sn22.151:1.6 -->

viññāṇe sati, viññāṇaṁ upādāya, viññāṇaṁ abhinivissa:

<!-- @segment sn22.151:1.7 -->

‘etaṁ mama, esohamasmi, eso me attā’ti samanupassati.

<!-- @segment sn22.151:1.8 -->

Taṁ kiṁ maññatha, bhikkhave,

<!-- @segment sn22.151:1.9 -->

rūpaṁ niccaṁ vā aniccaṁ vā”ti?

<!-- @segment sn22.151:1.10 -->

“Aniccaṁ, bhante” …pe…

<!-- @segment sn22.151:1.11 -->

vipariṇāmadhammaṁ, api nu taṁ anupādāya etaṁ mama, esohamasmi, eso me attāti samanupasseyyāti?

<!-- @segment sn22.151:1.12 -->

“No hetaṁ, bhante”.

<!-- @segment sn22.151:1.13 -->

“Vedanā …

<!-- @segment sn22.151:1.14 -->

saññā …

<!-- @segment sn22.151:1.15 -->

saṅkhārā …

<!-- @segment sn22.151:1.16 -->

viññāṇaṁ niccaṁ vā aniccaṁ vā”ti?

<!-- @segment sn22.151:1.17 -->

“Aniccaṁ, bhante” …pe…

<!-- @segment sn22.151:1.18 -->

vipariṇāmadhammaṁ, api nu taṁ anupādāya etaṁ mama, esohamasmi, eso me attāti samanupasseyyāti?

<!-- @segment sn22.151:1.19 -->

“No hetaṁ, bhante”.

<!-- @segment sn22.151:1.20 -->

“Evaṁ passaṁ …pe…

<!-- @segment sn22.151:1.21 -->

nāparaṁ itthattāyāti pajānātī”ti.

<!-- @segment sn22.151:1.22 -->

Dutiyaṁ.
