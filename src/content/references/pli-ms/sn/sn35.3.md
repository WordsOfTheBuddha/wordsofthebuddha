<!-- @segment sn35.3:1.1 -->

“Cakkhuṁ, bhikkhave, anattā.

<!-- @segment sn35.3:1.2 -->

Yadanattā taṁ ‘netaṁ mama, nesohamasmi, na meso attā’ti evametaṁ yathābhūtaṁ sammappaññāya daṭṭhabbaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn35.3:1.3 -->

Sotaṁ anattā …pe…

<!-- @segment sn35.3:1.4 -->

ghānaṁ anattā …

<!-- @segment sn35.3:1.5 -->

jivhā anattā …

<!-- @segment sn35.3:1.6 -->

kāyo anattā …

<!-- @segment sn35.3:1.7 -->

mano anattā.

<!-- @segment sn35.3:1.8 -->

Yadanattā taṁ ‘netaṁ mama, nesohamasmi, na meso attā’ti evametaṁ yathābhūtaṁ sammappaññāya daṭṭhabbaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn35.3:1.9 -->

Evaṁ passaṁ …pe…

<!-- @segment sn35.3:1.10 -->

nāparaṁ itthattāyāti pajānātī”ti.

<!-- @segment sn35.3:1.11 -->

Tatiyaṁ.
