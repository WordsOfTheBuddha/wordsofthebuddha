<!-- @segment sn35.101:1.1 -->

“Yaṁ, bhikkhave, na tumhākaṁ, taṁ pajahatha.

<!-- @segment sn35.101:1.2 -->

Taṁ vo pahīnaṁ hitāya sukhāya bhavissati.

<!-- @segment sn35.101:1.3 -->

Kiñca, bhikkhave, na tumhākaṁ?

<!-- @segment sn35.101:1.4 -->

Cakkhu, bhikkhave, na tumhākaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn35.101:1.5 -->

Taṁ pajahatha.

<!-- @segment sn35.101:1.6 -->

Taṁ vo pahīnaṁ hitāya sukhāya bhavissati.

<!-- @segment sn35.101:1.7 -->

Rūpā na tumhākaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn35.101:1.8 -->

Te pajahatha.

<!-- @segment sn35.101:1.9 -->

Te vo pahīnā hitāya sukhāya bhavissanti.

<!-- @segment sn35.101:1.10 -->

Cakkhuviññāṇaṁ na tumhākaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn35.101:1.11 -->

Taṁ pajahatha.

<!-- @segment sn35.101:1.12 -->

Taṁ vo pahīnaṁ hitāya sukhāya bhavissati.

<!-- @segment sn35.101:1.13 -->

Cakkhusamphasso na tumhākaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn35.101:1.14 -->

Taṁ pajahatha.

<!-- @segment sn35.101:1.15 -->

So vo pahīno hitāya sukhāya bhavissati.

<!-- @segment sn35.101:1.16 -->

Yampidaṁ cakkhusamphassapaccayā uppajjati vedayitaṁ sukhaṁ vā dukkhaṁ vā adukkhamasukhaṁ vā tampi na tumhākaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn35.101:1.17 -->

Taṁ pajahatha.

<!-- @segment sn35.101:1.18 -->

Taṁ vo pahīnaṁ hitāya sukhāya bhavissati.

<!-- @segment sn35.101:1.19 -->

…pe…

<!-- @segment sn35.101:2.1 -->

Jivhā na tumhākaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn35.101:2.2 -->

Taṁ pajahatha.

<!-- @segment sn35.101:2.3 -->

Sā vo pahīnā hitāya sukhāya bhavissati.

<!-- @segment sn35.101:2.4 -->

Rasā na tumhākaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn35.101:2.5 -->

Te pajahatha.

<!-- @segment sn35.101:2.6 -->

Te vo pahīnā hitāya sukhāya bhavissanti.

<!-- @segment sn35.101:2.7 -->

Jivhāviññāṇaṁ na tumhākaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn35.101:2.8 -->

Taṁ pajahatha.

<!-- @segment sn35.101:2.9 -->

Taṁ vo pahīnaṁ hitāya sukhāya bhavissati.

<!-- @segment sn35.101:2.10 -->

Jivhāsamphasso na tumhākaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn35.101:2.11 -->

Taṁ pajahatha.

<!-- @segment sn35.101:2.12 -->

So vo pahīno hitāya sukhāya bhavissati.

<!-- @segment sn35.101:2.13 -->

Yampidaṁ jivhāsamphassapaccayā uppajjati vedayitaṁ sukhaṁ vā dukkhaṁ vā adukkhamasukhaṁ vā tampi na tumhākaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn35.101:2.14 -->

Taṁ pajahatha. Taṁ vo pahīnaṁ hitāya sukhāya bhavissati …pe….

<!-- @segment sn35.101:3.1 -->

Mano na tumhākaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn35.101:3.2 -->

Taṁ pajahatha.

<!-- @segment sn35.101:3.3 -->

So vo pahīno hitāya sukhāya bhavissati.

<!-- @segment sn35.101:3.4 -->

Dhammā na tumhākaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn35.101:3.5 -->

Te pajahatha.

<!-- @segment sn35.101:3.6 -->

Te vo pahīnā hitāya sukhāya bhavissanti.

<!-- @segment sn35.101:3.7 -->

Manoviññāṇaṁ na tumhākaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn35.101:3.8 -->

Taṁ pajahatha.

<!-- @segment sn35.101:3.9 -->

Taṁ vo pahīnaṁ hitāya sukhāya bhavissati.

<!-- @segment sn35.101:3.10 -->

Manosamphasso na tumhākaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn35.101:3.11 -->

Taṁ pajahatha.

<!-- @segment sn35.101:3.12 -->

So vo pahīno hitāya sukhāya bhavissati.

<!-- @segment sn35.101:3.13 -->

Yampidaṁ manosamphassapaccayā uppajjati vedayitaṁ sukhaṁ vā dukkhaṁ vā adukkhamasukhaṁ vā tampi na tumhākaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn35.101:3.14 -->

Taṁ pajahatha.

<!-- @segment sn35.101:3.15 -->

Taṁ vo pahīnaṁ hitāya sukhāya bhavissati.

<!-- @segment sn35.101:4.1 -->

Seyyathāpi, bhikkhave, yaṁ imasmiṁ jetavane tiṇakaṭṭhasākhāpalāsaṁ taṁ jano hareyya vā ḍaheyya vā yathāpaccayaṁ vā kareyya,

<!-- @segment sn35.101:4.2 -->

api nu tumhākaṁ evamassa:

<!-- @segment sn35.101:4.3 -->

‘amhe jano harati vā ḍahati vā yathāpaccayaṁ vā karotī’”ti?

<!-- @segment sn35.101:5.1 -->

“No hetaṁ, bhante”.

<!-- @segment sn35.101:6.1 -->

“Taṁ kissa hetu”?

<!-- @segment sn35.101:7.1 -->

“Na hi no etaṁ, bhante, attā vā attaniyaṁ vā”ti.

<!-- @segment sn35.101:8.1 -->

“Evameva kho, bhikkhave, cakkhu na tumhākaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn35.101:8.2 -->

Taṁ pajahatha.

<!-- @segment sn35.101:8.3 -->

Taṁ vo pahīnaṁ hitāya sukhāya bhavissati.

<!-- @segment sn35.101:8.4 -->

Rūpā na tumhākaṁ …

<!-- @segment sn35.101:8.5 -->

cakkhuviññāṇaṁ …

<!-- @segment sn35.101:8.6 -->

cakkhusamphasso …pe…

<!-- @segment sn35.101:8.7 -->

yampidaṁ manosamphassapaccayā uppajjati vedayitaṁ sukhaṁ vā dukkhaṁ vā adukkhamasukhaṁ vā tampi na tumhākaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn35.101:8.8 -->

Taṁ pajahatha.

<!-- @segment sn35.101:8.9 -->

Taṁ vo pahīnaṁ hitāya sukhāya bhavissatī”ti.

<!-- @segment sn35.101:8.10 -->

Aṭṭhamaṁ.
