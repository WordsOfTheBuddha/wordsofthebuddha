<!-- @segment sn35.105:1.1 -->

“Kismiṁ nu kho, bhikkhave, sati kiṁ upādāya uppajjati ajjhattaṁ sukhaṁ dukkhan”ti?

<!-- @segment sn35.105:2.1 -->

“Bhagavaṁmūlakā no, bhante, dhammā …pe….

<!-- @segment sn35.105:3.1 -->

“Cakkhusmiṁ kho, bhikkhave, sati cakkhuṁ upādāya uppajjati ajjhattaṁ sukhaṁ dukkhaṁ …pe…

<!-- @segment sn35.105:3.2 -->

manasmiṁ sati manaṁ upādāya uppajjati ajjhattaṁ sukhaṁ dukkhaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn35.105:3.3 -->

Taṁ kiṁ maññatha, bhikkhave,

<!-- @segment sn35.105:3.4 -->

cakkhu niccaṁ vā aniccaṁ vā”ti?

<!-- @segment sn35.105:4.1 -->

“Aniccaṁ, bhante”.

<!-- @segment sn35.105:5.1 -->

“Yaṁ panāniccaṁ dukkhaṁ vā taṁ sukhaṁ vā”ti?

<!-- @segment sn35.105:6.1 -->

“Dukkhaṁ, bhante”.

<!-- @segment sn35.105:7.1 -->

“Yaṁ panāniccaṁ dukkhaṁ vipariṇāmadhammaṁ, api nu taṁ anupādāya uppajjeyya ajjhattaṁ sukhaṁ dukkhan”ti?

<!-- @segment sn35.105:8.1 -->

“No hetaṁ, bhante” …pe….

<!-- @segment sn35.105:9.1 -->

“Jivhā niccā vā aniccā vā”ti?

<!-- @segment sn35.105:10.1 -->

“Aniccā, bhante”.

<!-- @segment sn35.105:11.1 -->

“Yaṁ panāniccaṁ dukkhaṁ vā taṁ sukhaṁ vā”ti?

<!-- @segment sn35.105:12.1 -->

“Dukkhaṁ, bhante”.

<!-- @segment sn35.105:13.1 -->

“Yaṁ panāniccaṁ dukkhaṁ vipariṇāmadhammaṁ, api nu taṁ anupādāya uppajjeyya ajjhattaṁ sukhaṁ dukkhan”ti?

<!-- @segment sn35.105:14.1 -->

“No hetaṁ, bhante” …pe….

<!-- @segment sn35.105:15.1 -->

“Mano nicco vā anicco vā”ti?

<!-- @segment sn35.105:16.1 -->

“Anicco, bhante”.

<!-- @segment sn35.105:17.1 -->

“Yaṁ panāniccaṁ dukkhaṁ vā taṁ sukhaṁ vā”ti?

<!-- @segment sn35.105:18.1 -->

“Dukkhaṁ, bhante”.

<!-- @segment sn35.105:19.1 -->

“Yaṁ panāniccaṁ dukkhaṁ vipariṇāmadhammaṁ, api nu taṁ anupādāya uppajjeyya ajjhattaṁ sukhaṁ dukkhan”ti?

<!-- @segment sn35.105:20.1 -->

“No hetaṁ, bhante”.

<!-- @segment sn35.105:21.1 -->

“Evaṁ passaṁ, bhikkhave, sutavā ariyasāvako cakkhusmimpi nibbindati …pe… manasmimpi nibbindati.

<!-- @segment sn35.105:21.2 -->

Nibbindaṁ virajjati; virāgā vimuccati; vimuttasmiṁ vimuttamiti ñāṇaṁ hoti.

<!-- @segment sn35.105:21.3 -->

‘Khīṇā jāti, vusitaṁ brahmacariyaṁ, kataṁ karaṇīyaṁ, nāparaṁ itthattāyā’ti pajānātī”ti.

<!-- @segment sn35.105:21.4 -->

Dutiyaṁ.
