<!-- @segment sn25.4:1.1 -->

Sāvatthinidānaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn25.4:1.2 -->

“Cakkhusamphasso, bhikkhave, anicco vipariṇāmī aññathābhāvī;

<!-- @segment sn25.4:1.3 -->

sotasamphasso …

<!-- @segment sn25.4:1.4 -->

ghānasamphasso …

<!-- @segment sn25.4:1.5 -->

jivhāsamphasso …

<!-- @segment sn25.4:1.6 -->

kāyasamphasso …

<!-- @segment sn25.4:1.7 -->

manosamphasso anicco vipariṇāmī aññathābhāvī.

<!-- @segment sn25.4:1.8 -->

Yo, bhikkhave, ime dhamme evaṁ saddahati adhimuccati, ayaṁ vuccati ‘saddhānusārī …pe…

<!-- @segment sn25.4:1.9 -->

sambodhiparāyano’”ti.

<!-- @segment sn25.4:1.10 -->

Catutthaṁ.
