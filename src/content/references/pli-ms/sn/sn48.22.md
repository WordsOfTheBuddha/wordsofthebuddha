<!-- @segment sn48.22:1.1 -->

“Tīṇimāni, bhikkhave, indriyāni.

<!-- @segment sn48.22:1.2 -->

Katamāni tīṇi?

<!-- @segment sn48.22:1.3 -->

Itthindriyaṁ, purisindriyaṁ, jīvitindriyaṁ—

<!-- @segment sn48.22:1.4 -->

imāni kho, bhikkhave, tīṇi indriyānī”ti.

<!-- @segment sn48.22:1.5 -->

Dutiyaṁ.
