<!-- @segment sn9.14:1.1 -->

Ekaṁ samayaṁ aññataro bhikkhu kosalesu viharati aññatarasmiṁ vanasaṇḍe.

<!-- @segment sn9.14:1.2 -->

Tena kho pana samayena so bhikkhu pacchābhattaṁ piṇḍapātapaṭikkanto pokkharaṇiṁ ogāhetvā padumaṁ upasiṅghati.

<!-- @segment sn9.14:1.3 -->

Atha kho yā tasmiṁ vanasaṇḍe adhivatthā devatā tassa bhikkhuno anukampikā atthakāmā taṁ bhikkhuṁ saṁvejetukāmā yena so bhikkhu tenupasaṅkami; upasaṅkamitvā taṁ bhikkhuṁ gāthāya ajjhabhāsi:

<!-- @segment sn9.14:2.1 -->

“Yametaṁ vārijaṁ pupphaṁ,

<!-- @segment sn9.14:2.2 -->

adinnaṁ upasiṅghasi;

<!-- @segment sn9.14:2.3 -->

Ekaṅgametaṁ theyyānaṁ,

<!-- @segment sn9.14:2.4 -->

gandhatthenosi mārisā”ti.

<!-- @segment sn9.14:3.1 -->

“Na harāmi na bhañjāmi,

<!-- @segment sn9.14:3.2 -->

ārā siṅghāmi vārijaṁ;

<!-- @segment sn9.14:3.3 -->

Atha kena nu vaṇṇena,

<!-- @segment sn9.14:3.4 -->

gandhatthenoti vuccati.

<!-- @segment sn9.14:4.1 -->

Yvāyaṁ bhisāni khanati,

<!-- @segment sn9.14:4.2 -->

puṇḍarīkāni bhañjati;

<!-- @segment sn9.14:4.3 -->

Evaṁ ākiṇṇakammanto,

<!-- @segment sn9.14:4.4 -->

kasmā eso na vuccatī”ti.

<!-- @segment sn9.14:5.1 -->

“Ākiṇṇaluddo puriso,

<!-- @segment sn9.14:5.2 -->

dhāticelaṁva makkhito;

<!-- @segment sn9.14:5.3 -->

Tasmiṁ me vacanaṁ natthi,

<!-- @segment sn9.14:5.4 -->

tvañcārahāmi vattave.

<!-- @segment sn9.14:6.1 -->

Anaṅgaṇassa posassa,

<!-- @segment sn9.14:6.2 -->

niccaṁ sucigavesino;

<!-- @segment sn9.14:6.3 -->

Vālaggamattaṁ pāpassa,

<!-- @segment sn9.14:6.4 -->

abbhāmattaṁva khāyatī”ti.

<!-- @segment sn9.14:7.1 -->

“Addhā maṁ yakkha jānāsi,

<!-- @segment sn9.14:7.2 -->

atho me anukampasi;

<!-- @segment sn9.14:7.3 -->

Punapi yakkha vajjāsi,

<!-- @segment sn9.14:7.4 -->

yadā passasi edisan”ti.

<!-- @segment sn9.14:8.1 -->

“Neva taṁ upajīvāma,

<!-- @segment sn9.14:8.2 -->

napi te bhatakāmhase;

<!-- @segment sn9.14:8.3 -->

Tvameva bhikkhu jāneyya,

<!-- @segment sn9.14:8.4 -->

yena gaccheyya suggatin”ti.

<!-- @segment sn9.14:9.1 -->

Atha kho so bhikkhu tāya devatāya saṁvejito saṁvegamāpādīti.

<!-- @segment sn9.14:9.2 -->

Vanavaggo paṭhamo.

<!-- @segment sn9.14:10.0 -->

Tassuddānaṁ

<!-- @segment sn9.14:10.1 -->

Vivekaṁ upaṭṭhānañca,

<!-- @segment sn9.14:10.2 -->

Kassapagottena sambahulā;

<!-- @segment sn9.14:10.3 -->

Ānando anuruddho ca,

<!-- @segment sn9.14:10.4 -->

Nāgadattañca kulagharaṇī.

<!-- @segment sn9.14:11.1 -->

Vajjiputto ca vesālī,

<!-- @segment sn9.14:11.2 -->

Sajjhāyena ayoniso;

<!-- @segment sn9.14:11.3 -->

Majjhanhikālamhi pākatindriya—

<!-- @segment sn9.14:11.4 -->

Padumapupphena cuddasa bhaveti.

<!-- @segment sn9.14:11.5 -->

Vanasaṁyuttaṁ samattaṁ.
