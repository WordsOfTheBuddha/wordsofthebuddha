<!-- @segment sn2.8:1.1 -->

Sāvatthinidānaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn2.8:1.2 -->

Atha kho tāyano devaputto purāṇatitthakaro abhikkantāya rattiyā abhikkantavaṇṇo kevalakappaṁ jetavanaṁ obhāsetvā yena bhagavā tenupasaṅkami; upasaṅkamitvā bhagavantaṁ abhivādetvā ekamantaṁ aṭṭhāsi.

<!-- @segment sn2.8:1.3 -->

Ekamantaṁ ṭhito kho tāyano devaputto bhagavato santike imā gāthāyo abhāsi:

<!-- @segment sn2.8:2.1 -->

“Chinda sotaṁ parakkamma,

<!-- @segment sn2.8:2.2 -->

kāme panuda brāhmaṇa;

<!-- @segment sn2.8:2.3 -->

Nappahāya munī kāme,

<!-- @segment sn2.8:2.4 -->

nekattamupapajjati.

<!-- @segment sn2.8:3.1 -->

Kayirā ce kayirāthenaṁ,

<!-- @segment sn2.8:3.2 -->

daḷhamenaṁ parakkame;

<!-- @segment sn2.8:3.3 -->

Sithilo hi paribbājo,

<!-- @segment sn2.8:3.4 -->

bhiyyo ākirate rajaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn2.8:4.1 -->

Akataṁ dukkaṭaṁ seyyo,

<!-- @segment sn2.8:4.2 -->

pacchā tapati dukkaṭaṁ;

<!-- @segment sn2.8:4.3 -->

Katañca sukataṁ seyyo,

<!-- @segment sn2.8:4.4 -->

yaṁ katvā nānutappati.

<!-- @segment sn2.8:5.1 -->

Kuso yathā duggahito,

<!-- @segment sn2.8:5.2 -->

hatthamevānukantati;

<!-- @segment sn2.8:5.3 -->

Sāmaññaṁ dupparāmaṭṭhaṁ,

<!-- @segment sn2.8:5.4 -->

nirayāyūpakaḍḍhati.

<!-- @segment sn2.8:6.1 -->

Yaṁ kiñci sithilaṁ kammaṁ,

<!-- @segment sn2.8:6.2 -->

saṅkiliṭṭhañca yaṁ vataṁ;

<!-- @segment sn2.8:6.3 -->

Saṅkassaraṁ brahmacariyaṁ,

<!-- @segment sn2.8:6.4 -->

na taṁ hoti mahapphalan”ti.

<!-- @segment sn2.8:7.1 -->

Idamavoca tāyano devaputto;

<!-- @segment sn2.8:7.2 -->

idaṁ vatvā bhagavantaṁ abhivādetvā padakkhiṇaṁ katvā tatthevantaradhāyīti.

<!-- @segment sn2.8:8.1 -->

Atha kho bhagavā tassā rattiyā accayena bhikkhū āmantesi:

<!-- @segment sn2.8:8.2 -->

“imaṁ, bhikkhave, rattiṁ tāyano nāma devaputto purāṇatitthakaro abhikkantāya rattiyā abhikkantavaṇṇo kevalakappaṁ jetavanaṁ obhāsetvā yenāhaṁ tenupasaṅkami; upasaṅkamitvā maṁ abhivādetvā ekamantaṁ aṭṭhāsi.

<!-- @segment sn2.8:8.3 -->

Ekamantaṁ ṭhito kho, bhikkhave, tāyano devaputto mama santike imā gāthāyo abhāsi:

<!-- @segment sn2.8:9.1 -->

‘Chinda sotaṁ parakkamma,

<!-- @segment sn2.8:9.2 -->

kāme panuda brāhmaṇa;

<!-- @segment sn2.8:9.3 -->

Nappahāya munī kāme,

<!-- @segment sn2.8:9.4 -->

nekattamupapajjati.

<!-- @segment sn2.8:10.1 -->

Kayirā ce kayirāthenaṁ,

<!-- @segment sn2.8:10.2 -->

daḷhamenaṁ parakkame;

<!-- @segment sn2.8:10.3 -->

Sithilo hi paribbājo,

<!-- @segment sn2.8:10.4 -->

bhiyyo ākirate rajaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn2.8:11.1 -->

Akataṁ dukkaṭaṁ seyyo,

<!-- @segment sn2.8:11.2 -->

pacchā tapati dukkaṭaṁ;

<!-- @segment sn2.8:11.3 -->

Katañca sukataṁ seyyo,

<!-- @segment sn2.8:11.4 -->

yaṁ katvā nānutappati.

<!-- @segment sn2.8:12.1 -->

Kuso yathā duggahito,

<!-- @segment sn2.8:12.2 -->

hatthamevānukantati;

<!-- @segment sn2.8:12.3 -->

Sāmaññaṁ dupparāmaṭṭhaṁ,

<!-- @segment sn2.8:12.4 -->

nirayāyūpakaḍḍhati.

<!-- @segment sn2.8:13.1 -->

Yaṁ kiñci sithilaṁ kammaṁ,

<!-- @segment sn2.8:13.2 -->

saṅkiliṭṭhañca yaṁ vataṁ;

<!-- @segment sn2.8:13.3 -->

Saṅkassaraṁ brahmacariyaṁ,

<!-- @segment sn2.8:13.4 -->

na taṁ hoti mahapphalan’ti.

<!-- @segment sn2.8:14.1 -->

Idamavoca, bhikkhave, tāyano devaputto, idaṁ vatvā maṁ abhivādetvā padakkhiṇaṁ katvā tatthevantaradhāyi.

<!-- @segment sn2.8:14.2 -->

Uggaṇhātha, bhikkhave, tāyanagāthā;

<!-- @segment sn2.8:14.3 -->

pariyāpuṇātha, bhikkhave, tāyanagāthā;

<!-- @segment sn2.8:14.4 -->

dhāretha, bhikkhave, tāyanagāthā.

<!-- @segment sn2.8:14.5 -->

Atthasaṁhitā, bhikkhave, tāyanagāthā ādibrahmacariyikā”ti.
