<!-- @segment sn5.7:1.1 -->

Sāvatthinidānaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn5.7:1.2 -->

Atha kho upacālā bhikkhunī pubbaṇhasamayaṁ nivāsetvā …pe…

<!-- @segment sn5.7:1.3 -->

aññatarasmiṁ rukkhamūle divāvihāraṁ nisīdi.

<!-- @segment sn5.7:1.4 -->

Atha kho māro pāpimā yena upacālā bhikkhunī tenupasaṅkami; upasaṅkamitvā upacālaṁ bhikkhuniṁ etadavoca:

<!-- @segment sn5.7:1.5 -->

“kattha nu tvaṁ, bhikkhuni, uppajjitukāmā”ti?

<!-- @segment sn5.7:1.6 -->

“Na khvāhaṁ, āvuso, katthaci uppajjitukāmā”ti.

<!-- @segment sn5.7:2.1 -->

“Tāvatiṁsā ca yāmā ca,

<!-- @segment sn5.7:2.2 -->

tusitā cāpi devatā;

<!-- @segment sn5.7:2.3 -->

Nimmānaratino devā,

<!-- @segment sn5.7:2.4 -->

ye devā vasavattino;

<!-- @segment sn5.7:2.5 -->

Tattha cittaṁ paṇidhehi,

<!-- @segment sn5.7:2.6 -->

ratiṁ paccanubhossasī”ti.

<!-- @segment sn5.7:3.1 -->

“Tāvatiṁsā ca yāmā ca,

<!-- @segment sn5.7:3.2 -->

tusitā cāpi devatā;

<!-- @segment sn5.7:3.3 -->

Nimmānaratino devā,

<!-- @segment sn5.7:3.4 -->

ye devā vasavattino;

<!-- @segment sn5.7:3.5 -->

Kāmabandhanabaddhā te,

<!-- @segment sn5.7:3.6 -->

enti māravasaṁ puna.

<!-- @segment sn5.7:4.1 -->

Sabbo ādīpito loko,

<!-- @segment sn5.7:4.2 -->

sabbo loko padhūpito;

<!-- @segment sn5.7:4.3 -->

Sabbo pajjālito loko,

<!-- @segment sn5.7:4.4 -->

sabbo loko pakampito.

<!-- @segment sn5.7:5.1 -->

Akampitaṁ apajjalitaṁ,

<!-- @segment sn5.7:5.2 -->

aputhujjanasevitaṁ;

<!-- @segment sn5.7:5.3 -->

Agati yattha mārassa,

<!-- @segment sn5.7:5.4 -->

tattha me nirato mano”ti.

<!-- @segment sn5.7:6.1 -->

Atha kho māro pāpimā “jānāti maṁ upacālā bhikkhunī”ti dukkhī dummano tatthevantaradhāyīti.
