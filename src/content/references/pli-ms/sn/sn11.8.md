<!-- @segment sn11.8:1.1 -->

Sāvatthiyaṁ jetavane.

<!-- @segment sn11.8:1.2 -->

Tena kho pana samayena bhagavā divāvihāragato hoti paṭisallīno.

<!-- @segment sn11.8:1.3 -->

Atha kho sakko ca devānamindo verocano ca asurindo yena bhagavā tenupasaṅkamiṁsu; upasaṅkamitvā paccekaṁ dvārabāhaṁ nissāya aṭṭhaṁsu.

<!-- @segment sn11.8:1.4 -->

Atha kho verocano asurindo bhagavato santike imaṁ gāthaṁ abhāsi:

<!-- @segment sn11.8:2.1 -->

“Vāyametheva puriso,

<!-- @segment sn11.8:2.2 -->

yāva atthassa nipphadā;

<!-- @segment sn11.8:2.3 -->

Nipphannasobhano attho,

<!-- @segment sn11.8:2.4 -->

verocanavaco idan”ti.

<!-- @segment sn11.8:3.1 -->

“Vāyametheva puriso,

<!-- @segment sn11.8:3.2 -->

yāva atthassa nipphadā;

<!-- @segment sn11.8:3.3 -->

Nipphannasobhano attho,

<!-- @segment sn11.8:3.4 -->

khantyā bhiyyo na vijjatī”ti.

<!-- @segment sn11.8:4.1 -->

“Sabbe sattā atthajātā,

<!-- @segment sn11.8:4.2 -->

tattha tattha yathārahaṁ;

<!-- @segment sn11.8:4.3 -->

Saṁyogaparamā tveva,

<!-- @segment sn11.8:4.4 -->

sambhogā sabbapāṇinaṁ;

<!-- @segment sn11.8:4.5 -->

Nipphannasobhano attho,

<!-- @segment sn11.8:4.6 -->

verocanavaco idan”ti.

<!-- @segment sn11.8:5.1 -->

“Sabbe sattā atthajātā,

<!-- @segment sn11.8:5.2 -->

tattha tattha yathārahaṁ;

<!-- @segment sn11.8:5.3 -->

Saṁyogaparamā tveva,

<!-- @segment sn11.8:5.4 -->

sambhogā sabbapāṇinaṁ;

<!-- @segment sn11.8:5.5 -->

Nipphannasobhano attho,

<!-- @segment sn11.8:5.6 -->

khantyā bhiyyo na vijjatī”ti.
