<!-- @segment sn10.12:1.1 -->

Evaṁ me sutaṁ—

<!-- @segment sn10.12:1.2 -->

ekaṁ samayaṁ bhagavā āḷaviyaṁ viharati āḷavakassa yakkhassa bhavane.

<!-- @segment sn10.12:1.3 -->

Atha kho āḷavako yakkho yena bhagavā tenupasaṅkami; upasaṅkamitvā bhagavantaṁ etadavoca:

<!-- @segment sn10.12:1.4 -->

“nikkhama, samaṇā”ti.

<!-- @segment sn10.12:1.5 -->

“Sādhāvuso”ti bhagavā nikkhami.

<!-- @segment sn10.12:1.6 -->

“Pavisa, samaṇā”ti.

<!-- @segment sn10.12:1.7 -->

“Sādhāvuso”ti bhagavā pāvisi.

<!-- @segment sn10.12:1.8 -->

Dutiyampi kho āḷavako yakkho bhagavantaṁ etadavoca:

<!-- @segment sn10.12:1.9 -->

“nikkhama, samaṇā”ti.

<!-- @segment sn10.12:1.10 -->

“Sādhāvuso”ti bhagavā nikkhami.

<!-- @segment sn10.12:1.11 -->

“Pavisa, samaṇā”ti.

<!-- @segment sn10.12:1.12 -->

“Sādhāvuso”ti bhagavā pāvisi.

<!-- @segment sn10.12:1.13 -->

Tatiyampi kho āḷavako yakkho bhagavantaṁ etadavoca:

<!-- @segment sn10.12:1.14 -->

“nikkhama, samaṇā”ti.

<!-- @segment sn10.12:1.15 -->

“Sādhāvuso”ti bhagavā nikkhami.

<!-- @segment sn10.12:1.16 -->

“Pavisa, samaṇā”ti.

<!-- @segment sn10.12:1.17 -->

“Sādhāvuso”ti bhagavā pāvisi.

<!-- @segment sn10.12:1.18 -->

Catutthampi kho āḷavako yakkho bhagavantaṁ etadavoca:

<!-- @segment sn10.12:1.19 -->

“nikkhama, samaṇā”ti.

<!-- @segment sn10.12:1.20 -->

“Na khvāhaṁ taṁ, āvuso, nikkhamissāmi.

<!-- @segment sn10.12:1.21 -->

Yaṁ te karaṇīyaṁ taṁ karohī”ti.

<!-- @segment sn10.12:1.22 -->

“Pañhaṁ taṁ, samaṇa, pucchissāmi.

<!-- @segment sn10.12:1.23 -->

Sace me na byākarissasi, cittaṁ vā te khipissāmi, hadayaṁ vā te phālessāmi, pādesu vā gahetvā pāragaṅgāya khipissāmī”ti.

<!-- @segment sn10.12:1.24 -->

“Na khvāhaṁ taṁ, āvuso, passāmi sadevake loke samārake sabrahmake sassamaṇabrāhmaṇiyā pajāya sadevamanussāya, yo me cittaṁ vā khipeyya hadayaṁ vā phāleyya, pādesu vā gahetvā pāragaṅgāya khipeyya.

<!-- @segment sn10.12:1.25 -->

Api ca tvaṁ, āvuso, puccha yadā kaṅkhasī”ti (…).

<!-- @segment sn10.12:2.1 -->

“Kiṁsūdha vittaṁ purisassa seṭṭhaṁ,

<!-- @segment sn10.12:2.2 -->

Kiṁsu suciṇṇaṁ sukhamāvahāti;

<!-- @segment sn10.12:2.3 -->

Kiṁsu have sādutaraṁ rasānaṁ,

<!-- @segment sn10.12:2.4 -->

Kathaṁjīviṁ jīvitamāhu seṭṭhan”ti.

<!-- @segment sn10.12:3.1 -->

“Saddhīdha vittaṁ purisassa seṭṭhaṁ,

<!-- @segment sn10.12:3.2 -->

Dhammo suciṇṇo sukhamāvahāti;

<!-- @segment sn10.12:3.3 -->

Saccaṁ have sādutaraṁ rasānaṁ,

<!-- @segment sn10.12:3.4 -->

Paññājīviṁ jīvitamāhu seṭṭhan”ti.

<!-- @segment sn10.12:4.1 -->

“Kathaṁsu tarati oghaṁ,

<!-- @segment sn10.12:4.2 -->

kathaṁsu tarati aṇṇavaṁ;

<!-- @segment sn10.12:4.3 -->

Kathaṁsu dukkhamacceti,

<!-- @segment sn10.12:4.4 -->

kathaṁsu parisujjhatī”ti.

<!-- @segment sn10.12:5.1 -->

“Saddhāya tarati oghaṁ,

<!-- @segment sn10.12:5.2 -->

appamādena aṇṇavaṁ;

<!-- @segment sn10.12:5.3 -->

Vīriyena dukkhamacceti,

<!-- @segment sn10.12:5.4 -->

paññāya parisujjhatī”ti.

<!-- @segment sn10.12:6.1 -->

“Kathaṁsu labhate paññaṁ,

<!-- @segment sn10.12:6.2 -->

kathaṁsu vindate dhanaṁ;

<!-- @segment sn10.12:6.3 -->

Kathaṁsu kittiṁ pappoti,

<!-- @segment sn10.12:6.4 -->

kathaṁ mittāni ganthati;

<!-- @segment sn10.12:6.5 -->

Asmā lokā paraṁ lokaṁ,

<!-- @segment sn10.12:6.6 -->

kathaṁ pecca na socatī”ti.

<!-- @segment sn10.12:7.1 -->

“Saddahāno arahataṁ,

<!-- @segment sn10.12:7.2 -->

dhammaṁ nibbānapattiyā;

<!-- @segment sn10.12:7.3 -->

Sussūsaṁ labhate paññaṁ,

<!-- @segment sn10.12:7.4 -->

appamatto vicakkhaṇo.

<!-- @segment sn10.12:8.1 -->

Patirūpakārī dhuravā,

<!-- @segment sn10.12:8.2 -->

uṭṭhātā vindate dhanaṁ;

<!-- @segment sn10.12:8.3 -->

Saccena kittiṁ pappoti,

<!-- @segment sn10.12:8.4 -->

dadaṁ mittāni ganthati;

<!-- @segment sn10.12:8.5 -->

Asmā lokā paraṁ lokaṁ,

<!-- @segment sn10.12:8.6 -->

evaṁ pecca na socati.

<!-- @segment sn10.12:9.1 -->

Yassete caturo dhammā,

<!-- @segment sn10.12:9.2 -->

saddhassa gharamesino;

<!-- @segment sn10.12:9.3 -->

Saccaṁ dhammo dhiti cāgo,

<!-- @segment sn10.12:9.4 -->

sa ve pecca na socati.

<!-- @segment sn10.12:10.1 -->

Iṅgha aññepi pucchassu,

<!-- @segment sn10.12:10.2 -->

puthū samaṇabrāhmaṇe;

<!-- @segment sn10.12:10.3 -->

Yadi saccā dhammā cāgā,

<!-- @segment sn10.12:10.4 -->

khantyā bhiyyodha vijjatī”ti.

<!-- @segment sn10.12:11.1 -->

“Kathaṁ nu dāni puccheyyaṁ,

<!-- @segment sn10.12:11.2 -->

puthū samaṇabrāhmaṇe;

<!-- @segment sn10.12:11.3 -->

Yohaṁ ajja pajānāmi,

<!-- @segment sn10.12:11.4 -->

yo attho samparāyiko.

<!-- @segment sn10.12:12.1 -->

Atthāya vata me buddho,

<!-- @segment sn10.12:12.2 -->

vāsāyāḷavimāgamā;

<!-- @segment sn10.12:12.3 -->

Yohaṁ ajja pajānāmi,

<!-- @segment sn10.12:12.4 -->

yattha dinnaṁ mahapphalaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn10.12:13.1 -->

So ahaṁ vicarissāmi,

<!-- @segment sn10.12:13.2 -->

Gāmā gāmaṁ purā puraṁ;

<!-- @segment sn10.12:13.3 -->

Namassamāno sambuddhaṁ,

<!-- @segment sn10.12:13.4 -->

Dhammassa ca sudhammatan”ti.

<!-- @segment sn10.12:13.5 -->

Indakavaggo paṭhamo.

<!-- @segment sn10.12:14.0 -->

Tassuddānaṁ

<!-- @segment sn10.12:14.1 -->

Indako sakka sūci ca,

<!-- @segment sn10.12:14.2 -->

Maṇibhaddo ca sānu ca;

<!-- @segment sn10.12:14.3 -->

Piyaṅkara punabbasu sudatto ca,

<!-- @segment sn10.12:14.4 -->

Dve sukkā cīraāḷavīti dvādasa.

<!-- @segment sn10.12:14.5 -->

Yakkhasaṁyuttaṁ samattaṁ.
