<!-- @segment sn35.107:1.1 -->

“Lokassa, bhikkhave, samudayañca atthaṅgamañca desessāmi.

<!-- @segment sn35.107:1.2 -->

Taṁ suṇātha.

<!-- @segment sn35.107:1.3 -->

Katamo ca, bhikkhave, lokassa samudayo?

<!-- @segment sn35.107:1.4 -->

Cakkhuñca paṭicca rūpe ca uppajjati cakkhuviññāṇaṁ. Tiṇṇaṁ saṅgati phasso.

<!-- @segment sn35.107:1.5 -->

Phassapaccayā vedanā;

<!-- @segment sn35.107:1.6 -->

vedanāpaccayā taṇhā;

<!-- @segment sn35.107:1.7 -->

taṇhāpaccayā upādānaṁ;

<!-- @segment sn35.107:1.8 -->

upādānapaccayā bhavo;

<!-- @segment sn35.107:1.9 -->

bhavapaccayā jāti;

<!-- @segment sn35.107:1.10 -->

jātipaccayā jarāmaraṇaṁ sokaparidevadukkhadomanassupāyāsā sambhavanti.

<!-- @segment sn35.107:1.11 -->

Ayaṁ kho, bhikkhave, lokassa samudayo …pe…

<!-- @segment sn35.107:1.12 -->

jivhañca paṭicca rase ca uppajjati jivhāviññāṇaṁ …pe…

<!-- @segment sn35.107:1.13 -->

manañca paṭicca dhamme ca uppajjati manoviññāṇaṁ. Tiṇṇaṁ saṅgati phasso.

<!-- @segment sn35.107:1.14 -->

Phassapaccayā vedanā;

<!-- @segment sn35.107:1.15 -->

vedanāpaccayā taṇhā;

<!-- @segment sn35.107:1.16 -->

taṇhāpaccayā upādānaṁ;

<!-- @segment sn35.107:1.17 -->

upādānapaccayā bhavo;

<!-- @segment sn35.107:1.18 -->

bhavapaccayā jāti;

<!-- @segment sn35.107:1.19 -->

jātipaccayā jarāmaraṇaṁ sokaparidevadukkhadomanassupāyāsā sambhavanti.

<!-- @segment sn35.107:1.20 -->

Ayaṁ kho, bhikkhave, lokassa samudayo.

<!-- @segment sn35.107:2.1 -->

Katamo ca, bhikkhave, lokassa atthaṅgamo?

<!-- @segment sn35.107:2.2 -->

Cakkhuñca paṭicca rūpe ca uppajjati cakkhuviññāṇaṁ. Tiṇṇaṁ saṅgati phasso.

<!-- @segment sn35.107:2.3 -->

Phassapaccayā vedanā;

<!-- @segment sn35.107:2.4 -->

vedanāpaccayā taṇhā.

<!-- @segment sn35.107:2.5 -->

Tassāyeva taṇhāya asesavirāganirodhā upādānanirodho;

<!-- @segment sn35.107:2.6 -->

upādānanirodhā bhavanirodho;

<!-- @segment sn35.107:2.7 -->

bhavanirodhā jātinirodho;

<!-- @segment sn35.107:2.8 -->

jātinirodhā jarāmaraṇaṁ sokaparidevadukkhadomanassupāyāsā nirujjhanti.

<!-- @segment sn35.107:2.9 -->

Evametassa kevalassa dukkhakkhandhassa nirodho hoti.

<!-- @segment sn35.107:2.10 -->

Ayaṁ kho, bhikkhave, lokassa atthaṅgamo …pe…

<!-- @segment sn35.107:2.11 -->

jivhañca paṭicca rase ca uppajjati …pe…

<!-- @segment sn35.107:2.12 -->

manañca paṭicca dhamme ca uppajjati manoviññāṇaṁ. Tiṇṇaṁ saṅgati phasso.

<!-- @segment sn35.107:2.13 -->

Phassapaccayā vedanā;

<!-- @segment sn35.107:2.14 -->

vedanāpaccayā taṇhā.

<!-- @segment sn35.107:2.15 -->

Tassāyeva taṇhāya asesavirāganirodhā upādānanirodho;

<!-- @segment sn35.107:2.16 -->

upādānanirodhā …pe…

<!-- @segment sn35.107:2.17 -->

evametassa kevalassa dukkhakkhandhassa nirodho hoti.

<!-- @segment sn35.107:2.18 -->

Ayaṁ kho, bhikkhave, lokassa atthaṅgamo”ti.

<!-- @segment sn35.107:2.19 -->

Catutthaṁ.
