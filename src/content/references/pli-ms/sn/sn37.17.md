<!-- @segment sn37.17:1.1 -->

“Pañcahi, anuruddha, dhammehi samannāgato mātugāmo kāyassa bhedā paraṁ maraṇā sugatiṁ saggaṁ lokaṁ upapajjati.

<!-- @segment sn37.17:1.2 -->

Katamehi pañcahi?

<!-- @segment sn37.17:1.3 -->

Saddho ca hoti, hirimā ca hoti, ottappī ca hoti, anissukī ca hoti, paññavā ca hoti …pe….

<!-- @segment sn37.17:1.4 -->

Tatiyaṁ.
