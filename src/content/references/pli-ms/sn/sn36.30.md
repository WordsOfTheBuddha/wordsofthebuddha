<!-- @segment sn36.30:1.1 -->

“Tisso imā, bhikkhave, vedanā.

<!-- @segment sn36.30:1.2 -->

Katamā tisso?

<!-- @segment sn36.30:1.3 -->

Sukhā vedanā, dukkhā vedanā, adukkhamasukhā vedanā—

<!-- @segment sn36.30:1.4 -->

imā kho, bhikkhave, tisso vedanā”ti.

<!-- @segment sn36.30:1.5 -->

Dasamaṁ.
