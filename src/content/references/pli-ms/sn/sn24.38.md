<!-- @segment sn24.38:1.1 -->

Sāvatthinidānaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn24.38:1.2 -->

“Kismiṁ nu kho, bhikkhave, sati, kiṁ upādāya, kiṁ abhinivissa evaṁ diṭṭhi uppajjati:

<!-- @segment sn24.38:1.3 -->

‘arūpī attā hoti arogo paraṁ maraṇā’”ti?

<!-- @segment sn24.38:1.4 -->

(Peyyālo.)

<!-- @segment sn24.38:1.5 -->

Vīsatimaṁ.
