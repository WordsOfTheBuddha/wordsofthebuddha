<!-- @segment sn18.9:1.1 -->

Sāvatthiyaṁ viharati.

<!-- @segment sn18.9:1.2 -->

“Taṁ kiṁ maññasi, rāhula,

<!-- @segment sn18.9:1.3 -->

pathavīdhātu niccā vā aniccā vā”ti?

<!-- @segment sn18.9:1.4 -->

“Aniccā, bhante” …

<!-- @segment sn18.9:1.5 -->

“āpodhātu …pe…

<!-- @segment sn18.9:1.6 -->

tejodhātu …

<!-- @segment sn18.9:1.7 -->

vāyodhātu …

<!-- @segment sn18.9:1.8 -->

ākāsadhātu …

<!-- @segment sn18.9:1.9 -->

viññāṇadhātu niccā vā aniccā vā”ti?

<!-- @segment sn18.9:1.10 -->

“Aniccā, bhante” …

<!-- @segment sn18.9:1.11 -->

“evaṁ passaṁ, rāhula, sutavā ariyasāvako pathavīdhātuyāpi nibbindati …pe… āpodhātuyāpi nibbindati … tejodhātuyāpi nibbindati … vāyodhātuyāpi nibbindati … ākāsadhātuyāpi nibbindati … viññāṇadhātuyāpi nibbindati …pe…

<!-- @segment sn18.9:1.12 -->

pajānātī”ti.

<!-- @segment sn18.9:1.13 -->

Navamaṁ.
