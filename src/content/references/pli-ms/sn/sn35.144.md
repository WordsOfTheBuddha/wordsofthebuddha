<!-- @segment sn35.144:1.1 -->

“Rūpā, bhikkhave, dukkhā.

<!-- @segment sn35.144:1.2 -->

Yopi hetu, yopi paccayo rūpānaṁ uppādāya, sopi dukkho.

<!-- @segment sn35.144:1.3 -->

Dukkhasambhūtā, bhikkhave, rūpā kuto sukhā bhavissanti.

<!-- @segment sn35.144:1.4 -->

Saddā …

<!-- @segment sn35.144:1.5 -->

gandhā …

<!-- @segment sn35.144:1.6 -->

rasā …

<!-- @segment sn35.144:1.7 -->

phoṭṭhabbā …

<!-- @segment sn35.144:1.8 -->

dhammā dukkhā.

<!-- @segment sn35.144:1.9 -->

Yopi hetu, yopi paccayo dhammānaṁ uppādāya, sopi dukkho.

<!-- @segment sn35.144:1.10 -->

Dukkhasambhūtā, bhikkhave, dhammā kuto sukhā bhavissanti.

<!-- @segment sn35.144:1.11 -->

Evaṁ passaṁ …pe…

<!-- @segment sn35.144:1.12 -->

nāparaṁ itthattāyā’ti pajānātī”ti.

<!-- @segment sn35.144:1.13 -->

Ekādasamaṁ.
