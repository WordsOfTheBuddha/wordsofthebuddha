<!-- @segment sn15.14:1.1 -->

Sāvatthiyaṁ viharati.

<!-- @segment sn15.14:1.2 -->

“Anamataggoyaṁ, bhikkhave, saṁsāro …pe…

<!-- @segment sn15.14:1.3 -->

na so, bhikkhave, satto sulabharūpo yo namātābhūtapubbo iminā dīghena addhunā.

<!-- @segment sn15.14:1.4 -->

Taṁ kissa hetu?

<!-- @segment sn15.14:1.5 -->

Anamataggoyaṁ, bhikkhave, saṁsāro …pe…

<!-- @segment sn15.14:1.6 -->

alaṁ vimuccitun”ti.

<!-- @segment sn15.14:1.7 -->

Catutthaṁ.
