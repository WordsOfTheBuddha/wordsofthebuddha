<!-- @segment sn11.20:1.1 -->

Sāvatthiyaṁ jetavane.

<!-- @segment sn11.20:1.2 -->

Tatra kho …pe… etadavoca:

<!-- @segment sn11.20:1.3 -->

“bhūtapubbaṁ, bhikkhave, sakko devānamindo mātaliṁ saṅgāhakaṁ āmantesi:

<!-- @segment sn11.20:1.4 -->

‘yojehi, samma mātali, sahassayuttaṁ ājaññarathaṁ, uyyānabhūmiṁ gacchāma subhūmiṁ dassanāyā’ti.

<!-- @segment sn11.20:1.5 -->

‘Evaṁ, bhaddantavā’ti kho, bhikkhave, mātali saṅgāhako sakkassa devānamindassa paṭissutvā, sahassayuttaṁ ājaññarathaṁ yojetvā sakkassa devānamindassa paṭivedesi:

<!-- @segment sn11.20:1.6 -->

‘yutto kho te, mārisa, sahassayutto ājaññaratho,

<!-- @segment sn11.20:1.7 -->

yassadāni kālaṁ maññasī’ti.

<!-- @segment sn11.20:1.8 -->

Atha kho, bhikkhave, sakko devānamindo vejayantapāsādā orohanto añjaliṁ katvā sudaṁ bhikkhusaṅghaṁ namassati.

<!-- @segment sn11.20:1.9 -->

Atha kho, bhikkhave, mātali saṅgāhako sakkaṁ devānamindaṁ gāthāya ajjhabhāsi:

<!-- @segment sn11.20:2.1 -->

‘Tañhi ete namasseyyuṁ,

<!-- @segment sn11.20:2.2 -->

pūtidehasayā narā;

<!-- @segment sn11.20:2.3 -->

Nimuggā kuṇapamhete,

<!-- @segment sn11.20:2.4 -->

khuppipāsasamappitā.

<!-- @segment sn11.20:3.1 -->

Kiṁ nu tesaṁ pihayasi,

<!-- @segment sn11.20:3.2 -->

anāgārāna vāsava;

<!-- @segment sn11.20:3.3 -->

Ācāraṁ isinaṁ brūhi,

<!-- @segment sn11.20:3.4 -->

taṁ suṇoma vaco tavā’ti.

<!-- @segment sn11.20:4.1 -->

‘Etaṁ tesaṁ pihayāmi,

<!-- @segment sn11.20:4.2 -->

anāgārāna mātali;

<!-- @segment sn11.20:4.3 -->

Yamhā gāmā pakkamanti,

<!-- @segment sn11.20:4.4 -->

anapekkhā vajanti te.

<!-- @segment sn11.20:5.1 -->

Na tesaṁ koṭṭhe openti,

<!-- @segment sn11.20:5.2 -->

na kumbhi na kaḷopiyaṁ;

<!-- @segment sn11.20:5.3 -->

Paraniṭṭhitamesānā,

<!-- @segment sn11.20:5.4 -->

tena yāpenti subbatā.

<!-- @segment sn11.20:6.1 -->

Sumantamantino dhīrā,

<!-- @segment sn11.20:6.2 -->

tuṇhībhūtā samañcarā;

<!-- @segment sn11.20:6.3 -->

Devā viruddhā asurehi,

<!-- @segment sn11.20:6.4 -->

puthu maccā ca mātali.

<!-- @segment sn11.20:7.1 -->

Aviruddhā viruddhesu,

<!-- @segment sn11.20:7.2 -->

attadaṇḍesu nibbutā;

<!-- @segment sn11.20:7.3 -->

Sādānesu anādānā,

<!-- @segment sn11.20:7.4 -->

te namassāmi mātalī’ti.

<!-- @segment sn11.20:8.1 -->

‘Seṭṭhā hi kira lokasmiṁ,

<!-- @segment sn11.20:8.2 -->

ye tvaṁ sakka namassasi;

<!-- @segment sn11.20:8.3 -->

Ahampi te namassāmi,

<!-- @segment sn11.20:8.4 -->

ye namassasi vāsavā’”ti.

<!-- @segment sn11.20:9.1 -->

Idaṁ vatvāna maghavā,

<!-- @segment sn11.20:9.2 -->

devarājā sujampati;

<!-- @segment sn11.20:9.3 -->

Bhikkhusaṅghaṁ namassitvā,

<!-- @segment sn11.20:9.4 -->

pamukho rathamāruhīti.

<!-- @segment sn11.20:9.5 -->

Dutiyo vaggo.

<!-- @segment sn11.20:10.0 -->

Tassuddānaṁ

<!-- @segment sn11.20:10.1 -->

Devā pana tayo vuttā,

<!-- @segment sn11.20:10.2 -->

daliddañca rāmaṇeyyakaṁ;

<!-- @segment sn11.20:10.3 -->

Yajamānañca vandanā,

<!-- @segment sn11.20:10.4 -->

tayo sakkanamassanāti.
