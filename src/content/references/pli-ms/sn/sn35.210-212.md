<!-- @segment sn35.210-212:1.1 -->

“Cakkhu, bhikkhave, anattā atītaṁ anāgataṁ paccuppannaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn35.210-212:1.2 -->

Yadanattā, taṁ ‘netaṁ mama, nesohamasmi, na meso attā’ti evametaṁ yathābhūtaṁ sammappaññāya daṭṭhabbaṁ …pe…

<!-- @segment sn35.210-212:1.3 -->

jivhā anattā …pe…

<!-- @segment sn35.210-212:1.4 -->

mano anattā atīto anāgato paccuppanno.

<!-- @segment sn35.210-212:1.5 -->

Yadanattā taṁ ‘netaṁ mama, nesohamasmi, na meso attā’ti evametaṁ yathābhūtaṁ sammappaññāya daṭṭhabbaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn35.210-212:1.6 -->

Evaṁ passaṁ …pe…

<!-- @segment sn35.210-212:1.7 -->

nāparaṁ itthattāyāti pajānātī”ti.
