<!-- @segment sn47.73-84:1.1 -->

“Seyyathāpi, bhikkhave, ye keci balakaraṇīyā kammantā karīyantī”ti

<!-- @segment sn47.73-84:1.2 -->

vitthāretabbaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn47.73-84:1.3 -->

Balakaraṇīyavaggo aṭṭhamo.

<!-- @segment sn47.73-84:2.0 -->

Tassuddānaṁ

<!-- @segment sn47.73-84:2.1 -->

Balaṁ bījañca nāgo ca,

<!-- @segment sn47.73-84:2.2 -->

rukkho kumbhena sūkiyā;

<!-- @segment sn47.73-84:2.3 -->

Ākāsena ca dve meghā,

<!-- @segment sn47.73-84:2.4 -->

nāvā āgantukā nadīti.
