<!-- @segment sn22.45:1.1 -->

Sāvatthinidānaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn22.45:1.2 -->

“Rūpaṁ, bhikkhave, aniccaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn22.45:1.3 -->

Yadaniccaṁ taṁ dukkhaṁ;

<!-- @segment sn22.45:1.4 -->

yaṁ dukkhaṁ tadanattā;

<!-- @segment sn22.45:1.5 -->

yadanattā taṁ ‘netaṁ mama, nesohamasmi, na meso attā’ti evametaṁ yathābhūtaṁ sammappaññāya daṭṭhabbaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn22.45:1.6 -->

Evametaṁ yathābhūtaṁ sammappaññāya passato cittaṁ virajjati vimuccati anupādāya āsavehi.

<!-- @segment sn22.45:1.7 -->

Vedanā aniccā …

<!-- @segment sn22.45:1.8 -->

saññā …

<!-- @segment sn22.45:1.9 -->

saṅkhārā …

<!-- @segment sn22.45:1.10 -->

viññāṇaṁ aniccaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn22.45:1.11 -->

Yadaniccaṁ taṁ dukkhaṁ;

<!-- @segment sn22.45:1.12 -->

yaṁ dukkhaṁ tadanattā;

<!-- @segment sn22.45:1.13 -->

yadanattā taṁ ‘netaṁ mama, nesohamasmi, na meso attā’ti evametaṁ yathābhūtaṁ sammappaññāya daṭṭhabbaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn22.45:1.14 -->

Evametaṁ yathābhūtaṁ sammappaññāya passato cittaṁ virajjati vimuccati anupādāya āsavehi.

<!-- @segment sn22.45:1.15 -->

Rūpadhātuyā ce, bhikkhave, bhikkhuno cittaṁ virattaṁ vimuttaṁ hoti anupādāya āsavehi, vedanādhātuyā …pe…

<!-- @segment sn22.45:1.16 -->

saññādhātuyā …

<!-- @segment sn22.45:1.17 -->

saṅkhāradhātuyā …

<!-- @segment sn22.45:1.18 -->

viññāṇadhātuyā ce, bhikkhave, bhikkhuno cittaṁ virattaṁ vimuttaṁ hoti anupādāya āsavehi.

<!-- @segment sn22.45:1.19 -->

Vimuttattā ṭhitaṁ. Ṭhitattā santusitaṁ. Santusitattā na paritassati. Aparitassaṁ paccattaññeva parinibbāyati.

<!-- @segment sn22.45:1.20 -->

‘Khīṇā jāti, vusitaṁ brahmacariyaṁ, kataṁ karaṇīyaṁ, nāparaṁ itthattāyā’ti pajānātī”ti.

<!-- @segment sn22.45:1.21 -->

Tatiyaṁ.
