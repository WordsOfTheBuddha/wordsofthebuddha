<!-- @segment sn22.46:1.1 -->

Sāvatthinidānaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn22.46:1.2 -->

“Rūpaṁ, bhikkhave, aniccaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn22.46:1.3 -->

Yadaniccaṁ taṁ dukkhaṁ;

<!-- @segment sn22.46:1.4 -->

yaṁ dukkhaṁ tadanattā;

<!-- @segment sn22.46:1.5 -->

yadanattā taṁ ‘netaṁ mama, nesohamasmi, na meso attā’ti evametaṁ yathābhūtaṁ sammappaññāya daṭṭhabbaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn22.46:1.6 -->

Vedanā aniccā …

<!-- @segment sn22.46:1.7 -->

saññā aniccā …

<!-- @segment sn22.46:1.8 -->

saṅkhārā aniccā …

<!-- @segment sn22.46:1.9 -->

viññāṇaṁ aniccaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn22.46:1.10 -->

Yadaniccaṁ taṁ dukkhaṁ;

<!-- @segment sn22.46:1.11 -->

yaṁ dukkhaṁ tadanattā;

<!-- @segment sn22.46:1.12 -->

yadanattā taṁ ‘netaṁ mama, nesohamasmi, na meso attā’ti evametaṁ yathābhūtaṁ sammappaññāya daṭṭhabbaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn22.46:2.1 -->

Evametaṁ yathābhūtaṁ sammappaññāya passato pubbantānudiṭṭhiyo na honti.

<!-- @segment sn22.46:2.2 -->

Pubbantānudiṭṭhīnaṁ asati, aparantānudiṭṭhiyo na honti.

<!-- @segment sn22.46:2.3 -->

Aparantānudiṭṭhīnaṁ asati, thāmaso parāmāso na hoti.

<!-- @segment sn22.46:2.4 -->

Thāmase parāmāse asati rūpasmiṁ …

<!-- @segment sn22.46:2.5 -->

vedanāya …

<!-- @segment sn22.46:2.6 -->

saññāya …

<!-- @segment sn22.46:2.7 -->

saṅkhāresu …

<!-- @segment sn22.46:2.8 -->

viññāṇasmiṁ cittaṁ virajjati vimuccati anupādāya āsavehi.

<!-- @segment sn22.46:2.9 -->

Vimuttattā ṭhitaṁ. Ṭhitattā santusitaṁ. Santusitattā na paritassati. Aparitassaṁ paccattaññeva parinibbāyati.

<!-- @segment sn22.46:2.10 -->

‘Khīṇā jāti, vusitaṁ brahmacariyaṁ, kataṁ karaṇīyaṁ, nāparaṁ itthattāyā’ti pajānātī”ti.

<!-- @segment sn22.46:2.11 -->

Catutthaṁ.
