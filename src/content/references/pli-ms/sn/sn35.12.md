<!-- @segment sn35.12:1.1 -->

“Rūpā, bhikkhave, anattā atītānāgatā;

<!-- @segment sn35.12:1.2 -->

ko pana vādo paccuppannānaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn35.12:1.3 -->

Evaṁ passaṁ, bhikkhave, sutavā ariyasāvako atītesu rūpesu anapekkho hoti;

<!-- @segment sn35.12:1.4 -->

anāgate rūpe nābhinandati;

<!-- @segment sn35.12:1.5 -->

paccuppannānaṁ rūpānaṁ nibbidāya virāgāya nirodhāya paṭipanno hoti.

<!-- @segment sn35.12:1.6 -->

Saddā …

<!-- @segment sn35.12:1.7 -->

gandhā …

<!-- @segment sn35.12:1.8 -->

rasā …

<!-- @segment sn35.12:1.9 -->

phoṭṭhabbā …

<!-- @segment sn35.12:1.10 -->

dhammā anattā atītānāgatā;

<!-- @segment sn35.12:1.11 -->

ko pana vādo paccuppannānaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn35.12:1.12 -->

Evaṁ passaṁ, bhikkhave, sutavā ariyasāvako atītesu dhammesu anapekkho hoti;

<!-- @segment sn35.12:1.13 -->

anāgate dhamme nābhinandati;

<!-- @segment sn35.12:1.14 -->

paccuppannānaṁ dhammānaṁ nibbidāya virāgāya nirodhāya paṭipanno hotī”ti.

<!-- @segment sn35.12:1.15 -->

Dvādasamaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn35.12:1.16 -->

Aniccavaggo paṭhamo.

<!-- @segment sn35.12:2.0 -->

Tassuddānaṁ

<!-- @segment sn35.12:2.1 -->

Aniccaṁ dukkhaṁ anattā ca,

<!-- @segment sn35.12:2.2 -->

tayo ajjhattabāhirā;

<!-- @segment sn35.12:2.3 -->

Yadaniccena tayo vuttā,

<!-- @segment sn35.12:2.4 -->

te te ajjhattabāhirāti.
