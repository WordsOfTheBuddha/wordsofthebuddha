<!-- @segment sn52.17:1.1 -->

“Imesañca panāhaṁ, āvuso, catunnaṁ satipaṭṭhānānaṁ bhāvitattā bahulīkatattā sabbatthagāminippaṭipadaṁ yathābhūtaṁ pajānāmī”ti.

<!-- @segment sn52.17:1.2 -->

Sattamaṁ.
