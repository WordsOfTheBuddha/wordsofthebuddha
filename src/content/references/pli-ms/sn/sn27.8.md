<!-- @segment sn27.8:1.1 -->

Sāvatthinidānaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn27.8:1.2 -->

“Yo, bhikkhave, rūpataṇhāya chandarāgo, cittasseso upakkileso.

<!-- @segment sn27.8:1.3 -->

Yo saddataṇhāya …

<!-- @segment sn27.8:1.4 -->

yo gandhataṇhāya …

<!-- @segment sn27.8:1.5 -->

yo rasataṇhāya …

<!-- @segment sn27.8:1.6 -->

yo phoṭṭhabbataṇhāya …

<!-- @segment sn27.8:1.7 -->

yo dhammataṇhāya chandarāgo, cittasseso upakkileso.

<!-- @segment sn27.8:1.8 -->

Yato kho, bhikkhave, bhikkhuno …pe…

<!-- @segment sn27.8:1.9 -->

abhiññā sacchikaraṇīyesu dhammesū”ti.

<!-- @segment sn27.8:1.10 -->

Aṭṭhamaṁ.
