<!-- @segment sn22.32:1.1 -->

Sāvatthinidānaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn22.32:1.2 -->

“Pabhaṅguñca, bhikkhave, desessāmi appabhaṅguñca.

<!-- @segment sn22.32:1.3 -->

Taṁ suṇātha.

<!-- @segment sn22.32:1.4 -->

Kiñca, bhikkhave, pabhaṅgu, kiṁ appabhaṅgu?

<!-- @segment sn22.32:1.5 -->

Rūpaṁ, bhikkhave, pabhaṅgu.

<!-- @segment sn22.32:1.6 -->

Yo tassa nirodho vūpasamo atthaṅgamo, idaṁ appabhaṅgu.

<!-- @segment sn22.32:1.7 -->

Vedanā pabhaṅgu.

<!-- @segment sn22.32:1.8 -->

Yo tassā nirodho vūpasamo atthaṅgamo, idaṁ appabhaṅgu.

<!-- @segment sn22.32:1.9 -->

Saññā pabhaṅgu …

<!-- @segment sn22.32:1.10 -->

saṅkhārā pabhaṅgu.

<!-- @segment sn22.32:1.11 -->

Yo tesaṁ nirodho vūpasamo atthaṅgamo, idaṁ appabhaṅgu.

<!-- @segment sn22.32:1.12 -->

Viññāṇaṁ pabhaṅgu.

<!-- @segment sn22.32:1.13 -->

Yo tassa nirodho vūpasamo atthaṅgamo, idaṁ appabhaṅgū”ti.

<!-- @segment sn22.32:1.14 -->

Ekādasamaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn22.32:1.15 -->

Bhāravaggo tatiyo.

<!-- @segment sn22.32:2.0 -->

Tassuddānaṁ

<!-- @segment sn22.32:2.1 -->

Bhāraṁ pariññaṁ abhijānaṁ,

<!-- @segment sn22.32:2.2 -->

chandarāgaṁ catutthakaṁ;

<!-- @segment sn22.32:2.3 -->

Assādā ca tayo vuttā,

<!-- @segment sn22.32:2.4 -->

abhinandanamaṭṭhamaṁ;

<!-- @segment sn22.32:2.5 -->

Uppādaṁ aghamūlañca,

<!-- @segment sn22.32:2.6 -->

ekādasamo pabhaṅgūti.
