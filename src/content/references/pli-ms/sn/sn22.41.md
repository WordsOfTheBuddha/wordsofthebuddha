<!-- @segment sn22.41:1.1 -->

Sāvatthinidānaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn22.41:1.2 -->

“Dhammānudhammappaṭipannassa, bhikkhave, bhikkhuno ayamanudhammo hoti yaṁ rūpe dukkhānupassī vihareyya …pe…

<!-- @segment sn22.41:1.3 -->

‘parimuccati dukkhasmā’ti vadāmī”ti.

<!-- @segment sn22.41:1.4 -->

Navamaṁ.
