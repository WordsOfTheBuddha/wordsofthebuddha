<!-- @segment sn25.3:1.1 -->

Sāvatthinidānaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn25.3:1.2 -->

“Cakkhuviññāṇaṁ, bhikkhave, aniccaṁ vipariṇāmi aññathābhāvi;

<!-- @segment sn25.3:1.3 -->

sotaviññāṇaṁ …

<!-- @segment sn25.3:1.4 -->

ghānaviññāṇaṁ …

<!-- @segment sn25.3:1.5 -->

jivhāviññāṇaṁ …

<!-- @segment sn25.3:1.6 -->

kāyaviññāṇaṁ …

<!-- @segment sn25.3:1.7 -->

manoviññāṇaṁ aniccaṁ vipariṇāmi aññathābhāvi.

<!-- @segment sn25.3:1.8 -->

Yo bhikkhave …pe…

<!-- @segment sn25.3:1.9 -->

sambodhiparāyano”ti.

<!-- @segment sn25.3:1.10 -->

Tatiyaṁ.
