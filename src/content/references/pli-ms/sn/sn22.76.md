<!-- @segment sn22.76:1.1 -->

Sāvatthinidānaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn22.76:1.2 -->

“Rūpaṁ, bhikkhave, aniccaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn22.76:1.3 -->

Yadaniccaṁ taṁ dukkhaṁ;

<!-- @segment sn22.76:1.4 -->

yaṁ dukkhaṁ tadanattā;

<!-- @segment sn22.76:1.5 -->

yadanattā taṁ ‘netaṁ mama, nesohamasmi, na meso attā’ti evametaṁ yathābhūtaṁ sammappaññāya daṭṭhabbaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn22.76:1.6 -->

Vedanā …

<!-- @segment sn22.76:1.7 -->

saññā …

<!-- @segment sn22.76:1.8 -->

saṅkhārā …

<!-- @segment sn22.76:1.9 -->

viññāṇaṁ aniccaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn22.76:1.10 -->

Yadaniccaṁ taṁ dukkhaṁ;

<!-- @segment sn22.76:1.11 -->

yaṁ dukkhaṁ tadanattā;

<!-- @segment sn22.76:1.12 -->

yadanattā taṁ ‘netaṁ mama, nesohamasmi, na meso attā’ti evametaṁ yathābhūtaṁ sammappaññāya daṭṭhabbaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn22.76:2.1 -->

Evaṁ passaṁ, bhikkhave, sutavā ariyasāvako rūpasmimpi nibbindati, vedanāyapi … saññāyapi … saṅkhāresupi … viññāṇasmimpi nibbindati.

<!-- @segment sn22.76:2.2 -->

Nibbindaṁ virajjati; virāgā vimuccati. Vimuttasmiṁ vimuttamiti ñāṇaṁ hoti.

<!-- @segment sn22.76:2.3 -->

‘Khīṇā jāti, vusitaṁ brahmacariyaṁ, kataṁ karaṇīyaṁ, nāparaṁ itthattāyā’ti pajānāti.

<!-- @segment sn22.76:2.4 -->

Yāvatā, bhikkhave, sattāvāsā, yāvatā bhavaggaṁ, ete aggā, ete seṭṭhā lokasmiṁ yadidaṁ arahanto”ti.

<!-- @segment sn22.76:3.1 -->

Idamavoca bhagavā.

<!-- @segment sn22.76:3.2 -->

Idaṁ vatvāna sugato athāparaṁ etadavoca satthā:

<!-- @segment sn22.76:4.1 -->

“Sukhino vata arahanto,

<!-- @segment sn22.76:4.2 -->

taṇhā tesaṁ na vijjati;

<!-- @segment sn22.76:4.3 -->

Asmimāno samucchinno,

<!-- @segment sn22.76:4.4 -->

mohajālaṁ padālitaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn22.76:5.1 -->

Anejaṁ te anuppattā,

<!-- @segment sn22.76:5.2 -->

cittaṁ tesaṁ anāvilaṁ;

<!-- @segment sn22.76:5.3 -->

Loke anupalittā te,

<!-- @segment sn22.76:5.4 -->

brahmabhūtā anāsavā.

<!-- @segment sn22.76:6.1 -->

Pañcakkhandhe pariññāya,

<!-- @segment sn22.76:6.2 -->

satta saddhammagocarā;

<!-- @segment sn22.76:6.3 -->

Pasaṁsiyā sappurisā,

<!-- @segment sn22.76:6.4 -->

puttā buddhassa orasā.

<!-- @segment sn22.76:7.1 -->

Sattaratanasampannā,

<!-- @segment sn22.76:7.2 -->

Tīsu sikkhāsu sikkhitā;

<!-- @segment sn22.76:7.3 -->

Anuvicaranti mahāvīrā,

<!-- @segment sn22.76:7.4 -->

Pahīnabhayabheravā.

<!-- @segment sn22.76:8.1 -->

Dasahaṅgehi sampannā,

<!-- @segment sn22.76:8.2 -->

mahānāgā samāhitā;

<!-- @segment sn22.76:8.3 -->

Ete kho seṭṭhā lokasmiṁ,

<!-- @segment sn22.76:8.4 -->

taṇhā tesaṁ na vijjati.

<!-- @segment sn22.76:9.1 -->

Asekhañāṇamuppannaṁ,

<!-- @segment sn22.76:9.2 -->

antimoyaṁ samussayo;

<!-- @segment sn22.76:9.3 -->

Yo sāro brahmacariyassa,

<!-- @segment sn22.76:9.4 -->

tasmiṁ aparapaccayā.

<!-- @segment sn22.76:10.1 -->

Vidhāsu na vikampanti,

<!-- @segment sn22.76:10.2 -->

vippamuttā punabbhavā;

<!-- @segment sn22.76:10.3 -->

Dantabhūmimanuppattā,

<!-- @segment sn22.76:10.4 -->

te loke vijitāvino.

<!-- @segment sn22.76:11.1 -->

Uddhaṁ tiriyaṁ apācīnaṁ,

<!-- @segment sn22.76:11.2 -->

nandī tesaṁ na vijjati;

<!-- @segment sn22.76:11.3 -->

Nadanti te sīhanādaṁ,

<!-- @segment sn22.76:11.4 -->

buddhā loke anuttarā”ti.

<!-- @segment sn22.76:11.5 -->

Catutthaṁ.
