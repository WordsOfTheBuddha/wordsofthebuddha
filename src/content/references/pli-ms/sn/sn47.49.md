<!-- @segment sn47.49:1.1 -->

“Tisso imā, bhikkhave, vedanā.

<!-- @segment sn47.49:1.2 -->

Katamā tisso?

<!-- @segment sn47.49:1.3 -->

Sukhā vedanā, dukkhā vedanā, adukkhamasukhā vedanā—

<!-- @segment sn47.49:1.4 -->

imā kho, bhikkhave, tisso vedanā.

<!-- @segment sn47.49:1.5 -->

Imāsaṁ kho, bhikkhave, tissannaṁ vedanānaṁ pariññāya cattāro satipaṭṭhānā bhāvetabbā.

<!-- @segment sn47.49:2.1 -->

Katame cattāro?

<!-- @segment sn47.49:2.2 -->

Idha, bhikkhave, bhikkhu kāye kāyānupassī viharati ātāpī sampajāno satimā, vineyya loke abhijjhādomanassaṁ;

<!-- @segment sn47.49:2.3 -->

vedanāsu …pe…

<!-- @segment sn47.49:2.4 -->

citte …pe…

<!-- @segment sn47.49:2.5 -->

dhammesu dhammānupassī viharati ātāpī sampajāno satimā, vineyya loke abhijjhādomanassaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn47.49:2.6 -->

Imāsaṁ kho, bhikkhave, tissannaṁ vedanānaṁ pariññāya ime cattāro satipaṭṭhānā bhāvetabbā”ti.

<!-- @segment sn47.49:2.7 -->

Navamaṁ.
