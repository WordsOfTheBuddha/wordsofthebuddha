“Kiṁsu sabbaṁ addhabhavi,

kismā bhiyyo na vijjati;

Kissassu ekadhammassa,

sabbeva vasamanvagū”ti.

“Nāmaṁ sabbaṁ addhabhavi,

nāmā bhiyyo na vijjati;

Nāmassa ekadhammassa,

sabbeva vasamanvagū”ti.
