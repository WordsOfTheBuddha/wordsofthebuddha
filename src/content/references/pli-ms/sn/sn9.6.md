<!-- @segment sn9.6:1.1 -->

Ekaṁ samayaṁ āyasmā anuruddho kosalesu viharati aññatarasmiṁ vanasaṇḍe.

<!-- @segment sn9.6:1.2 -->

Atha kho aññatarā tāvatiṁsakāyikā devatā jālinī nāma āyasmato anuruddhassa purāṇadutiyikā yenāyasmā anuruddho tenupasaṅkami; upasaṅkamitvā āyasmantaṁ anuruddhaṁ gāthāya ajjhabhāsi:

<!-- @segment sn9.6:2.1 -->

“Tattha cittaṁ paṇidhehi,

<!-- @segment sn9.6:2.2 -->

yattha te vusitaṁ pure;

<!-- @segment sn9.6:2.3 -->

Tāvatiṁsesu devesu,

<!-- @segment sn9.6:2.4 -->

sabbakāmasamiddhisu;

<!-- @segment sn9.6:2.5 -->

Purakkhato parivuto,

<!-- @segment sn9.6:2.6 -->

devakaññāhi sobhasī”ti.

<!-- @segment sn9.6:3.1 -->

“Duggatā devakaññāyo,

<!-- @segment sn9.6:3.2 -->

sakkāyasmiṁ patiṭṭhitā;

<!-- @segment sn9.6:3.3 -->

Te cāpi duggatā sattā,

<!-- @segment sn9.6:3.4 -->

devakaññāhi patthitā”ti.

<!-- @segment sn9.6:4.1 -->

“Na te sukhaṁ pajānanti,

<!-- @segment sn9.6:4.2 -->

ye na passanti nandanaṁ;

<!-- @segment sn9.6:4.3 -->

Āvāsaṁ naradevānaṁ,

<!-- @segment sn9.6:4.4 -->

tidasānaṁ yasassinan”ti.

<!-- @segment sn9.6:5.1 -->

“Na tvaṁ bāle vijānāsi,

<!-- @segment sn9.6:5.2 -->

yathā arahataṁ vaco;

<!-- @segment sn9.6:5.3 -->

Aniccā sabbasaṅkhārā,

<!-- @segment sn9.6:5.4 -->

uppādavayadhammino;

<!-- @segment sn9.6:5.5 -->

Uppajjitvā nirujjhanti,

<!-- @segment sn9.6:5.6 -->

tesaṁ vūpasamo sukho.

<!-- @segment sn9.6:6.1 -->

Natthi dāni punāvāso,

<!-- @segment sn9.6:6.2 -->

devakāyasmi jālini;

<!-- @segment sn9.6:6.3 -->

Vikkhīṇo jātisaṁsāro,

<!-- @segment sn9.6:6.4 -->

natthi dāni punabbhavo”ti.
