<!-- @segment sn27.7:1.1 -->

Sāvatthinidānaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn27.7:1.2 -->

“Yo, bhikkhave, rūpasañcetanāya chandarāgo, cittasseso upakkileso.

<!-- @segment sn27.7:1.3 -->

Yo saddasañcetanāya …

<!-- @segment sn27.7:1.4 -->

yo gandhasañcetanāya …

<!-- @segment sn27.7:1.5 -->

yo rasasañcetanāya …

<!-- @segment sn27.7:1.6 -->

yo phoṭṭhabbasañcetanāya …

<!-- @segment sn27.7:1.7 -->

yo dhammasañcetanāya chandarāgo, cittasseso upakkileso.

<!-- @segment sn27.7:1.8 -->

Yato kho, bhikkhave, bhikkhuno …pe…

<!-- @segment sn27.7:1.9 -->

abhiññā sacchikaraṇīyesu dhammesū”ti.

<!-- @segment sn27.7:1.10 -->

Sattamaṁ.
