title: Bandhanasutta

“Kiṁsu sambandhano loko,

kiṁsu tassa vicāraṇaṁ;

Kissassu vippahānena,

sabbaṁ chindati bandhanan”ti.

“Nandīsambandhano loko,

vitakkassa vicāraṇaṁ;

Taṇhāya vippahānena,

sabbaṁ chindati bandhanan”ti.
