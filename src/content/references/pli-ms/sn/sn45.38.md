<!-- @segment sn45.38:1.1 -->

Sāvatthinidānaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn45.38:1.2 -->

“Brahmaññañca vo, bhikkhave, desessāmi, brahmaññatthañca.

<!-- @segment sn45.38:1.3 -->

Taṁ suṇātha.

<!-- @segment sn45.38:1.4 -->

Katamañca, bhikkhave, brahmaññaṁ?

<!-- @segment sn45.38:1.5 -->

Ayameva ariyo aṭṭhaṅgiko maggo, seyyathidaṁ—

<!-- @segment sn45.38:1.6 -->

sammādiṭṭhi …pe… sammāsamādhi.

<!-- @segment sn45.38:1.7 -->

Idaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, brahmaññaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn45.38:1.8 -->

Katamo ca, bhikkhave, brahmaññattho?

<!-- @segment sn45.38:1.9 -->

Yo kho, bhikkhave, rāgakkhayo dosakkhayo mohakkhayo—

<!-- @segment sn45.38:1.10 -->

ayaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, brahmaññattho”ti.

<!-- @segment sn45.38:1.11 -->

Aṭṭhamaṁ.
