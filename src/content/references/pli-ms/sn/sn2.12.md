<!-- @segment sn2.12:1.1 -->

Ekamantaṁ ṭhito kho veṇḍu devaputto bhagavato santike imaṁ gāthaṁ abhāsi:

<!-- @segment sn2.12:2.1 -->

“Sukhitāva te manujā,

<!-- @segment sn2.12:2.2 -->

sugataṁ payirupāsiya;

<!-- @segment sn2.12:2.3 -->

Yuñjaṁ gotamasāsane,

<!-- @segment sn2.12:2.4 -->

appamattā nu sikkhare”ti.

<!-- @segment sn2.12:3.1 -->

“Ye me pavutte siṭṭhipade,

<!-- @segment sn2.12:3.2 -->

(veṇḍūti bhagavā)

<!-- @segment sn2.12:3.3 -->

Anusikkhanti jhāyino;

<!-- @segment sn2.12:3.4 -->

Kāle te appamajjantā,

<!-- @segment sn2.12:3.5 -->

Na maccuvasagā siyun”ti.
