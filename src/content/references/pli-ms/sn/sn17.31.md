Sāvatthiyaṁ viharati.

“Dāruṇo, bhikkhave, lābhasakkārasiloko.

Lābhasakkārasilokena abhibhūto pariyādiṇṇacitto, bhikkhave, devadatto saṅghaṁ bhindi.

Evaṁ dāruṇo kho, bhikkhave, lābhasakkārasiloko …pe…

sikkhitabban”ti.

Paṭhamaṁ.
