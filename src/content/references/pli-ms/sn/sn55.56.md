<!-- @segment sn55.56:1.1 -->

“Cattārome, bhikkhave, dhammā bhāvitā bahulīkatā sakadāgāmiphalasacchikiriyāya saṁvattanti.

<!-- @segment sn55.56:1.2 -->

Katame cattāro?

<!-- @segment sn55.56:1.3 -->

… pe Saṁvattantī”ti.

<!-- @segment sn55.56:1.4 -->

Chaṭṭhaṁ.
