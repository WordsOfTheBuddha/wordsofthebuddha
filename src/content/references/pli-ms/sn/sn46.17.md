<!-- @segment sn46.17:1.1 -->

“Sattime, bhikkhave, bojjhaṅgā bhāvitā bahulīkatā apārā pāraṁ gamanāya saṁvattanti.

<!-- @segment sn46.17:1.2 -->

Katame satta?

<!-- @segment sn46.17:1.3 -->

Satisambojjhaṅgo …pe… upekkhāsambojjhaṅgo—

<!-- @segment sn46.17:1.4 -->

ime kho, bhikkhave, satta bojjhaṅgā bhāvitā bahulīkatā apārā pāraṁ gamanāya saṁvattantīti.

<!-- @segment sn46.17:2.1 -->

Appakā te manussesu,

<!-- @segment sn46.17:2.2 -->

ye janā pāragāmino;

<!-- @segment sn46.17:2.3 -->

Athāyaṁ itarā pajā,

<!-- @segment sn46.17:2.4 -->

tīramevānudhāvati.

<!-- @segment sn46.17:3.1 -->

Ye ca kho sammadakkhāte,

<!-- @segment sn46.17:3.2 -->

dhamme dhammānuvattino;

<!-- @segment sn46.17:3.3 -->

Te janā pāramessanti,

<!-- @segment sn46.17:3.4 -->

maccudheyyaṁ suduttaraṁ.

<!-- @segment sn46.17:4.1 -->

Kaṇhaṁ dhammaṁ vippahāya,

<!-- @segment sn46.17:4.2 -->

sukkaṁ bhāvetha paṇḍito;

<!-- @segment sn46.17:4.3 -->

Okā anokamāgamma,

<!-- @segment sn46.17:4.4 -->

viveke yattha dūramaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn46.17:5.1 -->

Tatrābhiratimiccheyya,

<!-- @segment sn46.17:5.2 -->

hitvā kāme akiñcano;

<!-- @segment sn46.17:5.3 -->

Pariyodapeyya attānaṁ,

<!-- @segment sn46.17:5.4 -->

cittaklesehi paṇḍito.

<!-- @segment sn46.17:6.1 -->

Yesaṁ sambodhiyaṅgesu,

<!-- @segment sn46.17:6.2 -->

sammā cittaṁ subhāvitaṁ;

<!-- @segment sn46.17:6.3 -->

Ādānappaṭinissagge,

<!-- @segment sn46.17:6.4 -->

anupādāya ye ratā;

<!-- @segment sn46.17:6.5 -->

Khīṇāsavā jutimanto,

<!-- @segment sn46.17:6.6 -->

te loke parinibbutā”ti.

<!-- @segment sn46.17:6.7 -->

Sattamaṁ.
