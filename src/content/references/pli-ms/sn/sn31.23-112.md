<!-- @segment sn31.23-112:1.1 -->

Sāvatthinidānaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn31.23-112:1.2 -->

Ekamantaṁ nisinno kho so bhikkhu bhagavantaṁ etadavoca:

<!-- @segment sn31.23-112:1.3 -->

“ko nu kho, bhante, hetu, ko paccayo, yena midhekacco kāyassa bhedā paraṁ maraṇā sāragandhe adhivatthānaṁ devānaṁ …pe…

<!-- @segment sn31.23-112:1.4 -->

pheggugandhe adhivatthānaṁ devānaṁ …

<!-- @segment sn31.23-112:1.5 -->

tacagandhe adhivatthānaṁ devānaṁ …

<!-- @segment sn31.23-112:1.6 -->

papaṭikagandhe adhivatthānaṁ devānaṁ …

<!-- @segment sn31.23-112:1.7 -->

pattagandhe adhivatthānaṁ devānaṁ …

<!-- @segment sn31.23-112:1.8 -->

pupphagandhe adhivatthānaṁ devānaṁ …

<!-- @segment sn31.23-112:1.9 -->

phalagandhe adhivatthānaṁ devānaṁ …

<!-- @segment sn31.23-112:1.10 -->

rasagandhe adhivatthānaṁ devānaṁ …

<!-- @segment sn31.23-112:1.11 -->

gandhagandhe adhivatthānaṁ devānaṁ sahabyataṁ upapajjatī”ti?

<!-- @segment sn31.23-112:1.12 -->

“Idha, bhikkhu, ekacco kāyena sucaritaṁ carati, vācāya sucaritaṁ carati, manasā sucaritaṁ carati.

<!-- @segment sn31.23-112:1.13 -->

Tassa sutaṁ hoti:

<!-- @segment sn31.23-112:1.14 -->

‘gandhagandhe adhivatthā devā dīghāyukā vaṇṇavanto sukhabahulā’ti.

<!-- @segment sn31.23-112:1.15 -->

Tassa evaṁ hoti:

<!-- @segment sn31.23-112:1.16 -->

‘aho vatāhaṁ kāyassa bhedā paraṁ maraṇā gandhagandhe adhivatthānaṁ devānaṁ sahabyataṁ upapajjeyyan’ti.

<!-- @segment sn31.23-112:1.17 -->

So annaṁ deti …pe…

<!-- @segment sn31.23-112:1.18 -->

pānaṁ deti …

<!-- @segment sn31.23-112:1.19 -->

vatthaṁ deti …

<!-- @segment sn31.23-112:1.20 -->

yānaṁ deti …

<!-- @segment sn31.23-112:1.21 -->

mālaṁ deti …

<!-- @segment sn31.23-112:1.22 -->

gandhaṁ deti …

<!-- @segment sn31.23-112:1.23 -->

vilepanaṁ deti …

<!-- @segment sn31.23-112:1.24 -->

seyyaṁ deti …

<!-- @segment sn31.23-112:1.25 -->

āvasathaṁ deti …

<!-- @segment sn31.23-112:1.26 -->

padīpeyyaṁ deti.

<!-- @segment sn31.23-112:1.27 -->

So kāyassa bhedā paraṁ maraṇā gandhagandhe adhivatthānaṁ devānaṁ sahabyataṁ upapajjati.

<!-- @segment sn31.23-112:1.28 -->

Ayaṁ kho, bhikkhu, hetu, ayaṁ paccayo, yena midhekacco kāyassa bhedā paraṁ maraṇā gandhagandhe adhivatthānaṁ devānaṁ sahabyataṁ upapajjatī”ti.

<!-- @segment sn31.23-112:1.29 -->

Dvādasasatimaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn31.23-112:2.1 -->

(Evaṁ piṇḍakena ekasatañca dvādasa ca suttantā honti.)

<!-- @segment sn31.23-112:2.2 -->

Gandhabbavaggo paṭhamo.

<!-- @segment sn31.23-112:3.0 -->

Tassuddānaṁ

<!-- @segment sn31.23-112:3.1 -->

Suddhikañca sucaritaṁ,

<!-- @segment sn31.23-112:3.2 -->

dātā hi apare dasa;

<!-- @segment sn31.23-112:3.3 -->

Dānūpakārā satadhā,

<!-- @segment sn31.23-112:3.4 -->

gandhabbe suppakāsitāti.

<!-- @segment sn31.23-112:3.5 -->

Gandhabbakāyasaṁyuttaṁ samattaṁ.
