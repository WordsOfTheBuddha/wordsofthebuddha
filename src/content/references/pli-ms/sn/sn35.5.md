<!-- @segment sn35.5:1.1 -->

“Rūpā, bhikkhave, dukkhā.

<!-- @segment sn35.5:1.2 -->

Yaṁ dukkhaṁ tadanattā;

<!-- @segment sn35.5:1.3 -->

yadanattā taṁ ‘netaṁ mama, nesohamasmi, na meso attā’ti evametaṁ yathābhūtaṁ sammappaññāya daṭṭhabbaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn35.5:1.4 -->

Saddā …

<!-- @segment sn35.5:1.5 -->

gandhā …

<!-- @segment sn35.5:1.6 -->

rasā …

<!-- @segment sn35.5:1.7 -->

phoṭṭhabbā …

<!-- @segment sn35.5:1.8 -->

dhammā dukkhā.

<!-- @segment sn35.5:1.9 -->

Yaṁ dukkhaṁ tadanattā.

<!-- @segment sn35.5:1.10 -->

Yadanattā taṁ ‘netaṁ mama, nesohamasmi, na meso attā’ti evametaṁ yathābhūtaṁ sammappaññāya daṭṭhabbaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn35.5:1.11 -->

Evaṁ passaṁ …pe…

<!-- @segment sn35.5:1.12 -->

nāparaṁ itthattāyāti pajānātī”ti.

<!-- @segment sn35.5:1.13 -->

Pañcamaṁ.
