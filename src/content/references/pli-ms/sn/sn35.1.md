<!-- @segment sn35.1:1.1 -->

Evaṁ me sutaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn35.1:1.2 -->

Ekaṁ samayaṁ bhagavā sāvatthiyaṁ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme.

<!-- @segment sn35.1:1.3 -->

Tatra kho bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi:

<!-- @segment sn35.1:1.4 -->

“bhikkhavo”ti.

<!-- @segment sn35.1:1.5 -->

“Bhadante”ti te bhikkhū bhagavato paccassosuṁ.

<!-- @segment sn35.1:1.6 -->

Bhagavā etadavoca:

<!-- @segment sn35.1:2.1 -->

“Cakkhuṁ, bhikkhave, aniccaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn35.1:2.2 -->

Yadaniccaṁ taṁ dukkhaṁ;

<!-- @segment sn35.1:2.3 -->

yaṁ dukkhaṁ tadanattā.

<!-- @segment sn35.1:2.4 -->

Yadanattā taṁ ‘netaṁ mama, nesohamasmi, na meso attā’ti evametaṁ yathābhūtaṁ sammappaññāya daṭṭhabbaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn35.1:2.5 -->

Sotaṁ aniccaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn35.1:2.6 -->

Yadaniccaṁ …pe…

<!-- @segment sn35.1:2.7 -->

ghānaṁ aniccaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn35.1:2.8 -->

Yadaniccaṁ …pe…

<!-- @segment sn35.1:2.9 -->

jivhā aniccā.

<!-- @segment sn35.1:2.10 -->

Yadaniccaṁ taṁ dukkhaṁ;

<!-- @segment sn35.1:2.11 -->

yaṁ dukkhaṁ tadanattā.

<!-- @segment sn35.1:2.12 -->

Yadanattā taṁ ‘netaṁ mama, nesohamasmi, na meso attā’ti evametaṁ yathābhūtaṁ sammappaññāya daṭṭhabbaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn35.1:2.13 -->

Kāyo anicco.

<!-- @segment sn35.1:2.14 -->

Yadaniccaṁ …pe…

<!-- @segment sn35.1:2.15 -->

mano anicco.

<!-- @segment sn35.1:2.16 -->

Yadaniccaṁ taṁ dukkhaṁ;

<!-- @segment sn35.1:2.17 -->

yaṁ dukkhaṁ tadanattā.

<!-- @segment sn35.1:2.18 -->

Yadanattā taṁ ‘netaṁ mama, nesohamasmi, na meso attā’ti evametaṁ yathābhūtaṁ sammappaññāya daṭṭhabbaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn35.1:2.19 -->

Evaṁ passaṁ, bhikkhave, sutavā ariyasāvako cakkhusmimpi nibbindati, sotasmimpi nibbindati, ghānasmimpi nibbindati, jivhāyapi nibbindati, kāyasmimpi nibbindati, manasmimpi nibbindati.

<!-- @segment sn35.1:2.20 -->

Nibbindaṁ virajjati; virāgā vimuccati; vimuttasmiṁ vimuttamiti ñāṇaṁ hoti.

<!-- @segment sn35.1:2.21 -->

‘Khīṇā jāti, vusitaṁ brahmacariyaṁ, kataṁ karaṇīyaṁ, nāparaṁ itthattāyā’ti pajānātī”ti.

<!-- @segment sn35.1:2.22 -->

Paṭhamaṁ.
