<!-- @segment sn22.83:1.1 -->

Sāvatthinidānaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn22.83:1.2 -->

Tatra kho āyasmā ānando bhikkhū āmantesi:

<!-- @segment sn22.83:1.3 -->

“āvuso bhikkhave”ti.

<!-- @segment sn22.83:1.4 -->

“Āvuso”ti kho te bhikkhū āyasmato ānandassa paccassosuṁ.

<!-- @segment sn22.83:1.5 -->

Āyasmā ānando etadavoca:

<!-- @segment sn22.83:2.1 -->

“Puṇṇo nāma, āvuso, āyasmā mantāṇiputto amhākaṁ navakānaṁ sataṁ bahūpakāro hoti.

<!-- @segment sn22.83:2.2 -->

So amhe iminā ovādena ovadati:

<!-- @segment sn22.83:2.3 -->

‘upādāya, āvuso ānanda, asmīti hoti, no anupādāya.

<!-- @segment sn22.83:2.4 -->

Kiñca upādāya asmīti hoti, no anupādāya?

<!-- @segment sn22.83:2.5 -->

Rūpaṁ upādāya asmīti hoti, no anupādāya.

<!-- @segment sn22.83:2.6 -->

Vedanaṁ …

<!-- @segment sn22.83:2.7 -->

saññaṁ …

<!-- @segment sn22.83:2.8 -->

saṅkhāre …

<!-- @segment sn22.83:2.9 -->

viññāṇaṁ upādāya asmīti hoti, no anupādāya.

<!-- @segment sn22.83:3.1 -->

Seyyathāpi, āvuso ānanda, itthī vā puriso vā daharo yuvā maṇḍanakajātiko ādāse vā parisuddhe pariyodāte acche vā udakapatte sakaṁ mukhanimittaṁ paccavekkhamāno upādāya passeyya, no anupādāya;

<!-- @segment sn22.83:3.2 -->

evameva kho, āvuso ānanda, rūpaṁ upādāya asmīti hoti, no anupādāya.

<!-- @segment sn22.83:3.3 -->

Vedanaṁ …

<!-- @segment sn22.83:3.4 -->

saññaṁ …

<!-- @segment sn22.83:3.5 -->

saṅkhāre …

<!-- @segment sn22.83:3.6 -->

viññāṇaṁ upādāya asmīti hoti, no anupādāya.

<!-- @segment sn22.83:4.1 -->

Taṁ kiṁ maññasi, āvuso ānanda,

<!-- @segment sn22.83:4.2 -->

rūpaṁ niccaṁ vā aniccaṁ vā’ti?

<!-- @segment sn22.83:4.3 -->

‘Aniccaṁ, āvuso’.

<!-- @segment sn22.83:4.4 -->

‘Vedanā …

<!-- @segment sn22.83:4.5 -->

saññā …

<!-- @segment sn22.83:4.6 -->

saṅkhārā …

<!-- @segment sn22.83:4.7 -->

viññāṇaṁ niccaṁ vā aniccaṁ vā’ti?

<!-- @segment sn22.83:4.8 -->

‘Aniccaṁ, āvuso’ …pe….

<!-- @segment sn22.83:4.9 -->

Tasmātiha …pe…

<!-- @segment sn22.83:4.10 -->

evaṁ passaṁ …pe…

<!-- @segment sn22.83:4.11 -->

nāparaṁ itthattāyāti pajānātīti.

<!-- @segment sn22.83:4.12 -->

Puṇṇo nāma, āvuso, āyasmā mantāṇiputto amhākaṁ navakānaṁ sataṁ bahūpakāro hoti.

<!-- @segment sn22.83:4.13 -->

So amhe iminā ovādena ovadati.

<!-- @segment sn22.83:4.14 -->

Idañca pana me āyasmato puṇṇassa mantāṇiputtassa dhammadesanaṁ sutvā dhammo abhisamito”ti.

<!-- @segment sn22.83:4.15 -->

Paṭhamaṁ.
