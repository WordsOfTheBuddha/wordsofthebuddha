<!-- @segment sn22.118:1.1 -->

Sāvatthinidānaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn22.118:1.2 -->

“Taṁ kiṁ maññatha, bhikkhave,

<!-- @segment sn22.118:1.3 -->

rūpaṁ ‘etaṁ mama, esohamasmi, eso me attā’ti samanupassathā”ti?

<!-- @segment sn22.118:1.4 -->

“No hetaṁ, bhante”.

<!-- @segment sn22.118:1.5 -->

“Sādhu, bhikkhave.

<!-- @segment sn22.118:1.6 -->

Rūpaṁ, bhikkhave, ‘netaṁ mama, nesohamasmi, na meso attā’ti evametaṁ yathābhūtaṁ sammappaññāya daṭṭhabbaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn22.118:1.7 -->

Vedanaṁ …

<!-- @segment sn22.118:1.8 -->

saññaṁ …

<!-- @segment sn22.118:1.9 -->

saṅkhāre …

<!-- @segment sn22.118:1.10 -->

viññāṇaṁ ‘etaṁ mama, esohamasmi, eso me attā’ti samanupassathā”ti?

<!-- @segment sn22.118:1.11 -->

“No hetaṁ, bhante”.

<!-- @segment sn22.118:1.12 -->

“Sādhu, bhikkhave.

<!-- @segment sn22.118:1.13 -->

Viññāṇaṁ, bhikkhave, ‘netaṁ mama, nesohamasmi, na meso attā’ti evametaṁ yathābhūtaṁ sammappaññāya daṭṭhabbaṁ …pe…

<!-- @segment sn22.118:1.14 -->

evaṁ passaṁ …pe…

<!-- @segment sn22.118:1.15 -->

nāparaṁ itthattāyāti pajānātī”ti.

<!-- @segment sn22.118:1.16 -->

Chaṭṭhaṁ.
