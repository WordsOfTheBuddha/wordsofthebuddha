<!-- @segment sn45.40:1.1 -->

Sāvatthinidānaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn45.40:1.2 -->

“Brahmacariyañca vo, bhikkhave, desessāmi, brahmacariyatthañca.

<!-- @segment sn45.40:1.3 -->

Taṁ suṇātha.

<!-- @segment sn45.40:1.4 -->

Katamañca, bhikkhave, brahmacariyaṁ?

<!-- @segment sn45.40:1.5 -->

Ayameva ariyo aṭṭhaṅgiko maggo, seyyathidaṁ—

<!-- @segment sn45.40:1.6 -->

sammādiṭṭhi …pe… sammāsamādhi.

<!-- @segment sn45.40:1.7 -->

Idaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, brahmacariyaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn45.40:1.8 -->

Katamo ca, bhikkhave, brahmacariyattho?

<!-- @segment sn45.40:1.9 -->

Yo kho, bhikkhave, rāgakkhayo dosakkhayo mohakkhayo—

<!-- @segment sn45.40:1.10 -->

ayaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, brahmacariyattho”ti.

<!-- @segment sn45.40:1.11 -->

Dasamaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn45.40:1.12 -->

Paṭipattivaggo catuttho.

<!-- @segment sn45.40:2.0 -->

Tassuddānaṁ

<!-- @segment sn45.40:2.1 -->

Paṭipatti paṭipanno ca,

<!-- @segment sn45.40:2.2 -->

viraddhañca pāraṅgamā;

<!-- @segment sn45.40:2.3 -->

Sāmaññena ca dve vuttā,

<!-- @segment sn45.40:2.4 -->

brahmaññā apare duve;

<!-- @segment sn45.40:2.5 -->

Brahmacariyena dve vuttā,

<!-- @segment sn45.40:2.6 -->

vaggo tena pavuccatīti.
