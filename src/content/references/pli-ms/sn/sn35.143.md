<!-- @segment sn35.143:1.1 -->

“Rūpā, bhikkhave, aniccā.

<!-- @segment sn35.143:1.2 -->

Yopi hetu, yopi paccayo rūpānaṁ uppādāya, sopi anicco.

<!-- @segment sn35.143:1.3 -->

Aniccasambhūtā, bhikkhave, rūpā kuto niccā bhavissanti.

<!-- @segment sn35.143:1.4 -->

Saddā …

<!-- @segment sn35.143:1.5 -->

gandhā …

<!-- @segment sn35.143:1.6 -->

rasā …

<!-- @segment sn35.143:1.7 -->

phoṭṭhabbā …

<!-- @segment sn35.143:1.8 -->

dhammā aniccā.

<!-- @segment sn35.143:1.9 -->

Yopi hetu, yopi paccayo dhammānaṁ uppādāya, sopi anicco.

<!-- @segment sn35.143:1.10 -->

Aniccasambhūtā, bhikkhave, dhammā kuto niccā bhavissanti.

<!-- @segment sn35.143:1.11 -->

Evaṁ passaṁ …pe…

<!-- @segment sn35.143:1.12 -->

nāparaṁ itthattāyā’ti pajānātī”ti.

<!-- @segment sn35.143:1.13 -->

Dasamaṁ.
