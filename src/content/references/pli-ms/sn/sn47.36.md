<!-- @segment sn47.36:1.1 -->

“Cattārome, bhikkhave, satipaṭṭhānā.

<!-- @segment sn47.36:1.2 -->

Katame cattāro?

<!-- @segment sn47.36:1.3 -->

Idha, bhikkhave, bhikkhu kāye kāyānupassī viharati ātāpī sampajāno satimā, vineyya loke abhijjhādomanassaṁ;

<!-- @segment sn47.36:1.4 -->

vedanāsu …pe…

<!-- @segment sn47.36:1.5 -->

citte …pe…

<!-- @segment sn47.36:1.6 -->

dhammesu dhammānupassī viharati ātāpī sampajāno satimā, vineyya loke abhijjhādomanassaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn47.36:1.7 -->

Ime kho, bhikkhave, cattāro satipaṭṭhānā.

<!-- @segment sn47.36:1.8 -->

Imesaṁ kho, bhikkhave, catunnaṁ satipaṭṭhānānaṁ bhāvitattā bahulīkatattā dvinnaṁ phalānaṁ aññataraṁ phalaṁ pāṭikaṅkhaṁ—

<!-- @segment sn47.36:1.9 -->

diṭṭheva dhamme aññā, sati vā upādisese anāgāmitā”ti.

<!-- @segment sn47.36:1.10 -->

Chaṭṭhaṁ.
