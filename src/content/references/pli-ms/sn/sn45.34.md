<!-- @segment sn45.34:1.1 -->

Sāvatthinidānaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn45.34:1.2 -->

“Aṭṭhime, bhikkhave, dhammā bhāvitā bahulīkatā apārā pāraṁ gamanāya saṁvattanti.

<!-- @segment sn45.34:1.3 -->

Katame aṭṭha?

<!-- @segment sn45.34:1.4 -->

Seyyathidaṁ—sammādiṭṭhi …pe… sammāsamādhi.

<!-- @segment sn45.34:1.5 -->

Ime kho, bhikkhave, aṭṭha dhammā bhāvitā bahulīkatā apārā pāraṁ gamanāya saṁvattantī”ti.

<!-- @segment sn45.34:2.1 -->

Idamavoca bhagavā.

<!-- @segment sn45.34:2.2 -->

Idaṁ vatvāna sugato athāparaṁ etadavoca satthā:

<!-- @segment sn45.34:3.1 -->

“Appakā te manussesu,

<!-- @segment sn45.34:3.2 -->

ye janā pāragāmino;

<!-- @segment sn45.34:3.3 -->

Athāyaṁ itarā pajā,

<!-- @segment sn45.34:3.4 -->

tīramevānudhāvati.

<!-- @segment sn45.34:4.1 -->

Ye ca kho sammadakkhāte,

<!-- @segment sn45.34:4.2 -->

dhamme dhammānuvattino;

<!-- @segment sn45.34:4.3 -->

Te janā pāramessanti,

<!-- @segment sn45.34:4.4 -->

maccudheyyaṁ suduttaraṁ.

<!-- @segment sn45.34:5.1 -->

Kaṇhaṁ dhammaṁ vippahāya,

<!-- @segment sn45.34:5.2 -->

sukkaṁ bhāvetha paṇḍito;

<!-- @segment sn45.34:5.3 -->

Okā anokamāgamma,

<!-- @segment sn45.34:5.4 -->

viveke yattha dūramaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn45.34:6.1 -->

Tatrābhiratimiccheyya,

<!-- @segment sn45.34:6.2 -->

hitvā kāme akiñcano;

<!-- @segment sn45.34:6.3 -->

Pariyodapeyya attānaṁ,

<!-- @segment sn45.34:6.4 -->

cittaklesehi paṇḍito.

<!-- @segment sn45.34:7.1 -->

Yesaṁ sambodhiyaṅgesu,

<!-- @segment sn45.34:7.2 -->

sammā cittaṁ subhāvitaṁ;

<!-- @segment sn45.34:7.3 -->

Ādānapaṭinissagge,

<!-- @segment sn45.34:7.4 -->

anupādāya ye ratā;

<!-- @segment sn45.34:7.5 -->

Khīṇāsavā jutimanto,

<!-- @segment sn45.34:7.6 -->

te loke parinibbutā”ti.

<!-- @segment sn45.34:7.7 -->

Catutthaṁ.
