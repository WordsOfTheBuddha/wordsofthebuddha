<!-- @segment sn22.143:1.1 -->

Sāvatthinidānaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn22.143:1.2 -->

“Yo, bhikkhave, anattā; tatra vo chando pahātabbo.

<!-- @segment sn22.143:1.3 -->

Ko ca, bhikkhave, anattā?

<!-- @segment sn22.143:1.4 -->

Rūpaṁ, bhikkhave, anattā; tatra vo chando pahātabbo.

<!-- @segment sn22.143:1.5 -->

Vedanā anattā …

<!-- @segment sn22.143:1.6 -->

saññā …

<!-- @segment sn22.143:1.7 -->

saṅkhārā …

<!-- @segment sn22.143:1.8 -->

viññāṇaṁ anattā; tatra vo chando pahātabbo.

<!-- @segment sn22.143:1.9 -->

Yo, bhikkhave, anattā; tatra vo chando pahātabbo”ti.

<!-- @segment sn22.143:1.10 -->

Aṭṭhamaṁ.
