<!-- @segment sn10.4:1.1 -->

Ekaṁ samayaṁ bhagavā magadhesu viharati maṇimālike cetiye maṇibhaddassa yakkhassa bhavane.

<!-- @segment sn10.4:1.2 -->

Atha kho maṇibhaddo yakkho yena bhagavā tenupasaṅkami; upasaṅkamitvā bhagavato santike imaṁ gāthaṁ abhāsi:

<!-- @segment sn10.4:2.1 -->

“Satīmato sadā bhaddaṁ,

<!-- @segment sn10.4:2.2 -->

satimā sukhamedhati;

<!-- @segment sn10.4:2.3 -->

Satīmato suve seyyo,

<!-- @segment sn10.4:2.4 -->

verā ca parimuccatī”ti.

<!-- @segment sn10.4:3.1 -->

“Satīmato sadā bhaddaṁ,

<!-- @segment sn10.4:3.2 -->

satimā sukhamedhati;

<!-- @segment sn10.4:3.3 -->

Satīmato suve seyyo,

<!-- @segment sn10.4:3.4 -->

verā na parimuccati.

<!-- @segment sn10.4:4.1 -->

Yassa sabbamahorattaṁ,

<!-- @segment sn10.4:4.2 -->

ahiṁsāya rato mano;

<!-- @segment sn10.4:4.3 -->

Mettaṁso sabbabhūtesu,

<!-- @segment sn10.4:4.4 -->

veraṁ tassa na kenacī”ti.
