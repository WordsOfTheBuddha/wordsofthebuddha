<!-- @segment sn22.153:1.1 -->

Sāvatthinidānaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn22.153:1.2 -->

“Kismiṁ nu kho, bhikkhave, sati, kiṁ upādāya, kiṁ abhinivissa evaṁ diṭṭhi uppajjati:

<!-- @segment sn22.153:1.3 -->

‘no cassaṁ, no ca me siyā, nābhavissa, na me bhavissatī’”ti?

<!-- @segment sn22.153:1.4 -->

Bhagavaṁmūlakā no, bhante, dhammā …pe…

<!-- @segment sn22.153:1.5 -->

“rūpe kho, bhikkhave, sati, rūpaṁ upādāya, rūpaṁ abhinivissa evaṁ diṭṭhi uppajjati:

<!-- @segment sn22.153:1.6 -->

‘no cassaṁ, no ca me siyā, nābhavissa, na me bhavissatī’ti.

<!-- @segment sn22.153:1.7 -->

Vedanāya sati …

<!-- @segment sn22.153:1.8 -->

saññāya sati …

<!-- @segment sn22.153:1.9 -->

saṅkhāresu sati …

<!-- @segment sn22.153:1.10 -->

viññāṇe sati, viññāṇaṁ upādāya, viññāṇaṁ abhinivissa, evaṁ diṭṭhi uppajjati:

<!-- @segment sn22.153:1.11 -->

‘no cassaṁ, no ca me siyā, nābhavissa, na me bhavissatī’ti.

<!-- @segment sn22.153:1.12 -->

Taṁ kiṁ maññatha, bhikkhave,

<!-- @segment sn22.153:1.13 -->

rūpaṁ niccaṁ vā aniccaṁ vā”ti?

<!-- @segment sn22.153:1.14 -->

“Aniccaṁ, bhante”.

<!-- @segment sn22.153:1.15 -->

“Yaṁ panāniccaṁ dukkhaṁ vā taṁ sukhaṁ vā”ti?

<!-- @segment sn22.153:1.16 -->

“Dukkhaṁ, bhante”.

<!-- @segment sn22.153:1.17 -->

“Yaṁ panāniccaṁ dukkhaṁ vipariṇāmadhammaṁ, api nu taṁ anupādāya evaṁ diṭṭhi uppajjeyya:

<!-- @segment sn22.153:1.18 -->

‘no cassaṁ, no ca me siyā, nābhavissa, na me bhavissatī’”ti?

<!-- @segment sn22.153:1.19 -->

“No hetaṁ, bhante”.

<!-- @segment sn22.153:1.20 -->

“Vedanā …

<!-- @segment sn22.153:1.21 -->

saññā …

<!-- @segment sn22.153:1.22 -->

saṅkhārā …

<!-- @segment sn22.153:1.23 -->

viññāṇaṁ niccaṁ vā aniccaṁ vā”ti?

<!-- @segment sn22.153:1.24 -->

“Aniccaṁ, bhante”.

<!-- @segment sn22.153:1.25 -->

“Yaṁ panāniccaṁ dukkhaṁ vā taṁ sukhaṁ vā”ti?

<!-- @segment sn22.153:1.26 -->

“Dukkhaṁ, bhante”.

<!-- @segment sn22.153:1.27 -->

“Yaṁ panāniccaṁ dukkhaṁ vipariṇāmadhammaṁ, api nu taṁ anupādāya evaṁ diṭṭhi uppajjeyya:

<!-- @segment sn22.153:1.28 -->

‘no cassaṁ, no ca me siyā, nābhavissa, na me bhavissatī’”ti?

<!-- @segment sn22.153:1.29 -->

“No hetaṁ, bhante”.

<!-- @segment sn22.153:1.30 -->

“Evaṁ passaṁ …pe…

<!-- @segment sn22.153:1.31 -->

nāparaṁ itthattāyāti pajānātī”ti.

<!-- @segment sn22.153:1.32 -->

Catutthaṁ.
