<!-- @segment sn15.10:1.1 -->

Ekaṁ samayaṁ bhagavā rājagahe viharati gijjhakūṭe pabbate.

<!-- @segment sn15.10:1.2 -->

Tatra kho bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi:

<!-- @segment sn15.10:1.3 -->

“bhikkhavo”ti.

<!-- @segment sn15.10:1.4 -->

“Bhadante”ti te bhikkhū bhagavato paccassosuṁ.

<!-- @segment sn15.10:1.5 -->

Bhagavā etadavoca:

<!-- @segment sn15.10:2.1 -->

“Anamataggoyaṁ, bhikkhave, saṁsāro …pe…

<!-- @segment sn15.10:2.2 -->

ekapuggalassa, bhikkhave, kappaṁ sandhāvato saṁsarato siyā evaṁ mahā aṭṭhikaṅkalo aṭṭhipuñjo aṭṭhirāsi yathāyaṁ vepullo pabbato, sace saṁhārako assa, sambhatañca na vinasseyya.

<!-- @segment sn15.10:2.3 -->

Taṁ kissa hetu?

<!-- @segment sn15.10:2.4 -->

Anamataggoyaṁ, bhikkhave, saṁsāro …pe…

<!-- @segment sn15.10:2.5 -->

alaṁ vimuccitun”ti.

<!-- @segment sn15.10:3.1 -->

Idamavoca bhagavā.

<!-- @segment sn15.10:3.2 -->

Idaṁ vatvāna sugato athāparaṁ etadavoca satthā:

<!-- @segment sn15.10:4.1 -->

“Ekassekena kappena,

<!-- @segment sn15.10:4.2 -->

puggalassaṭṭhisañcayo;

<!-- @segment sn15.10:4.3 -->

Siyā pabbatasamo rāsi,

<!-- @segment sn15.10:4.4 -->

iti vuttaṁ mahesinā.

<!-- @segment sn15.10:5.1 -->

So kho panāyaṁ akkhāto,

<!-- @segment sn15.10:5.2 -->

vepullo pabbato mahā;

<!-- @segment sn15.10:5.3 -->

Uttaro gijjhakūṭassa,

<!-- @segment sn15.10:5.4 -->

magadhānaṁ giribbaje.

<!-- @segment sn15.10:6.1 -->

Yato ca ariyasaccāni,

<!-- @segment sn15.10:6.2 -->

sammappaññāya passati;

<!-- @segment sn15.10:6.3 -->

Dukkhaṁ dukkhasamuppādaṁ,

<!-- @segment sn15.10:6.4 -->

dukkhassa ca atikkamaṁ;

<!-- @segment sn15.10:6.5 -->

Ariyaṁ caṭṭhaṅgikaṁ maggaṁ,

<!-- @segment sn15.10:6.6 -->

dukkhūpasamagāminaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn15.10:7.1 -->

Sa sattakkhattuṁparamaṁ,

<!-- @segment sn15.10:7.2 -->

sandhāvitvāna puggalo;

<!-- @segment sn15.10:7.3 -->

Dukkhassantakaro hoti,

<!-- @segment sn15.10:7.4 -->

sabbasaṁyojanakkhayā”ti.

<!-- @segment sn15.10:7.5 -->

Dasamaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn15.10:7.6 -->

Paṭhamo vaggo.

<!-- @segment sn15.10:8.0 -->

Tassuddānaṁ

<!-- @segment sn15.10:8.1 -->

Tiṇakaṭṭhañca pathavī,

<!-- @segment sn15.10:8.2 -->

assu khīrañca pabbataṁ;

<!-- @segment sn15.10:8.3 -->

Sāsapā sāvakā gaṅgā,

<!-- @segment sn15.10:8.4 -->

daṇḍo ca puggalena cāti.
