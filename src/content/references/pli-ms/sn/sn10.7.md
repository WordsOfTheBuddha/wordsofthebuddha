<!-- @segment sn10.7:1.1 -->

Ekaṁ samayaṁ bhagavā sāvatthiyaṁ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme.

<!-- @segment sn10.7:1.2 -->

Tena kho pana samayena bhagavā bhikkhū nibbānapaṭisaṁyuttāya dhammiyā kathāya sandasseti samādapeti samuttejeti sampahaṁseti.

<!-- @segment sn10.7:1.3 -->

Te ca bhikkhū aṭṭhiṁ katvā manasi katvā sabbacetasā samannāharitvā ohitasotā dhammaṁ suṇanti.

<!-- @segment sn10.7:1.4 -->

Atha kho punabbasumātā yakkhinī puttake evaṁ tosesi:

<!-- @segment sn10.7:2.1 -->

“Tuṇhī uttarike hohi,

<!-- @segment sn10.7:2.2 -->

tuṇhī hohi punabbasu;

<!-- @segment sn10.7:2.3 -->

Yāvāhaṁ buddhaseṭṭhassa,

<!-- @segment sn10.7:2.4 -->

dhammaṁ sossāmi satthuno.

<!-- @segment sn10.7:3.1 -->

Nibbānaṁ bhagavā āha,

<!-- @segment sn10.7:3.2 -->

sabbaganthappamocanaṁ;

<!-- @segment sn10.7:3.3 -->

Ativelā ca me hoti,

<!-- @segment sn10.7:3.4 -->

asmiṁ dhamme piyāyanā.

<!-- @segment sn10.7:4.1 -->

Piyo loke sako putto,

<!-- @segment sn10.7:4.2 -->

piyo loke sako pati;

<!-- @segment sn10.7:4.3 -->

Tato piyatarā mayhaṁ,

<!-- @segment sn10.7:4.4 -->

assa dhammassa magganā.

<!-- @segment sn10.7:5.1 -->

Na hi putto pati vāpi,

<!-- @segment sn10.7:5.2 -->

piyo dukkhā pamocaye;

<!-- @segment sn10.7:5.3 -->

Yathā saddhammassavanaṁ,

<!-- @segment sn10.7:5.4 -->

dukkhā moceti pāṇinaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn10.7:6.1 -->

Loke dukkhaparetasmiṁ,

<!-- @segment sn10.7:6.2 -->

Jarāmaraṇasaṁyute;

<!-- @segment sn10.7:6.3 -->

Jarāmaraṇamokkhāya,

<!-- @segment sn10.7:6.4 -->

Yaṁ dhammaṁ abhisambudhaṁ;

<!-- @segment sn10.7:6.5 -->

Taṁ dhammaṁ sotumicchāmi,

<!-- @segment sn10.7:6.6 -->

Tuṇhī hohi punabbasū”ti.

<!-- @segment sn10.7:7.1 -->

“Ammā na byāharissāmi,

<!-- @segment sn10.7:7.2 -->

tuṇhībhūtāyamuttarā;

<!-- @segment sn10.7:7.3 -->

Dhammameva nisāmehi,

<!-- @segment sn10.7:7.4 -->

saddhammassavanaṁ sukhaṁ;

<!-- @segment sn10.7:7.5 -->

Saddhammassa anaññāya,

<!-- @segment sn10.7:7.6 -->

ammā dukkhaṁ carāmase.

<!-- @segment sn10.7:8.1 -->

Esa devamanussānaṁ,

<!-- @segment sn10.7:8.2 -->

sammūḷhānaṁ pabhaṅkaro;

<!-- @segment sn10.7:8.3 -->

Buddho antimasārīro,

<!-- @segment sn10.7:8.4 -->

dhammaṁ deseti cakkhumā”ti.

<!-- @segment sn10.7:9.1 -->

“Sādhu kho paṇḍito nāma,

<!-- @segment sn10.7:9.2 -->

putto jāto uresayo;

<!-- @segment sn10.7:9.3 -->

Putto me buddhaseṭṭhassa,

<!-- @segment sn10.7:9.4 -->

dhammaṁ suddhaṁ piyāyati.

<!-- @segment sn10.7:10.1 -->

Punabbasu sukhī hohi,

<!-- @segment sn10.7:10.2 -->

ajjāhamhi samuggatā;

<!-- @segment sn10.7:10.3 -->

Diṭṭhāni ariyasaccāni,

<!-- @segment sn10.7:10.4 -->

uttarāpi suṇātu me”ti.
