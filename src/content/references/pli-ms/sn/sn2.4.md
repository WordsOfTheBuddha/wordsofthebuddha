<!-- @segment sn2.4:1.1 -->

Sāvatthinidānaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn2.4:1.2 -->

Ekamantaṁ ṭhito kho māgadho devaputto bhagavantaṁ gāthāya ajjhabhāsi:

<!-- @segment sn2.4:2.1 -->

“Kati lokasmiṁ pajjotā,

<!-- @segment sn2.4:2.2 -->

yehi loko pakāsati;

<!-- @segment sn2.4:2.3 -->

Bhavantaṁ puṭṭhumāgamma,

<!-- @segment sn2.4:2.4 -->

kathaṁ jānemu taṁ mayan”ti.

<!-- @segment sn2.4:3.1 -->

“Cattāro loke pajjotā,

<!-- @segment sn2.4:3.2 -->

pañcamettha na vijjati;

<!-- @segment sn2.4:3.3 -->

Divā tapati ādicco,

<!-- @segment sn2.4:3.4 -->

rattimābhāti candimā.

<!-- @segment sn2.4:4.1 -->

Atha aggi divārattiṁ,

<!-- @segment sn2.4:4.2 -->

tattha tattha pakāsati;

<!-- @segment sn2.4:4.3 -->

Sambuddho tapataṁ seṭṭho,

<!-- @segment sn2.4:4.4 -->

esā ābhā anuttarā”ti.
