<!-- @segment sn25.7:1.1 -->

Sāvatthinidānaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn25.7:1.2 -->

“Rūpasañcetanā, bhikkhave, aniccā vipariṇāmī aññathābhāvī;

<!-- @segment sn25.7:1.3 -->

saddasañcetanā …

<!-- @segment sn25.7:1.4 -->

gandhasañcetanā …

<!-- @segment sn25.7:1.5 -->

rasasañcetanā …

<!-- @segment sn25.7:1.6 -->

phoṭṭhabbasañcetanā …

<!-- @segment sn25.7:1.7 -->

dhammasañcetanā aniccā vipariṇāmī aññathābhāvī.

<!-- @segment sn25.7:1.8 -->

Yo, bhikkhave, ime dhamme evaṁ saddahati adhimuccati, ayaṁ vuccati ‘saddhānusārī …pe…

<!-- @segment sn25.7:1.9 -->

sambodhiparāyano’”ti.

<!-- @segment sn25.7:1.10 -->

Sattamaṁ.
