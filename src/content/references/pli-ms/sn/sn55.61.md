<!-- @segment sn55.61:1.1 -->

“…. Paññāvepullāya … saṁvattantī”ti.

<!-- @segment sn55.61:1.2 -->

Ekādasamaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn55.61:1.3 -->

Sappaññavaggo chaṭṭho.

<!-- @segment sn55.61:2.0 -->

Tassuddānaṁ

<!-- @segment sn55.61:2.1 -->

Sagāthakaṁ vassaṁvutthaṁ,

<!-- @segment sn55.61:2.2 -->

dhammadinnañca gilānaṁ;

<!-- @segment sn55.61:2.3 -->

Caturo phalā paṭilābho,

<!-- @segment sn55.61:2.4 -->

vuddhi vepullatāya cāti.
