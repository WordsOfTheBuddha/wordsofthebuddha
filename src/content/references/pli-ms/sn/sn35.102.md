<!-- @segment sn35.102:1.1 -->

“Yaṁ, bhikkhave, na tumhākaṁ taṁ pajahatha.

<!-- @segment sn35.102:1.2 -->

Taṁ vo pahīnaṁ hitāya sukhāya bhavissati.

<!-- @segment sn35.102:1.3 -->

Kiñca, bhikkhave, na tumhākaṁ?

<!-- @segment sn35.102:1.4 -->

Cakkhu, bhikkhave, na tumhākaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn35.102:1.5 -->

Taṁ pajahatha.

<!-- @segment sn35.102:1.6 -->

Taṁ vo pahīnaṁ hitāya sukhāya bhavissati.

<!-- @segment sn35.102:1.7 -->

Rūpā na tumhākaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn35.102:1.8 -->

Te pajahatha.

<!-- @segment sn35.102:1.9 -->

Te vo pahīnā hitāya sukhāya bhavissanti.

<!-- @segment sn35.102:1.10 -->

Cakkhuviññāṇaṁ na tumhākaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn35.102:1.11 -->

Taṁ pajahatha.

<!-- @segment sn35.102:1.12 -->

Taṁ vo pahīnaṁ hitāya sukhāya bhavissati.

<!-- @segment sn35.102:1.13 -->

Cakkhusamphasso na tumhākaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn35.102:1.14 -->

Taṁ pajahatha.

<!-- @segment sn35.102:1.15 -->

So vo pahīno hitāya sukhāya bhavissati …pe…

<!-- @segment sn35.102:1.16 -->

yampidaṁ manosamphassapaccayā uppajjati vedayitaṁ sukhaṁ vā dukkhaṁ vā adukkhamasukhaṁ vā tampi na tumhākaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn35.102:1.17 -->

Taṁ pajahatha.

<!-- @segment sn35.102:1.18 -->

Taṁ vo pahīnaṁ hitāya sukhāya bhavissati.

<!-- @segment sn35.102:1.19 -->

Yampi, bhikkhave, na tumhākaṁ, taṁ pajahatha.

<!-- @segment sn35.102:1.20 -->

Taṁ vo pahīnaṁ hitāya sukhāya bhavissatī”ti.

<!-- @segment sn35.102:1.21 -->

Navamaṁ.
