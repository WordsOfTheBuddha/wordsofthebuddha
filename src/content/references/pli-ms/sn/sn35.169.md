<!-- @segment sn35.169:1.1 -->

“Yaṁ, bhikkhave, aniccaṁ, tatra vo rāgo pahātabbo.

<!-- @segment sn35.169:1.2 -->

Kiñca, bhikkhave, aniccaṁ?

<!-- @segment sn35.169:1.3 -->

Cakkhu, bhikkhave, aniccaṁ;

<!-- @segment sn35.169:1.4 -->

tatra vo rāgo pahātabbo …pe…

<!-- @segment sn35.169:1.5 -->

jivhā aniccā;

<!-- @segment sn35.169:1.6 -->

tatra vo rāgo pahātabbo …pe…

<!-- @segment sn35.169:1.7 -->

mano anicco;

<!-- @segment sn35.169:1.8 -->

tatra vo rāgo pahātabbo.

<!-- @segment sn35.169:1.9 -->

Yaṁ, bhikkhave, aniccaṁ, tatra vo rāgo pahātabbo”ti.
