<!-- @segment sn11.25:1.1 -->

Evaṁ me sutaṁ—

<!-- @segment sn11.25:1.2 -->

ekaṁ samayaṁ bhagavā sāvatthiyaṁ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme.

<!-- @segment sn11.25:1.3 -->

Tatra kho bhagavā bhikkhū …pe… bhagavā etadavoca:

<!-- @segment sn11.25:1.4 -->

“bhūtapubbaṁ, bhikkhave, sakko devānamindo sudhammāyaṁ sabhāyaṁ deve tāvatiṁse anunayamāno tāyaṁ velāyaṁ imaṁ gāthaṁ abhāsi:

<!-- @segment sn11.25:2.1 -->

‘Mā vo kodho ajjhabhavi,

<!-- @segment sn11.25:2.2 -->

mā ca kujjhittha kujjhataṁ;

<!-- @segment sn11.25:2.3 -->

Akkodho avihiṁsā ca,

<!-- @segment sn11.25:2.4 -->

ariyesu ca paṭipadā;

<!-- @segment sn11.25:2.5 -->

Atha pāpajanaṁ kodho,

<!-- @segment sn11.25:2.6 -->

pabbatovābhimaddatī’”ti.

<!-- @segment sn11.25:2.7 -->

Tatiyo vaggo.

<!-- @segment sn11.25:3.0 -->

Tassuddānaṁ

<!-- @segment sn11.25:3.1 -->

Chetvā dubbaṇṇiya māyā,

<!-- @segment sn11.25:3.2 -->

accayena akodhano;

<!-- @segment sn11.25:3.3 -->

Desitaṁ buddhaseṭṭhena,

<!-- @segment sn11.25:3.4 -->

idañhi sakkapañcakanti.

<!-- @segment sn11.25:3.5 -->

Sakkasaṁyuttaṁ samattaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn11.25:3.6 -->

Sagāthāvaggo paṭhamo.

<!-- @segment sn11.25:4.0 -->

Tassuddānaṁ

<!-- @segment sn11.25:4.1 -->

Devatā devaputto ca,

<!-- @segment sn11.25:4.2 -->

rājā māro ca bhikkhunī;

<!-- @segment sn11.25:4.3 -->

Brahmā brāhmaṇa vaṅgīso,

<!-- @segment sn11.25:4.4 -->

vanayakkhena vāsavoti.

<!-- @segment sn11.25:4.5 -->

Sagāthāvaggasaṁyuttapāḷi niṭṭhitā.
