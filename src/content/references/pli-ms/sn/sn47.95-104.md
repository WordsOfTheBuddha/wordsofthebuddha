<!-- @segment sn47.95-104:1.1 -->

“Pañcimāni, bhikkhave, uddhambhāgiyāni saṁyojanāni.

<!-- @segment sn47.95-104:1.2 -->

Katamāni pañca?

<!-- @segment sn47.95-104:1.3 -->

Rūparāgo, arūparāgo, māno, uddhaccaṁ, avijjā—

<!-- @segment sn47.95-104:1.4 -->

imāni kho, bhikkhave, pañcuddhambhāgiyāni saṁyojanāni.

<!-- @segment sn47.95-104:1.5 -->

Imesaṁ kho, bhikkhave, pañcannaṁ uddhambhāgiyānaṁ saṁyojanānaṁ abhiññāya pariññāya parikkhayāya pahānāya cattāro satipaṭṭhānā bhāvetabbā.

<!-- @segment sn47.95-104:2.1 -->

Katame cattāro?

<!-- @segment sn47.95-104:2.2 -->

Idha, bhikkhave, bhikkhu kāye kāyānupassī viharati ātāpī sampajāno satimā, vineyya loke abhijjhādomanassaṁ;

<!-- @segment sn47.95-104:2.3 -->

vedanāsu …pe…

<!-- @segment sn47.95-104:2.4 -->

citte …pe…

<!-- @segment sn47.95-104:2.5 -->

dhammesu dhammānupassī viharati ātāpī sampajāno satimā, vineyya loke abhijjhādomanassaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn47.95-104:2.6 -->

Imesaṁ kho, bhikkhave, pañcannaṁ uddhambhāgiyānaṁ saṁyojanānaṁ abhiññāya pariññāya parikkhayāya pahānāya ime cattāro satipaṭṭhānā bhāvetabbā”ti.

<!-- @segment sn47.95-104:2.7 -->

Dasamaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn47.95-104:3.1 -->

(Yathā maggasaṁyuttaṁ tathā satipaṭṭhānasaṁyuttaṁ vitthāretabbaṁ.)

<!-- @segment sn47.95-104:3.2 -->

Oghavaggo dasamo.

<!-- @segment sn47.95-104:4.0 -->

Tassuddānaṁ

<!-- @segment sn47.95-104:4.1 -->

Ogho yogo upādānaṁ,

<!-- @segment sn47.95-104:4.2 -->

ganthā anusayena ca;

<!-- @segment sn47.95-104:4.3 -->

Kāmaguṇā nīvaraṇā,

<!-- @segment sn47.95-104:4.4 -->

khandhā oruddhambhāgiyāti.

<!-- @segment sn47.95-104:4.5 -->

Satipaṭṭhānasaṁyuttaṁ tatiyaṁ.
