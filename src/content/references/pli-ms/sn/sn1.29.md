<!-- @segment sn1.29:1.1 -->

“Catucakkaṁ navadvāraṁ,

<!-- @segment sn1.29:1.2 -->

puṇṇaṁ lobhena saṁyutaṁ;

<!-- @segment sn1.29:1.3 -->

Paṅkajātaṁ mahāvīra,

<!-- @segment sn1.29:1.4 -->

kathaṁ yātrā bhavissatī”ti.

<!-- @segment sn1.29:2.1 -->

“Chetvā naddhiṁ varattañca,

<!-- @segment sn1.29:2.2 -->

icchā lobhañca pāpakaṁ;

<!-- @segment sn1.29:2.3 -->

Samūlaṁ taṇhamabbuyha,

<!-- @segment sn1.29:2.4 -->

evaṁ yātrā bhavissatī”ti.
