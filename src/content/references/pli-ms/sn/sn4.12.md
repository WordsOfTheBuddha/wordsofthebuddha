<!-- @segment sn4.12:1.1 -->

Ekaṁ samayaṁ bhagavā sāvatthiyaṁ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme.

<!-- @segment sn4.12:1.2 -->

Tena kho pana samayena bhagavā mahatiyā parisāya parivuto dhammaṁ deseti.

<!-- @segment sn4.12:2.1 -->

Atha kho mārassa pāpimato etadahosi:

<!-- @segment sn4.12:2.2 -->

“ayaṁ kho samaṇo gotamo mahatiyā parisāya parivuto dhammaṁ deseti.

<!-- @segment sn4.12:2.3 -->

Yannūnāhaṁ yena samaṇo gotamo tenupasaṅkameyyaṁ vicakkhukammāyā”ti.

<!-- @segment sn4.12:2.4 -->

Atha kho māro pāpimā yena bhagavā tenupasaṅkami; upasaṅkamitvā bhagavantaṁ gāthāya ajjhabhāsi:

<!-- @segment sn4.12:3.1 -->

“Kinnu sīhova nadasi,

<!-- @segment sn4.12:3.2 -->

parisāyaṁ visārado;

<!-- @segment sn4.12:3.3 -->

Paṭimallo hi te atthi,

<!-- @segment sn4.12:3.4 -->

vijitāvī nu maññasī”ti.

<!-- @segment sn4.12:4.1 -->

“Nadanti ve mahāvīrā,

<!-- @segment sn4.12:4.2 -->

parisāsu visāradā;

<!-- @segment sn4.12:4.3 -->

Tathāgatā balappattā,

<!-- @segment sn4.12:4.4 -->

tiṇṇā loke visattikan”ti.

<!-- @segment sn4.12:5.1 -->

Atha kho māro pāpimā “jānāti maṁ bhagavā, jānāti maṁ sugato”ti dukkhī dummano tatthevantaradhāyīti.
