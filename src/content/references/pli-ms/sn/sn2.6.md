<!-- @segment sn2.6:1.1 -->

Sāvatthinidānaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn2.6:1.2 -->

Ekamantaṁ ṭhito kho kāmado devaputto bhagavantaṁ etadavoca:

<!-- @segment sn2.6:1.3 -->

“dukkaraṁ, bhagavā, sudukkaraṁ, bhagavā”ti.

<!-- @segment sn2.6:2.1 -->

“Dukkaraṁ vāpi karonti,

<!-- @segment sn2.6:2.2 -->

(kāmadāti bhagavā)

<!-- @segment sn2.6:2.3 -->

Sekhā sīlasamāhitā;

<!-- @segment sn2.6:2.4 -->

Ṭhitattā anagāriyupetassa,

<!-- @segment sn2.6:2.5 -->

Tuṭṭhi hoti sukhāvahā”ti.

<!-- @segment sn2.6:3.1 -->

“Dullabhā, bhagavā, yadidaṁ tuṭṭhī”ti.

<!-- @segment sn2.6:4.1 -->

“Dullabhaṁ vāpi labhanti,

<!-- @segment sn2.6:4.2 -->

(kāmadāti bhagavā)

<!-- @segment sn2.6:4.3 -->

Cittavūpasame ratā;

<!-- @segment sn2.6:4.4 -->

Yesaṁ divā ca ratto ca,

<!-- @segment sn2.6:4.5 -->

Bhāvanāya rato mano”ti.

<!-- @segment sn2.6:5.1 -->

“Dussamādahaṁ, bhagavā, yadidaṁ cittan”ti.

<!-- @segment sn2.6:6.1 -->

“Dussamādahaṁ vāpi samādahanti,

<!-- @segment sn2.6:6.2 -->

(kāmadāti bhagavā)

<!-- @segment sn2.6:6.3 -->

Indriyūpasame ratā;

<!-- @segment sn2.6:6.4 -->

Te chetvā maccuno jālaṁ,

<!-- @segment sn2.6:6.5 -->

Ariyā gacchanti kāmadā”ti.

<!-- @segment sn2.6:7.1 -->

“Duggamo, bhagavā, visamo maggo”ti.

<!-- @segment sn2.6:8.1 -->

“Duggame visame vāpi,

<!-- @segment sn2.6:8.2 -->

Ariyā gacchanti kāmada;

<!-- @segment sn2.6:8.3 -->

Anariyā visame magge,

<!-- @segment sn2.6:8.4 -->

Papatanti avaṁsirā;

<!-- @segment sn2.6:8.5 -->

Ariyānaṁ samo maggo,

<!-- @segment sn2.6:8.6 -->

Ariyā hi visame samā”ti.
