<!-- @segment sn55.15:1.1 -->

“Catūhi, bhikkhave, dhammehi samannāgato ariyasāvako sabbaduggativinipātabhayaṁ samatikkanto hoti.

<!-- @segment sn55.15:1.2 -->

Katamehi catūhi?

<!-- @segment sn55.15:1.3 -->

Idha, bhikkhave, ariyasāvako buddhe aveccappasādena samannāgato hoti—

<!-- @segment sn55.15:1.4 -->

itipi so bhagavā …pe… satthā devamanussānaṁ buddho bhagavāti.

<!-- @segment sn55.15:1.5 -->

Dhamme …pe…

<!-- @segment sn55.15:1.6 -->

saṅghe …pe…

<!-- @segment sn55.15:1.7 -->

ariyakantehi sīlehi samannāgato hoti akhaṇḍehi …pe… samādhisaṁvattanikehi.

<!-- @segment sn55.15:1.8 -->

Imehi kho, bhikkhave, catūhi dhammehi samannāgato ariyasāvako sabbaduggativinipātabhayaṁ samatikkanto hotī”ti.

<!-- @segment sn55.15:1.9 -->

Pañcamaṁ.
