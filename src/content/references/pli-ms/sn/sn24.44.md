<!-- @segment sn24.44:1.1 -->

“Adukkhamasukhī attā hoti arogo paraṁ maraṇā”ti?

<!-- @segment sn24.44:1.2 -->

“Bhagavaṁmūlakā no, bhante, dhammā …pe…

<!-- @segment sn24.44:2.1 -->

“Rūpe kho, bhikkhave, sati, rūpaṁ upādāya, rūpaṁ abhinivissa evaṁ diṭṭhi uppajjati:

<!-- @segment sn24.44:2.2 -->

‘adukkhamasukhī attā hoti arogo paraṁ maraṇā’ti.

<!-- @segment sn24.44:2.3 -->

Vedanāya sati …

<!-- @segment sn24.44:2.4 -->

saññāya sati …

<!-- @segment sn24.44:2.5 -->

saṅkhāresu sati …

<!-- @segment sn24.44:2.6 -->

viññāṇe sati, viññāṇaṁ upādāya, viññāṇaṁ abhinivissa evaṁ diṭṭhi uppajjati:

<!-- @segment sn24.44:2.7 -->

‘adukkhamasukhī attā hoti arogo paraṁ maraṇā’ti.

<!-- @segment sn24.44:3.1 -->

Taṁ kiṁ maññatha, bhikkhave, rūpaṁ niccaṁ vā aniccaṁ vā”ti?

<!-- @segment sn24.44:3.2 -->

“Aniccaṁ, bhante” …pe…

<!-- @segment sn24.44:3.3 -->

vipariṇāmadhammaṁ, api nu taṁ anupādāya evaṁ diṭṭhi uppajjeyya:

<!-- @segment sn24.44:3.4 -->

‘adukkhamasukhī attā hoti arogo paraṁ maraṇā’”ti?

<!-- @segment sn24.44:3.5 -->

“No hetaṁ, bhante”.

<!-- @segment sn24.44:3.6 -->

“Iti kho, bhikkhave, dukkhe sati, dukkhaṁ upādāya, dukkhaṁ abhinivissa evaṁ diṭṭhi uppajjati:

<!-- @segment sn24.44:3.7 -->

‘adukkhamasukhī attā hoti arogo paraṁ maraṇā’”ti.

<!-- @segment sn24.44:3.8 -->

“Vedanā …

<!-- @segment sn24.44:3.9 -->

saññā …

<!-- @segment sn24.44:3.10 -->

saṅkhārā …

<!-- @segment sn24.44:3.11 -->

viññāṇaṁ niccaṁ vā aniccaṁ vā”ti?

<!-- @segment sn24.44:3.12 -->

“Aniccaṁ, bhante” …pe…

<!-- @segment sn24.44:3.13 -->

vipariṇāmadhammaṁ, api nu taṁ anupādāya evaṁ diṭṭhi uppajjeyya:

<!-- @segment sn24.44:3.14 -->

‘adukkhamasukhī attā hoti arogo paraṁ maraṇā’”ti?

<!-- @segment sn24.44:3.15 -->

“No hetaṁ, bhante”.

<!-- @segment sn24.44:3.16 -->

“Iti kho, bhikkhave, dukkhe sati, dukkhaṁ upādāya, dukkhaṁ abhinivissa evaṁ diṭṭhi uppajjati:

<!-- @segment sn24.44:3.17 -->

‘adukkhamasukhī attā hoti arogo paraṁ maraṇā’”ti.

<!-- @segment sn24.44:3.18 -->

Chabbīsatimaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn24.44:3.19 -->

Dutiyapeyyālo.

<!-- @segment sn24.44:4.0 -->

Tassuddānaṁ

<!-- @segment sn24.44:4.1 -->

Vātaṁ etaṁ mama so,

<!-- @segment sn24.44:4.2 -->

attā no ca me siyā;

<!-- @segment sn24.44:4.3 -->

Natthi karoto hetu ca,

<!-- @segment sn24.44:4.4 -->

mahādiṭṭhena aṭṭhamaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn24.44:5.1 -->

Sassato asassato ceva,

<!-- @segment sn24.44:5.2 -->

antānantavā ca vuccati;

<!-- @segment sn24.44:5.3 -->

Taṁ jīvaṁ aññaṁ jīvañca,

<!-- @segment sn24.44:5.4 -->

tathāgatena cattāro.

<!-- @segment sn24.44:6.1 -->

Rūpī attā hoti arūpī ca attā hoti,

<!-- @segment sn24.44:6.2 -->

Rūpī ca arūpī ca attā hoti;

<!-- @segment sn24.44:6.3 -->

Neva rūpī nārūpī attā hoti,

<!-- @segment sn24.44:6.4 -->

Ekantasukhī attā hoti.

<!-- @segment sn24.44:7.1 -->

Ekantadukkhī attā hoti,

<!-- @segment sn24.44:7.2 -->

Sukhadukkhī attā hoti;

<!-- @segment sn24.44:7.3 -->

Adukkhamasukhī attā hoti,

<!-- @segment sn24.44:7.4 -->

Arogo paraṁ maraṇāti;

<!-- @segment sn24.44:7.5 -->

Ime chabbīsati suttā,

<!-- @segment sn24.44:7.6 -->

Dutiyavārena desitā.
