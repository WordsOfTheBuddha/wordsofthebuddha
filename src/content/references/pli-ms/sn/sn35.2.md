<!-- @segment sn35.2:1.1 -->

“Cakkhuṁ, bhikkhave, dukkhaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn35.2:1.2 -->

Yaṁ dukkhaṁ tadanattā;

<!-- @segment sn35.2:1.3 -->

yadanattā taṁ ‘netaṁ mama, nesohamasmi, na meso attā’ti evametaṁ yathābhūtaṁ sammappaññāya daṭṭhabbaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn35.2:1.4 -->

Sotaṁ dukkhaṁ …pe…

<!-- @segment sn35.2:1.5 -->

ghānaṁ dukkhaṁ …

<!-- @segment sn35.2:1.6 -->

jivhā dukkhā …

<!-- @segment sn35.2:1.7 -->

kāyo dukkho …

<!-- @segment sn35.2:1.8 -->

mano dukkho.

<!-- @segment sn35.2:1.9 -->

Yaṁ dukkhaṁ tadanattā;

<!-- @segment sn35.2:1.10 -->

yadanattā taṁ ‘netaṁ mama, nesohamasmi, na meso attā’ti evametaṁ yathābhūtaṁ sammappaññāya daṭṭhabbaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn35.2:1.11 -->

Evaṁ passaṁ …pe…

<!-- @segment sn35.2:1.12 -->

nāparaṁ itthattāyāti pajānātī”ti.

<!-- @segment sn35.2:1.13 -->

Dutiyaṁ.
