<!-- @segment sn1.10:1.1 -->

Sāvatthinidānaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn1.10:1.2 -->

Ekamantaṁ ṭhitā kho sā devatā bhagavantaṁ gāthāya ajjhabhāsi:

<!-- @segment sn1.10:2.1 -->

“Araññe viharantānaṁ,

<!-- @segment sn1.10:2.2 -->

santānaṁ brahmacārinaṁ;

<!-- @segment sn1.10:2.3 -->

Ekabhattaṁ bhuñjamānānaṁ,

<!-- @segment sn1.10:2.4 -->

kena vaṇṇo pasīdatī”ti.

<!-- @segment sn1.10:3.1 -->

“Atītaṁ nānusocanti,

<!-- @segment sn1.10:3.2 -->

nappajappanti nāgataṁ;

<!-- @segment sn1.10:3.3 -->

Paccuppannena yāpenti,

<!-- @segment sn1.10:3.4 -->

tena vaṇṇo pasīdati.

<!-- @segment sn1.10:4.1 -->

Anāgatappajappāya,

<!-- @segment sn1.10:4.2 -->

atītassānusocanā;

<!-- @segment sn1.10:4.3 -->

Etena bālā sussanti,

<!-- @segment sn1.10:4.4 -->

naḷova harito luto”ti.

<!-- @segment sn1.10:4.5 -->

Naḷavaggo paṭhamo.

<!-- @segment sn1.10:5.0 -->

Tassuddānaṁ

<!-- @segment sn1.10:5.1 -->

Oghaṁ nimokkhaṁ upaneyyaṁ,

<!-- @segment sn1.10:5.2 -->

Accenti katichindi ca;

<!-- @segment sn1.10:5.3 -->

Jāgaraṁ appaṭividitā,

<!-- @segment sn1.10:5.4 -->

Susammuṭṭhā mānakāminā;

<!-- @segment sn1.10:5.5 -->

Araññe dasamo vutto,

<!-- @segment sn1.10:5.6 -->

Vaggo tena pavuccati.
