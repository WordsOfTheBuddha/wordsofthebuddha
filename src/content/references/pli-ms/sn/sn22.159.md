<!-- @segment sn22.159:1.1 -->

Sāvatthinidānaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn22.159:1.2 -->

Atha kho āyasmā ānando yena bhagavā tenupasaṅkami; upasaṅkamitvā …pe… bhagavantaṁ etadavoca:

<!-- @segment sn22.159:1.3 -->

“sādhu me, bhante, bhagavā saṅkhittena dhammaṁ desetu, yamahaṁ bhagavato dhammaṁ sutvā eko vūpakaṭṭho appamatto ātāpī pahitatto vihareyyan”ti.

<!-- @segment sn22.159:2.1 -->

“Taṁ kiṁ maññasi, ānanda,

<!-- @segment sn22.159:2.2 -->

rūpaṁ niccaṁ vā aniccaṁ vā”ti?

<!-- @segment sn22.159:2.3 -->

“Aniccaṁ, bhante”.

<!-- @segment sn22.159:2.4 -->

“Yaṁ panāniccaṁ dukkhaṁ vā taṁ sukhaṁ vā”ti?

<!-- @segment sn22.159:2.5 -->

“Dukkhaṁ, bhante”.

<!-- @segment sn22.159:2.6 -->

“Yaṁ panāniccaṁ dukkhaṁ vipariṇāmadhammaṁ, kallaṁ nu taṁ samanupassituṁ:

<!-- @segment sn22.159:2.7 -->

‘etaṁ mama, esohamasmi, eso me attā’”ti?

<!-- @segment sn22.159:2.8 -->

“No hetaṁ, bhante”.

<!-- @segment sn22.159:2.9 -->

“Vedanā …

<!-- @segment sn22.159:2.10 -->

saññā …

<!-- @segment sn22.159:2.11 -->

saṅkhārā …

<!-- @segment sn22.159:2.12 -->

viññāṇaṁ niccaṁ vā aniccaṁ vā”ti?

<!-- @segment sn22.159:2.13 -->

“Aniccaṁ, bhante”.

<!-- @segment sn22.159:2.14 -->

“Yaṁ panāniccaṁ dukkhaṁ vā taṁ sukhaṁ vā”ti?

<!-- @segment sn22.159:2.15 -->

“Dukkhaṁ, bhante”.

<!-- @segment sn22.159:2.16 -->

“Yaṁ panāniccaṁ dukkhaṁ vipariṇāmadhammaṁ, kallaṁ nu taṁ samanupassituṁ:

<!-- @segment sn22.159:2.17 -->

‘etaṁ mama, esohamasmi, eso me attā’”ti?

<!-- @segment sn22.159:2.18 -->

“No hetaṁ, bhante”.

<!-- @segment sn22.159:2.19 -->

“Evaṁ passaṁ …pe…

<!-- @segment sn22.159:2.20 -->

nāparaṁ itthattāyāti pajānātī”ti.

<!-- @segment sn22.159:2.21 -->

Dasamaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn22.159:2.22 -->

Diṭṭhivaggo pañcamo.

<!-- @segment sn22.159:3.0 -->

Tassuddānaṁ

<!-- @segment sn22.159:3.1 -->

Ajjhattikaṁ etaṁmama,

<!-- @segment sn22.159:3.2 -->

Soattā nocamesiyā;

<!-- @segment sn22.159:3.3 -->

Micchāsakkāyattānu dve,

<!-- @segment sn22.159:3.4 -->

Abhinivesā ānandenāti.

<!-- @segment sn22.159:3.5 -->

Uparipaṇṇāsako samatto.

<!-- @segment sn22.159:4.0 -->

Tassa uparipaṇṇāsakassa vagguddānaṁ

<!-- @segment sn22.159:4.1 -->

Anto dhammakathikā vijjā,

<!-- @segment sn22.159:4.2 -->

kukkuḷaṁ diṭṭhipañcamaṁ;

<!-- @segment sn22.159:4.3 -->

Tatiyo paṇṇāsako vutto,

<!-- @segment sn22.159:4.4 -->

nipātoti pavuccatīti.

<!-- @segment sn22.159:4.5 -->

Khandhasaṁyuttaṁ samattaṁ.
