<!-- @segment sn22.121:1.1 -->

Sāvatthinidānaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn22.121:1.2 -->

“Upādāniye ca, bhikkhave, dhamme desessāmi upādānañca.

<!-- @segment sn22.121:1.3 -->

Taṁ suṇātha.

<!-- @segment sn22.121:1.4 -->

Katame ca, bhikkhave, upādāniyā dhammā, katamaṁ upādānaṁ?

<!-- @segment sn22.121:1.5 -->

Rūpaṁ, bhikkhave, upādāniyo dhammo,

<!-- @segment sn22.121:1.6 -->

yo tattha chandarāgo, taṁ tattha upādānaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn22.121:1.7 -->

Vedanā …pe…

<!-- @segment sn22.121:1.8 -->

saññā …

<!-- @segment sn22.121:1.9 -->

saṅkhārā …

<!-- @segment sn22.121:1.10 -->

viññāṇaṁ upādāniyo dhammo;

<!-- @segment sn22.121:1.11 -->

yo tattha chandarāgo, taṁ tattha upādānaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn22.121:1.12 -->

Ime vuccanti, bhikkhave, upādāniyā dhammā, idaṁ upādānan”ti.

<!-- @segment sn22.121:1.13 -->

Navamaṁ.
