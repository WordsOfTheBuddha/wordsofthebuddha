<!-- @segment sn35.204:1.1 -->

“Cakkhu, bhikkhave, aniccaṁ atītaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn35.204:1.2 -->

Yadaniccaṁ, taṁ dukkhaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn35.204:1.3 -->

Yaṁ dukkhaṁ, tadanattā.

<!-- @segment sn35.204:1.4 -->

Yadanattā, taṁ ‘netaṁ mama, nesohamasmi, na meso attā’ti evametaṁ yathābhūtaṁ sammappaññāya daṭṭhabbaṁ …pe…

<!-- @segment sn35.204:1.5 -->

jivhā aniccā atītā.

<!-- @segment sn35.204:1.6 -->

Yadaniccaṁ, taṁ dukkhaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn35.204:1.7 -->

Yaṁ dukkhaṁ, tadanattā.

<!-- @segment sn35.204:1.8 -->

Yadanattā, taṁ ‘netaṁ mama, nesohamasmi, na meso attā’ti evametaṁ yathābhūtaṁ sammappaññāya daṭṭhabbaṁ …pe…

<!-- @segment sn35.204:1.9 -->

mano anicco atīto.

<!-- @segment sn35.204:1.10 -->

Yadaniccaṁ, taṁ dukkhaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn35.204:1.11 -->

Yaṁ dukkhaṁ, tadanattā.

<!-- @segment sn35.204:1.12 -->

Yadanattā, taṁ ‘netaṁ mama, nesohamasmi, na meso attā’ti evametaṁ yathābhūtaṁ sammappaññāya daṭṭhabbaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn35.204:1.13 -->

Evaṁ passaṁ …pe…

<!-- @segment sn35.204:1.14 -->

nāparaṁ itthattāyāti pajānātī”ti.
