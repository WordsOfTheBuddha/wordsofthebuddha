<!-- @segment sn46.66:1.1 -->

“Ānāpānassati, bhikkhave, bhāvitā …pe…”

<!-- @segment sn46.66:1.2 -->

dasamaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn46.66:1.3 -->

Ānāpānavaggo sattamo.

<!-- @segment sn46.66:2.0 -->

Tassuddānaṁ

<!-- @segment sn46.66:2.1 -->

Aṭṭhikapuḷavakaṁ vinīlakaṁ,

<!-- @segment sn46.66:2.2 -->

Vicchiddakaṁ uddhumātena pañcamaṁ;

<!-- @segment sn46.66:2.3 -->

Mettā karuṇā muditā,

<!-- @segment sn46.66:2.4 -->

Upekkhā ānāpānena te dasāti.
