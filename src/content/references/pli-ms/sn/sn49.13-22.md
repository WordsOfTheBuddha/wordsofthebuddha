<!-- @segment sn49.13-22:1.1 -->

(Appamādavaggo sammappadhānavasena vitthāretabbo.)

<!-- @segment sn49.13-22:2.0 -->

Tassuddānaṁ

<!-- @segment sn49.13-22:2.1 -->

Tathāgataṁ padaṁ kūṭaṁ,

<!-- @segment sn49.13-22:2.2 -->

mūlaṁ sārena vassikaṁ;

<!-- @segment sn49.13-22:2.3 -->

Rājā candimasūriyā,

<!-- @segment sn49.13-22:2.4 -->

vatthena dasamaṁ padanti.
