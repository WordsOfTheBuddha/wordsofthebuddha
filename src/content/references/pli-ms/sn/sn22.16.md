<!-- @segment sn22.16:1.1 -->

Sāvatthinidānaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn22.16:1.2 -->

“Rūpaṁ, bhikkhave, dukkhaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn22.16:1.3 -->

Yaṁ dukkhaṁ tadanattā;

<!-- @segment sn22.16:1.4 -->

yadanattā taṁ ‘netaṁ mama, nesohamasmi, na meso attā’ti evametaṁ yathābhūtaṁ sammappaññāya daṭṭhabbaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn22.16:1.5 -->

Vedanā dukkhā …

<!-- @segment sn22.16:1.6 -->

saññā dukkhā …

<!-- @segment sn22.16:1.7 -->

saṅkhārā dukkhā …

<!-- @segment sn22.16:1.8 -->

viññāṇaṁ dukkhaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn22.16:1.9 -->

Yaṁ dukkhaṁ tadanattā;

<!-- @segment sn22.16:1.10 -->

yadanattā taṁ ‘netaṁ mama, nesohamasmi, na meso attā’ti evametaṁ yathābhūtaṁ sammappaññāya daṭṭhabbaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn22.16:1.11 -->

Evaṁ passaṁ …pe…

<!-- @segment sn22.16:1.12 -->

nāparaṁ itthattāyā’ti pajānātī”ti.

<!-- @segment sn22.16:1.13 -->

Pañcamaṁ.
