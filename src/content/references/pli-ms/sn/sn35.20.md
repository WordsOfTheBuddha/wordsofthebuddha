<!-- @segment sn35.20:1.1 -->

“Yo, bhikkhave, rūpe abhinandati, dukkhaṁ so abhinandati.

<!-- @segment sn35.20:1.2 -->

Yo dukkhaṁ abhinandati, ‘aparimutto so dukkhasmā’ti vadāmi.

<!-- @segment sn35.20:1.3 -->

Yo sadde …pe…

<!-- @segment sn35.20:1.4 -->

gandhe …

<!-- @segment sn35.20:1.5 -->

rase …

<!-- @segment sn35.20:1.6 -->

phoṭṭhabbe …

<!-- @segment sn35.20:1.7 -->

dhamme abhinandati, dukkhaṁ so abhinandati.

<!-- @segment sn35.20:1.8 -->

Yo dukkhaṁ abhinandati, ‘aparimutto so dukkhasmā’ti vadāmi.

<!-- @segment sn35.20:2.1 -->

“Yo ca kho, bhikkhave, rūpe nābhinandati, dukkhaṁ so nābhinandati.

<!-- @segment sn35.20:2.2 -->

Yo dukkhaṁ nābhinandati, ‘aparimutto so dukkhasmā’ti vadāmi.

<!-- @segment sn35.20:2.3 -->

Yo sadde …pe…

<!-- @segment sn35.20:2.4 -->

gandhe …

<!-- @segment sn35.20:2.5 -->

rase …

<!-- @segment sn35.20:2.6 -->

phoṭṭhabbe …

<!-- @segment sn35.20:2.7 -->

dhamme nābhinandati, dukkhaṁ so nābhinandati.

<!-- @segment sn35.20:2.8 -->

Yo dukkhaṁ nābhinandati, ‘aparimutto so dukkhasmā’ti vadāmi.”

<!-- @segment sn35.20:2.9 -->

Aṭṭhamaṁ.
