<!-- @segment sn18.2:1.1 -->

Sāvatthiyaṁ viharati.

<!-- @segment sn18.2:1.2 -->

“Taṁ kiṁ maññasi, rāhula,

<!-- @segment sn18.2:1.3 -->

rūpā niccā vā aniccā vā”ti?

<!-- @segment sn18.2:1.4 -->

“Aniccā, bhante” …pe…

<!-- @segment sn18.2:1.5 -->

saddā …

<!-- @segment sn18.2:1.6 -->

gandhā …

<!-- @segment sn18.2:1.7 -->

rasā …

<!-- @segment sn18.2:1.8 -->

phoṭṭhabbā …

<!-- @segment sn18.2:1.9 -->

dhammā niccā vā aniccā vā”ti?

<!-- @segment sn18.2:1.10 -->

“Aniccā, bhante” …

<!-- @segment sn18.2:1.11 -->

“evaṁ passaṁ, rāhula, sutavā ariyasāvako rūpesupi nibbindati … saddesupi nibbindati … gandhesupi nibbindati … rasesupi nibbindati … phoṭṭhabbesupi nibbindati … dhammesupi nibbindati;

<!-- @segment sn18.2:1.12 -->

nibbindaṁ virajjati …pe…

<!-- @segment sn18.2:1.13 -->

pajānātī”ti.

<!-- @segment sn18.2:1.14 -->

Dutiyaṁ.
