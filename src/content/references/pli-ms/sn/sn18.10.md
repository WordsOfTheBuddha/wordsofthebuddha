<!-- @segment sn18.10:1.1 -->

Sāvatthiyaṁ viharati.

<!-- @segment sn18.10:1.2 -->

“Taṁ kiṁ maññasi, rāhula,

<!-- @segment sn18.10:1.3 -->

rūpaṁ niccaṁ vā aniccaṁ vā”ti?

<!-- @segment sn18.10:1.4 -->

“Aniccaṁ, bhante” …

<!-- @segment sn18.10:1.5 -->

“vedanā …pe…

<!-- @segment sn18.10:1.6 -->

saññā …

<!-- @segment sn18.10:1.7 -->

saṅkhārā …

<!-- @segment sn18.10:1.8 -->

viññāṇaṁ niccaṁ vā aniccaṁ vā”ti?

<!-- @segment sn18.10:1.9 -->

“Aniccaṁ, bhante” …

<!-- @segment sn18.10:1.10 -->

“evaṁ passaṁ, rāhula, sutavā ariyasāvako rūpasmimpi nibbindati …pe… vedanāyapi nibbindati … saññāyapi nibbindati … saṅkhāresupi nibbindati … viññāṇasmimpi nibbindati;

<!-- @segment sn18.10:1.11 -->

nibbindaṁ virajjati; virāgā vimuccati; vimuttasmiṁ vimuttamiti ñāṇaṁ hoti.

<!-- @segment sn18.10:1.12 -->

‘Khīṇā jāti, vusitaṁ brahmacariyaṁ, kataṁ karaṇīyaṁ, nāparaṁ itthattāyā’ti pajānātī”ti.

<!-- @segment sn18.10:1.13 -->

Dasamaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn18.10:1.14 -->

Paṭhamo vaggo.

<!-- @segment sn18.10:2.0 -->

Tassuddānaṁ

<!-- @segment sn18.10:2.1 -->

Cakkhu rūpañca viññāṇaṁ,

<!-- @segment sn18.10:2.2 -->

samphasso vedanāya ca;

<!-- @segment sn18.10:2.3 -->

Saññā sañcetanā taṇhā,

<!-- @segment sn18.10:2.4 -->

dhātu khandhena te dasāti.
