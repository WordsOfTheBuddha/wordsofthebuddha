<!-- @segment sn22.36:1.1 -->

Sāvatthinidānaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn22.36:1.2 -->

Atha kho aññataro bhikkhu yena bhagavā …pe… ekamantaṁ nisinno kho so bhikkhu bhagavantaṁ etadavoca:

<!-- @segment sn22.36:1.3 -->

“sādhu me, bhante, bhagavā saṅkhittena dhammaṁ desetu yamahaṁ bhagavato dhammaṁ sutvā eko vūpakaṭṭho appamatto ātāpī pahitatto vihareyyan”ti.

<!-- @segment sn22.36:1.4 -->

“Yaṁ kho, bhikkhu, anuseti taṁ anumīyati;

<!-- @segment sn22.36:1.5 -->

yaṁ anumīyati tena saṅkhaṁ gacchati.

<!-- @segment sn22.36:1.6 -->

Yaṁ nānuseti na taṁ anumīyati;

<!-- @segment sn22.36:1.7 -->

yaṁ nānumīyati na tena saṅkhaṁ gacchatī”ti.

<!-- @segment sn22.36:1.8 -->

“Aññātaṁ, bhagavā, aññātaṁ, sugatā”ti.

<!-- @segment sn22.36:2.1 -->

“Yathā kathaṁ pana tvaṁ, bhikkhu, mayā saṅkhittena bhāsitassa vitthārena atthaṁ ājānāsī”ti?

<!-- @segment sn22.36:2.2 -->

“Rūpañce, bhante, anuseti taṁ anumīyati;

<!-- @segment sn22.36:2.3 -->

yaṁ anumīyati tena saṅkhaṁ gacchati.

<!-- @segment sn22.36:2.4 -->

Vedanañce anuseti …

<!-- @segment sn22.36:2.5 -->

saññañce anuseti …

<!-- @segment sn22.36:2.6 -->

saṅkhāre ce anuseti …

<!-- @segment sn22.36:2.7 -->

viññāṇañce anuseti taṁ anumīyati;

<!-- @segment sn22.36:2.8 -->

yaṁ anumīyati tena saṅkhaṁ gacchati.

<!-- @segment sn22.36:2.9 -->

Rūpañce, bhante, nānuseti na taṁ anumīyati;

<!-- @segment sn22.36:2.10 -->

yaṁ nānumīyati na tena saṅkhaṁ gacchati.

<!-- @segment sn22.36:2.11 -->

Vedanañce nānuseti …

<!-- @segment sn22.36:2.12 -->

saññañce nānuseti …

<!-- @segment sn22.36:2.13 -->

saṅkhāre ce nānuseti …

<!-- @segment sn22.36:2.14 -->

viññāṇañce nānuseti na taṁ anumīyati;

<!-- @segment sn22.36:2.15 -->

yaṁ nānumīyati na tena saṅkhaṁ gacchati.

<!-- @segment sn22.36:2.16 -->

Imassa khvāhaṁ, bhante, bhagavatā saṅkhittena bhāsitassa evaṁ vitthārena atthaṁ ājānāmī”ti.

<!-- @segment sn22.36:3.1 -->

“Sādhu sādhu, bhikkhu.

<!-- @segment sn22.36:3.2 -->

Sādhu kho tvaṁ, bhikkhu, mayā saṅkhittena bhāsitassa vitthārena atthaṁ ājānāsi.

<!-- @segment sn22.36:3.3 -->

Rūpañce, bhikkhu, anuseti taṁ anumīyati;

<!-- @segment sn22.36:3.4 -->

yaṁ anumīyati tena saṅkhaṁ gacchati.

<!-- @segment sn22.36:3.5 -->

Vedanañce, bhikkhu …

<!-- @segment sn22.36:3.6 -->

saññañce, bhikkhu …

<!-- @segment sn22.36:3.7 -->

saṅkhāre ce, bhikkhu …

<!-- @segment sn22.36:3.8 -->

viññāṇañce, bhikkhu, anuseti taṁ anumīyati;

<!-- @segment sn22.36:3.9 -->

yaṁ anumīyati tena saṅkhaṁ gacchati.

<!-- @segment sn22.36:3.10 -->

Rūpañce, bhikkhu, nānuseti na taṁ anumīyati;

<!-- @segment sn22.36:3.11 -->

yaṁ nānumīyati na tena saṅkhaṁ gacchati.

<!-- @segment sn22.36:3.12 -->

Vedanañce nānuseti …

<!-- @segment sn22.36:3.13 -->

saññañce nānuseti …

<!-- @segment sn22.36:3.14 -->

saṅkhāre ce nānuseti …

<!-- @segment sn22.36:3.15 -->

viññāṇañce nānuseti na taṁ anumīyati;

<!-- @segment sn22.36:3.16 -->

yaṁ nānumīyati na tena saṅkhaṁ gacchati.

<!-- @segment sn22.36:3.17 -->

Imassa kho, bhikkhu, mayā saṅkhittena bhāsitassa evaṁ vitthārena attho daṭṭhabbo”ti …pe…

<!-- @segment sn22.36:3.18 -->

aññataro ca pana so bhikkhu arahataṁ ahosīti.

<!-- @segment sn22.36:3.19 -->

Catutthaṁ.
