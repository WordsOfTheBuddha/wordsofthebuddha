<!-- @segment sn7.10:1.1 -->

Ekaṁ samayaṁ bhagavā kosalesu viharati aññatarasmiṁ vanasaṇḍe.

<!-- @segment sn7.10:1.2 -->

Tena kho pana samayena aññatarassa bhāradvājagottassa brāhmaṇassa catuddasa balībaddā naṭṭhā honti.

<!-- @segment sn7.10:1.3 -->

Atha kho bhāradvājagotto brāhmaṇo te balībadde gavesanto yena so vanasaṇḍo tenupasaṅkami; upasaṅkamitvā addasa bhagavantaṁ tasmiṁ vanasaṇḍe nisinnaṁ pallaṅkaṁ ābhujitvā ujuṁ kāyaṁ paṇidhāya parimukhaṁ satiṁ upaṭṭhapetvā.

<!-- @segment sn7.10:1.4 -->

Disvāna yena bhagavā tenupasaṅkami; upasaṅkamitvā bhagavato santike imā gāthāyo abhāsi:

<!-- @segment sn7.10:2.1 -->

“Na hi nūnimassa samaṇassa,

<!-- @segment sn7.10:2.2 -->

Balībaddā catuddasa;

<!-- @segment sn7.10:2.3 -->

Ajjasaṭṭhiṁ na dissanti,

<!-- @segment sn7.10:2.4 -->

Tenāyaṁ samaṇo sukhī.

<!-- @segment sn7.10:3.1 -->

Na hi nūnimassa samaṇassa,

<!-- @segment sn7.10:3.2 -->

Tilākhettasmi pāpakā;

<!-- @segment sn7.10:3.3 -->

Ekapaṇṇā dupaṇṇā ca,

<!-- @segment sn7.10:3.4 -->

Tenāyaṁ samaṇo sukhī.

<!-- @segment sn7.10:4.1 -->

Na hi nūnimassa samaṇassa,

<!-- @segment sn7.10:4.2 -->

Tucchakoṭṭhasmi mūsikā;

<!-- @segment sn7.10:4.3 -->

Ussoḷhikāya naccanti,

<!-- @segment sn7.10:4.4 -->

Tenāyaṁ samaṇo sukhī.

<!-- @segment sn7.10:5.1 -->

Na hi nūnimassa samaṇassa,

<!-- @segment sn7.10:5.2 -->

Santhāro sattamāsiko;

<!-- @segment sn7.10:5.3 -->

Uppāṭakehi sañchanno,

<!-- @segment sn7.10:5.4 -->

Tenāyaṁ samaṇo sukhī.

<!-- @segment sn7.10:6.1 -->

Na hi nūnimassa samaṇassa,

<!-- @segment sn7.10:6.2 -->

Vidhavā satta dhītaro;

<!-- @segment sn7.10:6.3 -->

Ekaputtā duputtā ca,

<!-- @segment sn7.10:6.4 -->

Tenāyaṁ samaṇo sukhī.

<!-- @segment sn7.10:7.1 -->

Na hi nūnimassa samaṇassa,

<!-- @segment sn7.10:7.2 -->

Piṅgalā tilakāhatā;

<!-- @segment sn7.10:7.3 -->

Sottaṁ pādena bodheti,

<!-- @segment sn7.10:7.4 -->

Tenāyaṁ samaṇo sukhī.

<!-- @segment sn7.10:8.1 -->

Na hi nūnimassa samaṇassa,

<!-- @segment sn7.10:8.2 -->

Paccūsamhi iṇāyikā;

<!-- @segment sn7.10:8.3 -->

Detha dethāti codenti,

<!-- @segment sn7.10:8.4 -->

Tenāyaṁ samaṇo sukhī”ti.

<!-- @segment sn7.10:9.1 -->

“Na hi mayhaṁ brāhmaṇa,

<!-- @segment sn7.10:9.2 -->

balībaddā catuddasa;

<!-- @segment sn7.10:9.3 -->

Ajjasaṭṭhiṁ na dissanti,

<!-- @segment sn7.10:9.4 -->

tenāhaṁ brāhmaṇā sukhī.

<!-- @segment sn7.10:10.1 -->

Na hi mayhaṁ brāhmaṇa,

<!-- @segment sn7.10:10.2 -->

tilākhettasmi pāpakā;

<!-- @segment sn7.10:10.3 -->

Ekapaṇṇā dupaṇṇā ca,

<!-- @segment sn7.10:10.4 -->

tenāhaṁ brāhmaṇā sukhī.

<!-- @segment sn7.10:11.1 -->

Na hi mayhaṁ brāhmaṇa,

<!-- @segment sn7.10:11.2 -->

tucchakoṭṭhasmi mūsikā;

<!-- @segment sn7.10:11.3 -->

Ussoḷhikāya naccanti,

<!-- @segment sn7.10:11.4 -->

tenāhaṁ brāhmaṇā sukhī.

<!-- @segment sn7.10:12.1 -->

Na hi mayhaṁ brāhmaṇa,

<!-- @segment sn7.10:12.2 -->

santhāro sattamāsiko;

<!-- @segment sn7.10:12.3 -->

Uppāṭakehi sañchanno,

<!-- @segment sn7.10:12.4 -->

tenāhaṁ brāhmaṇā sukhī.

<!-- @segment sn7.10:13.1 -->

Na hi mayhaṁ brāhmaṇa,

<!-- @segment sn7.10:13.2 -->

vidhavā satta dhītaro;

<!-- @segment sn7.10:13.3 -->

Ekaputtā duputtā ca,

<!-- @segment sn7.10:13.4 -->

tenāhaṁ brāhmaṇā sukhī.

<!-- @segment sn7.10:14.1 -->

Na hi mayhaṁ brāhmaṇa,

<!-- @segment sn7.10:14.2 -->

piṅgalā tilakāhatā;

<!-- @segment sn7.10:14.3 -->

Sottaṁ pādena bodheti,

<!-- @segment sn7.10:14.4 -->

tenāhaṁ brāhmaṇā sukhī.

<!-- @segment sn7.10:15.1 -->

Na hi mayhaṁ brāhmaṇa,

<!-- @segment sn7.10:15.2 -->

paccūsamhi iṇāyikā;

<!-- @segment sn7.10:15.3 -->

Detha dethāti codenti,

<!-- @segment sn7.10:15.4 -->

tenāhaṁ brāhmaṇā sukhī”ti.

<!-- @segment sn7.10:16.1 -->

Evaṁ vutte, bhāradvājagotto brāhmaṇo bhagavantaṁ etadavoca:

<!-- @segment sn7.10:16.2 -->

“abhikkantaṁ, bho gotama, abhikkantaṁ, bho gotama.

<!-- @segment sn7.10:16.3 -->

Seyyathāpi, bho gotama, nikkujjitaṁ vā ukkujjeyya, paṭicchannaṁ vā vivareyya, mūḷhassa vā maggaṁ ācikkheyya, andhakāre vā telapajjotaṁ dhāreyya: ‘cakkhumanto rūpāni dakkhantī’ti; evameva bhotā gotamena anekapariyāyena dhammo pakāsito.

<!-- @segment sn7.10:16.4 -->

Esāhaṁ bhavantaṁ gotamaṁ saraṇaṁ gacchāmi dhammañca bhikkhusaṅghañca.

<!-- @segment sn7.10:16.5 -->

Labheyyāhaṁ bhoto gotamassa santike pabbajjaṁ, labheyyaṁ upasampadan”ti.

<!-- @segment sn7.10:17.1 -->

Alattha kho bhāradvājagotto brāhmaṇo bhagavato santike pabbajjaṁ, alattha upasampadaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn7.10:17.2 -->

Acirūpasampanno panāyasmā bhāradvājo eko vūpakaṭṭho appamatto ātāpī pahitatto viharanto nacirasseva—yassatthāya kulaputtā sammadeva agārasmā anagāriyaṁ pabbajanti, tadanuttaraṁ—brahmacariyapariyosānaṁ diṭṭheva dhamme sayaṁ abhiññā sacchikatvā upasampajja vihāsi.

<!-- @segment sn7.10:17.3 -->

“Khīṇā jāti, vusitaṁ brahmacariyaṁ, kataṁ karaṇīyaṁ, nāparaṁ itthattāyā”ti abbhaññāsi.

<!-- @segment sn7.10:17.4 -->

Aññataro ca panāyasmā bhāradvājo arahataṁ ahosīti.

<!-- @segment sn7.10:17.5 -->

Arahantavaggo paṭhamo.

<!-- @segment sn7.10:18.0 -->

Tassuddānaṁ

<!-- @segment sn7.10:18.1 -->

Dhanañjānī ca akkosaṁ,

<!-- @segment sn7.10:18.2 -->

asurindaṁ bilaṅgikaṁ;

<!-- @segment sn7.10:18.3 -->

Ahiṁsakaṁ jaṭā ceva,

<!-- @segment sn7.10:18.4 -->

suddhikañceva aggikā;

<!-- @segment sn7.10:18.5 -->

Sundarikaṁ bahudhīta—

<!-- @segment sn7.10:18.6 -->

rena ca te dasāti.
