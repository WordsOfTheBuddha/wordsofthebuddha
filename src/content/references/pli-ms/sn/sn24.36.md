<!-- @segment sn24.36:1.1 -->

Sāvatthinidānaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn24.36:1.2 -->

“Kismiṁ nu kho, bhikkhave, sati, kiṁ upādāya, kiṁ abhinivissa evaṁ diṭṭhi uppajjati:

<!-- @segment sn24.36:1.3 -->

‘neva hoti na na hoti tathāgato paraṁ maraṇā’”ti?

<!-- @segment sn24.36:1.4 -->

“Bhagavaṁmūlakā no, bhante, dhammā …pe…

<!-- @segment sn24.36:2.1 -->

“Rūpe kho, bhikkhave, sati, rūpaṁ upādāya, rūpaṁ abhinivissa evaṁ diṭṭhi uppajjati:

<!-- @segment sn24.36:2.2 -->

‘neva hoti na na hoti tathāgato paraṁ maraṇā’”ti.

<!-- @segment sn24.36:2.3 -->

“Vedanāya sati …

<!-- @segment sn24.36:2.4 -->

saññāya sati …

<!-- @segment sn24.36:2.5 -->

saṅkhāresu sati …

<!-- @segment sn24.36:2.6 -->

viññāṇe sati, viññāṇaṁ upādāya, viññāṇaṁ abhinivissa evaṁ diṭṭhi uppajjati:

<!-- @segment sn24.36:2.7 -->

‘neva hoti na na hoti tathāgato paraṁ maraṇā’ti.

<!-- @segment sn24.36:3.1 -->

Taṁ kiṁ maññatha, bhikkhave,

<!-- @segment sn24.36:3.2 -->

rūpaṁ niccaṁ vā aniccaṁ vā”ti?

<!-- @segment sn24.36:3.3 -->

“Aniccaṁ, bhante” …pe… vipariṇāmadhammaṁ,

<!-- @segment sn24.36:3.4 -->

api nu taṁ anupādāya evaṁ diṭṭhi uppajjeyya:

<!-- @segment sn24.36:3.5 -->

‘neva hoti na na hoti tathāgato paraṁ maraṇā’”ti?

<!-- @segment sn24.36:3.6 -->

“No hetaṁ, bhante”.

<!-- @segment sn24.36:3.7 -->

“Iti kho, bhikkhave, dukkhe sati, dukkhaṁ upādāya, dukkhaṁ abhinivissa evaṁ diṭṭhi uppajjati:

<!-- @segment sn24.36:3.8 -->

‘neva hoti na na hoti tathāgato paraṁ maraṇā’”ti.

<!-- @segment sn24.36:3.9 -->

“Vedanā …

<!-- @segment sn24.36:3.10 -->

saññā …

<!-- @segment sn24.36:3.11 -->

saṅkhārā …

<!-- @segment sn24.36:3.12 -->

viññāṇaṁ niccaṁ vā aniccaṁ vā”ti?

<!-- @segment sn24.36:3.13 -->

“Aniccaṁ, bhante” …pe… vipariṇāmadhammaṁ,

<!-- @segment sn24.36:3.14 -->

api nu taṁ anupādāya evaṁ diṭṭhi uppajjeyya:

<!-- @segment sn24.36:3.15 -->

‘neva hoti na na hoti tathāgato paraṁ maraṇā’”ti?

<!-- @segment sn24.36:3.16 -->

“No hetaṁ, bhante”.

<!-- @segment sn24.36:3.17 -->

“Iti kho, bhikkhave, dukkhe sati, dukkhaṁ upādāya dukkhaṁ abhinivissa evaṁ diṭṭhi uppajjati:

<!-- @segment sn24.36:3.18 -->

‘neva hoti na na hoti tathāgato paraṁ maraṇā’”ti.

<!-- @segment sn24.36:3.19 -->

Aṭṭhārasamaṁ.
