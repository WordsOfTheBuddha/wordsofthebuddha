<!-- @segment sn45.36:1.1 -->

Sāvatthinidānaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn45.36:1.2 -->

“Sāmaññañca vo, bhikkhave, desessāmi, sāmaññatthañca.

<!-- @segment sn45.36:1.3 -->

Taṁ suṇātha.

<!-- @segment sn45.36:1.4 -->

Katamañca kho, bhikkhave, sāmaññaṁ?

<!-- @segment sn45.36:1.5 -->

Ayameva ariyo aṭṭhaṅgiko maggo, seyyathidaṁ—

<!-- @segment sn45.36:1.6 -->

sammādiṭṭhi …pe… sammāsamādhi.

<!-- @segment sn45.36:1.7 -->

Idaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, sāmaññaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn45.36:1.8 -->

Katamo ca, bhikkhave, sāmaññattho?

<!-- @segment sn45.36:1.9 -->

Yo kho, bhikkhave, rāgakkhayo dosakkhayo mohakkhayo—

<!-- @segment sn45.36:1.10 -->

ayaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, sāmaññattho”ti.

<!-- @segment sn45.36:1.11 -->

Chaṭṭhaṁ.
