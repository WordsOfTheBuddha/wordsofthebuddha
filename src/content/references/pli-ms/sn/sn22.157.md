<!-- @segment sn22.157:1.1 -->

Sāvatthinidānaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn22.157:1.2 -->

“Kismiṁ nu kho, bhikkhave, sati, kiṁ upādāya, kiṁ abhinivissa uppajjanti saṁyojanābhinivesavinibandhā”ti?

<!-- @segment sn22.157:1.3 -->

Bhagavaṁmūlakā no, bhante, dhammā …pe…

<!-- @segment sn22.157:1.4 -->

“rūpe kho, bhikkhave, sati, rūpaṁ upādāya, rūpaṁ abhinivissa uppajjanti saṁyojanābhinivesavinibandhā.

<!-- @segment sn22.157:1.5 -->

Vedanāya sati …

<!-- @segment sn22.157:1.6 -->

saññāya sati …

<!-- @segment sn22.157:1.7 -->

saṅkhāresu sati …

<!-- @segment sn22.157:1.8 -->

viññāṇe sati, viññāṇaṁ upādāya, viññāṇaṁ abhinivissa uppajjanti saṁyojanābhinivesavinibandhā.

<!-- @segment sn22.157:1.9 -->

Taṁ kiṁ maññatha, bhikkhave,

<!-- @segment sn22.157:1.10 -->

rūpaṁ niccaṁ vā aniccaṁ vā”ti?

<!-- @segment sn22.157:1.11 -->

“Aniccaṁ, bhante”.

<!-- @segment sn22.157:1.12 -->

“Yaṁ panāniccaṁ …pe…

<!-- @segment sn22.157:1.13 -->

api nu taṁ anupādāya uppajjeyyuṁ saṁyojanābhinivesavinibandhā”ti?

<!-- @segment sn22.157:1.14 -->

“No hetaṁ, bhante” …pe…

<!-- @segment sn22.157:1.15 -->

“evaṁ passaṁ …pe…

<!-- @segment sn22.157:1.16 -->

nāparaṁ itthattāyāti pajānātī”ti.

<!-- @segment sn22.157:1.17 -->

Aṭṭhamaṁ.
