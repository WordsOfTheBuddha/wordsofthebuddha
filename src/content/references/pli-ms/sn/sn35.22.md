<!-- @segment sn35.22:1.1 -->

“Yo, bhikkhave, rūpānaṁ uppādo ṭhiti abhinibbatti pātubhāvo, dukkhasseso uppādo, rogānaṁ ṭhiti, jarāmaraṇassa pātubhāvo.

<!-- @segment sn35.22:1.2 -->

Yo saddānaṁ …pe…

<!-- @segment sn35.22:1.3 -->

yo gandhānaṁ …

<!-- @segment sn35.22:1.4 -->

yo rasānaṁ …

<!-- @segment sn35.22:1.5 -->

yo phoṭṭhabbānaṁ …

<!-- @segment sn35.22:1.6 -->

yo dhammānaṁ uppādo ṭhiti abhinibbatti pātubhāvo, dukkhasseso uppādo, rogānaṁ ṭhiti, jarāmaraṇassa pātubhāvo.

<!-- @segment sn35.22:2.1 -->

Yo ca kho, bhikkhave, rūpānaṁ nirodho vūpasamo atthaṅgamo, dukkhasseso nirodho, rogānaṁ vūpasamo, jarāmaraṇassa atthaṅgamo.

<!-- @segment sn35.22:2.2 -->

Yo saddānaṁ …pe…

<!-- @segment sn35.22:2.3 -->

yo gandhānaṁ …

<!-- @segment sn35.22:2.4 -->

yo rasānaṁ …

<!-- @segment sn35.22:2.5 -->

yo phoṭṭhabbānaṁ …

<!-- @segment sn35.22:2.6 -->

yo dhammānaṁ nirodho vūpasamo atthaṅgamo, dukkhasseso nirodho, rogānaṁ vūpasamo, jarāmaraṇassa atthaṅgamo”ti.

<!-- @segment sn35.22:2.7 -->

Dasamaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn35.22:2.8 -->

Yamakavaggo dutiyo.

<!-- @segment sn35.22:3.0 -->

Tassuddānaṁ

<!-- @segment sn35.22:3.1 -->

Sambodhena duve vuttā,

<!-- @segment sn35.22:3.2 -->

Assādena apare duve;

<!-- @segment sn35.22:3.3 -->

No cetena duve vuttā,

<!-- @segment sn35.22:3.4 -->

Abhinandena apare duve;

<!-- @segment sn35.22:3.5 -->

Uppādena duve vuttā,

<!-- @segment sn35.22:3.6 -->

Vaggo tena pavuccatīti.
