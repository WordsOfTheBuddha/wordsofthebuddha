<!-- @segment sn49.35-44:1.1 -->

“Tisso imā, bhikkhave, esanā.

<!-- @segment sn49.35-44:1.2 -->

Katamā tisso?

<!-- @segment sn49.35-44:1.3 -->

Kāmesanā, bhavesanā, brahmacariyesanā—

<!-- @segment sn49.35-44:1.4 -->

imā kho, bhikkhave, tisso esanā.

<!-- @segment sn49.35-44:1.5 -->

Imāsaṁ kho, bhikkhave, tissannaṁ esanānaṁ abhiññāya pariññāya parikkhayāya pahānāya cattāro sammappadhānā bhāvetabbā.

<!-- @segment sn49.35-44:1.6 -->

Katame cattāro?

<!-- @segment sn49.35-44:1.7 -->

Idha, bhikkhave, bhikkhu anuppannānaṁ …pe…

<!-- @segment sn49.35-44:1.8 -->

uppannānaṁ kusalānaṁ dhammānaṁ ṭhitiyā asammosāya bhiyyobhāvāya vepullāya bhāvanāya pāripūriyā chandaṁ janeti vāyamati vīriyaṁ ārabhati cittaṁ paggaṇhāti padahati.

<!-- @segment sn49.35-44:1.9 -->

Imāsaṁ kho, bhikkhave, tissannaṁ esanānaṁ abhiññāya pariññāya parikkhayāya pahānāya ime cattāro sammappadhānā bhāvetabbā”ti.

<!-- @segment sn49.35-44:1.10 -->

(Vitthāretabbaṁ.)

<!-- @segment sn49.35-44:1.11 -->

Dasamaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn49.35-44:1.12 -->

Esanāvaggo catuttho.

<!-- @segment sn49.35-44:2.0 -->

Tassuddānaṁ

<!-- @segment sn49.35-44:2.1 -->

Esanā vidhā āsavo,

<!-- @segment sn49.35-44:2.2 -->

Bhavo ca dukkhatā tisso;

<!-- @segment sn49.35-44:2.3 -->

Khilaṁ malañca nīgho ca,

<!-- @segment sn49.35-44:2.4 -->

Vedanā taṇhā tasinā cāti.
