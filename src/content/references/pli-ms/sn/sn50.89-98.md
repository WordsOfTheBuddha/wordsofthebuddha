<!-- @segment sn50.89-98:1.1 -->

(Evaṁ esanāpāḷi vitthāretabbā—rāgavinayapariyosānaṁ dosavinayapariyosānaṁ mohavinayapariyosānaṁ.)

<!-- @segment sn50.89-98:1.2 -->

Esanāvaggo navamo.

<!-- @segment sn50.89-98:2.0 -->

Tassuddānaṁ

<!-- @segment sn50.89-98:2.1 -->

Esanā vidhā āsavo,

<!-- @segment sn50.89-98:2.2 -->

Bhavo ca dukkhatā tisso;

<!-- @segment sn50.89-98:2.3 -->

Khilaṁ malañca nīgho ca,

<!-- @segment sn50.89-98:2.4 -->

Vedanā taṇhā tasinā cāti.
