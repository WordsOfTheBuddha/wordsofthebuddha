<!-- @segment sn1.16:1.1 -->

“Niddā tandī vijambhitā,

<!-- @segment sn1.16:1.2 -->

Aratī bhattasammado;

<!-- @segment sn1.16:1.3 -->

Etena nappakāsati,

<!-- @segment sn1.16:1.4 -->

Ariyamaggo idha pāṇinan”ti.

<!-- @segment sn1.16:2.1 -->

“Niddaṁ tandiṁ vijambhitaṁ,

<!-- @segment sn1.16:2.2 -->

Aratiṁ bhattasammadaṁ;

<!-- @segment sn1.16:2.3 -->

Vīriyena naṁ paṇāmetvā,

<!-- @segment sn1.16:2.4 -->

Ariyamaggo visujjhatī”ti.
