<!-- @segment sn37.14:1.1 -->

“Pañcahi, anuruddha, dhammehi samannāgato mātugāmo …pe… nirayaṁ upapajjati.

<!-- @segment sn37.14:1.2 -->

Katamehi pañcahi?

<!-- @segment sn37.14:1.3 -->

Pāṇātipātī ca hoti, adinnādāyī ca hoti, kāmesumicchācārī ca hoti, musāvādī ca hoti, surāmerayamajjappamādaṭṭhāyī ca hoti—

<!-- @segment sn37.14:1.4 -->

imehi kho, anuruddha, pañcahi dhammehi samannāgato mātugāmo kāyassa bhedā paraṁ maraṇā apāyaṁ duggatiṁ vinipātaṁ nirayaṁ upapajjatī”ti.

<!-- @segment sn37.14:1.5 -->

Cuddasamaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn37.14:1.6 -->

Paṭhamapeyyālavaggo.

<!-- @segment sn37.14:2.0 -->

Tassuddānaṁ

<!-- @segment sn37.14:2.1 -->

Mātugāmo puriso ca,

<!-- @segment sn37.14:2.2 -->

āveṇikā tidhammo ca;

<!-- @segment sn37.14:2.3 -->

Kodhano upanāhī ca,

<!-- @segment sn37.14:2.4 -->

issukī maccharena ca;

<!-- @segment sn37.14:2.5 -->

Aticārī ca dussīlo,

<!-- @segment sn37.14:2.6 -->

appassuto ca kusīto;

<!-- @segment sn37.14:2.7 -->

Muṭṭhassati pañcaveraṁ,

<!-- @segment sn37.14:2.8 -->

kaṇhapakkhe pakāsito.
