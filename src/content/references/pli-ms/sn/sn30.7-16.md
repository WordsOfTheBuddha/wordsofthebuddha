<!-- @segment sn30.7-16:1.1 -->

Sāvatthinidānaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn30.7-16:1.2 -->

Ekamantaṁ nisinno kho so bhikkhu bhagavantaṁ etadavoca:

<!-- @segment sn30.7-16:1.3 -->

“ko nu kho, bhante, hetu, ko paccayo, yena midhekacco kāyassa bhedā paraṁ maraṇā aṇḍajānaṁ supaṇṇānaṁ sahabyataṁ upapajjatī”ti?

<!-- @segment sn30.7-16:1.4 -->

“Idha, bhikkhu, ekacco kāyena dvayakārī hoti, vācāya dvayakārī, manasā dvayakārī.

<!-- @segment sn30.7-16:1.5 -->

Tassa sutaṁ hoti:

<!-- @segment sn30.7-16:1.6 -->

‘aṇḍajā supaṇṇā dīghāyukā vaṇṇavanto sukhabahulā’ti.

<!-- @segment sn30.7-16:1.7 -->

Tassa evaṁ hoti:

<!-- @segment sn30.7-16:1.8 -->

‘aho vatāhaṁ kāyassa bhedā paraṁ maraṇā aṇḍajānaṁ supaṇṇānaṁ sahabyataṁ upapajjeyyan’ti.

<!-- @segment sn30.7-16:1.9 -->

So annaṁ deti …pe…

<!-- @segment sn30.7-16:1.10 -->

pānaṁ deti …

<!-- @segment sn30.7-16:1.11 -->

vatthaṁ deti …

<!-- @segment sn30.7-16:1.12 -->

yānaṁ deti …

<!-- @segment sn30.7-16:1.13 -->

mālaṁ deti …

<!-- @segment sn30.7-16:1.14 -->

gandhaṁ deti …

<!-- @segment sn30.7-16:1.15 -->

vilepanaṁ deti …

<!-- @segment sn30.7-16:1.16 -->

seyyaṁ deti …

<!-- @segment sn30.7-16:1.17 -->

āvasathaṁ deti …

<!-- @segment sn30.7-16:1.18 -->

padīpeyyaṁ deti.

<!-- @segment sn30.7-16:1.19 -->

So kāyassa bhedā paraṁ maraṇā aṇḍajānaṁ supaṇṇānaṁ sahabyataṁ upapajjati.

<!-- @segment sn30.7-16:1.20 -->

Ayaṁ kho, bhikkhu, hetu, ayaṁ paccayo, yena midhekacco kāyassa bhedā paraṁ maraṇā aṇḍajānaṁ supaṇṇānaṁ sahabyataṁ upapajjatī”ti.

<!-- @segment sn30.7-16:1.21 -->

Soḷasamaṁ.
