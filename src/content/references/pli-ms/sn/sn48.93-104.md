<!-- @segment sn48.93-104:1.1 -->

(Balakaraṇīyavaggo vitthāretabbo.)

<!-- @segment sn48.93-104:2.0 -->

Tassuddānaṁ

<!-- @segment sn48.93-104:2.1 -->

Balaṁ bījañca nāgo ca,

<!-- @segment sn48.93-104:2.2 -->

rukkho kumbhena sūkiyā;

<!-- @segment sn48.93-104:2.3 -->

Ākāsena ca dve meghā,

<!-- @segment sn48.93-104:2.4 -->

nāvā āgantukā nadīti.
