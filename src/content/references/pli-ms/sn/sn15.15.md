<!-- @segment sn15.15:1.1 -->

Sāvatthiyaṁ viharati.

<!-- @segment sn15.15:1.2 -->

“Anamataggoyaṁ, bhikkhave, saṁsāro …pe…

<!-- @segment sn15.15:1.3 -->

na so, bhikkhave, satto sulabharūpo yo napitābhūtapubbo …pe…

<!-- @segment sn15.15:1.4 -->

alaṁ vimuccitun”ti.

<!-- @segment sn15.15:1.5 -->

Pañcamaṁ.
