<!-- @segment sn24.7:1.1 -->

Sāvatthinidānaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn24.7:1.2 -->

“Kismiṁ nu kho, bhikkhave, sati, kiṁ upādāya, kiṁ abhinivissa evaṁ diṭṭhi uppajjati:

<!-- @segment sn24.7:1.3 -->

‘natthi hetu, natthi paccayo sattānaṁ saṅkilesāya.

<!-- @segment sn24.7:1.4 -->

Ahetū appaccayā sattā saṅkilissanti.

<!-- @segment sn24.7:1.5 -->

Natthi hetu, natthi paccayo sattānaṁ visuddhiyā.

<!-- @segment sn24.7:1.6 -->

Ahetū appaccayā sattā visujjhanti.

<!-- @segment sn24.7:1.7 -->

Natthi balaṁ natthi vīriyaṁ natthi purisathāmo natthi purisaparakkamo.

<!-- @segment sn24.7:1.8 -->

Sabbe sattā sabbe pāṇā sabbe bhūtā sabbe jīvā avasā abalā avīriyā niyatisaṅgatibhāvapariṇatā chasvevābhijātīsu sukhadukkhaṁ paṭisaṁvedentī’”ti?

<!-- @segment sn24.7:1.9 -->

“Bhagavaṁmūlakā no, bhante, dhammā …pe…

<!-- @segment sn24.7:2.1 -->

“Rūpe kho, bhikkhave, sati, rūpaṁ upādāya, rūpaṁ abhinivissa evaṁ diṭṭhi uppajjati:

<!-- @segment sn24.7:2.2 -->

‘natthi hetu, natthi paccayo …pe…

<!-- @segment sn24.7:2.3 -->

sukhadukkhaṁ paṭisaṁvedentī’ti.

<!-- @segment sn24.7:2.4 -->

Vedanāya sati …pe…

<!-- @segment sn24.7:2.5 -->

saññāya sati …

<!-- @segment sn24.7:2.6 -->

saṅkhāresu sati …

<!-- @segment sn24.7:2.7 -->

viññāṇe sati, viññāṇaṁ upādāya, viññāṇaṁ abhinivissa evaṁ diṭṭhi uppajjati:

<!-- @segment sn24.7:2.8 -->

‘natthi hetu, natthi paccayo …pe…

<!-- @segment sn24.7:2.9 -->

sukhadukkhaṁ paṭisaṁvedentī’ti.

<!-- @segment sn24.7:3.1 -->

Taṁ kiṁ maññatha, bhikkhave,

<!-- @segment sn24.7:3.2 -->

rūpaṁ niccaṁ vā aniccaṁ vā”ti?

<!-- @segment sn24.7:3.3 -->

“Aniccaṁ, bhante …pe…

<!-- @segment sn24.7:3.4 -->

vipariṇāmadhammaṁ, api nu taṁ anupādāya evaṁ diṭṭhi uppajjeyya:

<!-- @segment sn24.7:3.5 -->

‘natthi hetu, natthi paccayo …pe…

<!-- @segment sn24.7:3.6 -->

sukhadukkhaṁ paṭisaṁvedentī’”ti?

<!-- @segment sn24.7:3.7 -->

“No hetaṁ, bhante”.

<!-- @segment sn24.7:3.8 -->

“Vedanā …

<!-- @segment sn24.7:3.9 -->

saññā …

<!-- @segment sn24.7:3.10 -->

saṅkhārā …

<!-- @segment sn24.7:3.11 -->

viññāṇaṁ niccaṁ vā aniccaṁ vā”ti?

<!-- @segment sn24.7:3.12 -->

“Aniccaṁ, bhante …pe…

<!-- @segment sn24.7:3.13 -->

api nu taṁ anupādāya evaṁ diṭṭhi uppajjeyya:

<!-- @segment sn24.7:3.14 -->

‘natthi hetu, natthi paccayo …pe…

<!-- @segment sn24.7:3.15 -->

sukhadukkhaṁ paṭisaṁvedentī’”ti?

<!-- @segment sn24.7:3.16 -->

“No hetaṁ, bhante”.

<!-- @segment sn24.7:3.17 -->

“Yampidaṁ diṭṭhaṁ sutaṁ mutaṁ viññātaṁ pattaṁ pariyesitaṁ anuvicaritaṁ manasā tampi niccaṁ vā aniccaṁ vā”ti?

<!-- @segment sn24.7:3.18 -->

“Aniccaṁ, bhante …pe…

<!-- @segment sn24.7:3.19 -->

api nu taṁ anupādāya evaṁ diṭṭhi uppajjeyya:

<!-- @segment sn24.7:3.20 -->

‘natthi hetu natthi paccayo …pe…

<!-- @segment sn24.7:3.21 -->

sukhadukkhaṁ paṭisaṁvedentī’”ti?

<!-- @segment sn24.7:3.22 -->

“No hetaṁ, bhante”.

<!-- @segment sn24.7:4.1 -->

“Yato kho, bhikkhave, ariyasāvakassa imesu ca ṭhānesu kaṅkhā pahīnā hoti, dukkhepissa kaṅkhā pahīnā hoti …pe… dukkhanirodhagāminiyā paṭipadāyapissa kaṅkhā pahīnā hoti—

<!-- @segment sn24.7:4.2 -->

ayaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, ariyasāvako sotāpanno avinipātadhammo niyato sambodhiparāyano”ti.

<!-- @segment sn24.7:4.3 -->

Sattamaṁ.
