<!-- @segment sn46.25:1.1 -->

“Satta vo, bhikkhave, aparihāniye dhamme desessāmi;

<!-- @segment sn46.25:1.2 -->

taṁ suṇātha.

<!-- @segment sn46.25:1.3 -->

Katame ca, bhikkhave, satta aparihāniyā dhammā?

<!-- @segment sn46.25:1.4 -->

Yadidaṁ—

<!-- @segment sn46.25:1.5 -->

satta bojjhaṅgā.

<!-- @segment sn46.25:1.6 -->

Katame satta?

<!-- @segment sn46.25:1.7 -->

Satisambojjhaṅgo …pe… upekkhāsambojjhaṅgo—

<!-- @segment sn46.25:1.8 -->

ime kho, bhikkhave, satta aparihāniyā dhammā”ti.

<!-- @segment sn46.25:1.9 -->

Pañcamaṁ.
