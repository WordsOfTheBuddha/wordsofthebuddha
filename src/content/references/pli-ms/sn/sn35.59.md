<!-- @segment sn35.59:1.1 -->

“Kathaṁ nu kho …pe…

<!-- @segment sn35.59:1.2 -->

anusayā samugghātaṁ gacchantī”ti?

<!-- @segment sn35.59:1.3 -->

“Cakkhuṁ kho, bhikkhu, anattato jānato passato anusayā samugghātaṁ gacchanti …pe…

<!-- @segment sn35.59:1.4 -->

sotaṁ …

<!-- @segment sn35.59:1.5 -->

ghānaṁ …

<!-- @segment sn35.59:1.6 -->

jivhaṁ …

<!-- @segment sn35.59:1.7 -->

kāyaṁ …

<!-- @segment sn35.59:1.8 -->

manaṁ …

<!-- @segment sn35.59:1.9 -->

dhamme …

<!-- @segment sn35.59:1.10 -->

manoviññāṇaṁ …

<!-- @segment sn35.59:1.11 -->

manosamphassaṁ …

<!-- @segment sn35.59:1.12 -->

yampidaṁ manosamphassapaccayā uppajjati vedayitaṁ sukhaṁ vā dukkhaṁ vā adukkhamasukhaṁ vā tampi anattato jānato passato anusayā samugghātaṁ gacchanti.

<!-- @segment sn35.59:1.13 -->

Evaṁ kho, bhikkhu, jānato evaṁ passato anusayā samugghātaṁ gacchantī”ti.

<!-- @segment sn35.59:1.14 -->

Sattamaṁ.
