<!-- @segment sn14.29:1.1 -->

Sāvatthiyaṁ viharati.

<!-- @segment sn14.29:1.2 -->

“Dhātusova, bhikkhave, sattā saṁsandanti samenti.

<!-- @segment sn14.29:1.3 -->

Micchādiṭṭhikā micchādiṭṭhikehi saddhiṁ saṁsandanti samenti;

<!-- @segment sn14.29:1.4 -->

micchāsaṅkappā …pe…

<!-- @segment sn14.29:1.5 -->

micchāvācā …

<!-- @segment sn14.29:1.6 -->

micchākammantā …

<!-- @segment sn14.29:1.7 -->

micchāājīvā …

<!-- @segment sn14.29:1.8 -->

micchāvāyāmā …

<!-- @segment sn14.29:1.9 -->

micchāsatino …

<!-- @segment sn14.29:1.10 -->

micchāsamādhino micchāsamādhīhi saddhiṁ saṁsandanti samenti;

<!-- @segment sn14.29:1.11 -->

micchāñāṇino micchāñāṇīhi saddhiṁ saṁsandanti samenti;

<!-- @segment sn14.29:1.12 -->

micchāvimuttino micchāvimuttīhi saddhiṁ saṁsandanti samenti.

<!-- @segment sn14.29:2.1 -->

Sammādiṭṭhikā sammādiṭṭhikehi saddhiṁ saṁsandanti samenti;

<!-- @segment sn14.29:2.2 -->

sammāsaṅkappā …pe…

<!-- @segment sn14.29:2.3 -->

sammāvācā …

<!-- @segment sn14.29:2.4 -->

sammākammantā …

<!-- @segment sn14.29:2.5 -->

sammāājīvā …

<!-- @segment sn14.29:2.6 -->

sammāvāyāmā …

<!-- @segment sn14.29:2.7 -->

sammāsatino …

<!-- @segment sn14.29:2.8 -->

sammāsamādhino …

<!-- @segment sn14.29:2.9 -->

sammāñāṇino sammāñāṇīhi saddhiṁ saṁsandanti samenti;

<!-- @segment sn14.29:2.10 -->

sammāvimuttino sammāvimuttīhi saddhiṁ saṁsandanti samentī”ti.

<!-- @segment sn14.29:2.11 -->

Sattamaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn14.29:2.12 -->

(Sabbattha atītānāgatapaccuppannaṁ kātabbaṁ.)

<!-- @segment sn14.29:3.0 -->

Sattannaṁ suttantānaṁ uddānaṁ

<!-- @segment sn14.29:3.1 -->

Asamāhitaṁ dussīlaṁ,

<!-- @segment sn14.29:3.2 -->

pañca sikkhāpadāni ca;

<!-- @segment sn14.29:3.3 -->

Satta kammapathā vuttā,

<!-- @segment sn14.29:3.4 -->

dasakammapathena ca;

<!-- @segment sn14.29:3.5 -->

Chaṭṭhaṁ aṭṭhaṅgiko vutto,

<!-- @segment sn14.29:3.6 -->

dasaṅgena ca sattamaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn14.29:3.7 -->

Kammapathavaggo tatiyo.
