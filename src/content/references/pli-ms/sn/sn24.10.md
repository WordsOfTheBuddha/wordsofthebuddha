<!-- @segment sn24.10:1.1 -->

Sāvatthinidānaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn24.10:1.2 -->

“Kismiṁ nu kho, bhikkhave, sati, kiṁ upādāya, kiṁ abhinivissa evaṁ diṭṭhi uppajjati:

<!-- @segment sn24.10:1.3 -->

‘asassato loko’”ti?

<!-- @segment sn24.10:1.4 -->

“Bhagavaṁmūlakā no, bhante, dhammā …pe…

<!-- @segment sn24.10:2.1 -->

“Rūpe kho, bhikkhave, sati …pe…

<!-- @segment sn24.10:2.2 -->

viññāṇaṁ niccaṁ vā aniccaṁ vā”ti?

<!-- @segment sn24.10:2.3 -->

“Aniccaṁ, bhante” …pe…

<!-- @segment sn24.10:2.4 -->

api nu taṁ anupādāya evaṁ diṭṭhi uppajjeyya:

<!-- @segment sn24.10:2.5 -->

‘asassato loko’”ti?

<!-- @segment sn24.10:2.6 -->

“No hetaṁ, bhante”.

<!-- @segment sn24.10:2.7 -->

“Yampidaṁ diṭṭhaṁ sutaṁ mutaṁ viññātaṁ pattaṁ pariyesitaṁ anuvicaritaṁ manasā tampi niccaṁ vā aniccaṁ vā”ti?

<!-- @segment sn24.10:2.8 -->

“Aniccaṁ, bhante …pe…

<!-- @segment sn24.10:2.9 -->

api nu taṁ anupādāya evaṁ diṭṭhi uppajjeyya:

<!-- @segment sn24.10:2.10 -->

‘asassato loko’”ti?

<!-- @segment sn24.10:2.11 -->

“No hetaṁ, bhante”.

<!-- @segment sn24.10:3.1 -->

“Yato kho, bhikkhave, ariyasāvakassa imesu ca ṭhānesu kaṅkhā pahīnā hoti, dukkhepissa kaṅkhā pahīnā hoti …pe… dukkhanirodhagāminiyā paṭipadāyapissa kaṅkhā pahīnā hoti—

<!-- @segment sn24.10:3.2 -->

ayaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, ariyasāvako sotāpanno avinipātadhammo niyato sambodhiparāyano”ti.

<!-- @segment sn24.10:3.3 -->

Dasamaṁ.
