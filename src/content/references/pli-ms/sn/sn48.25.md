<!-- @segment sn48.25:1.1 -->

“Chayimāni, bhikkhave, indriyāni.

<!-- @segment sn48.25:1.2 -->

Katamāni cha?

<!-- @segment sn48.25:1.3 -->

Cakkhundriyaṁ, sotindriyaṁ, ghānindriyaṁ, jivhindriyaṁ, kāyindriyaṁ, manindriyaṁ—

<!-- @segment sn48.25:1.4 -->

imāni kho, bhikkhave, cha indriyānī”ti.

<!-- @segment sn48.25:1.5 -->

Pañcamaṁ.
