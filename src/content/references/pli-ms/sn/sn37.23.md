<!-- @segment sn37.23:1.1 -->

“Upaṭṭhitassati ca hoti, paññavā ca hoti …pe…

<!-- @segment sn37.23:1.2 -->

imehi kho, anuruddha, pañcahi dhammehi samannāgato mātugāmo kāyassa bhedā paraṁ maraṇā sugatiṁ saggaṁ lokaṁ upapajjatī”ti.

<!-- @segment sn37.23:1.3 -->

Navamaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn37.23:2.1 -->

Ime aṭṭha suttantasaṅkhepā.
