<!-- @segment sn1.59:1.1 -->

“Kiṁsu dutiyā purisassa hoti,

<!-- @segment sn1.59:1.2 -->

Kiṁsu cenaṁ pasāsati;

<!-- @segment sn1.59:1.3 -->

Kissa cābhirato macco,

<!-- @segment sn1.59:1.4 -->

Sabbadukkhā pamuccatī”ti.

<!-- @segment sn1.59:2.1 -->

“Saddhā dutiyā purisassa hoti,

<!-- @segment sn1.59:2.2 -->

Paññā cenaṁ pasāsati;

<!-- @segment sn1.59:2.3 -->

Nibbānābhirato macco,

<!-- @segment sn1.59:2.4 -->

Sabbadukkhā pamuccatī”ti.
