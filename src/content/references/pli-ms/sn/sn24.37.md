<!-- @segment sn24.37:1.1 -->

Sāvatthinidānaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn24.37:1.2 -->

“Kismiṁ nu kho, bhikkhave, sati, kiṁ upādāya, kiṁ abhinivissa evaṁ diṭṭhi uppajjati:

<!-- @segment sn24.37:1.3 -->

‘rūpī attā hoti arogo paraṁ maraṇā’”ti?

<!-- @segment sn24.37:1.4 -->

“Bhagavaṁmūlakā no, bhante, dhammā …pe…

<!-- @segment sn24.37:2.1 -->

“Rūpe kho, bhikkhave, sati, rūpaṁ upādāya, rūpaṁ abhinivissa evaṁ diṭṭhi uppajjati:

<!-- @segment sn24.37:2.2 -->

‘rūpī attā hoti arogo paraṁ maraṇā’ti.

<!-- @segment sn24.37:2.3 -->

Vedanāya sati …pe…

<!-- @segment sn24.37:2.4 -->

saññāya sati …

<!-- @segment sn24.37:2.5 -->

saṅkhāresu sati …

<!-- @segment sn24.37:2.6 -->

viññāṇe sati, viññāṇaṁ upādāya, viññāṇaṁ abhinivissa evaṁ diṭṭhi uppajjati:

<!-- @segment sn24.37:2.7 -->

‘rūpī attā hoti arogo paraṁ maraṇā’ti.

<!-- @segment sn24.37:3.1 -->

Taṁ kiṁ maññatha, bhikkhave, rūpaṁ niccaṁ vā aniccaṁ vā”ti?

<!-- @segment sn24.37:3.2 -->

“Aniccaṁ, bhante” …pe…

<!-- @segment sn24.37:3.3 -->

vipariṇāmadhammaṁ, api nu taṁ anupādāya evaṁ diṭṭhi uppajjeyya:

<!-- @segment sn24.37:3.4 -->

‘rūpī attā hoti arogo paraṁ maraṇā’”ti?

<!-- @segment sn24.37:3.5 -->

“No hetaṁ, bhante”.

<!-- @segment sn24.37:3.6 -->

“Iti kho, bhikkhave, dukkhe sati, dukkhaṁ upādāya, dukkhaṁ abhinivissa evaṁ diṭṭhi uppajjati:

<!-- @segment sn24.37:3.7 -->

‘rūpī attā hoti arogo paraṁ maraṇā’”ti?

<!-- @segment sn24.37:3.8 -->

“Vedanā …pe…

<!-- @segment sn24.37:3.9 -->

“no hetaṁ, bhante”.

<!-- @segment sn24.37:3.10 -->

“Iti kho, bhikkhave, dukkhe sati, dukkhaṁ upādāya, dukkhaṁ abhinivissa evaṁ diṭṭhi uppajjati:

<!-- @segment sn24.37:3.11 -->

‘rūpī attā hoti arogo paraṁ maraṇā’”ti.

<!-- @segment sn24.37:3.12 -->

Ekūnavīsatimaṁ.
