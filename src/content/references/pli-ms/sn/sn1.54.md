<!-- @segment sn1.54:1.1 -->

“Kiṁsu vatthu manussānaṁ,

<!-- @segment sn1.54:1.2 -->

kiṁsūdha paramo sakhā;

<!-- @segment sn1.54:1.3 -->

Kiṁsu bhūtā upajīvanti,

<!-- @segment sn1.54:1.4 -->

ye pāṇā pathavissitā”ti.

<!-- @segment sn1.54:2.1 -->

“Puttā vatthu manussānaṁ,

<!-- @segment sn1.54:2.2 -->

bhariyā ca paramo sakhā;

<!-- @segment sn1.54:2.3 -->

Vuṭṭhiṁ bhūtā upajīvanti,

<!-- @segment sn1.54:2.4 -->

ye pāṇā pathavissitā”ti.
