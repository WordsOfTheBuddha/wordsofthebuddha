<!-- @segment sn35.78:1.1 -->

“Yo kho, rādha, anattā tatra te chando pahātabbo.

<!-- @segment sn35.78:1.2 -->

Ko ca, rādha, anattā?

<!-- @segment sn35.78:1.3 -->

Cakkhu kho, rādha, anattā. Tatra te chando pahātabbo.

<!-- @segment sn35.78:1.4 -->

Rūpā …

<!-- @segment sn35.78:1.5 -->

cakkhuviññāṇaṁ …

<!-- @segment sn35.78:1.6 -->

cakkhusamphasso …

<!-- @segment sn35.78:1.7 -->

yampidaṁ cakkhusamphassapaccayā …pe…

<!-- @segment sn35.78:1.8 -->

mano anattā …

<!-- @segment sn35.78:1.9 -->

dhammā …

<!-- @segment sn35.78:1.10 -->

manoviññāṇaṁ …

<!-- @segment sn35.78:1.11 -->

manosamphasso …

<!-- @segment sn35.78:1.12 -->

yampidaṁ manosamphassapaccayā uppajjati vedayitaṁ sukhaṁ vā dukkhaṁ vā adukkhamasukhaṁ vā tampi anattā. Tatra te chando pahātabbo.

<!-- @segment sn35.78:1.13 -->

Yo kho, rādha, anattā tatra te chando pahātabbo”ti.

<!-- @segment sn35.78:1.14 -->

Pañcamaṁ.
