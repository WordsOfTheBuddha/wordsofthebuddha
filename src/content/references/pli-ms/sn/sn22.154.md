<!-- @segment sn22.154:1.1 -->

Sāvatthinidānaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn22.154:1.2 -->

“Kismiṁ nu kho, bhikkhave, sati, kiṁ upādāya, kiṁ abhinivissa micchādiṭṭhi uppajjatī”ti?

<!-- @segment sn22.154:1.3 -->

Bhagavaṁmūlakā no, bhante, dhammā …pe…

<!-- @segment sn22.154:1.4 -->

“rūpe kho, bhikkhave, sati, rūpaṁ upādāya, rūpaṁ abhinivissa micchādiṭṭhi uppajjati.

<!-- @segment sn22.154:1.5 -->

Vedanāya sati …

<!-- @segment sn22.154:1.6 -->

saññāya sati …

<!-- @segment sn22.154:1.7 -->

saṅkhāresu sati …

<!-- @segment sn22.154:1.8 -->

viññāṇe sati, viññāṇaṁ upādāya, viññāṇaṁ abhinivissa micchādiṭṭhi uppajjati.

<!-- @segment sn22.154:1.9 -->

Taṁ kiṁ maññatha, bhikkhave,

<!-- @segment sn22.154:1.10 -->

rūpaṁ niccaṁ vā aniccaṁ vā”ti?

<!-- @segment sn22.154:1.11 -->

“Aniccaṁ, bhante”.

<!-- @segment sn22.154:1.12 -->

“Yaṁ panāniccaṁ …pe…

<!-- @segment sn22.154:1.13 -->

api nu taṁ anupādāya micchādiṭṭhi uppajjeyyā”ti?

<!-- @segment sn22.154:1.14 -->

“No hetaṁ, bhante”.

<!-- @segment sn22.154:1.15 -->

“Vedanā …

<!-- @segment sn22.154:1.16 -->

saññā …

<!-- @segment sn22.154:1.17 -->

saṅkhārā …

<!-- @segment sn22.154:1.18 -->

viññāṇaṁ niccaṁ vā aniccaṁ vā”ti?

<!-- @segment sn22.154:1.19 -->

“Aniccaṁ, bhante”.

<!-- @segment sn22.154:1.20 -->

“Yaṁ panāniccaṁ dukkhaṁ vā taṁ sukhaṁ vā”ti?

<!-- @segment sn22.154:1.21 -->

“Dukkhaṁ, bhante”.

<!-- @segment sn22.154:1.22 -->

“Yaṁ panāniccaṁ dukkhaṁ vipariṇāmadhammaṁ, api nu taṁ anupādāya micchādiṭṭhi uppajjeyyā”ti?

<!-- @segment sn22.154:1.23 -->

“No hetaṁ, bhante”.

<!-- @segment sn22.154:1.24 -->

“Evaṁ passaṁ …pe…

<!-- @segment sn22.154:1.25 -->

nāparaṁ itthattāyāti pajānātī”ti.

<!-- @segment sn22.154:1.26 -->

Pañcamaṁ.
