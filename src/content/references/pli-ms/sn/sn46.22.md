<!-- @segment sn46.22:1.1 -->

“Satta vo, bhikkhave, bojjhaṅge desessāmi;

<!-- @segment sn46.22:1.2 -->

taṁ suṇātha.

<!-- @segment sn46.22:1.3 -->

Katame ca, bhikkhave, satta bojjhaṅgā?

<!-- @segment sn46.22:1.4 -->

Satisambojjhaṅgo …pe… upekkhāsambojjhaṅgo—

<!-- @segment sn46.22:1.5 -->

ime kho, bhikkhave, satta bojjhaṅgā”ti.

<!-- @segment sn46.22:1.6 -->

Dutiyaṁ.
