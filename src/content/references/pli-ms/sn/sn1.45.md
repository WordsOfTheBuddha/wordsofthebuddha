<!-- @segment sn1.45:1.1 -->

“Anomanāmaṁ nipuṇatthadassiṁ,

<!-- @segment sn1.45:1.2 -->

Paññādadaṁ kāmālaye asattaṁ;

<!-- @segment sn1.45:1.3 -->

Taṁ passatha sabbaviduṁ sumedhaṁ,

<!-- @segment sn1.45:1.4 -->

Ariye pathe kamamānaṁ mahesin”ti.
