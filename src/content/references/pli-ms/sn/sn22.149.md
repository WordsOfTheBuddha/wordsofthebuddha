<!-- @segment sn22.149:1.1 -->

Sāvatthinidānaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn22.149:1.2 -->

“Saddhāpabbajitassa, bhikkhave, kulaputtassa ayamanudhammo hoti—

<!-- @segment sn22.149:1.3 -->

yaṁ rūpe anattānupassī vihareyya.

<!-- @segment sn22.149:1.4 -->

Vedanāya …

<!-- @segment sn22.149:1.5 -->

saññāya …

<!-- @segment sn22.149:1.6 -->

saṅkhāresu …

<!-- @segment sn22.149:1.7 -->

viññāṇe anattānupassī vihareyya.

<!-- @segment sn22.149:1.8 -->

Anattānupassī viharanto, vedanāya …

<!-- @segment sn22.149:1.9 -->

saññāya …

<!-- @segment sn22.149:1.10 -->

saṅkhāresu …

<!-- @segment sn22.149:1.11 -->

viññāṇe anattānupassī viharanto rūpaṁ parijānāti, vedanaṁ …pe…

<!-- @segment sn22.149:1.12 -->

saññaṁ …

<!-- @segment sn22.149:1.13 -->

saṅkhāre …

<!-- @segment sn22.149:1.14 -->

viññāṇaṁ parijānāti.

<!-- @segment sn22.149:1.15 -->

So rūpaṁ parijānaṁ vedanaṁ parijānaṁ saññaṁ parijānaṁ saṅkhāre parijānaṁ viññāṇaṁ parijānaṁ parimuccati rūpamhā, parimuccati vedanāya, parimuccati saññāya, parimuccati saṅkhārehi, parimuccati viññāṇamhā, parimuccati jātiyā jarāya maraṇena sokehi paridevehi dukkhehi domanassehi upāyāsehi;

<!-- @segment sn22.149:1.16 -->

‘parimuccati dukkhasmā’ti vadāmī”ti.

<!-- @segment sn22.149:1.17 -->

Cuddasamaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn22.149:1.18 -->

Kukkuḷavaggo catuttho.

<!-- @segment sn22.149:2.0 -->

Tassuddānaṁ

<!-- @segment sn22.149:2.1 -->

Kukkuḷā tayo aniccena,

<!-- @segment sn22.149:2.2 -->

dukkhena apare tayo;

<!-- @segment sn22.149:2.3 -->

Anattena tayo vuttā,

<!-- @segment sn22.149:2.4 -->

kulaputtena dve dukāti.
