<!-- @segment sn22.139:1.1 -->

Sāvatthinidānaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn22.139:1.2 -->

“Yaṁ, bhikkhave, aniccaṁ; tatra vo chandarāgo pahātabbo.

<!-- @segment sn22.139:1.3 -->

Kiñca, bhikkhave, aniccaṁ?

<!-- @segment sn22.139:1.4 -->

Rūpaṁ, bhikkhave, aniccaṁ, tatra vo chandarāgo pahātabbo.

<!-- @segment sn22.139:1.5 -->

Vedanā aniccā …

<!-- @segment sn22.139:1.6 -->

saññā …

<!-- @segment sn22.139:1.7 -->

saṅkhārā …

<!-- @segment sn22.139:1.8 -->

viññāṇaṁ aniccaṁ; tatra vo chandarāgo pahātabbo.

<!-- @segment sn22.139:1.9 -->

Yaṁ, bhikkhave, aniccaṁ; tatra vo chandarāgo pahātabbo”ti.

<!-- @segment sn22.139:1.10 -->

Catutthaṁ.
