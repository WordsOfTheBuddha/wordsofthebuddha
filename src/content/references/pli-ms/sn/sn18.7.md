<!-- @segment sn18.7:1.1 -->

Sāvatthiyaṁ viharati.

<!-- @segment sn18.7:1.2 -->

“Taṁ kiṁ maññasi, rāhula,

<!-- @segment sn18.7:1.3 -->

rūpasañcetanā niccā vā aniccā vā”ti?

<!-- @segment sn18.7:1.4 -->

“Aniccā, bhante” …

<!-- @segment sn18.7:1.5 -->

“saddasañcetanā …pe…

<!-- @segment sn18.7:1.6 -->

gandhasañcetanā …

<!-- @segment sn18.7:1.7 -->

rasasañcetanā …

<!-- @segment sn18.7:1.8 -->

phoṭṭhabbasañcetanā …

<!-- @segment sn18.7:1.9 -->

dhammasañcetanā niccā vā aniccā vā”ti?

<!-- @segment sn18.7:1.10 -->

“Aniccā, bhante” …

<!-- @segment sn18.7:1.11 -->

“evaṁ passaṁ, rāhula, sutavā ariyasāvako rūpasañcetanāyapi nibbindati …pe… saddasañcetanāyapi nibbindati … gandhasañcetanāyapi nibbindati … rasasañcetanāyapi nibbindati … phoṭṭhabbasañcetanāyapi nibbindati … dhammasañcetanāyapi nibbindati …pe…

<!-- @segment sn18.7:1.12 -->

pajānātī”ti.

<!-- @segment sn18.7:1.13 -->

Sattamaṁ.
