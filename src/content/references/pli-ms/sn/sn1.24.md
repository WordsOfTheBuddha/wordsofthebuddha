<!-- @segment sn1.24:1.1 -->

“Yato yato mano nivāraye,

<!-- @segment sn1.24:1.2 -->

Na dukkhameti naṁ tato tato;

<!-- @segment sn1.24:1.3 -->

Sa sabbato mano nivāraye,

<!-- @segment sn1.24:1.4 -->

Sa sabbato dukkhā pamuccatī”ti.

<!-- @segment sn1.24:2.1 -->

“Na sabbato mano nivāraye,

<!-- @segment sn1.24:2.2 -->

Na mano saṁyatattamāgataṁ;

<!-- @segment sn1.24:2.3 -->

Yato yato ca pāpakaṁ,

<!-- @segment sn1.24:2.4 -->

Tato tato mano nivāraye”ti.
