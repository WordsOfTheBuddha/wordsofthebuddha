<!-- @segment sn24.17:1.1 -->

Sāvatthinidānaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn24.17:1.2 -->

“Kismiṁ nu kho, bhikkhave, sati, kiṁ upādāya, kiṁ abhinivissa evaṁ diṭṭhi uppajjati:

<!-- @segment sn24.17:1.3 -->

‘hoti ca na ca hoti tathāgato paraṁ maraṇā’”ti?

<!-- @segment sn24.17:1.4 -->

“Bhagavaṁmūlakā no, bhante, dhammā …pe…

<!-- @segment sn24.17:1.5 -->

niyato sambodhiparāyano”ti.

<!-- @segment sn24.17:1.6 -->

Sattarasamaṁ.
