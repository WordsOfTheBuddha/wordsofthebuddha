<!-- @segment sn12.43:1.1 -->

Sāvatthiyaṁ viharati.

<!-- @segment sn12.43:1.2 -->

“Dukkhassa, bhikkhave, samudayañca atthaṅgamañca desessāmi.

<!-- @segment sn12.43:1.3 -->

Taṁ suṇātha, sādhukaṁ manasi karotha, bhāsissāmī”ti.

<!-- @segment sn12.43:1.4 -->

“Evaṁ, bhante”ti kho te bhikkhū bhagavato paccassosuṁ.

<!-- @segment sn12.43:1.5 -->

Bhagavā etadavoca:

<!-- @segment sn12.43:2.1 -->

“Katamo ca, bhikkhave, dukkhassa samudayo?

<!-- @segment sn12.43:2.2 -->

Cakkhuñca paṭicca rūpe ca uppajjati cakkhuviññāṇaṁ. Tiṇṇaṁ saṅgati phasso.

<!-- @segment sn12.43:2.3 -->

Phassapaccayā vedanā;

<!-- @segment sn12.43:2.4 -->

vedanāpaccayā taṇhā.

<!-- @segment sn12.43:2.5 -->

Ayaṁ kho, bhikkhave, dukkhassa samudayo.

<!-- @segment sn12.43:3.1 -->

Sotañca paṭicca sadde ca uppajjati sotaviññāṇaṁ …pe…

<!-- @segment sn12.43:3.2 -->

ghānañca paṭicca gandhe ca …pe…

<!-- @segment sn12.43:3.3 -->

jivhañca paṭicca rase ca …pe…

<!-- @segment sn12.43:3.4 -->

kāyañca paṭicca phoṭṭhabbe ca …pe…

<!-- @segment sn12.43:3.5 -->

manañca paṭicca dhamme ca uppajjati manoviññāṇaṁ. Tiṇṇaṁ saṅgati phasso.

<!-- @segment sn12.43:3.6 -->

Phassapaccayā vedanā;

<!-- @segment sn12.43:3.7 -->

vedanāpaccayā taṇhā.

<!-- @segment sn12.43:3.8 -->

Ayaṁ kho, bhikkhave, dukkhassa samudayo.

<!-- @segment sn12.43:4.1 -->

Katamo ca, bhikkhave, dukkhassa atthaṅgamo?

<!-- @segment sn12.43:4.2 -->

Cakkhuñca paṭicca rūpe ca uppajjati cakkhuviññāṇaṁ. Tiṇṇaṁ saṅgati phasso.

<!-- @segment sn12.43:4.3 -->

Phassapaccayā vedanā;

<!-- @segment sn12.43:4.4 -->

vedanāpaccayā taṇhā.

<!-- @segment sn12.43:4.5 -->

Tassāyeva taṇhāya asesavirāganirodhā upādānanirodho;

<!-- @segment sn12.43:4.6 -->

upādānanirodhā bhavanirodho;

<!-- @segment sn12.43:4.7 -->

bhavanirodhā jātinirodho;

<!-- @segment sn12.43:4.8 -->

jātinirodhā jarāmaraṇaṁ sokaparidevadukkhadomanassupāyāsā nirujjhanti.

<!-- @segment sn12.43:4.9 -->

Evametassa kevalassa dukkhakkhandhassa nirodho hoti.

<!-- @segment sn12.43:4.10 -->

Ayaṁ kho, bhikkhave, dukkhassa atthaṅgamo.

<!-- @segment sn12.43:5.1 -->

Sotañca paṭicca sadde ca uppajjati sotaviññāṇaṁ …pe…

<!-- @segment sn12.43:5.2 -->

ghānañca paṭicca gandhe ca …pe…

<!-- @segment sn12.43:5.3 -->

jivhañca paṭicca rase ca …pe…

<!-- @segment sn12.43:5.4 -->

kāyañca paṭicca phoṭṭhabbe ca …pe…

<!-- @segment sn12.43:5.5 -->

manañca paṭicca dhamme ca uppajjati manoviññāṇaṁ. Tiṇṇaṁ saṅgati phasso.

<!-- @segment sn12.43:5.6 -->

Phassapaccayā vedanā;

<!-- @segment sn12.43:5.7 -->

vedanāpaccayā taṇhā.

<!-- @segment sn12.43:5.8 -->

Tassāyeva taṇhāya asesavirāganirodhā upādānanirodho;

<!-- @segment sn12.43:5.9 -->

upādānanirodhā bhavanirodho;

<!-- @segment sn12.43:5.10 -->

bhavanirodhā jātinirodho;

<!-- @segment sn12.43:5.11 -->

jātinirodhā jarāmaraṇaṁ sokaparidevadukkhadomanassupāyāsā nirujjhanti.

<!-- @segment sn12.43:5.12 -->

Evametassa kevalassa dukkhakkhandhassa nirodho hoti.

<!-- @segment sn12.43:5.13 -->

Ayaṁ kho, bhikkhave, dukkhassa atthaṅgamo”ti.

<!-- @segment sn12.43:5.14 -->

Tatiyaṁ.
