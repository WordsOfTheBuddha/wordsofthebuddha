<!-- @segment sn22.148:1.1 -->

Sāvatthinidānaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn22.148:1.2 -->

“Saddhāpabbajitassa, bhikkhave, kulaputtassa ayamanudhammo hoti—

<!-- @segment sn22.148:1.3 -->

yaṁ rūpe dukkhānupassī vihareyya.

<!-- @segment sn22.148:1.4 -->

Vedanāya …

<!-- @segment sn22.148:1.5 -->

saññāya …

<!-- @segment sn22.148:1.6 -->

saṅkhāresu …

<!-- @segment sn22.148:1.7 -->

viññāṇe dukkhānupassī vihareyya …pe…

<!-- @segment sn22.148:1.8 -->

‘parimuccati dukkhasmā’ti vadāmī”ti.

<!-- @segment sn22.148:1.9 -->

Terasamaṁ.
