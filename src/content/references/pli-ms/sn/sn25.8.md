<!-- @segment sn25.8:1.1 -->

Sāvatthinidānaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn25.8:1.2 -->

“Rūpataṇhā, bhikkhave, aniccā vipariṇāmī aññathābhāvī;

<!-- @segment sn25.8:1.3 -->

saddataṇhā …

<!-- @segment sn25.8:1.4 -->

gandhataṇhā …

<!-- @segment sn25.8:1.5 -->

rasataṇhā …

<!-- @segment sn25.8:1.6 -->

phoṭṭhabbataṇhā …

<!-- @segment sn25.8:1.7 -->

dhammataṇhā aniccā vipariṇāmī aññathābhāvī.

<!-- @segment sn25.8:1.8 -->

Yo, bhikkhave, ime dhamme evaṁ saddahati adhimuccati, ayaṁ vuccati ‘saddhānusārī …pe…

<!-- @segment sn25.8:1.9 -->

sambodhiparāyano’”ti.

<!-- @segment sn25.8:1.10 -->

Aṭṭhamaṁ.
