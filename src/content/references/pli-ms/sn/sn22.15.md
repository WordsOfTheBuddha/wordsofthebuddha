<!-- @segment sn22.15:1.1 -->

Sāvatthinidānaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn22.15:1.2 -->

“Rūpaṁ, bhikkhave, aniccaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn22.15:1.3 -->

Yadaniccaṁ taṁ dukkhaṁ;

<!-- @segment sn22.15:1.4 -->

yaṁ dukkhaṁ tadanattā;

<!-- @segment sn22.15:1.5 -->

yadanattā taṁ ‘netaṁ mama, nesohamasmi, na meso attā’ti evametaṁ yathābhūtaṁ sammappaññāya daṭṭhabbaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn22.15:1.6 -->

Vedanā aniccā.

<!-- @segment sn22.15:1.7 -->

Yadaniccaṁ taṁ dukkhaṁ;

<!-- @segment sn22.15:1.8 -->

yaṁ dukkhaṁ tadanattā;

<!-- @segment sn22.15:1.9 -->

yadanattā taṁ ‘netaṁ mama, nesohamasmi, na meso attā’ti evametaṁ yathābhūtaṁ sammappaññāya daṭṭhabbaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn22.15:1.10 -->

Saññā aniccā …pe…

<!-- @segment sn22.15:1.11 -->

saṅkhārā aniccā …

<!-- @segment sn22.15:1.12 -->

viññāṇaṁ aniccaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn22.15:1.13 -->

Yadaniccaṁ taṁ dukkhaṁ;

<!-- @segment sn22.15:1.14 -->

yaṁ dukkhaṁ tadanattā;

<!-- @segment sn22.15:1.15 -->

yadanattā taṁ ‘netaṁ mama, nesohamasmi, na meso attā’ti evametaṁ yathābhūtaṁ sammappaññāya daṭṭhabbaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn22.15:1.16 -->

Evaṁ passaṁ …pe…

<!-- @segment sn22.15:1.17 -->

nāparaṁ itthattāyā’ti pajānātī”ti.

<!-- @segment sn22.15:1.18 -->

Catutthaṁ.
