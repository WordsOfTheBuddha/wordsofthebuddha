<!-- @segment sn27.6:1.1 -->

Sāvatthinidānaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn27.6:1.2 -->

“Yo, bhikkhave, rūpasaññāya chandarāgo, cittasseso upakkileso.

<!-- @segment sn27.6:1.3 -->

Yo saddasaññāya …

<!-- @segment sn27.6:1.4 -->

yo gandhasaññāya …

<!-- @segment sn27.6:1.5 -->

yo rasasaññāya …

<!-- @segment sn27.6:1.6 -->

yo phoṭṭhabbasaññāya …

<!-- @segment sn27.6:1.7 -->

yo dhammasaññāya chandarāgo, cittasseso upakkileso.

<!-- @segment sn27.6:1.8 -->

Yato kho, bhikkhave, bhikkhuno …pe…

<!-- @segment sn27.6:1.9 -->

abhiññā sacchikaraṇīyesu dhammesū”ti.

<!-- @segment sn27.6:1.10 -->

Chaṭṭhaṁ.
