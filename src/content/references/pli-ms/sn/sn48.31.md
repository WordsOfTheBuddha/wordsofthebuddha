<!-- @segment sn48.31:1.1 -->

“Pañcimāni, bhikkhave, indriyāni.

<!-- @segment sn48.31:1.2 -->

Katamāni pañca?

<!-- @segment sn48.31:1.3 -->

Sukhindriyaṁ, dukkhindriyaṁ, somanassindriyaṁ, domanassindriyaṁ, upekkhindriyaṁ—

<!-- @segment sn48.31:1.4 -->

imāni kho, bhikkhave, pañcindriyānī”ti.

<!-- @segment sn48.31:1.5 -->

Paṭhamaṁ.
