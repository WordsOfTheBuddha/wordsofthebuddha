<!-- @segment sn2.16:1.1 -->

Ekamantaṁ ṭhito kho vāsudatto devaputto bhagavato santike imaṁ gāthaṁ abhāsi:

<!-- @segment sn2.16:2.1 -->

“Sattiyā viya omaṭṭho,

<!-- @segment sn2.16:2.2 -->

ḍayhamānova matthake;

<!-- @segment sn2.16:2.3 -->

Kāmarāgappahānāya,

<!-- @segment sn2.16:2.4 -->

sato bhikkhu paribbaje”ti.

<!-- @segment sn2.16:3.1 -->

“Sattiyā viya omaṭṭho,

<!-- @segment sn2.16:3.2 -->

ḍayhamānova matthake;

<!-- @segment sn2.16:3.3 -->

Sakkāyadiṭṭhippahānāya,

<!-- @segment sn2.16:3.4 -->

sato bhikkhu paribbaje”ti.
