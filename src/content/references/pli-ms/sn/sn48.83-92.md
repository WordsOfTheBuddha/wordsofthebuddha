<!-- @segment sn48.83-92:1.1 -->

(Appamādavaggo vitthāretabbo.)

<!-- @segment sn48.83-92:2.0 -->

Tassuddānaṁ

<!-- @segment sn48.83-92:2.1 -->

Tathāgataṁ padaṁ kūṭaṁ,

<!-- @segment sn48.83-92:2.2 -->

mūlaṁ sārena vassikaṁ;

<!-- @segment sn48.83-92:2.3 -->

Rājā candimasūriyā,

<!-- @segment sn48.83-92:2.4 -->

vatthena dasamaṁ padanti.
