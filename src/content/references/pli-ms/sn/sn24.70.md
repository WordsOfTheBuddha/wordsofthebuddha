<!-- @segment sn24.70:1.1 -->

Sāvatthinidānaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn24.70:1.2 -->

“Kismiṁ nu kho, bhikkhave, sati, kiṁ upādāya, kiṁ abhinivissa evaṁ diṭṭhi uppajjati:

<!-- @segment sn24.70:1.3 -->

‘adukkhamasukhī attā hoti arogo paraṁ maraṇā’”ti?

<!-- @segment sn24.70:1.4 -->

“Bhagavaṁmūlakā no, bhante, dhammā …pe….

<!-- @segment sn24.70:2.1 -->

“Rūpe kho, bhikkhave, sati, rūpaṁ upādāya, rūpaṁ abhinivissa evaṁ diṭṭhi uppajjati:

<!-- @segment sn24.70:2.2 -->

‘adukkhamasukhī attā hoti arogo paraṁ maraṇā’”ti.

<!-- @segment sn24.70:2.3 -->

“Vedanāya sati …pe…

<!-- @segment sn24.70:2.4 -->

saññāya sati …

<!-- @segment sn24.70:2.5 -->

saṅkhāresu sati …

<!-- @segment sn24.70:2.6 -->

viññāṇe sati, viññāṇaṁ upādāya, viññāṇaṁ abhinivissa evaṁ diṭṭhi uppajjati:

<!-- @segment sn24.70:2.7 -->

‘adukkhamasukhī attā hoti arogo paraṁ maraṇā’ti.

<!-- @segment sn24.70:3.1 -->

Taṁ kiṁ maññatha, bhikkhave,

<!-- @segment sn24.70:3.2 -->

rūpaṁ niccaṁ vā aniccaṁ vā”ti?

<!-- @segment sn24.70:3.3 -->

“Aniccaṁ, bhante” …pe… vipariṇāmadhammaṁ,

<!-- @segment sn24.70:3.4 -->

api nu taṁ anupādāya evaṁ diṭṭhi uppajjeyya:

<!-- @segment sn24.70:3.5 -->

‘adukkhamasukhī attā hoti arogo paraṁ maraṇā’”ti?

<!-- @segment sn24.70:3.6 -->

“No hetaṁ, bhante”.

<!-- @segment sn24.70:3.7 -->

“Iti kho, bhikkhave, yadaniccaṁ taṁ dukkhaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn24.70:3.8 -->

Tasmiṁ sati, tadupādāya evaṁ diṭṭhi uppajjati:

<!-- @segment sn24.70:3.9 -->

‘adukkhamasukhī attā hoti arogo paraṁ maraṇā’”ti.

<!-- @segment sn24.70:3.10 -->

“Vedanā …pe…

<!-- @segment sn24.70:3.11 -->

saññā …

<!-- @segment sn24.70:3.12 -->

saṅkhārā …

<!-- @segment sn24.70:3.13 -->

viññāṇaṁ niccaṁ vā aniccaṁ vā”ti?

<!-- @segment sn24.70:3.14 -->

“Aniccaṁ, bhante” …pe… vipariṇāmadhammaṁ,

<!-- @segment sn24.70:3.15 -->

api nu taṁ anupādāya evaṁ diṭṭhi uppajjeyya:

<!-- @segment sn24.70:3.16 -->

‘adukkhamasukhī attā hoti arogo paraṁ maraṇā’”ti?

<!-- @segment sn24.70:3.17 -->

“No hetaṁ, bhante”.

<!-- @segment sn24.70:3.18 -->

“Iti kho, bhikkhave, yadaniccaṁ taṁ dukkhaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn24.70:3.19 -->

Tasmiṁ sati, tadupādāya evaṁ diṭṭhi uppajjati:

<!-- @segment sn24.70:3.20 -->

‘adukkhamasukhī attā hoti arogo paraṁ maraṇā’”ti.

<!-- @segment sn24.70:3.21 -->

Chabbīsatimaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn24.70:3.22 -->

Tatiyapeyyālo.
