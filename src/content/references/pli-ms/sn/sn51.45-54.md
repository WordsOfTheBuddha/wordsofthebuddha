<!-- @segment sn51.45-54:1.1 -->

(Appamādavaggo vitthāretabbo.)

<!-- @segment sn51.45-54:2.0 -->

Tassuddānaṁ

<!-- @segment sn51.45-54:2.1 -->

Tathāgataṁ padaṁ kūṭaṁ,

<!-- @segment sn51.45-54:2.2 -->

mūlaṁ sāro ca vassikaṁ;

<!-- @segment sn51.45-54:2.3 -->

Rājā candimasūriyā,

<!-- @segment sn51.45-54:2.4 -->

vatthena dasamaṁ padanti.
