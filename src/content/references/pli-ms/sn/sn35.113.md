<!-- @segment sn35.113:1.1 -->

Ekaṁ samayaṁ bhagavā nātike viharati giñjakāvasathe.

<!-- @segment sn35.113:1.2 -->

Atha kho bhagavā rahogato paṭisallīno imaṁ dhammapariyāyaṁ abhāsi:

<!-- @segment sn35.113:1.3 -->

“cakkhuñca paṭicca rūpe ca uppajjati cakkhuviññāṇaṁ. Tiṇṇaṁ saṅgati phasso.

<!-- @segment sn35.113:1.4 -->

Phassapaccayā vedanā;

<!-- @segment sn35.113:1.5 -->

vedanāpaccayā taṇhā;

<!-- @segment sn35.113:1.6 -->

taṇhāpaccayā upādānaṁ;

<!-- @segment sn35.113:1.7 -->

upādānapaccayā bhavo;

<!-- @segment sn35.113:1.8 -->

bhavapaccayā jāti;

<!-- @segment sn35.113:1.9 -->

jātipaccayā jarāmaraṇaṁ sokaparidevadukkhadomanassupāyāsā sambhavanti.

<!-- @segment sn35.113:1.10 -->

Evametassa kevalassa dukkhakkhandhassa samudayo hoti.

<!-- @segment sn35.113:1.11 -->

Jivhañca paṭicca rase ca uppajjati …pe…

<!-- @segment sn35.113:1.12 -->

manañca paṭicca dhamme ca uppajjati manoviññāṇaṁ. Tiṇṇaṁ saṅgati phasso.

<!-- @segment sn35.113:1.13 -->

Phassapaccayā vedanā;

<!-- @segment sn35.113:1.14 -->

vedanāpaccayā taṇhā;

<!-- @segment sn35.113:1.15 -->

taṇhāpaccayā upādānaṁ;

<!-- @segment sn35.113:1.16 -->

upādānapaccayā bhavo;

<!-- @segment sn35.113:1.17 -->

bhavapaccayā jāti;

<!-- @segment sn35.113:1.18 -->

jātipaccayā jarāmaraṇaṁ sokaparidevadukkhadomanassupāyāsā sambhavanti.

<!-- @segment sn35.113:1.19 -->

Evametassa kevalassa dukkhakkhandhassa samudayo hoti.

<!-- @segment sn35.113:2.1 -->

Cakkhuñca paṭicca rūpe ca uppajjati cakkhuviññāṇaṁ. Tiṇṇaṁ saṅgati phasso.

<!-- @segment sn35.113:2.2 -->

Phassapaccayā vedanā;

<!-- @segment sn35.113:2.3 -->

vedanāpaccayā taṇhā.

<!-- @segment sn35.113:2.4 -->

Tassāyeva taṇhāya asesavirāganirodhā upādānanirodho;

<!-- @segment sn35.113:2.5 -->

upādānanirodhā bhavanirodho;

<!-- @segment sn35.113:2.6 -->

bhavanirodhā jātinirodho;

<!-- @segment sn35.113:2.7 -->

jātinirodhā jarāmaraṇaṁ sokaparidevadukkhadomanassupāyāsā nirujjhanti.

<!-- @segment sn35.113:2.8 -->

Evametassa kevalassa dukkhakkhandhassa nirodho hoti …pe…

<!-- @segment sn35.113:2.9 -->

jivhañca paṭicca rase ca uppajjati …pe…

<!-- @segment sn35.113:2.10 -->

manañca paṭicca dhamme ca uppajjati manoviññāṇaṁ. Tiṇṇaṁ saṅgati phasso.

<!-- @segment sn35.113:2.11 -->

Phassapaccayā vedanā;

<!-- @segment sn35.113:2.12 -->

vedanāpaccayā taṇhā.

<!-- @segment sn35.113:2.13 -->

Tassāyeva taṇhāya asesavirāganirodhā upādānanirodho;

<!-- @segment sn35.113:2.14 -->

upādānanirodhā …pe…

<!-- @segment sn35.113:2.15 -->

evametassa kevalassa dukkhakkhandhassa nirodho hotī”ti.

<!-- @segment sn35.113:3.1 -->

Tena kho pana samayena aññataro bhikkhu bhagavato upassuti ṭhito hoti.

<!-- @segment sn35.113:3.2 -->

Addasā kho bhagavā taṁ bhikkhuṁ upassuti ṭhitaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn35.113:3.3 -->

Disvāna taṁ bhikkhuṁ etadavoca:

<!-- @segment sn35.113:3.4 -->

“assosi no tvaṁ, bhikkhu, imaṁ dhammapariyāyan”ti?

<!-- @segment sn35.113:3.5 -->

“Evaṁ, bhante”.

<!-- @segment sn35.113:3.6 -->

“Uggaṇhāhi tvaṁ, bhikkhu, imaṁ dhammapariyāyaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn35.113:3.7 -->

Pariyāpuṇāhi tvaṁ, bhikkhu, imaṁ dhammapariyāyaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn35.113:3.8 -->

Dhārehi tvaṁ, bhikkhu, imaṁ dhammapariyāyaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn35.113:3.9 -->

Atthasañhitoyaṁ, bhikkhu, dhammapariyāyo ādibrahmacariyako”ti.

<!-- @segment sn35.113:3.10 -->

Dasamaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn35.113:3.11 -->

Yogakkhemivaggo paṭhamo.

<!-- @segment sn35.113:4.0 -->

Tassuddānaṁ

<!-- @segment sn35.113:4.1 -->

Yogakkhemi upādāya,

<!-- @segment sn35.113:4.2 -->

Dukkhaṁ loko ca seyyo ca;

<!-- @segment sn35.113:4.3 -->

Saṁyojanaṁ upādānaṁ,

<!-- @segment sn35.113:4.4 -->

Dve parijānaṁ upassutīti.
