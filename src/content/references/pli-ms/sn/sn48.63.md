<!-- @segment sn48.63:1.1 -->

“Pañcimāni, bhikkhave, indriyāni bhāvitāni bahulīkatāni addhānapariññāya saṁvattanti.

<!-- @segment sn48.63:1.2 -->

Katamāni pañca?

<!-- @segment sn48.63:1.3 -->

Saddhindriyaṁ …pe… paññindriyaṁ—

<!-- @segment sn48.63:1.4 -->

imāni kho, bhikkhave, pañcindriyāni bhāvitāni bahulīkatāni addhānapariññāya saṁvattantī”ti.

<!-- @segment sn48.63:1.5 -->

Tatiyaṁ.
