<!-- @segment sn35.227:1.1 -->

“Rūpā, bhikkhave, anattā. Saddā … gandhā … rasā … phoṭṭhabbā … dhammā anattā.

<!-- @segment sn35.227:1.2 -->

Evaṁ passaṁ …pe…

<!-- @segment sn35.227:1.3 -->

nāparaṁ itthattāyāti pajānātī”ti.

<!-- @segment sn35.227:1.4 -->

Saṭṭhipeyyālo samatto.

<!-- @segment sn35.227:2.0 -->

Tassuddānaṁ

<!-- @segment sn35.227:2.1 -->

Chandenaṭṭhārasa honti,

<!-- @segment sn35.227:2.2 -->

atītena ca dve nava;

<!-- @segment sn35.227:2.3 -->

Yadaniccāṭṭhārasa vuttā,

<!-- @segment sn35.227:2.4 -->

tayo ajjhattabāhirā;

<!-- @segment sn35.227:2.5 -->

Peyyālo saṭṭhiko vutto,

<!-- @segment sn35.227:2.6 -->

buddhenādiccabandhunāti.

<!-- @segment sn35.227:2.7 -->

Suttantāni saṭṭhi.
