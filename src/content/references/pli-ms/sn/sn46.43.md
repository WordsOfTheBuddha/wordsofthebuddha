<!-- @segment sn46.43:1.1 -->

“Mārasenappamaddanaṁ vo, bhikkhave, maggaṁ desessāmi;

<!-- @segment sn46.43:1.2 -->

taṁ suṇātha.

<!-- @segment sn46.43:1.3 -->

Katamo ca, bhikkhave, mārasenappamaddano maggo?

<!-- @segment sn46.43:1.4 -->

Yadidaṁ—

<!-- @segment sn46.43:1.5 -->

satta bojjhaṅgā.

<!-- @segment sn46.43:1.6 -->

Katame satta?

<!-- @segment sn46.43:1.7 -->

Satisambojjhaṅgo …pe… upekkhāsambojjhaṅgo—

<!-- @segment sn46.43:1.8 -->

ayaṁ kho, bhikkhave, mārasenappamaddano maggo”ti.

<!-- @segment sn46.43:1.9 -->

Tatiyaṁ.
