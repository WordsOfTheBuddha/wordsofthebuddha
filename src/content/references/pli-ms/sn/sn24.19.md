<!-- @segment sn24.19:1.1 -->

Sāvatthinidānaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn24.19:1.2 -->

“Kismiṁ nu kho, bhikkhave, sati, kiṁ upādāya, kiṁ abhinivissa evaṁ diṭṭhi uppajjati:

<!-- @segment sn24.19:1.3 -->

‘na vātā vāyanti, na najjo sandanti, na gabbhiniyo vijāyanti, na candimasūriyā udenti vā apenti vā, esikaṭṭhāyiṭṭhitā’”ti?

<!-- @segment sn24.19:1.4 -->

“Bhagavaṁmūlakā no, bhante, dhammā …pe…

<!-- @segment sn24.19:2.1 -->

“Rūpe kho, bhikkhave, sati, rūpaṁ upādāya, rūpaṁ abhinivissa evaṁ diṭṭhi uppajjati:

<!-- @segment sn24.19:2.2 -->

‘na vātā vāyanti …pe… esikaṭṭhāyiṭṭhitā’ti.

<!-- @segment sn24.19:2.3 -->

Vedanāya sati …pe…

<!-- @segment sn24.19:2.4 -->

saññāya sati …pe…

<!-- @segment sn24.19:2.5 -->

saṅkhāresu sati …

<!-- @segment sn24.19:2.6 -->

viññāṇe sati, viññāṇaṁ upādāya, viññāṇaṁ abhinivissa evaṁ diṭṭhi uppajjati:

<!-- @segment sn24.19:2.7 -->

‘na vātā vāyanti …pe… esikaṭṭhāyiṭṭhitā’ti.

<!-- @segment sn24.19:3.1 -->

Taṁ kiṁ maññatha, bhikkhave,

<!-- @segment sn24.19:3.2 -->

rūpaṁ niccaṁ vā aniccaṁ vā”ti?

<!-- @segment sn24.19:3.3 -->

“Aniccaṁ, bhante” …pe… vipariṇāmadhammaṁ,

<!-- @segment sn24.19:3.4 -->

api nu taṁ anupādāya evaṁ diṭṭhi uppajjeyya—

<!-- @segment sn24.19:3.5 -->

na vātā vāyanti …pe… esikaṭṭhāyiṭṭhitā”ti?

<!-- @segment sn24.19:3.6 -->

“No hetaṁ, bhante”.

<!-- @segment sn24.19:3.7 -->

“Iti kho, bhikkhave, dukkhe sati, dukkhaṁ upādāya, dukkhaṁ abhinivissa evaṁ diṭṭhi uppajjati:

<!-- @segment sn24.19:3.8 -->

‘na vātā vāyanti …pe… esikaṭṭhāyiṭṭhitā’”ti.

<!-- @segment sn24.19:3.9 -->

“Vedanā …

<!-- @segment sn24.19:3.10 -->

saññā …

<!-- @segment sn24.19:3.11 -->

saṅkhārā …

<!-- @segment sn24.19:3.12 -->

viññāṇaṁ niccaṁ vā aniccaṁ vā”ti?

<!-- @segment sn24.19:3.13 -->

“Aniccaṁ, bhante” …pe… vipariṇāmadhammaṁ,

<!-- @segment sn24.19:3.14 -->

api nu taṁ anupādāya evaṁ diṭṭhi uppajjeyya

<!-- @segment sn24.19:3.15 -->

‘na vātā vāyanti …pe… esikaṭṭhāyiṭṭhitā’”ti?

<!-- @segment sn24.19:3.16 -->

“No hetaṁ, bhante”.

<!-- @segment sn24.19:3.17 -->

“Iti kho, bhikkhave, dukkhe sati, dukkhaṁ upādāya, dukkhaṁ abhinivissa evaṁ diṭṭhi uppajjeyya:

<!-- @segment sn24.19:3.18 -->

‘na vātā vāyanti, na najjo sandanti, na gabbhiniyo vijāyanti, na candimasūriyā udenti vā apenti vā, esikaṭṭhāyiṭṭhitā’”ti.

<!-- @segment sn24.19:3.19 -->

Paṭhamaṁ.
