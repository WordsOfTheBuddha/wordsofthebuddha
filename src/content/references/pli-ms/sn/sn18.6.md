<!-- @segment sn18.6:1.1 -->

Sāvatthiyaṁ viharati.

<!-- @segment sn18.6:1.2 -->

“Taṁ kiṁ maññasi, rāhula,

<!-- @segment sn18.6:1.3 -->

rūpasaññā niccā vā aniccā vā”ti?

<!-- @segment sn18.6:1.4 -->

“Aniccā, bhante” …

<!-- @segment sn18.6:1.5 -->

“saddasaññā …pe…

<!-- @segment sn18.6:1.6 -->

gandhasaññā …

<!-- @segment sn18.6:1.7 -->

rasasaññā …

<!-- @segment sn18.6:1.8 -->

phoṭṭhabbasaññā …

<!-- @segment sn18.6:1.9 -->

dhammasaññā niccā vā aniccā vā”ti?

<!-- @segment sn18.6:1.10 -->

“Aniccā, bhante” …

<!-- @segment sn18.6:1.11 -->

“evaṁ passaṁ, rāhula, sutavā ariyasāvako rūpasaññāyapi nibbindati …pe… saddasaññāyapi nibbindati … gandhasaññāyapi nibbindati … rasasaññāyapi nibbindati … phoṭṭhabbasaññāyapi nibbindati … dhammasaññāyapi nibbindati …pe…

<!-- @segment sn18.6:1.12 -->

pajānātī”ti.

<!-- @segment sn18.6:1.13 -->

Chaṭṭhaṁ.
