<!-- @segment sn48.1:1.1 -->

Sāvatthinidānaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn48.1:1.2 -->

Tatra kho bhagavā etadavoca.

<!-- @segment sn48.1:1.3 -->

“Pañcimāni, bhikkhave, indriyāni.

<!-- @segment sn48.1:1.4 -->

Katamāni pañca?

<!-- @segment sn48.1:1.5 -->

Saddhindriyaṁ, vīriyindriyaṁ, satindriyaṁ, samādhindriyaṁ, paññindriyaṁ—

<!-- @segment sn48.1:1.6 -->

imāni kho, bhikkhave, pañcindriyānī”ti.

<!-- @segment sn48.1:1.7 -->

Paṭhamaṁ.
