title: Ajarasāsutta

“Kiṁsu ajarasā sādhu,

kiṁsu sādhu adhiṭṭhitaṁ;

Kiṁsu narānaṁ ratanaṁ,

kiṁsu corehyahāriyan”ti.

“Sīlaṁ ajarasā sādhu,

saddhā sādhu adhiṭṭhitā;

Paññā narānaṁ ratanaṁ,

puññaṁ corehyahāriyan”ti.
