<!-- @segment sn1.52:1.1 -->

“Kiṁsu ajarasā sādhu,

<!-- @segment sn1.52:1.2 -->

kiṁsu sādhu adhiṭṭhitaṁ;

<!-- @segment sn1.52:1.3 -->

Kiṁsu narānaṁ ratanaṁ,

<!-- @segment sn1.52:1.4 -->

kiṁsu corehyahāriyan”ti.

<!-- @segment sn1.52:2.1 -->

“Sīlaṁ ajarasā sādhu,

<!-- @segment sn1.52:2.2 -->

saddhā sādhu adhiṭṭhitā;

<!-- @segment sn1.52:2.3 -->

Paññā narānaṁ ratanaṁ,

<!-- @segment sn1.52:2.4 -->

puññaṁ corehyahāriyan”ti.
