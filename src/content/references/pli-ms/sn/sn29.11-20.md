<!-- @segment sn29.11-20:1.1 -->

Ekamantaṁ nisinno kho so bhikkhu bhagavantaṁ etadavoca:

<!-- @segment sn29.11-20:1.2 -->

“ko nu kho, bhante, hetu, ko paccayo, yena midhekacco kāyassa bhedā paraṁ maraṇā aṇḍajānaṁ nāgānaṁ sahabyataṁ upapajjatī”ti?

<!-- @segment sn29.11-20:2.1 -->

“Idha, bhikkhu, ekacco kāyena dvayakārī hoti, vācāya dvayakārī, manasā dvayakārī.

<!-- @segment sn29.11-20:2.2 -->

Tassa sutaṁ hoti:

<!-- @segment sn29.11-20:2.3 -->

‘aṇḍajā nāgā dīghāyukā vaṇṇavanto sukhabahulā’ti.

<!-- @segment sn29.11-20:2.4 -->

Tassa evaṁ hoti:

<!-- @segment sn29.11-20:2.5 -->

‘aho vatāhaṁ kāyassa bhedā paraṁ maraṇā aṇḍajānaṁ nāgānaṁ sahabyataṁ upapajjeyyan’ti.

<!-- @segment sn29.11-20:2.6 -->

So annaṁ deti.

<!-- @segment sn29.11-20:2.7 -->

So kāyassa bhedā paraṁ maraṇā aṇḍajānaṁ nāgānaṁ sahabyataṁ upapajjati.

<!-- @segment sn29.11-20:2.8 -->

Ayaṁ kho, bhikkhu, hetu …pe… upapajjatīti …pe…

<!-- @segment sn29.11-20:2.9 -->

so pānaṁ deti …pe…

<!-- @segment sn29.11-20:2.10 -->

vatthaṁ deti …pe…

<!-- @segment sn29.11-20:2.11 -->

yānaṁ deti …pe…

<!-- @segment sn29.11-20:2.12 -->

mālaṁ deti …pe…

<!-- @segment sn29.11-20:2.13 -->

gandhaṁ deti …pe…

<!-- @segment sn29.11-20:2.14 -->

vilepanaṁ deti …pe…

<!-- @segment sn29.11-20:2.15 -->

seyyaṁ deti …pe…

<!-- @segment sn29.11-20:2.16 -->

āvasathaṁ deti …pe…

<!-- @segment sn29.11-20:2.17 -->

padīpeyyaṁ deti.

<!-- @segment sn29.11-20:2.18 -->

So kāyassa bhedā paraṁ maraṇā aṇḍajānaṁ nāgānaṁ sahabyataṁ upapajjati.

<!-- @segment sn29.11-20:2.19 -->

Ayaṁ kho, bhikkhu, hetu, ayaṁ paccayo, yena midhekacco kāyassa bhedā paraṁ maraṇā aṇḍajānaṁ nāgānaṁ sahabyataṁ upapajjatī”ti.

<!-- @segment sn29.11-20:2.20 -->

Vīsatimaṁ.
