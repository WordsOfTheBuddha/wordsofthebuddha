<!-- @segment sn35.106:1.1 -->

“Dukkhassa, bhikkhave, samudayañca atthaṅgamañca desessāmi.

<!-- @segment sn35.106:1.2 -->

Taṁ suṇātha.

<!-- @segment sn35.106:1.3 -->

Katamo ca, bhikkhave, dukkhassa samudayo?

<!-- @segment sn35.106:1.4 -->

Cakkhuñca paṭicca rūpe ca uppajjati cakkhuviññāṇaṁ. Tiṇṇaṁ saṅgati phasso.

<!-- @segment sn35.106:1.5 -->

Phassapaccayā vedanā;

<!-- @segment sn35.106:1.6 -->

vedanāpaccayā taṇhā.

<!-- @segment sn35.106:1.7 -->

Ayaṁ dukkhassa samudayo …pe…

<!-- @segment sn35.106:1.8 -->

jivhañca paṭicca rase ca uppajjati jivhāviññāṇaṁ. Tiṇṇaṁ saṅgati phasso.

<!-- @segment sn35.106:1.9 -->

Phassapaccayā vedanā;

<!-- @segment sn35.106:1.10 -->

vedanāpaccayā taṇhā.

<!-- @segment sn35.106:1.11 -->

Ayaṁ dukkhassa samudayo …pe… manañca paṭicca dhamme ca uppajjati manoviññāṇaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn35.106:1.12 -->

Tiṇṇaṁ saṅgati phasso.

<!-- @segment sn35.106:1.13 -->

Phassapaccayā vedanā;

<!-- @segment sn35.106:1.14 -->

vedanāpaccayā taṇhā.

<!-- @segment sn35.106:1.15 -->

Ayaṁ kho, bhikkhave, dukkhassa samudayo.

<!-- @segment sn35.106:2.1 -->

Katamo ca, bhikkhave, dukkhassa atthaṅgamo?

<!-- @segment sn35.106:2.2 -->

Cakkhuñca paṭicca rūpe ca uppajjati cakkhuviññāṇaṁ. Tiṇṇaṁ saṅgati phasso.

<!-- @segment sn35.106:2.3 -->

Phassapaccayā vedanā;

<!-- @segment sn35.106:2.4 -->

vedanāpaccayā taṇhā.

<!-- @segment sn35.106:2.5 -->

Tassāyeva taṇhāya asesavirāganirodhā upādānanirodho;

<!-- @segment sn35.106:2.6 -->

upādānanirodhā bhavanirodho;

<!-- @segment sn35.106:2.7 -->

bhavanirodhā jātinirodho;

<!-- @segment sn35.106:2.8 -->

jātinirodhā jarāmaraṇaṁ sokaparidevadukkhadomanassupāyāsā nirujjhanti.

<!-- @segment sn35.106:2.9 -->

Evametassa kevalassa dukkhakkhandhassa nirodho hoti.

<!-- @segment sn35.106:2.10 -->

Ayaṁ dukkhassa atthaṅgamo …pe…

<!-- @segment sn35.106:2.11 -->

jivhañca paṭicca rase ca uppajjati jivhāviññāṇaṁ …pe…

<!-- @segment sn35.106:2.12 -->

manañca paṭicca dhamme ca uppajjati manoviññāṇaṁ. Tiṇṇaṁ saṅgati phasso.

<!-- @segment sn35.106:2.13 -->

Phassapaccayā vedanā;

<!-- @segment sn35.106:2.14 -->

vedanāpaccayā taṇhā.

<!-- @segment sn35.106:2.15 -->

Tassāyeva taṇhāya asesavirāganirodhā upādānanirodho;

<!-- @segment sn35.106:2.16 -->

upādānanirodhā bhavanirodho;

<!-- @segment sn35.106:2.17 -->

bhavanirodhā jātinirodho;

<!-- @segment sn35.106:2.18 -->

jātinirodhā jarāmaraṇaṁ sokaparidevadukkhadomanassupāyāsā nirujjhanti.

<!-- @segment sn35.106:2.19 -->

Evametassa kevalassa dukkhakkhandhassa nirodho hoti.

<!-- @segment sn35.106:2.20 -->

Ayaṁ kho, bhikkhave, dukkhassa atthaṅgamo”ti.

<!-- @segment sn35.106:2.21 -->

Tatiyaṁ.
