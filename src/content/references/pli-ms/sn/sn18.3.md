<!-- @segment sn18.3:1.1 -->

Sāvatthiyaṁ viharati.

<!-- @segment sn18.3:1.2 -->

“Taṁ kiṁ maññasi, rāhula,

<!-- @segment sn18.3:1.3 -->

cakkhuviññāṇaṁ niccaṁ vā aniccaṁ vā”ti?

<!-- @segment sn18.3:1.4 -->

“Aniccaṁ, bhante” …

<!-- @segment sn18.3:1.5 -->

“sotaviññāṇaṁ …pe…

<!-- @segment sn18.3:1.6 -->

ghānaviññāṇaṁ …

<!-- @segment sn18.3:1.7 -->

jivhāviññāṇaṁ …

<!-- @segment sn18.3:1.8 -->

kāyaviññāṇaṁ …

<!-- @segment sn18.3:1.9 -->

manoviññāṇaṁ niccaṁ vā aniccaṁ vā”ti?

<!-- @segment sn18.3:1.10 -->

“Aniccaṁ, bhante” …

<!-- @segment sn18.3:1.11 -->

“evaṁ passaṁ, rāhula, sutavā ariyasāvako cakkhuviññāṇasmimpi nibbindati …pe… sotaviññāṇasmimpi nibbindati … ghānaviññāṇasmimpi nibbindati … jivhāviññāṇasmimpi nibbindati … kāyaviññāṇasmimpi nibbindati … manoviññāṇasmimpi nibbindati;

<!-- @segment sn18.3:1.12 -->

nibbindaṁ virajjati …pe…

<!-- @segment sn18.3:1.13 -->

pajānātī”ti.

<!-- @segment sn18.3:1.14 -->

Tatiyaṁ.
