<!-- @segment sn25.6:1.1 -->

Sāvatthinidānaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn25.6:1.2 -->

“Rūpasaññā, bhikkhave, aniccā vipariṇāmī aññathābhāvī;

<!-- @segment sn25.6:1.3 -->

saddasaññā …

<!-- @segment sn25.6:1.4 -->

gandhasaññā …

<!-- @segment sn25.6:1.5 -->

rasasaññā …

<!-- @segment sn25.6:1.6 -->

phoṭṭhabbasaññā …

<!-- @segment sn25.6:1.7 -->

dhammasaññā aniccā vipariṇāmī aññathābhāvī.

<!-- @segment sn25.6:1.8 -->

Yo, bhikkhave, ime dhamme evaṁ saddahati adhimuccati, ayaṁ vuccati ‘saddhānusārī …pe…

<!-- @segment sn25.6:1.9 -->

sambodhiparāyano’”ti.

<!-- @segment sn25.6:1.10 -->

Chaṭṭhaṁ.
