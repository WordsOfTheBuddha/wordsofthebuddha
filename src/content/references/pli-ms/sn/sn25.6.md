Sāvatthinidānaṁ.

“Rūpasaññā, bhikkhave, aniccā vipariṇāmī aññathābhāvī;

saddasaññā …

gandhasaññā …

rasasaññā …

phoṭṭhabbasaññā …

dhammasaññā aniccā vipariṇāmī aññathābhāvī.

Yo, bhikkhave, ime dhamme evaṁ saddahati adhimuccati, ayaṁ vuccati ‘saddhānusārī …pe…

sambodhiparāyano’”ti.

Chaṭṭhaṁ.
