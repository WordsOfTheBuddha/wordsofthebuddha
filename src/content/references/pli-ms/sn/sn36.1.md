<!-- @segment sn36.1:1.1 -->

“Tisso imā, bhikkhave, vedanā.

<!-- @segment sn36.1:1.2 -->

Katamā tisso?

<!-- @segment sn36.1:1.3 -->

Sukhā vedanā, dukkhā vedanā, adukkhamasukhā vedanā—

<!-- @segment sn36.1:1.4 -->

imā kho, bhikkhave, tisso vedanāti.

<!-- @segment sn36.1:2.1 -->

Samāhito sampajāno,

<!-- @segment sn36.1:2.2 -->

sato buddhassa sāvako;

<!-- @segment sn36.1:2.3 -->

Vedanā ca pajānāti,

<!-- @segment sn36.1:2.4 -->

vedanānañca sambhavaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn36.1:3.1 -->

Yattha cetā nirujjhanti,

<!-- @segment sn36.1:3.2 -->

maggañca khayagāminaṁ;

<!-- @segment sn36.1:3.3 -->

Vedanānaṁ khayā bhikkhu,

<!-- @segment sn36.1:3.4 -->

nicchāto parinibbuto”ti.

<!-- @segment sn36.1:3.5 -->

Paṭhamaṁ.
