<!-- @segment sn12.45:1.1 -->

Evaṁ me sutaṁ—

<!-- @segment sn12.45:1.2 -->

ekaṁ samayaṁ bhagavā ñātike viharati giñjakāvasathe.

<!-- @segment sn12.45:1.3 -->

Atha kho bhagavā rahogato paṭisallāno imaṁ dhammapariyāyaṁ abhāsi:

<!-- @segment sn12.45:2.1 -->

“Cakkhuñca paṭicca rūpe ca uppajjati cakkhuviññāṇaṁ. Tiṇṇaṁ saṅgati phasso.

<!-- @segment sn12.45:2.2 -->

Phassapaccayā vedanā,

<!-- @segment sn12.45:2.3 -->

vedanāpaccayā taṇhā;

<!-- @segment sn12.45:2.4 -->

taṇhāpaccayā upādānaṁ …pe…

<!-- @segment sn12.45:2.5 -->

evametassa kevalassa dukkhakkhandhassa samudayo hoti.

<!-- @segment sn12.45:3.1 -->

Sotañca paṭicca sadde ca …pe…

<!-- @segment sn12.45:3.2 -->

ghānañca paṭicca gandhe ca …

<!-- @segment sn12.45:3.3 -->

jivhañca paṭicca rase ca …

<!-- @segment sn12.45:3.4 -->

kāyañca paṭicca phoṭṭhabbe ca …

<!-- @segment sn12.45:3.5 -->

manañca paṭicca dhamme ca uppajjati manoviññāṇaṁ. Tiṇṇaṁ saṅgati phasso.

<!-- @segment sn12.45:3.6 -->

Phassapaccayā vedanā;

<!-- @segment sn12.45:3.7 -->

vedanāpaccayā taṇhā;

<!-- @segment sn12.45:3.8 -->

taṇhāpaccayā upādānaṁ …pe…

<!-- @segment sn12.45:3.9 -->

evametassa kevalassa dukkhakkhandhassa samudayo hoti.

<!-- @segment sn12.45:4.1 -->

Cakkhuñca paṭicca rūpe ca uppajjati cakkhuviññāṇaṁ. Tiṇṇaṁ saṅgati phasso.

<!-- @segment sn12.45:4.2 -->

Phassapaccayā vedanā;

<!-- @segment sn12.45:4.3 -->

vedanāpaccayā taṇhā.

<!-- @segment sn12.45:4.4 -->

Tassāyeva taṇhāya asesavirāganirodhā upādānanirodho;

<!-- @segment sn12.45:4.5 -->

upādānanirodhā bhavanirodho …pe…

<!-- @segment sn12.45:4.6 -->

evametassa kevalassa dukkhakkhandhassa nirodho hoti.

<!-- @segment sn12.45:5.1 -->

Sotañca paṭicca sadde ca …pe…

<!-- @segment sn12.45:5.2 -->

manañca paṭicca dhamme ca uppajjati manoviññāṇaṁ. Tiṇṇaṁ saṅgati phasso.

<!-- @segment sn12.45:5.3 -->

Phassapaccayā vedanā;

<!-- @segment sn12.45:5.4 -->

vedanāpaccayā taṇhā.

<!-- @segment sn12.45:5.5 -->

Tassāyeva taṇhāya asesavirāganirodhā upādānanirodho;

<!-- @segment sn12.45:5.6 -->

upādānanirodhā bhavanirodho …pe…

<!-- @segment sn12.45:5.7 -->

evametassa kevalassa dukkhakkhandhassa nirodho hotī”ti.

<!-- @segment sn12.45:6.1 -->

Tena kho pana samayena aññataro bhikkhu bhagavato upassuti ṭhito hoti.

<!-- @segment sn12.45:6.2 -->

Addasā kho bhagavā taṁ bhikkhuṁ upassuti ṭhitaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn12.45:6.3 -->

Disvāna taṁ bhikkhuṁ etadavoca:

<!-- @segment sn12.45:6.4 -->

“assosi no tvaṁ, bhikkhu, imaṁ dhammapariyāyan”ti?

<!-- @segment sn12.45:6.5 -->

“Evaṁ, bhante”ti.

<!-- @segment sn12.45:6.6 -->

“Uggaṇhāhi tvaṁ, bhikkhu, imaṁ dhammapariyāyaṁ;

<!-- @segment sn12.45:6.7 -->

pariyāpuṇāhi tvaṁ, bhikkhu, imaṁ dhammapariyāyaṁ;

<!-- @segment sn12.45:6.8 -->

dhārehi tvaṁ, bhikkhu, imaṁ dhammapariyāyaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn12.45:6.9 -->

Atthasaṁhito ayaṁ, bhikkhu, dhammapariyāyo ādibrahmacariyako”ti.

<!-- @segment sn12.45:6.10 -->

Pañcamaṁ.
