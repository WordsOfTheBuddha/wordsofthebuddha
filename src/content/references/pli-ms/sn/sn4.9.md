<!-- @segment sn4.9:1.1 -->

Evaṁ me sutaṁ—

<!-- @segment sn4.9:1.2 -->

ekaṁ samayaṁ bhagavā rājagahe viharati veḷuvane kalandakanivāpe.

<!-- @segment sn4.9:1.3 -->

Tatra kho bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi:

<!-- @segment sn4.9:1.4 -->

“bhikkhavo”ti.

<!-- @segment sn4.9:1.5 -->

“Bhadante”ti te bhikkhū bhagavato paccassosuṁ.

<!-- @segment sn4.9:1.6 -->

Bhagavā etadavoca:

<!-- @segment sn4.9:2.1 -->

“Appamidaṁ, bhikkhave, manussānaṁ āyu.

<!-- @segment sn4.9:2.2 -->

Gamanīyo samparāyo, kattabbaṁ kusalaṁ, caritabbaṁ brahmacariyaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn4.9:2.3 -->

Natthi jātassa amaraṇaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn4.9:2.4 -->

Yo, bhikkhave, ciraṁ jīvati, so vassasataṁ appaṁ vā bhiyyo”ti.

<!-- @segment sn4.9:3.1 -->

Atha kho māro pāpimā yena bhagavā tenupasaṅkami; upasaṅkamitvā bhagavantaṁ gāthāya ajjhabhāsi:

<!-- @segment sn4.9:4.1 -->

“Dīghamāyu manussānaṁ,

<!-- @segment sn4.9:4.2 -->

na naṁ hīḷe suporiso;

<!-- @segment sn4.9:4.3 -->

Careyya khīramattova,

<!-- @segment sn4.9:4.4 -->

natthi maccussa āgamo”ti.

<!-- @segment sn4.9:5.1 -->

“Appamāyu manussānaṁ,

<!-- @segment sn4.9:5.2 -->

hīḷeyya naṁ suporiso;

<!-- @segment sn4.9:5.3 -->

Careyyādittasīsova,

<!-- @segment sn4.9:5.4 -->

natthi maccussa nāgamo”ti.

<!-- @segment sn4.9:6.1 -->

Atha kho māro …pe… tatthevantaradhāyīti.
