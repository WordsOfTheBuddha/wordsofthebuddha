<!-- @segment sn8.12:1.1 -->

Ekaṁ samayaṁ āyasmā vaṅgīso sāvatthiyaṁ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme.

<!-- @segment sn8.12:1.2 -->

Tena kho pana samayena āyasmā vaṅgīso aciraarahattappatto hutvā vimuttisukhaṁ paṭisaṁvedī tāyaṁ velāyaṁ imā gāthāyo abhāsi:

<!-- @segment sn8.12:2.1 -->

“Kāveyyamattā vicarimha pubbe,

<!-- @segment sn8.12:2.2 -->

Gāmā gāmaṁ purā puraṁ;

<!-- @segment sn8.12:2.3 -->

Athaddasāma sambuddhaṁ,

<!-- @segment sn8.12:2.4 -->

Saddhā no upapajjatha.

<!-- @segment sn8.12:3.1 -->

So me dhammamadesesi,

<!-- @segment sn8.12:3.2 -->

khandhāyatanadhātuyo;

<!-- @segment sn8.12:3.3 -->

Tassāhaṁ dhammaṁ sutvāna,

<!-- @segment sn8.12:3.4 -->

pabbajiṁ anagāriyaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn8.12:4.1 -->

Bahunnaṁ vata atthāya,

<!-- @segment sn8.12:4.2 -->

bodhiṁ ajjhagamā muni;

<!-- @segment sn8.12:4.3 -->

Bhikkhūnaṁ bhikkhunīnañca,

<!-- @segment sn8.12:4.4 -->

ye niyāmagataddasā.

<!-- @segment sn8.12:5.1 -->

Svāgataṁ vata me āsi,

<!-- @segment sn8.12:5.2 -->

mama buddhassa santike;

<!-- @segment sn8.12:5.3 -->

Tisso vijjā anuppattā,

<!-- @segment sn8.12:5.4 -->

kataṁ buddhassa sāsanaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn8.12:6.1 -->

Pubbenivāsaṁ jānāmi,

<!-- @segment sn8.12:6.2 -->

Dibbacakkhuṁ visodhitaṁ;

<!-- @segment sn8.12:6.3 -->

Tevijjo iddhipattomhi,

<!-- @segment sn8.12:6.4 -->

Cetopariyāyakovido”ti.

<!-- @segment sn8.12:7.0 -->

Tassuddānaṁ

<!-- @segment sn8.12:7.1 -->

Nikkhantaṁ arati ceva,

<!-- @segment sn8.12:7.2 -->

pesalā atimaññanā;

<!-- @segment sn8.12:7.3 -->

Ānandena subhāsitā,

<!-- @segment sn8.12:7.4 -->

sāriputtapavāraṇā;

<!-- @segment sn8.12:7.5 -->

Parosahassaṁ koṇḍañño,

<!-- @segment sn8.12:7.6 -->

moggallānena gaggarā;

<!-- @segment sn8.12:7.7 -->

Vaṅgīsena dvādasāti.

<!-- @segment sn8.12:7.8 -->

Vaṅgīsasaṁyuttaṁ samattaṁ.
