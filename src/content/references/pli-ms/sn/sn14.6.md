<!-- @segment sn14.6:1.1 -->

Sāvatthiyaṁ viharati.

<!-- @segment sn14.6:1.2 -->

“Dhātunānattaṁ vo, bhikkhave, desessāmi.

<!-- @segment sn14.6:1.3 -->

Taṁ suṇātha …pe…

<!-- @segment sn14.6:1.4 -->

katamañca, bhikkhave, dhātunānattaṁ?

<!-- @segment sn14.6:1.5 -->

Rūpadhātu saddadhātu gandhadhātu rasadhātu phoṭṭhabbadhātu dhammadhātu—

<!-- @segment sn14.6:1.6 -->

idaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, dhātunānattan”ti.

<!-- @segment sn14.6:1.7 -->

Chaṭṭhaṁ.
