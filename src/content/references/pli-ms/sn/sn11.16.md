<!-- @segment sn11.16:1.1 -->

Ekaṁ samayaṁ bhagavā rājagahe viharati gijjhakūṭe pabbate.

<!-- @segment sn11.16:1.2 -->

Atha kho sakko devānamindo yena bhagavā tenupasaṅkami; upasaṅkamitvā bhagavantaṁ abhivādetvā ekamantaṁ aṭṭhāsi.

<!-- @segment sn11.16:1.3 -->

Ekamantaṁ ṭhito kho sakko devānamindo bhagavantaṁ gāthāya ajjhabhāsi:

<!-- @segment sn11.16:2.1 -->

“Yajamānānaṁ manussānaṁ,

<!-- @segment sn11.16:2.2 -->

puññapekkhāna pāṇinaṁ;

<!-- @segment sn11.16:2.3 -->

Karotaṁ opadhikaṁ puññaṁ,

<!-- @segment sn11.16:2.4 -->

kattha dinnaṁ mahapphalan”ti.

<!-- @segment sn11.16:3.1 -->

“Cattāro ca paṭipannā,

<!-- @segment sn11.16:3.2 -->

cattāro ca phale ṭhitā;

<!-- @segment sn11.16:3.3 -->

Esa saṅgho ujubhūto,

<!-- @segment sn11.16:3.4 -->

paññāsīlasamāhito.

<!-- @segment sn11.16:4.1 -->

Yajamānānaṁ manussānaṁ,

<!-- @segment sn11.16:4.2 -->

Puññapekkhāna pāṇinaṁ;

<!-- @segment sn11.16:4.3 -->

Karotaṁ opadhikaṁ puññaṁ,

<!-- @segment sn11.16:4.4 -->

Saṅghe dinnaṁ mahapphalan”ti.
