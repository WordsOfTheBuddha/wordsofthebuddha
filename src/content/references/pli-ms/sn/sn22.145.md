<!-- @segment sn22.145:1.1 -->

Sāvatthinidānaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn22.145:1.2 -->

“Yo, bhikkhave, anattā; tatra vo chandarāgo pahātabbo.

<!-- @segment sn22.145:1.3 -->

Ko ca, bhikkhave, anattā?

<!-- @segment sn22.145:1.4 -->

Rūpaṁ, bhikkhave, anattā; tatra vo chandarāgo pahātabbo.

<!-- @segment sn22.145:1.5 -->

Vedanā anattā …

<!-- @segment sn22.145:1.6 -->

saññā …

<!-- @segment sn22.145:1.7 -->

saṅkhārā …

<!-- @segment sn22.145:1.8 -->

viññāṇaṁ anattā; tatra vo chandarāgo pahātabbo.

<!-- @segment sn22.145:1.9 -->

Yo, bhikkhave, anattā; tatra vo chandarāgo pahātabbo”ti.

<!-- @segment sn22.145:1.10 -->

Dasamaṁ.
