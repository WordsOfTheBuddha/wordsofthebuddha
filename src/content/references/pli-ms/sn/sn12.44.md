<!-- @segment sn12.44:1.1 -->

Sāvatthiyaṁ viharati.

<!-- @segment sn12.44:1.2 -->

“Lokassa, bhikkhave, samudayañca atthaṅgamañca desessāmi.

<!-- @segment sn12.44:1.3 -->

Taṁ suṇātha, sādhukaṁ manasi karotha, bhāsissāmī”ti.

<!-- @segment sn12.44:1.4 -->

“Evaṁ, bhante”ti kho te bhikkhū bhagavato paccassosuṁ.

<!-- @segment sn12.44:1.5 -->

Bhagavā etadavoca:

<!-- @segment sn12.44:2.1 -->

“Katamo ca, bhikkhave, lokassa samudayo?

<!-- @segment sn12.44:2.2 -->

Cakkhuñca paṭicca rūpe ca uppajjati cakkhuviññāṇaṁ. Tiṇṇaṁ saṅgati phasso.

<!-- @segment sn12.44:2.3 -->

Phassapaccayā vedanā;

<!-- @segment sn12.44:2.4 -->

vedanāpaccayā taṇhā;

<!-- @segment sn12.44:2.5 -->

taṇhāpaccayā upādānaṁ;

<!-- @segment sn12.44:2.6 -->

upādānapaccayā bhavo;

<!-- @segment sn12.44:2.7 -->

bhavapaccayā jāti;

<!-- @segment sn12.44:2.8 -->

jātipaccayā jarāmaraṇaṁ sokaparidevadukkhadomanassupāyāsā sambhavanti.

<!-- @segment sn12.44:2.9 -->

Ayaṁ kho, bhikkhave, lokassa samudayo.

<!-- @segment sn12.44:3.1 -->

Sotañca paṭicca sadde ca …pe…

<!-- @segment sn12.44:3.2 -->

ghānañca paṭicca gandhe ca …

<!-- @segment sn12.44:3.3 -->

jivhañca paṭicca rase ca …

<!-- @segment sn12.44:3.4 -->

kāyañca paṭicca phoṭṭhabbe ca …

<!-- @segment sn12.44:3.5 -->

manañca paṭicca dhamme ca uppajjati manoviññāṇaṁ. Tiṇṇaṁ saṅgati phasso.

<!-- @segment sn12.44:3.6 -->

Phassapaccayā vedanā …pe…

<!-- @segment sn12.44:3.7 -->

jātipaccayā jarāmaraṇaṁ sokaparidevadukkhadomanassupāyāsā sambhavanti.

<!-- @segment sn12.44:3.8 -->

Ayaṁ kho, bhikkhave, lokassa samudayo.

<!-- @segment sn12.44:4.1 -->

Katamo ca, bhikkhave, lokassa atthaṅgamo?

<!-- @segment sn12.44:4.2 -->

Cakkhuñca paṭicca rūpe ca uppajjati cakkhuviññāṇaṁ. Tiṇṇaṁ saṅgati phasso.

<!-- @segment sn12.44:4.3 -->

Phassapaccayā vedanā;

<!-- @segment sn12.44:4.4 -->

vedanāpaccayā taṇhā.

<!-- @segment sn12.44:4.5 -->

Tassāyeva taṇhāya asesavirāganirodhā upādānanirodho;

<!-- @segment sn12.44:4.6 -->

upādānanirodhā bhavanirodho …pe…

<!-- @segment sn12.44:4.7 -->

evametassa kevalassa dukkhakkhandhassa nirodho hoti.

<!-- @segment sn12.44:4.8 -->

Ayaṁ kho, bhikkhave, lokassa atthaṅgamo.

<!-- @segment sn12.44:5.1 -->

Sotañca paṭicca sadde ca …pe…

<!-- @segment sn12.44:5.2 -->

ghānañca paṭicca gandhe ca …

<!-- @segment sn12.44:5.3 -->

jivhañca paṭicca rase ca …

<!-- @segment sn12.44:5.4 -->

kāyañca paṭicca phoṭṭhabbe ca …

<!-- @segment sn12.44:5.5 -->

manañca paṭicca dhamme ca uppajjati manoviññāṇaṁ. Tiṇṇaṁ saṅgati phasso.

<!-- @segment sn12.44:5.6 -->

Phassapaccayā vedanā;

<!-- @segment sn12.44:5.7 -->

vedanāpaccayā taṇhā.

<!-- @segment sn12.44:5.8 -->

Tassāyeva taṇhāya asesavirāganirodhā upādānanirodho;

<!-- @segment sn12.44:5.9 -->

upādānanirodhā bhavanirodho …pe…

<!-- @segment sn12.44:5.10 -->

evametassa kevalassa dukkhakkhandhassa nirodho hoti.

<!-- @segment sn12.44:5.11 -->

Ayaṁ kho, bhikkhave, lokassa atthaṅgamo”ti.

<!-- @segment sn12.44:5.12 -->

Catutthaṁ.
