<!-- @segment sn35.62:1.1 -->

“Sabbupādānapariyādānāya vo, bhikkhave, dhammaṁ desessāmi.

<!-- @segment sn35.62:1.2 -->

Taṁ suṇātha.

<!-- @segment sn35.62:1.3 -->

Katamo ca, bhikkhave, sabbupādānapariyādānāya dhammo?

<!-- @segment sn35.62:2.1 -->

Taṁ kiṁ maññatha, bhikkhave,

<!-- @segment sn35.62:2.2 -->

cakkhu niccaṁ vā aniccaṁ vā”ti?

<!-- @segment sn35.62:3.1 -->

“Aniccaṁ, bhante”.

<!-- @segment sn35.62:4.1 -->

“Yaṁ panāniccaṁ dukkhaṁ vā taṁ sukhaṁ vā”ti?

<!-- @segment sn35.62:5.1 -->

“Dukkhaṁ, bhante”.

<!-- @segment sn35.62:6.1 -->

“Yaṁ panāniccaṁ dukkhaṁ vipariṇāmadhammaṁ, kallaṁ nu taṁ samanupassituṁ:

<!-- @segment sn35.62:6.2 -->

‘etaṁ mama, esohamasmi, eso me attā’”ti?

<!-- @segment sn35.62:7.1 -->

“No hetaṁ, bhante”.

<!-- @segment sn35.62:8.1 -->

“Rūpā …pe…

<!-- @segment sn35.62:8.2 -->

cakkhuviññāṇaṁ niccaṁ vā aniccaṁ vā”ti?

<!-- @segment sn35.62:9.1 -->

“Aniccaṁ, bhante” …pe….

<!-- @segment sn35.62:10.1 -->

“Cakkhusamphasso nicco vā anicco vā”ti?

<!-- @segment sn35.62:11.1 -->

“Anicco, bhante” …pe….

<!-- @segment sn35.62:12.1 -->

“Yampidaṁ cakkhusamphassapaccayā uppajjati vedayitaṁ sukhaṁ vā dukkhaṁ vā adukkhamasukhaṁ vā tampi niccaṁ vā aniccaṁ vā”ti?

<!-- @segment sn35.62:13.1 -->

“Aniccaṁ, bhante” …pe….

<!-- @segment sn35.62:14.1 -->

“Sotaṁ …

<!-- @segment sn35.62:14.2 -->

ghānaṁ …

<!-- @segment sn35.62:14.3 -->

jivhā …

<!-- @segment sn35.62:14.4 -->

kāyo …

<!-- @segment sn35.62:14.5 -->

mano …

<!-- @segment sn35.62:14.6 -->

dhammā …

<!-- @segment sn35.62:14.7 -->

manoviññāṇaṁ …

<!-- @segment sn35.62:14.8 -->

manosamphasso …

<!-- @segment sn35.62:14.9 -->

yampidaṁ manosamphassapaccayā uppajjati vedayitaṁ sukhaṁ vā dukkhaṁ vā adukkhamasukhaṁ vā, tampi niccaṁ vā aniccaṁ vā”ti?

<!-- @segment sn35.62:15.1 -->

“Aniccaṁ, bhante”.

<!-- @segment sn35.62:16.1 -->

“Yaṁ panāniccaṁ dukkhaṁ vā taṁ sukhaṁ vā”ti?

<!-- @segment sn35.62:17.1 -->

“Dukkhaṁ, bhante”.

<!-- @segment sn35.62:18.1 -->

“Yaṁ panāniccaṁ dukkhaṁ vipariṇāmadhammaṁ, kallaṁ nu taṁ samanupassituṁ:

<!-- @segment sn35.62:18.2 -->

‘etaṁ mama, esohamasmi, eso me attā’”ti?

<!-- @segment sn35.62:19.1 -->

“No hetaṁ, bhante”.

<!-- @segment sn35.62:20.1 -->

“Evaṁ passaṁ, bhikkhave, sutavā ariyasāvako cakkhusmimpi nibbindati, rūpesupi nibbindati, cakkhuviññāṇepi nibbindati, cakkhusamphassepi nibbindati.

<!-- @segment sn35.62:20.2 -->

Yampidaṁ cakkhusamphassapaccayā uppajjati vedayitaṁ sukhaṁ vā dukkhaṁ vā adukkhamasukhaṁ vā tasmimpi nibbindati …pe…

<!-- @segment sn35.62:20.3 -->

jivhāyapi nibbindati, rasesupi nibbindati, jivhāviññāṇepi nibbindati, jivhāsamphassepi nibbindati, yampidaṁ jivhāsamphassapaccayā uppajjati …pe…

<!-- @segment sn35.62:20.4 -->

manasmimpi nibbindati, dhammesupi nibbindati, manoviññāṇepi nibbindati, manosamphassepi nibbindati.

<!-- @segment sn35.62:20.5 -->

Yampidaṁ manosamphassapaccayā uppajjati vedayitaṁ sukhaṁ vā dukkhaṁ vā adukkhamasukhaṁ vā tasmimpi nibbindati.

<!-- @segment sn35.62:20.6 -->

Nibbindaṁ virajjati; virāgā vimuccati; vimuttasmiṁ vimuttamiti ñāṇaṁ hoti.

<!-- @segment sn35.62:20.7 -->

‘Khīṇā jāti, vusitaṁ brahmacariyaṁ, kataṁ karaṇīyaṁ, nāparaṁ itthattāyā’ti pajānāti.

<!-- @segment sn35.62:20.8 -->

Ayaṁ kho, bhikkhave, sabbupādānapariyādānāya dhammo”ti.

<!-- @segment sn35.62:20.9 -->

Dasamaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn35.62:20.10 -->

Avijjāvaggo paṭhamo.

<!-- @segment sn35.62:21.0 -->

Tassuddānaṁ

<!-- @segment sn35.62:21.1 -->

Avijjā saṁyojanā dve,

<!-- @segment sn35.62:21.2 -->

āsavena duve vuttā;

<!-- @segment sn35.62:21.3 -->

Anusayā apare dve,

<!-- @segment sn35.62:21.4 -->

pariññā dve pariyādinnaṁ;

<!-- @segment sn35.62:21.5 -->

Vaggo tena pavuccatīti.
