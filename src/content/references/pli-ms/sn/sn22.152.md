<!-- @segment sn22.152:1.1 -->

Sāvatthinidānaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn22.152:1.2 -->

“Kismiṁ nu kho, bhikkhave, sati, kiṁ upādāya, kiṁ abhinivissa evaṁ diṭṭhi uppajjati:

<!-- @segment sn22.152:1.3 -->

‘so attā, so loko, so pecca bhavissāmi nicco dhuvo sassato avipariṇāmadhammo’”ti?

<!-- @segment sn22.152:1.4 -->

Bhagavaṁmūlakā no, bhante, dhammā …pe….

<!-- @segment sn22.152:1.5 -->

“Rūpe kho, bhikkhave, sati, rūpaṁ upādāya, rūpaṁ abhinivissa evaṁ diṭṭhi uppajjati:

<!-- @segment sn22.152:1.6 -->

‘so attā, so loko, so pecca bhavissāmi nicco dhuvo sassato avipariṇāmadhammo’ti.

<!-- @segment sn22.152:1.7 -->

Vedanāya …pe…

<!-- @segment sn22.152:1.8 -->

saññāya …

<!-- @segment sn22.152:1.9 -->

saṅkhāresu …pe…

<!-- @segment sn22.152:1.10 -->

viññāṇe sati, viññāṇaṁ upādāya, viññāṇaṁ abhinivissa evaṁ diṭṭhi uppajjati:

<!-- @segment sn22.152:1.11 -->

‘so attā, so loko, so pecca bhavissāmi nicco dhuvo sassato avipariṇāmadhammo’ti.

<!-- @segment sn22.152:2.1 -->

Taṁ kiṁ maññatha, bhikkhave,

<!-- @segment sn22.152:2.2 -->

rūpaṁ niccaṁ vā aniccaṁ vā”ti?

<!-- @segment sn22.152:2.3 -->

“Aniccaṁ, bhante”.

<!-- @segment sn22.152:2.4 -->

“Yaṁ panāniccaṁ dukkhaṁ vā taṁ sukhaṁ vā”ti?

<!-- @segment sn22.152:2.5 -->

“Dukkhaṁ, bhante”.

<!-- @segment sn22.152:2.6 -->

“Yaṁ panāniccaṁ dukkhaṁ vipariṇāmadhammaṁ, api nu taṁ anupādāya evaṁ diṭṭhi uppajjeyya:

<!-- @segment sn22.152:2.7 -->

‘so attā, so loko, so pecca bhavissāmi nicco dhuvo sassato avipariṇāmadhammo’”ti?

<!-- @segment sn22.152:2.8 -->

“No hetaṁ, bhante”.

<!-- @segment sn22.152:2.9 -->

“Vedanā …

<!-- @segment sn22.152:2.10 -->

saññā …

<!-- @segment sn22.152:2.11 -->

saṅkhārā …

<!-- @segment sn22.152:2.12 -->

viññāṇaṁ niccaṁ vā aniccaṁ vā”ti?

<!-- @segment sn22.152:2.13 -->

“Aniccaṁ, bhante”.

<!-- @segment sn22.152:2.14 -->

“Yaṁ panāniccaṁ dukkhaṁ vā taṁ sukhaṁ vā”ti?

<!-- @segment sn22.152:2.15 -->

“Dukkhaṁ, bhante”.

<!-- @segment sn22.152:2.16 -->

“Yaṁ panāniccaṁ dukkhaṁ vipariṇāmadhammaṁ, api nu taṁ anupādāya evaṁ diṭṭhi uppajjeyya:

<!-- @segment sn22.152:2.17 -->

‘so attā so loko, so pecca bhavissāmi nicco dhuvo sassato avipariṇāmadhammo’”ti?

<!-- @segment sn22.152:2.18 -->

“No hetaṁ, bhante”.

<!-- @segment sn22.152:2.19 -->

“Evaṁ passaṁ …pe…

<!-- @segment sn22.152:2.20 -->

nāparaṁ itthattāyāti pajānātī”ti.

<!-- @segment sn22.152:2.21 -->

Tatiyaṁ.
