<!-- @segment sn32.57:1.1 -->

Sāvatthinidānaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn32.57:1.2 -->

Ekamantaṁ nisinno kho so bhikkhu bhagavantaṁ etadavoca:

<!-- @segment sn32.57:1.3 -->

“ko nu kho, bhante, hetu, ko paccayo, yenekadā devo vassatī”ti?

<!-- @segment sn32.57:1.4 -->

“Santi, bhikkhu, vassavalāhakā nāma devā.

<!-- @segment sn32.57:1.5 -->

Tesaṁ yadā evaṁ hoti:

<!-- @segment sn32.57:1.6 -->

‘yannūna mayaṁ sakāya ratiyā vaseyyāmā’ti, tesaṁ taṁ cetopaṇidhimanvāya devo vassati.

<!-- @segment sn32.57:1.7 -->

Ayaṁ kho, bhikkhu, hetu, ayaṁ paccayo, yenekadā devo vassatī”ti.

<!-- @segment sn32.57:1.8 -->

Sattapaññāsamaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn32.57:1.9 -->

Sattapaññāsasuttantaṁ niṭṭhitaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn32.57:1.10 -->

Valāhakavaggo paṭhamo.

<!-- @segment sn32.57:2.0 -->

Tassuddānaṁ

<!-- @segment sn32.57:2.1 -->

Suddhikaṁ sucaritañca,

<!-- @segment sn32.57:2.2 -->

dānūpakārapaññāsaṁ;

<!-- @segment sn32.57:2.3 -->

Sītaṁ uṇhañca abbhañca,

<!-- @segment sn32.57:2.4 -->

vātavassavalāhakāti.

<!-- @segment sn32.57:2.5 -->

Valāhakasaṁyuttaṁ samattaṁ.
