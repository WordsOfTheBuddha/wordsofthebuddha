<!-- @segment sn25.9:1.1 -->

Sāvatthinidānaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn25.9:1.2 -->

“Pathavīdhātu, bhikkhave, aniccā vipariṇāmī aññathābhāvī;

<!-- @segment sn25.9:1.3 -->

āpodhātu …

<!-- @segment sn25.9:1.4 -->

tejodhātu …

<!-- @segment sn25.9:1.5 -->

vāyodhātu …

<!-- @segment sn25.9:1.6 -->

ākāsadhātu …

<!-- @segment sn25.9:1.7 -->

viññāṇadhātu aniccā vipariṇāmī aññathābhāvī.

<!-- @segment sn25.9:1.8 -->

Yo, bhikkhave, ime dhamme evaṁ saddahati adhimuccati, ayaṁ vuccati ‘saddhānusārī …pe…

<!-- @segment sn25.9:1.9 -->

sambodhiparāyano’”ti.

<!-- @segment sn25.9:1.10 -->

Navamaṁ.
