<!-- @segment sn22.17:1.1 -->

Sāvatthinidānaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn22.17:1.2 -->

“Rūpaṁ, bhikkhave, anattā.

<!-- @segment sn22.17:1.3 -->

Yadanattā taṁ ‘netaṁ mama, nesohamasmi, na meso attā’ti evametaṁ yathābhūtaṁ sammappaññāya daṭṭhabbaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn22.17:1.4 -->

Vedanā anattā …

<!-- @segment sn22.17:1.5 -->

saññā anattā …

<!-- @segment sn22.17:1.6 -->

saṅkhārā anattā …

<!-- @segment sn22.17:1.7 -->

viññāṇaṁ anattā.

<!-- @segment sn22.17:1.8 -->

Yadanattā taṁ ‘netaṁ mama, nesohamasmi, na meso attā’ti evametaṁ yathābhūtaṁ sammappaññāya daṭṭhabbaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn22.17:1.9 -->

Evaṁ passaṁ, bhikkhave …pe…

<!-- @segment sn22.17:1.10 -->

nāparaṁ itthattāyā’ti pajānātī”ti.

<!-- @segment sn22.17:1.11 -->

Chaṭṭhaṁ.
