<!-- @segment sn1.78:1.1 -->

“Kimatthakāmo na dade,

<!-- @segment sn1.78:1.2 -->

kiṁ macco na pariccaje;

<!-- @segment sn1.78:1.3 -->

Kiṁsu muñceyya kalyāṇaṁ,

<!-- @segment sn1.78:1.4 -->

pāpikaṁ na ca mocaye”ti.

<!-- @segment sn1.78:2.1 -->

“Attānaṁ na dade poso,

<!-- @segment sn1.78:2.2 -->

attānaṁ na pariccaje;

<!-- @segment sn1.78:2.3 -->

Vācaṁ muñceyya kalyāṇaṁ,

<!-- @segment sn1.78:2.4 -->

pāpikañca na mocaye”ti.
