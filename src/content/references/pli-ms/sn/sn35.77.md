<!-- @segment sn35.77:1.1 -->

“Yaṁ kho, rādha, dukkhaṁ tatra te chando pahātabbo.

<!-- @segment sn35.77:1.2 -->

Kiñca, rādha, dukkhaṁ?

<!-- @segment sn35.77:1.3 -->

Cakkhu kho, rādha, dukkhaṁ. Tatra te chando pahātabbo.

<!-- @segment sn35.77:1.4 -->

Rūpā …

<!-- @segment sn35.77:1.5 -->

cakkhuviññāṇaṁ …

<!-- @segment sn35.77:1.6 -->

cakkhusamphasso …

<!-- @segment sn35.77:1.7 -->

yampidaṁ cakkhusamphassa …pe… adukkhamasukhaṁ vā tampi dukkhaṁ. Tatra te chando pahātabbo …pe…

<!-- @segment sn35.77:1.8 -->

mano dukkho …

<!-- @segment sn35.77:1.9 -->

dhammā …

<!-- @segment sn35.77:1.10 -->

manoviññāṇaṁ …

<!-- @segment sn35.77:1.11 -->

manosamphasso …

<!-- @segment sn35.77:1.12 -->

yampidaṁ manosamphassapaccayā uppajjati vedayitaṁ sukhaṁ vā dukkhaṁ vā adukkhamasukhaṁ vā tampi dukkhaṁ. Tatra te chando pahātabbo.

<!-- @segment sn35.77:1.13 -->

Yaṁ kho, rādha, dukkhaṁ tatra te chando pahātabbo”ti.

<!-- @segment sn35.77:1.14 -->

Catutthaṁ.
