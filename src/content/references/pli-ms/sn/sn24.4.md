<!-- @segment sn24.4:1.1 -->

Sāvatthinidānaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn24.4:1.2 -->

“Kismiṁ nu kho, bhikkhave, sati, kiṁ upādāya, kiṁ abhinivissa evaṁ diṭṭhi uppajjati:

<!-- @segment sn24.4:1.3 -->

‘no cassaṁ, no ca me siyā, nābhavissa, na me bhavissatī’”ti?

<!-- @segment sn24.4:1.4 -->

“Bhagavaṁmūlakā no, bhante, dhammā …pe….

<!-- @segment sn24.4:2.1 -->

“Rūpe kho, bhikkhave, sati, rūpaṁ upādāya, rūpaṁ abhinivissa evaṁ diṭṭhi uppajjati:

<!-- @segment sn24.4:2.2 -->

‘no cassaṁ, no ca me siyā, nābhavissa, na me bhavissatī’ti.

<!-- @segment sn24.4:2.3 -->

Vedanāya sati …

<!-- @segment sn24.4:2.4 -->

saññāya sati …

<!-- @segment sn24.4:2.5 -->

saṅkhāresu sati …

<!-- @segment sn24.4:2.6 -->

viññāṇe sati, viññāṇaṁ upādāya, viññāṇaṁ abhinivissa evaṁ diṭṭhi uppajjati:

<!-- @segment sn24.4:2.7 -->

‘no cassaṁ, no ca me siyā, nābhavissa, na me bhavissatī’ti.

<!-- @segment sn24.4:3.1 -->

Taṁ kiṁ maññatha, bhikkhave,

<!-- @segment sn24.4:3.2 -->

rūpaṁ niccaṁ vā aniccaṁ vā”ti?

<!-- @segment sn24.4:3.3 -->

“Aniccaṁ, bhante” …pe…

<!-- @segment sn24.4:3.4 -->

api nu taṁ anupādāya evaṁ diṭṭhi uppajjeyya:

<!-- @segment sn24.4:3.5 -->

‘no cassaṁ, no ca me siyā, nābhavissa, na me bhavissatī’”ti?

<!-- @segment sn24.4:3.6 -->

“No hetaṁ, bhante”.

<!-- @segment sn24.4:3.7 -->

“Vedanā …

<!-- @segment sn24.4:3.8 -->

saññā …

<!-- @segment sn24.4:3.9 -->

saṅkhārā …

<!-- @segment sn24.4:3.10 -->

viññāṇaṁ niccaṁ vā aniccaṁ vā”ti?

<!-- @segment sn24.4:3.11 -->

“Aniccaṁ, bhante …pe…

<!-- @segment sn24.4:3.12 -->

api nu taṁ anupādāya evaṁ diṭṭhi uppajjeyya:

<!-- @segment sn24.4:3.13 -->

‘no cassaṁ, no ca me siyā, nābhavissa, na me bhavissatī’”ti?

<!-- @segment sn24.4:3.14 -->

“No hetaṁ, bhante”.

<!-- @segment sn24.4:3.15 -->

“Yampidaṁ diṭṭhaṁ sutaṁ mutaṁ viññātaṁ pattaṁ pariyesitaṁ anuvicaritaṁ manasā tampi niccaṁ vā aniccaṁ vā”ti?

<!-- @segment sn24.4:3.16 -->

“Aniccaṁ, bhante …pe…

<!-- @segment sn24.4:3.17 -->

api nu taṁ anupādāya evaṁ diṭṭhi uppajjeyya:

<!-- @segment sn24.4:3.18 -->

‘no cassaṁ, no ca me siyā, nābhavissa, na me bhavissatī’”ti?

<!-- @segment sn24.4:3.19 -->

“No hetaṁ, bhante”.

<!-- @segment sn24.4:4.1 -->

“Yato kho, bhikkhave, ariyasāvakassa imesu ca ṭhānesu kaṅkhā pahīnā hoti, dukkhepissa kaṅkhā pahīnā hoti …pe… dukkhanirodhagāminiyā paṭipadāyapissa kaṅkhā pahīnā hoti—

<!-- @segment sn24.4:4.2 -->

ayaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, ariyasāvako sotāpanno avinipātadhammo niyato sambodhiparāyano”ti.

<!-- @segment sn24.4:4.3 -->

Catutthaṁ.
