<!-- @segment sn47.85-94:1.1 -->

“Tisso imā, bhikkhave, esanā.

<!-- @segment sn47.85-94:1.2 -->

Katamā tisso?

<!-- @segment sn47.85-94:1.3 -->

Kāmesanā, bhavesanā, brahmacariyesanā”ti

<!-- @segment sn47.85-94:1.4 -->

vitthāretabbaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn47.85-94:1.5 -->

Esanāvaggo navamo.

<!-- @segment sn47.85-94:2.0 -->

Tassuddānaṁ

<!-- @segment sn47.85-94:2.1 -->

Esanā vidhā āsavo,

<!-- @segment sn47.85-94:2.2 -->

Bhavo ca dukkhatā tisso;

<!-- @segment sn47.85-94:2.3 -->

Khilaṁ malañca nīgho ca,

<!-- @segment sn47.85-94:2.4 -->

Vedanā taṇhā tasināya cāti.
