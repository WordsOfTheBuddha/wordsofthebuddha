<!-- @segment sn22.19:1.1 -->

Sāvatthinidānaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn22.19:1.2 -->

“Rūpaṁ, bhikkhave, dukkhaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn22.19:1.3 -->

Yopi hetu yopi paccayo rūpassa uppādāya, sopi dukkho.

<!-- @segment sn22.19:1.4 -->

Dukkhasambhūtaṁ, bhikkhave, rūpaṁ kuto sukhaṁ bhavissati.

<!-- @segment sn22.19:1.5 -->

Vedanā dukkhā …

<!-- @segment sn22.19:1.6 -->

saññā dukkhā …

<!-- @segment sn22.19:1.7 -->

saṅkhārā dukkhā …

<!-- @segment sn22.19:1.8 -->

viññāṇaṁ dukkhaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn22.19:1.9 -->

Yopi hetu yopi paccayo viññāṇassa uppādāya, sopi dukkho.

<!-- @segment sn22.19:1.10 -->

Dukkhasambhūtaṁ, bhikkhave, viññāṇaṁ kuto sukhaṁ bhavissati.

<!-- @segment sn22.19:1.11 -->

Evaṁ passaṁ …pe…

<!-- @segment sn22.19:1.12 -->

nāparaṁ itthattāyā’ti pajānātī”ti.

<!-- @segment sn22.19:1.13 -->

Aṭṭhamaṁ.
