<!-- @segment sn35.207-209:1.1 -->

“Cakkhu, bhikkhave, dukkhaṁ atītaṁ anāgataṁ paccuppannaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn35.207-209:1.2 -->

Yaṁ dukkhaṁ, tadanattā.

<!-- @segment sn35.207-209:1.3 -->

Yadanattā, taṁ ‘netaṁ mama, nesohamasmi, na meso attā’ti evametaṁ yathābhūtaṁ sammappaññāya daṭṭhabbaṁ …pe…

<!-- @segment sn35.207-209:1.4 -->

jivhā dukkhā …pe…

<!-- @segment sn35.207-209:1.5 -->

mano dukkho atīto anāgato paccuppanno.

<!-- @segment sn35.207-209:1.6 -->

Yaṁ dukkhaṁ, tadanattā.

<!-- @segment sn35.207-209:1.7 -->

Yadanattā, taṁ ‘netaṁ mama, nesohamasmi, na meso attā’ti evametaṁ yathābhūtaṁ sammappaññāya daṭṭhabbaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn35.207-209:1.8 -->

Evaṁ passaṁ …pe…

<!-- @segment sn35.207-209:1.9 -->

nāparaṁ itthattāyāti pajānātī”ti.
