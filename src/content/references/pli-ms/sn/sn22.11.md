<!-- @segment sn22.11:1.1 -->

Sāvatthinidānaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn22.11:1.2 -->

“Rūpaṁ, bhikkhave, anattā atītānāgataṁ;

<!-- @segment sn22.11:1.3 -->

ko pana vādo paccuppannassa.

<!-- @segment sn22.11:1.4 -->

Evaṁ passaṁ, bhikkhave, sutavā ariyasāvako atītasmiṁ rūpasmiṁ anapekkho hoti;

<!-- @segment sn22.11:1.5 -->

anāgataṁ rūpaṁ nābhinandati;

<!-- @segment sn22.11:1.6 -->

paccuppannassa rūpassa nibbidāya virāgāya nirodhāya paṭipanno hoti.

<!-- @segment sn22.11:1.7 -->

Vedanā anattā …

<!-- @segment sn22.11:1.8 -->

saññā anattā …

<!-- @segment sn22.11:1.9 -->

saṅkhārā anattā …

<!-- @segment sn22.11:1.10 -->

viññāṇaṁ anattā atītānāgataṁ;

<!-- @segment sn22.11:1.11 -->

ko pana vādo paccuppannassa.

<!-- @segment sn22.11:1.12 -->

Evaṁ passaṁ, bhikkhave, sutavā ariyasāvako atītasmiṁ viññāṇasmiṁ anapekkho hoti;

<!-- @segment sn22.11:1.13 -->

anāgataṁ viññāṇaṁ nābhinandati;

<!-- @segment sn22.11:1.14 -->

paccuppannassa viññāṇassa nibbidāya virāgāya nirodhāya paṭipanno hotī”ti.

<!-- @segment sn22.11:1.15 -->

Ekādasamaṁ.

<!-- @segment sn22.11:1.16 -->

Nakulapituvaggo paṭhamo.

<!-- @segment sn22.11:2.0 -->

Tassuddānaṁ

<!-- @segment sn22.11:2.1 -->

Nakulapitā devadahā,

<!-- @segment sn22.11:2.2 -->

Dvepi hāliddikāni ca;

<!-- @segment sn22.11:2.3 -->

Samādhipaṭisallāṇā,

<!-- @segment sn22.11:2.4 -->

Upādāparitassanā duve;

<!-- @segment sn22.11:2.5 -->

Atītānāgatapaccuppannā,

<!-- @segment sn22.11:2.6 -->

Vaggo tena pavuccati.
