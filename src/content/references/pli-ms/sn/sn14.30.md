<!-- @segment sn14.30:1.1 -->

Ekaṁ samayaṁ bhagavā sāvatthiyaṁ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme …

<!-- @segment sn14.30:1.2 -->

“catasso imā, bhikkhave, dhātuyo.

<!-- @segment sn14.30:1.3 -->

Katamā catasso?

<!-- @segment sn14.30:1.4 -->

Pathavīdhātu, āpodhātu, tejodhātu, vāyodhātu—

<!-- @segment sn14.30:1.5 -->

imā kho, bhikkhave, catasso dhātuyo”ti.

<!-- @segment sn14.30:1.6 -->

Paṭhamaṁ.
