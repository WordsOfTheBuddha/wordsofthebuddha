<!-- @segment snp1.6:1.1 -->

So I have heard.

<!-- @segment snp1.6:1.2 -->

At one time the Buddha was staying near Sāvatthī in Jeta’s Grove, Anāthapiṇḍika’s monastery.

<!-- @segment snp1.6:1.3 -->

Then, late at night, a glorious deity, lighting up the entire Jeta’s Grove, went up to the Buddha, bowed, and stood to one side.

<!-- @segment snp1.6:1.4 -->

Standing to one side, that deity addressed the Buddha in verse:

<!-- @segment snp1.6:2.1 -->

“We ask Gotama

<!-- @segment snp1.6:2.2 -->

about a man’s downfall.

<!-- @segment snp1.6:2.3 -->

We have come to ask you, sir:

<!-- @segment snp1.6:2.4 -->

what leads to downfall?”

<!-- @segment snp1.6:3.1 -->

“It’s easy to understand success,

<!-- @segment snp1.6:3.2 -->

understanding downfall is just as easy.

<!-- @segment snp1.6:3.3 -->

One who loves the teaching succeeds,

<!-- @segment snp1.6:3.4 -->

but a hater of the teaching meets their downfall.”

<!-- @segment snp1.6:4.1 -->

“We understand what you’re saying,

<!-- @segment snp1.6:4.2 -->

this is the first downfall.

<!-- @segment snp1.6:4.3 -->

Tell us the second, Blessed One:

<!-- @segment snp1.6:4.4 -->

what leads to downfall?”

<!-- @segment snp1.6:5.1 -->

“The bad are dear to him,

<!-- @segment snp1.6:5.2 -->

he has no love for the good.

<!-- @segment snp1.6:5.3 -->

He believes the teaching of the bad;

<!-- @segment snp1.6:5.4 -->

and that leads to his downfall.”

<!-- @segment snp1.6:6.1 -->

“We understand what you’re saying,

<!-- @segment snp1.6:6.2 -->

this is the second downfall.

<!-- @segment snp1.6:6.3 -->

Tell us the third, Blessed One:

<!-- @segment snp1.6:6.4 -->

what leads to downfall?”

<!-- @segment snp1.6:7.1 -->

“Fond of sleep, fond of company,

<!-- @segment snp1.6:7.2 -->

a man who does no work;

<!-- @segment snp1.6:7.3 -->

he’s lazy, marked by anger,

<!-- @segment snp1.6:7.4 -->

and that leads to his downfall.”

<!-- @segment snp1.6:8.1 -->

“We understand what you’re saying,

<!-- @segment snp1.6:8.2 -->

this is the third downfall.

<!-- @segment snp1.6:8.3 -->

Tell us the fourth, Blessed One:

<!-- @segment snp1.6:8.4 -->

what leads to downfall?”

<!-- @segment snp1.6:9.1 -->

“Though able, he does not look after

<!-- @segment snp1.6:9.2 -->

his mother and father

<!-- @segment snp1.6:9.3 -->

when elderly, past their prime,

<!-- @segment snp1.6:9.4 -->

and that leads to his downfall.”

<!-- @segment snp1.6:10.1 -->

“We understand what you’re saying,

<!-- @segment snp1.6:10.2 -->

this is the fourth downfall.

<!-- @segment snp1.6:10.3 -->

Tell us the fifth, Blessed One:

<!-- @segment snp1.6:10.4 -->

what leads to downfall?”

<!-- @segment snp1.6:11.1 -->

“He deceives with lies

<!-- @segment snp1.6:11.2 -->

ascetics and brahmins

<!-- @segment snp1.6:11.3 -->

and other supplicants,

<!-- @segment snp1.6:11.4 -->

and that leads to his downfall.”

<!-- @segment snp1.6:12.1 -->

“We understand what you’re saying,

<!-- @segment snp1.6:12.2 -->

this is the fifth downfall.

<!-- @segment snp1.6:12.3 -->

Tell us the sixth, Blessed One:

<!-- @segment snp1.6:12.4 -->

what leads to downfall?”

<!-- @segment snp1.6:13.1 -->

“A man with plenty of wealth,

<!-- @segment snp1.6:13.2 -->

including gold coin and food,

<!-- @segment snp1.6:13.3 -->

eats delicacies alone,

<!-- @segment snp1.6:13.4 -->

and that leads to his downfall.”

<!-- @segment snp1.6:14.1 -->

“We understand what you’re saying,

<!-- @segment snp1.6:14.2 -->

this is the sixth downfall.

<!-- @segment snp1.6:14.3 -->

Tell us the seventh, Blessed One:

<!-- @segment snp1.6:14.4 -->

what leads to downfall?”

<!-- @segment snp1.6:15.1 -->

“Vain of birth, wealth,

<!-- @segment snp1.6:15.2 -->

and clan, a man

<!-- @segment snp1.6:15.3 -->

looks down on his own family,

<!-- @segment snp1.6:15.4 -->

and that leads to his downfall.”

<!-- @segment snp1.6:16.1 -->

“We understand what you’re saying,

<!-- @segment snp1.6:16.2 -->

this is the seventh downfall.

<!-- @segment snp1.6:16.3 -->

Tell us the eighth, Blessed One:

<!-- @segment snp1.6:16.4 -->

what leads to downfall?”

<!-- @segment snp1.6:17.1 -->

“In womanizing, drinking,

<!-- @segment snp1.6:17.2 -->

and gambling, a man

<!-- @segment snp1.6:17.3 -->

wastes all that he has earned,

<!-- @segment snp1.6:17.4 -->

and that leads to his downfall.”

<!-- @segment snp1.6:18.1 -->

“We understand what you’re saying,

<!-- @segment snp1.6:18.2 -->

this is the eighth downfall.

<!-- @segment snp1.6:18.3 -->

Tell us the ninth, Blessed One:

<!-- @segment snp1.6:18.4 -->

what leads to downfall?”

<!-- @segment snp1.6:19.1 -->

“Not content with his own partners,

<!-- @segment snp1.6:19.2 -->

he debauches himself with prostitutes,

<!-- @segment snp1.6:19.3 -->

and with others’ partners,

<!-- @segment snp1.6:19.4 -->

and that leads to his downfall.”

<!-- @segment snp1.6:20.1 -->

“We understand what you’re saying,

<!-- @segment snp1.6:20.2 -->

this is the ninth downfall.

<!-- @segment snp1.6:20.3 -->

Tell us the tenth, Blessed One:

<!-- @segment snp1.6:20.4 -->

what leads to downfall?”

<!-- @segment snp1.6:21.1 -->

“A man well past his prime

<!-- @segment snp1.6:21.2 -->

marries a girl with budding breasts;

<!-- @segment snp1.6:21.3 -->

he cannot sleep for jealousy,

<!-- @segment snp1.6:21.4 -->

and that leads to his downfall.”

<!-- @segment snp1.6:22.1 -->

“We understand what you’re saying,

<!-- @segment snp1.6:22.2 -->

this is the tenth downfall.

<!-- @segment snp1.6:22.3 -->

Tell us the eleventh, Blessed One:

<!-- @segment snp1.6:22.4 -->

what leads to downfall?”

<!-- @segment snp1.6:23.1 -->

“He places in authority

<!-- @segment snp1.6:23.2 -->

a woman or a man

<!-- @segment snp1.6:23.3 -->

who’s a drunkard and a wastrel,

<!-- @segment snp1.6:23.4 -->

and that leads to his downfall.”

<!-- @segment snp1.6:24.1 -->

“We understand what you’re saying,

<!-- @segment snp1.6:24.2 -->

this is the eleventh downfall.

<!-- @segment snp1.6:24.3 -->

Tell us the twelfth, Blessed One:

<!-- @segment snp1.6:24.4 -->

what leads to downfall?”

<!-- @segment snp1.6:25.1 -->

“A man of little wealth and strong craving,

<!-- @segment snp1.6:25.2 -->

born into an aristocratic family,

<!-- @segment snp1.6:25.3 -->

sets his sights on kingship,

<!-- @segment snp1.6:25.4 -->

and that leads to his downfall.

<!-- @segment snp1.6:26.1 -->

Seeing these downfalls in the world,

<!-- @segment snp1.6:26.2 -->

an astute and noble person,

<!-- @segment snp1.6:26.3 -->

accomplished in vision,

<!-- @segment snp1.6:26.4 -->

will enjoy a world of grace.”
