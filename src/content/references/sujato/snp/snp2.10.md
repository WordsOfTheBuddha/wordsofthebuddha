title: Get Up!

<!-- @segment snp2.10:1.1 -->

Get up and meditate!

<!-- @segment snp2.10:1.2 -->

What’s the point in your sleeping?

<!-- @segment snp2.10:1.3 -->

How can the afflicted slumber

<!-- @segment snp2.10:1.4 -->

when inflicted by an arrow strike?

<!-- @segment snp2.10:2.1 -->

Get up and meditate!

<!-- @segment snp2.10:2.2 -->

Train hard for peace!

<!-- @segment snp2.10:2.3 -->

The King of Death has caught you heedless—

<!-- @segment snp2.10:2.4 -->

don’t let him fool you under his sway.

<!-- @segment snp2.10:3.1 -->

Needy gods and humans

<!-- @segment snp2.10:3.2 -->

are held back by clinging:

<!-- @segment snp2.10:3.3 -->

get over it.

<!-- @segment snp2.10:3.4 -->

Don’t let the moment pass you by.

<!-- @segment snp2.10:3.5 -->

For if you miss your moment

<!-- @segment snp2.10:3.6 -->

you’ll grieve when sent to hell.

<!-- @segment snp2.10:4.1 -->

Negligence is always dust;

<!-- @segment snp2.10:4.2 -->

dust follows right behind negligence.

<!-- @segment snp2.10:4.3 -->

Through diligence and knowledge,

<!-- @segment snp2.10:4.4 -->

pluck out the dart from yourself.
