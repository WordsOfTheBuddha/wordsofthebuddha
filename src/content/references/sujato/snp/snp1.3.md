<!-- @segment snp1.3:1.1 -->

Seeing this danger born of affection,

<!-- @segment snp1.3:2.4 -->

live alone like a horned rhino.

<!-- @segment snp1.3:3.1 -->

<!-- @segment snp1.3:3.3 -->

Seeing this peril in intimacy,

<!-- @segment snp1.3:3.4 -->

live alone like a horned rhino.

<!-- @segment snp1.3:4.1 -->

As a spreading bamboo gets entangled,

<!-- @segment snp1.3:4.2 -->

so does concern for partners and children.

<!-- @segment snp1.3:4.3 -->

<!-- @segment snp1.3:4.4 -->

live alone like a horned rhino.

<!-- @segment snp1.3:6.3 -->

<!-- @segment snp1.3:6.4 -->

<!-- @segment snp1.3:7.1 -->

<!-- @segment snp1.3:7.3 -->

<!-- @segment snp1.3:7.4 -->

live alone like a horned rhino.

<!-- @segment snp1.3:8.2 -->

content with whatever comes your way;

<!-- @segment snp1.3:8.3 -->

prevailing over adversities, dauntless,

<!-- @segment snp1.3:8.4 -->

live alone like a horned rhino.

<!-- @segment snp1.3:9.1 -->

<!-- @segment snp1.3:9.2 -->

as are some layfolk dwelling at home.

<!-- @segment snp1.3:9.3 -->

Don’t worry about others’ children,

<!-- @segment snp1.3:9.4 -->

live alone like a horned rhino.

<!-- @segment snp1.3:10.1 -->

Having shed the marks of the home life,

<!-- @segment snp1.3:10.2 -->

like the fallen leaves of the Shady Orchid Tree;

<!-- @segment snp1.3:10.3 -->

having cut the bonds of the home life, a hero would

<!-- @segment snp1.3:10.4 -->

live alone like a horned rhino.

<!-- @segment snp1.3:11.1 -->

If you find an alert companion,

<!-- @segment snp1.3:11.2 -->

an attentive friend to live happily together,

<!-- @segment snp1.3:11.3 -->

then, overcoming all adversities,

<!-- @segment snp1.3:11.4 -->

<!-- @segment snp1.3:12.3 -->

then, like a king who flees his conquered realm,

<!-- @segment snp1.3:12.4 -->

wander alone like a tusker in the wilds.

<!-- @segment snp1.3:13.1 -->

<!-- @segment snp1.3:13.3 -->

<!-- @segment snp1.3:13.4 -->

live alone like a horned rhino.

<!-- @segment snp1.3:14.1 -->

<!-- @segment snp1.3:14.3 -->

<!-- @segment snp1.3:14.4 -->

live alone like a horned rhino.

<!-- @segment snp1.3:15.1 -->

<!-- @segment snp1.3:15.2 -->

<!-- @segment snp1.3:15.3 -->

Seeing this peril in the future,

<!-- @segment snp1.3:15.4 -->

live alone like a horned rhino.

<!-- @segment snp1.3:16.1 -->

<!-- @segment snp1.3:16.2 -->

<!-- @segment snp1.3:16.3 -->

Seeing danger in sensual stimulations,

<!-- @segment snp1.3:16.4 -->

live alone like a horned rhino.

<!-- @segment snp1.3:17.1 -->

<!-- @segment snp1.3:17.2 -->

an illness, a dart, and a danger for me.

<!-- @segment snp1.3:17.3 -->

Seeing this peril in sensuality,

<!-- @segment snp1.3:17.4 -->

live alone like a horned rhino.

<!-- @segment snp1.3:18.1 -->

<!-- @segment snp1.3:18.2 -->

<!-- @segment snp1.3:18.3 -->

having put up with all these things,

<!-- @segment snp1.3:18.4 -->

live alone like a horned rhino.

<!-- @segment snp1.3:19.1 -->

<!-- @segment snp1.3:19.2 -->

forsaking the herd,

<!-- @segment snp1.3:19.3 -->

stays as it pleases in the forest,

<!-- @segment snp1.3:19.4 -->

live alone like a horned rhino.

<!-- @segment snp1.3:20.1 -->

It’s impossible for one who delights in company

<!-- @segment snp1.3:20.2 -->

to experience even temporary freedom.

<!-- @segment snp1.3:20.3 -->

Heeding the speech of the Kinsman of the Sun,

<!-- @segment snp1.3:20.4 -->

live alone like a horned rhino.

<!-- @segment snp1.3:21.1 -->

<!-- @segment snp1.3:21.2 -->

<!-- @segment snp1.3:21.3 -->

I’ve given rise to knowledge, <j>and need no-one to guide me”,

<!-- @segment snp1.3:21.4 -->

live alone like a horned rhino.

<!-- @segment snp1.3:22.1 -->

<!-- @segment snp1.3:22.3 -->

<!-- @segment snp1.3:22.4 -->

live alone like a horned rhino.

<!-- @segment snp1.3:23.1 -->

<!-- @segment snp1.3:23.2 -->

<!-- @segment snp1.3:23.3 -->

Don’t befriend the heedless and the hankering, but

<!-- @segment snp1.3:23.4 -->

live alone like a horned rhino.

<!-- @segment snp1.3:24.1 -->

<!-- @segment snp1.3:24.2 -->

<!-- @segment snp1.3:24.3 -->

When you understand the meanings <j>and have dispelled doubt,

<!-- @segment snp1.3:24.4 -->

live alone like a horned rhino.

<!-- @segment snp1.3:25.1 -->

<!-- @segment snp1.3:25.2 -->

<!-- @segment snp1.3:25.3 -->

<!-- @segment snp1.3:25.4 -->

live alone like a horned rhino.

<!-- @segment snp1.3:26.3 -->

having given up sensual pleasures to this extent,

<!-- @segment snp1.3:26.4 -->

live alone like a horned rhino.
