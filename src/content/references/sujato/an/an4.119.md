<!-- @segment an4.119:1.1 -->

“Mendicants, there are these four perils.

<!-- @segment an4.119:1.2 -->

What four?

<!-- @segment an4.119:1.3 -->

The perils of rebirth, old age, sickness, and death.

<!-- @segment an4.119:1.4 -->

These are the four perils.”
