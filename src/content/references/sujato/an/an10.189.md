<!-- @segment an10.189:1.1 -->

“I will teach you the noble path and the ignoble path. …

<!-- @segment an10.189:1.2 -->



<!-- @segment an10.189:1.3 -->

And what is the ignoble path?

<!-- @segment an10.189:1.4 -->

Killing living creatures …

<!-- @segment an10.189:1.5 -->

wrong view.

<!-- @segment an10.189:1.6 -->

This is called the ignoble path.

<!-- @segment an10.189:2.1 -->

And what is the noble path?

<!-- @segment an10.189:2.2 -->

Not killing living creatures …

<!-- @segment an10.189:2.3 -->

right view.

<!-- @segment an10.189:2.4 -->

This is called the noble path.”
