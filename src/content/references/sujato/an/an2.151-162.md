<!-- @segment an2.151:1.0 -->

151

<!-- @segment an2.151:1.1 -->

“There are these two kinds of welcome.

<!-- @segment an2.151:1.2 -->

What two?

<!-- @segment an2.151:1.3 -->

Welcome in things of the flesh and welcome in the teaching.

<!-- @segment an2.151:1.4 -->

These are the two kinds of welcome.

<!-- @segment an2.151:1.5 -->

The better of these two kinds of welcome is the welcome in the teaching.”
