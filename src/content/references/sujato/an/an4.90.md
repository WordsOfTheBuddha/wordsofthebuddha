<!-- @segment an4.90:1.1 -->

“Mendicants, these four individuals are found in the world.

<!-- @segment an4.90:1.2 -->

What four?

<!-- @segment an4.90:1.3 -->

The confirmed ascetic, the white lotus ascetic, the pink lotus ascetic, and the delicate ascetic of ascetics.

<!-- @segment an4.90:2.1 -->

And how is an individual a confirmed ascetic?

<!-- @segment an4.90:2.2 -->

It’s when a mendicant is a trainee who hasn’t achieved their heart’s desire, but lives aspiring to the supreme sanctuary from the yoke.

<!-- @segment an4.90:2.3 -->

That’s how an individual is a confirmed ascetic.

<!-- @segment an4.90:3.1 -->

And how is an individual a white lotus ascetic?

<!-- @segment an4.90:3.2 -->

It’s when a mendicant meditates observing rise and fall in the five grasping aggregates.

<!-- @segment an4.90:3.3 -->

‘Such is form, such is the origin of form, such is the ending of form.

<!-- @segment an4.90:3.4 -->

Such is feeling …

<!-- @segment an4.90:3.5 -->

Such is perception …

<!-- @segment an4.90:3.6 -->

Such are choices …

<!-- @segment an4.90:3.7 -->

Such is consciousness, such is the origin of consciousness, such is the ending of consciousness.’

<!-- @segment an4.90:3.8 -->

But they don’t have direct meditative experience of the eight liberations.

<!-- @segment an4.90:3.9 -->

That’s how an individual is a white lotus ascetic.

<!-- @segment an4.90:4.1 -->

And how is an individual a pink lotus ascetic?

<!-- @segment an4.90:4.2 -->

It’s when a mendicant meditates observing rise and fall in the five grasping aggregates.

<!-- @segment an4.90:4.3 -->

‘Such is form, such is the origin of form, such is the ending of form.

<!-- @segment an4.90:4.4 -->

Such is feeling …

<!-- @segment an4.90:4.5 -->

Such is perception …

<!-- @segment an4.90:4.6 -->

Such are choices …

<!-- @segment an4.90:4.7 -->

Such is consciousness, such is the origin of consciousness, such is the ending of consciousness.’

<!-- @segment an4.90:4.8 -->

And they have direct meditative experience of the eight liberations.

<!-- @segment an4.90:4.9 -->

That’s how an individual is a pink lotus ascetic.

<!-- @segment an4.90:5.1 -->

And how is an individual a delicate ascetic of ascetics?

<!-- @segment an4.90:5.2 -->

It’s when a mendicant usually uses only what they’ve been invited to accept … And if anyone should be rightly called a delicate ascetic of ascetics, it’s me.

<!-- @segment an4.90:5.3 -->

These are the four individuals found in the world.”
