<!-- @segment an5.92:1.1 -->

“Mendicants, there are five accomplishments.

<!-- @segment an5.92:1.2 -->

What five?

<!-- @segment an5.92:1.3 -->

Accomplishment in ethics, immersion, wisdom, freedom, and the knowledge and vision of freedom.

<!-- @segment an5.92:1.4 -->

These are the five accomplishments.”
