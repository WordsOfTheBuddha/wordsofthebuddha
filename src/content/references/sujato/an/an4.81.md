<!-- @segment an4.81:1.1 -->

“Mendicants, someone with four qualities is placed in hell as if delivered there.

<!-- @segment an4.81:1.2 -->

What four?

<!-- @segment an4.81:1.3 -->

They kill living creatures, steal, commit sexual misconduct, and lie.

<!-- @segment an4.81:1.4 -->

Someone with these four qualities is placed in hell as if delivered there.

<!-- @segment an4.81:2.1 -->

Someone with four qualities is placed in heaven as if delivered there.

<!-- @segment an4.81:2.2 -->

What four?

<!-- @segment an4.81:2.3 -->

They don’t kill living creatures, steal, commit sexual misconduct, or lie.

<!-- @segment an4.81:2.4 -->

Someone with these four qualities is placed in heaven as if delivered there.”
