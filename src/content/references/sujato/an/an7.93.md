<!-- @segment an7.93:1.1 -->

“Mendicants, there are these seven bad qualities.

<!-- @segment an7.93:1.2 -->

What seven?

<!-- @segment an7.93:1.3 -->

Someone is faithless, shameless, imprudent, unlearned, lazy, unmindful, and witless.

<!-- @segment an7.93:1.4 -->

These are the seven bad qualities.”
