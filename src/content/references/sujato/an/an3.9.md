<!-- @segment an3.9:1.1 -->

“When a foolish, incompetent, untrue person has three qualities they keep themselves broken and damaged. They deserve to be blamed and criticized by sensible people, and they brim with much wickedness.

<!-- @segment an3.9:1.2 -->

What three?

<!-- @segment an3.9:1.3 -->

Bad conduct by way of body, speech, and mind.

<!-- @segment an3.9:1.4 -->

When a foolish, incompetent untrue person possesses these three qualities they keep themselves broken and damaged. They deserve to be blamed and criticized by sensible people, and they create much wickedness.

<!-- @segment an3.9:2.1 -->

When an astute, competent true person has three qualities they keep themselves intact and unscathed. They don’t deserve to be blamed and criticized by sensible people, and they brim with much merit.

<!-- @segment an3.9:2.2 -->

What three?

<!-- @segment an3.9:2.3 -->

Good conduct by way of body, speech, and mind.

<!-- @segment an3.9:2.4 -->

When an astute, competent true person possesses these three qualities they keep themselves healthy and whole. They don’t deserve to be blamed and criticized by sensible people, and they create much merit.”
