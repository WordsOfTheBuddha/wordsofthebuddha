<!-- @segment an4.250:1.1 -->

“Mendicants, there are these four ignoble expressions.

<!-- @segment an4.250:1.2 -->

What four?

<!-- @segment an4.250:1.3 -->

Saying you’ve seen, heard, thought, or known something, but you haven’t.

<!-- @segment an4.250:1.4 -->

These are the four ignoble expressions.”
