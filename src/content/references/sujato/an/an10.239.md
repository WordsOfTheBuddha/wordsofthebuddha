title: Untitled Discourse on Greed (3rd)
---

“For insight into greed, ten things should be developed.

What ten?

Right view, right purpose, right speech, right action, right livelihood, right effort, right mindfulness, right immersion, right knowledge, and right freedom.

For insight into greed, these ten things should be developed.”
