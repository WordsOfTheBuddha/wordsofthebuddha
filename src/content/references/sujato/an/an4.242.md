<!-- @segment an4.242:1.1 -->

“Mendicants, you can expect four benefits from relying on a true person.

<!-- @segment an4.242:1.2 -->

What four?

<!-- @segment an4.242:1.3 -->

Growth in noble ethics, immersion, wisdom, and freedom.

<!-- @segment an4.242:1.4 -->

You can expect these four benefits from relying on a true person.”
