<!-- @segment an4.109:1.1 -->

“Mendicants, there are these four kinds of tree.

<!-- @segment an4.109:1.2 -->

What four?

<!-- @segment an4.109:1.3 -->

One is a softwood surrounded by softwoods,

<!-- @segment an4.109:1.4 -->

one is a softwood surrounded by hardwoods,

<!-- @segment an4.109:1.5 -->

one is a hardwood surrounded by softwoods, and

<!-- @segment an4.109:1.6 -->

one is a hardwood surrounded by hardwoods.

<!-- @segment an4.109:1.7 -->

These are the four kinds of tree.

<!-- @segment an4.109:1.8 -->

In the same way, these four individuals similar to trees are found in the world.

<!-- @segment an4.109:1.9 -->

What four?

<!-- @segment an4.109:1.10 -->

One is a softwood surrounded by softwoods,

<!-- @segment an4.109:1.11 -->

one is a softwood surrounded by hardwoods,

<!-- @segment an4.109:1.12 -->

one is a hardwood surrounded by softwoods, and

<!-- @segment an4.109:1.13 -->

one is a hardwood surrounded by hardwoods.

<!-- @segment an4.109:2.1 -->

And how is an individual a softwood surrounded by softwoods?

<!-- @segment an4.109:2.2 -->

It’s when an individual is unethical, of bad character,

<!-- @segment an4.109:2.3 -->

and their followers are the same.

<!-- @segment an4.109:2.4 -->

That’s how an individual is a softwood surrounded by softwoods.

<!-- @segment an4.109:2.5 -->

That individual is like a softwood tree that’s surrounded by softwoods, I say.

<!-- @segment an4.109:2.6 -->



<!-- @segment an4.109:3.1 -->

And how is an individual a softwood surrounded by hardwoods?

<!-- @segment an4.109:3.2 -->

It’s when an individual is unethical, of bad character,

<!-- @segment an4.109:3.3 -->

but their followers are ethical, of good character. …

<!-- @segment an4.109:3.4 -->



<!-- @segment an4.109:3.5 -->



<!-- @segment an4.109:3.6 -->



<!-- @segment an4.109:4.1 -->

And how is an individual a hardwood surrounded by softwoods?

<!-- @segment an4.109:4.2 -->

It’s when an individual is ethical, of good character,

<!-- @segment an4.109:4.3 -->

but their followers are unethical, of bad character. …

<!-- @segment an4.109:4.4 -->



<!-- @segment an4.109:4.5 -->



<!-- @segment an4.109:4.6 -->



<!-- @segment an4.109:5.1 -->

And how is an individual a hardwood surrounded by hardwoods?

<!-- @segment an4.109:5.2 -->

It’s when an individual is ethical, of good character,

<!-- @segment an4.109:5.3 -->

and their followers are the same.

<!-- @segment an4.109:5.4 -->



<!-- @segment an4.109:5.5 -->



<!-- @segment an4.109:5.6 -->



<!-- @segment an4.109:5.7 -->

These four individuals similar to trees are found in the world.”
