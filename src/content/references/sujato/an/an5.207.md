<!-- @segment an5.207:1.1 -->

“Mendicants, there are these five benefits of porridge.

<!-- @segment an5.207:1.2 -->

What five?

<!-- @segment an5.207:1.3 -->

It wards off hunger, quenches thirst, settles the wind, cleans the bladder, and helps digestion.

<!-- @segment an5.207:1.4 -->

These are the five benefits of porridge.”
