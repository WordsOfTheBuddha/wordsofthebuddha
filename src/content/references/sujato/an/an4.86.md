<!-- @segment an4.86:1.1 -->

“These four individuals are found in the world.

<!-- @segment an4.86:1.2 -->

What four?

<!-- @segment an4.86:1.3 -->

One sunk low who sinks lower,

<!-- @segment an4.86:1.4 -->

one sunk low who rises high,

<!-- @segment an4.86:1.5 -->

one risen high who sinks low, and

<!-- @segment an4.86:1.6 -->

one risen high who rises higher.

<!-- @segment an4.86:1.7 -->

… These are the four individuals found in the world.”
