<!-- @segment an4.258:1.1 -->

“Mendicants, when families don’t stay wealthy for long, it’s always for one or more of these four reasons.

<!-- @segment an4.258:1.2 -->

What four?

<!-- @segment an4.258:1.3 -->

They don’t look for what’s lost; they don’t repair old things; they eat and drink too much; or they put an unethical woman or man in charge.

<!-- @segment an4.258:1.4 -->

When families don’t stay wealthy for long, it’s always for one or more of these four reasons.

<!-- @segment an4.258:2.1 -->

When families do stay wealthy for long, it’s always for one or more of these four reasons.

<!-- @segment an4.258:2.2 -->

What four?

<!-- @segment an4.258:2.3 -->

They look for what’s lost; they repair old things; they eat and drink in moderation; and they put an ethical woman or man in charge.

<!-- @segment an4.258:2.4 -->

When families do stay wealthy for long, it’s always for one or more of these four reasons.”
