title: Principles That Should Be Developed

<!-- @segment an10.195:1.1 -->

“I will teach you the principle to develop and the principle not to develop. …

<!-- @segment an10.195:1.2 -->

Listen and apply your mind well, I will speak.

<!-- @segment an10.195:1.3 -->

And what is the principle not to develop?

<!-- @segment an10.195:1.4 -->

Killing living creatures …

<!-- @segment an10.195:1.5 -->

wrong view.

<!-- @segment an10.195:1.6 -->

This is called the principle not to develop.

<!-- @segment an10.195:2.1 -->

And what is the principle to develop?

<!-- @segment an10.195:2.2 -->

Not killing living creatures …

<!-- @segment an10.195:2.3 -->

right view.

<!-- @segment an10.195:2.4 -->

This is called the principle to develop.”
