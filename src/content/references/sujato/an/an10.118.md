<!-- @segment an10.118:1.1 -->

“Mendicants, I will teach you the near shore and the far shore.

<!-- @segment an10.118:1.2 -->

Listen and apply your mind well, I will speak.”

<!-- @segment an10.118:1.3 -->

“Yes, sir,” they replied.

<!-- @segment an10.118:1.4 -->

The Buddha said this:

<!-- @segment an10.118:2.1 -->

“And what, mendicants, is the near shore? What is the far shore?

<!-- @segment an10.118:2.2 -->

Wrong view is the near shore, and right view is the far shore. …

<!-- @segment an10.118:2.3 -->

Wrong freedom is the near shore, and right freedom is the far shore.

<!-- @segment an10.118:2.4 -->

This is the near shore, and this is the far shore.

<!-- @segment an10.118:3.1 -->

Few are those among humans

<!-- @segment an10.118:3.2 -->

who cross to the far shore.

<!-- @segment an10.118:3.3 -->

The rest just run

<!-- @segment an10.118:3.4 -->

around on the near shore.

<!-- @segment an10.118:4.1 -->

When the teaching is well explained,

<!-- @segment an10.118:4.2 -->

those who practice accordingly

<!-- @segment an10.118:4.3 -->

are the ones who will cross over

<!-- @segment an10.118:4.4 -->

Death’s dominion so hard to pass.

<!-- @segment an10.118:5.1 -->

Rid of dark qualities,

<!-- @segment an10.118:5.2 -->

an astute person should develop the bright.

<!-- @segment an10.118:5.3 -->

Leaving home behind

<!-- @segment an10.118:5.4 -->

for the seclusion so hard to enjoy,

<!-- @segment an10.118:6.1 -->

try to find satisfaction there,

<!-- @segment an10.118:6.2 -->

having left behind sensual pleasures.

<!-- @segment an10.118:6.3 -->

With no possessions, an astute person

<!-- @segment an10.118:6.4 -->

should cleanse themselves of mental corruptions.

<!-- @segment an10.118:7.1 -->

And those whose minds are rightly developed

<!-- @segment an10.118:7.2 -->

in the awakening factors;

<!-- @segment an10.118:7.3 -->

letting go of attachments,

<!-- @segment an10.118:7.4 -->

they delight in not grasping.

<!-- @segment an10.118:7.5 -->

With defilements ended, brilliant,

<!-- @segment an10.118:7.6 -->

they are quenched in this world.”
