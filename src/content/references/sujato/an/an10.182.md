<!-- @segment an10.182:1.1 -->

“I will teach you what is the teaching and what is not the teaching. …

<!-- @segment an10.182:1.2 -->



<!-- @segment an10.182:1.3 -->

And what is not the teaching?

<!-- @segment an10.182:1.4 -->

Killing living creatures …

<!-- @segment an10.182:1.5 -->

wrong view.

<!-- @segment an10.182:1.6 -->

This is called what is not the teaching.

<!-- @segment an10.182:2.1 -->

And what is the teaching?

<!-- @segment an10.182:2.2 -->

Not killing living creatures …

<!-- @segment an10.182:2.3 -->

right view.

<!-- @segment an10.182:2.4 -->

This is called the teaching.”
