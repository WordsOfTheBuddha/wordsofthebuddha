<!-- @segment an7.85:1.1 -->

“Mendicants, it’s because of breaking seven things that you become a mendicant.

<!-- @segment an7.85:1.2 -->

What seven?

<!-- @segment an7.85:1.3 -->

Substantialist view, doubt, misapprehension of precepts and observances, greed, hate, delusion, and conceit.

<!-- @segment an7.85:1.4 -->

It’s because of breaking these seven things that you become a mendicant.”
