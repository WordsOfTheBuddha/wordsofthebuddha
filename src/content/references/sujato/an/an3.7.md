<!-- @segment an3.7:1.1 -->

“A fool is known by three things.

<!-- @segment an3.7:1.2 -->

What three?

<!-- @segment an3.7:1.3 -->

Blameworthy deeds by way of body, speech, and mind. … An astute person is known by blameless deeds by way of body, speech, and mind. …”
