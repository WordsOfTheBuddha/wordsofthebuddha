<!-- @segment an4.50:1.1 -->

“Mendicants, these four corruptions obscure the sun and moon, so they don’t shine and glow and radiate.

<!-- @segment an4.50:1.2 -->

What four?

<!-- @segment an4.50:1.3 -->

Stormclouds …

<!-- @segment an4.50:2.1 -->

Fog …

<!-- @segment an4.50:3.1 -->

Smoke …

<!-- @segment an4.50:4.1 -->

An eclipse of Rāhu, lord of titans …

<!-- @segment an4.50:4.2 -->

These are four corruptions that obscure the sun and moon, so they don’t shine and glow and radiate.

<!-- @segment an4.50:5.1 -->

In the same way, these four things corrupt ascetics and brahmins, so they don’t shine and glow and radiate.

<!-- @segment an4.50:5.2 -->

What four?

<!-- @segment an4.50:5.3 -->

There are some ascetics and brahmins who drink beer and wine, not refraining from drinking beer and wine.

<!-- @segment an4.50:5.4 -->

This is the first thing that corrupts ascetics and brahmins …

<!-- @segment an4.50:6.1 -->

There are some ascetics and brahmins who have sex, not refraining from sex.

<!-- @segment an4.50:6.2 -->

This is the second thing that corrupts ascetics and brahmins …

<!-- @segment an4.50:7.1 -->

There are some ascetics and brahmins who accept gold and currency, not refraining from receiving gold and currency.

<!-- @segment an4.50:7.2 -->

This is the third thing that corrupts ascetics and brahmins …

<!-- @segment an4.50:8.1 -->

There are some ascetics and brahmins who make a living the wrong way, not refraining from wrong livelihood.

<!-- @segment an4.50:8.2 -->

This is the fourth thing that corrupts ascetics and brahmins …

<!-- @segment an4.50:8.3 -->

These are four things that corrupt ascetics and brahmins, so they don’t shine and glow and radiate.

<!-- @segment an4.50:9.1 -->

Some ascetics and brahmins

<!-- @segment an4.50:9.2 -->

are plagued by greed and hate;

<!-- @segment an4.50:9.3 -->

men shrouded by ignorance,

<!-- @segment an4.50:9.4 -->

enjoying things that seem pleasant.

<!-- @segment an4.50:10.1 -->

Drinking beer and wine,

<!-- @segment an4.50:10.2 -->

having sex,

<!-- @segment an4.50:10.3 -->

accepting currency and gold:

<!-- @segment an4.50:10.4 -->

they’re ignorant.

<!-- @segment an4.50:10.5 -->

Some ascetics and brahmins

<!-- @segment an4.50:10.6 -->

make a living the wrong way.

<!-- @segment an4.50:11.1 -->

These corruptions were spoken of

<!-- @segment an4.50:11.2 -->

by the Buddha, Kinsman of the Sun.

<!-- @segment an4.50:11.3 -->

When corrupted by these,

<!-- @segment an4.50:11.4 -->

some ascetics and brahmins

<!-- @segment an4.50:11.5 -->

don’t shine or glow.

<!-- @segment an4.50:11.6 -->

Impure, dirty creatures,

<!-- @segment an4.50:12.1 -->

swathed in darkness,

<!-- @segment an4.50:12.2 -->

bondservants of craving, full of attachments,

<!-- @segment an4.50:12.3 -->

swell the horrors of the charnel ground,

<!-- @segment an4.50:12.4 -->

taking up future lives.”
