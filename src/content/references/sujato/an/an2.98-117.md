98

<!-- @segment an2.98:1.1 -->

“Mendicants, there are two fools.

<!-- @segment an2.98:1.2 -->

What two?

<!-- @segment an2.98:1.3 -->

One who carries the burden of what has not come to pass, and one who doesn’t carry the burden of what has come to pass.

<!-- @segment an2.98:1.4 -->

These are the two fools.”
