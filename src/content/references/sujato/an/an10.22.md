<!-- @segment an10.22:1.1 -->

Then Venerable Ānanda went up to the Buddha, bowed, and sat down to one side. The Buddha said to him:

<!-- @segment an10.22:2.1 -->

“Ānanda, I claim to be self-assured regarding the teachings that lead to realizing by insight the various different hypotheses.

<!-- @segment an10.22:2.2 -->

So I am able to teach the Dhamma in appropriate ways to different people. Practicing accordingly, when something exists they’ll know it exists. When it doesn’t exist they’ll know it doesn’t exist. When something is inferior they’ll know it’s inferior. When it’s superior they’ll know it’s superior. When something is not supreme they’ll know it’s not supreme. When it is supreme they’ll know it’s supreme.

<!-- @segment an10.22:2.3 -->

And they will know or see or realize it in whatever way it should be known or seen or realized. This is possible.

<!-- @segment an10.22:2.4 -->

But this is the unsurpassable knowledge, that is: truly knowing each and every case.

<!-- @segment an10.22:2.5 -->

And Ānanda, I say that there is no other knowledge better or finer than this.

<!-- @segment an10.22:3.1 -->

The Realized One possesses ten powers of a Realized One. With these he claims the bull’s place, roars his lion’s roar in the assemblies, and turns the divine wheel.

<!-- @segment an10.22:3.2 -->

What ten?

<!-- @segment an10.22:3.3 -->

Firstly, the Realized One truly understands the possible as possible, and the impossible as impossible.

<!-- @segment an10.22:3.4 -->

Since he truly understands this, this is a power of the Realized One. Relying on this he claims the bull’s place, roars his lion’s roar in the assemblies, and turns the divine wheel.

<!-- @segment an10.22:4.1 -->

Furthermore, the Realized One truly understands the result of deeds undertaken in the past, future, and present in terms of grounds and causes.

<!-- @segment an10.22:4.2 -->

Since he truly understands this, this is a power of the Realized One. …

<!-- @segment an10.22:5.1 -->

Furthermore, the Realized One truly understands where all paths of practice lead.

<!-- @segment an10.22:5.2 -->

Since he truly understands this, this is a power of the Realized One. …

<!-- @segment an10.22:6.1 -->

Furthermore, the Realized One truly understands the world with its many and diverse elements.

<!-- @segment an10.22:6.2 -->

Since he truly understands this, this is a power of the Realized One. …

<!-- @segment an10.22:7.1 -->

Furthermore, the Realized One truly understands the diverse convictions of sentient beings.

<!-- @segment an10.22:7.2 -->

Since he truly understands this, this is a power of the Realized One. …

<!-- @segment an10.22:8.1 -->

Furthermore, the Realized One truly understands the faculties of other sentient beings and other individuals after encompassing them with his mind.

<!-- @segment an10.22:8.2 -->

Since he truly understands this, this is a power of the Realized One. …

<!-- @segment an10.22:9.1 -->

Furthermore, the Realized One truly understands corruption, cleansing, and emergence regarding the absorptions, liberations, immersions, and attainments.

<!-- @segment an10.22:9.2 -->

Since he truly understands this, this is a power of the Realized One. …

<!-- @segment an10.22:10.1 -->

Furthermore, the Realized One recollects many kinds of past lives, with features and details.

<!-- @segment an10.22:10.2 -->

Since he truly understands this, this is a power of the Realized One. …

<!-- @segment an10.22:11.1 -->

Furthermore, with clairvoyance that is purified and superhuman, the Realized One sees sentient beings passing away and being reborn—inferior and superior, beautiful and ugly, in a good place or a bad place. He understands how sentient beings pass on according to their deeds.

<!-- @segment an10.22:11.2 -->

Since he truly understands this, this is a power of the Realized One. …

<!-- @segment an10.22:12.1 -->

Furthermore, the Realized One has realized the undefiled freedom of heart and freedom by wisdom in this very life, and lives having realized it with his own insight due to the ending of defilements.

<!-- @segment an10.22:12.2 -->

Since he truly understands this, this is a power of the Realized One. …

<!-- @segment an10.22:13.1 -->

These are the ten powers of a Realized One that the Realized One possesses. With these he claims the bull’s place, roars his lion’s roar in the assemblies, and turns the divine wheel.”
