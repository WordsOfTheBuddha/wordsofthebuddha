<!-- @segment an4.253:1.1 -->

“Mendicants, there are these four noble expressions.

<!-- @segment an4.253:1.2 -->

What four?

<!-- @segment an4.253:1.3 -->

Saying you’ve seen, heard, thought, or known something, and you have.

<!-- @segment an4.253:1.4 -->

These are the four noble expressions.”
