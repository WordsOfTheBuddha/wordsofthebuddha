<!-- @segment an4.239:1.1 -->

“Mendicants, someone with four qualities is placed in hell as if delivered there.

<!-- @segment an4.239:1.2 -->

What four?

<!-- @segment an4.239:1.3 -->

Blameworthy deeds by way of body, speech, and mind, and blameworthy view.

<!-- @segment an4.239:1.4 -->

Someone with these four qualities is placed in hell as if delivered there.

<!-- @segment an4.239:2.1 -->

Someone with four qualities is placed in heaven as if delivered there.

<!-- @segment an4.239:2.2 -->

What four?

<!-- @segment an4.239:2.3 -->

Blameless deeds by way of body, speech, and mind, and blameless view.

<!-- @segment an4.239:2.4 -->

Someone with these four qualities is placed in heaven as if delivered there.”
