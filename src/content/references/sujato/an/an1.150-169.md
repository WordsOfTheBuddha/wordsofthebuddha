150

<!-- @segment an1.150:1.1 -->

“Mendicants, those mendicants who explain non-offense as an offense are acting for the detriment and suffering of the people, against the people, for the harm, detriment, and suffering of gods and humans.

<!-- @segment an1.150:1.2 -->

They brim with much wickedness and make the true teaching disappear.”

152–159

<!-- @segment an1.152-159:1.2 -->

Those mendicants brim with much wickedness and make the true teaching disappear.”
