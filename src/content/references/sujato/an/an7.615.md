<!-- @segment an7.615:1.1 -->

“Mendicants, for insight into greed, seven things should be developed.

<!-- @segment an7.615:1.2 -->

What seven?

<!-- @segment an7.615:1.3 -->

The awakening factor of mindfulness …

<!-- @segment an7.615:1.4 -->

the awakening factor of equanimity.

<!-- @segment an7.615:1.5 -->

These seven things should be developed for insight into greed.”
