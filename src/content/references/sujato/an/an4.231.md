<!-- @segment an4.231:1.1 -->

“Mendicants, there are these four poets.

<!-- @segment an4.231:1.2 -->

What four?

<!-- @segment an4.231:1.3 -->

A poet who thoughtfully composes their own work, a poet who repeats the oral transmission, a poet who educates, and a poet who improvises.

<!-- @segment an4.231:1.4 -->

These are the four poets.”
