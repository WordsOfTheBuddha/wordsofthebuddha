<!-- @segment an5.307:1.1 -->

“For insight into greed, five things should be developed.

<!-- @segment an5.307:1.2 -->

What five?

<!-- @segment an5.307:1.3 -->

The powers of faith, energy, mindfulness, immersion, and wisdom.

<!-- @segment an5.307:1.4 -->

For insight into greed, these five things should be developed.”
