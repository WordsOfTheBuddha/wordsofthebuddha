<!-- @segment an3.138:1.1 -->

“Mendicants, there are three accomplishments.

<!-- @segment an3.138:1.2 -->

What three?

<!-- @segment an3.138:1.3 -->

Accomplishment in faith, ethics, and wisdom.

<!-- @segment an3.138:1.4 -->

These are the three accomplishments.”
