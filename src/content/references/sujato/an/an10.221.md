<!-- @segment an10.221:1.1 -->

“Someone with ten qualities is placed in hell as if delivered there.

<!-- @segment an10.221:1.2 -->

What ten?

<!-- @segment an10.221:1.3 -->

They kill living creatures, steal, and commit sexual misconduct. They use speech that’s false, backbiting, harsh, or nonsensical. And they’re covetous, malicious, with wrong view.

<!-- @segment an10.221:1.4 -->

Someone with these ten qualities is placed in hell as if delivered there.

<!-- @segment an10.221:2.1 -->

Someone with ten qualities is placed in heaven as if delivered there.

<!-- @segment an10.221:2.2 -->

What ten?

<!-- @segment an10.221:2.3 -->

They don’t kill living creatures, steal, or commit sexual misconduct. They don’t use speech that’s false, backbiting, harsh, or nonsensical. They’re contented, kind-hearted, with right view.

<!-- @segment an10.221:2.4 -->

Someone with these ten qualities is placed in heaven as if delivered there.”
