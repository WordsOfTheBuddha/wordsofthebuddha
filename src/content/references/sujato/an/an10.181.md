<!-- @segment an10.181:1.1 -->

“I will teach you the beneficial and the harmful. …

<!-- @segment an10.181:1.2 -->



<!-- @segment an10.181:1.3 -->

And what is the harmful?

<!-- @segment an10.181:1.4 -->

Killing living creatures …

<!-- @segment an10.181:1.5 -->

wrong view.

<!-- @segment an10.181:1.6 -->

This is called the harmful.

<!-- @segment an10.181:2.1 -->

And what is the beneficial?

<!-- @segment an10.181:2.2 -->

Not killing living creatures …

<!-- @segment an10.181:2.3 -->

right view.

<!-- @segment an10.181:2.4 -->

This is called the beneficial.”
