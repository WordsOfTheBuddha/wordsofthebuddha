<!-- @segment an4.97:1.1 -->

“Mendicants, these four individuals are found in the world.

<!-- @segment an4.97:1.2 -->

What four?

<!-- @segment an4.97:1.3 -->

One who practices to benefit themselves, but not others;

<!-- @segment an4.97:1.4 -->

one who practices to benefit others, but not themselves;

<!-- @segment an4.97:1.5 -->

one who practices to benefit neither themselves nor others; and

<!-- @segment an4.97:1.6 -->

one who practices to benefit both themselves and others.

<!-- @segment an4.97:2.1 -->

And how does an individual practice to benefit themselves, but not others?

<!-- @segment an4.97:2.2 -->

It’s when an individual is quick-witted when it comes to skillful teachings. They readily memorize the teachings they’ve heard. They examine the meaning of teachings they’ve memorized. Understanding the meaning and the teaching, they practice in line with the teaching.

<!-- @segment an4.97:2.3 -->

But they’re not a good speaker and do not enunciate well. Their voice isn’t polished, clear, articulate, and doesn’t express the meaning. They don’t educate, encourage, fire up, and inspire their spiritual companions.

<!-- @segment an4.97:2.4 -->

That’s how an individual practices to benefit themselves, but not others.

<!-- @segment an4.97:3.1 -->

And how does an individual practice to benefit others, but not themselves?

<!-- @segment an4.97:3.2 -->

It’s when an individual is not quick-witted when it comes to skillful teachings. …

<!-- @segment an4.97:3.3 -->

But they’re a good speaker. …

<!-- @segment an4.97:3.4 -->

That’s how an individual practices to benefit others, but not themselves.

<!-- @segment an4.97:4.1 -->

And how does an individual practice to benefit neither themselves nor others?

<!-- @segment an4.97:4.2 -->

It’s when an individual is not quick-witted when it comes to skillful teachings. …

<!-- @segment an4.97:4.3 -->

Nor are they a good speaker. …

<!-- @segment an4.97:4.4 -->

That’s how an individual practices to benefit neither themselves nor others.

<!-- @segment an4.97:5.1 -->

And how does an individual practice to benefit both themselves and others?

<!-- @segment an4.97:5.2 -->

It’s when an individual is quick-witted when it comes to skillful teachings. …

<!-- @segment an4.97:5.3 -->

And they’re a good speaker. …

<!-- @segment an4.97:5.4 -->

That’s how an individual practices to benefit both themselves and others.

<!-- @segment an4.97:5.5 -->

These are the four individuals found in the world.”
