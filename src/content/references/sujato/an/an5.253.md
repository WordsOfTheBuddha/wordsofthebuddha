<!-- @segment an5.253:1.1 -->

“Mendicants, a novice should attend on a mendicant with five qualities.

<!-- @segment an5.253:1.2 -->

What five?

<!-- @segment an5.253:1.3 -->

It’s a mendicant who has the entire spectrum of an adept’s ethics, immersion, wisdom, freedom, and the knowledge and vision of freedom.

<!-- @segment an5.253:1.4 -->

A novice should attend on a mendicant with these five qualities.”
