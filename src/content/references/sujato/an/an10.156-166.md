<!-- @segment an10.156-166:1.1 -->

“Mendicants, you should not frequent an individual who has ten qualities. …

<!-- @segment an10.156-166:1.2 -->

you should frequent …

<!-- @segment an10.156-166:1.3 -->

you should not pay homage …

<!-- @segment an10.156-166:1.4 -->

you should pay homage …

<!-- @segment an10.156-166:1.5 -->

you should not venerate …

<!-- @segment an10.156-166:1.6 -->

you should venerate …

<!-- @segment an10.156-166:1.7 -->

you should not praise …

<!-- @segment an10.156-166:1.8 -->

you should praise …

<!-- @segment an10.156-166:1.9 -->

you should not respect …

<!-- @segment an10.156-166:1.10 -->

you should respect …

<!-- @segment an10.156-166:1.11 -->

you should not revere …

<!-- @segment an10.156-166:1.12 -->

you should revere …

<!-- @segment an10.156-166:1.13 -->

is not a success …

<!-- @segment an10.156-166:1.14 -->

is a success …

<!-- @segment an10.156-166:1.15 -->

is not pure …

<!-- @segment an10.156-166:1.16 -->

is pure …

<!-- @segment an10.156-166:1.17 -->

does not win over conceit …

<!-- @segment an10.156-166:1.18 -->

wins over conceit …

<!-- @segment an10.156-166:1.19 -->

does not grow in wisdom …

<!-- @segment an10.156-166:1.20 -->

grows in wisdom …

<!-- @segment an10.156-166:2.1 -->

brims with much wickedness …

<!-- @segment an10.156-166:2.2 -->

brims with much merit.

<!-- @segment an10.156-166:2.3 -->

What ten?

<!-- @segment an10.156-166:2.4 -->

Right view, right purpose, right speech, right action, right livelihood, right effort, right mindfulness, right immersion, right knowledge, and right freedom.

<!-- @segment an10.156-166:2.5 -->

An individual who has these ten qualities creates much merit.”
