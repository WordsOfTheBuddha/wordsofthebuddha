<!-- @segment an5.219:1.1 -->

“Mendicants, there are these five drawbacks of a fire.

<!-- @segment an5.219:1.2 -->

What five?

<!-- @segment an5.219:1.3 -->

It’s bad for your eyes. It’s bad for your complexion. It makes you weak. It draws in groups. And it encourages low talk.

<!-- @segment an5.219:1.4 -->

These are the five drawbacks of a fire.”
