<!-- @segment an5.160:1.1 -->

“Mendicants, these five things are hard to get rid of once they’ve arisen.

<!-- @segment an5.160:1.2 -->

What five?

<!-- @segment an5.160:1.3 -->

Greed, hate, delusion, the feeling of being inspired to speak out, and thoughts of traveling.

<!-- @segment an5.160:1.4 -->

These five things are hard to get rid of once they’ve arisen.”
