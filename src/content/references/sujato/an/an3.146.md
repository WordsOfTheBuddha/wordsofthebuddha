<!-- @segment an3.146:1.1 -->

“Someone with three qualities is placed in hell as if delivered there.

<!-- @segment an3.146:1.2 -->

What three?

<!-- @segment an3.146:1.3 -->

Unskillful deeds by way of body, speech, and mind.

<!-- @segment an3.146:1.4 -->

Someone with these three qualities is placed in hell as if delivered there.

<!-- @segment an3.146:2.1 -->

Someone with three qualities is placed in heaven as if delivered there.

<!-- @segment an3.146:2.2 -->

What three?

<!-- @segment an3.146:2.3 -->

Skillful deeds by way of body, speech, and mind.

<!-- @segment an3.146:2.4 -->

Someone with these three qualities is placed in heaven as if delivered there.”
