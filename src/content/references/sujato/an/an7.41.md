<!-- @segment an7.41:1.1 -->

“Mendicants, having seven qualities Sāriputta has mastered his mind and is not mastered by it.

<!-- @segment an7.41:1.2 -->

What seven?

<!-- @segment an7.41:1.3 -->

Sāriputta is skilled at immersion, skilled in entering immersion, skilled in remaining in immersion, skilled in emerging from immersion, skilled in positivity for immersion, skilled in the territory of immersion, and skilled in projecting the mind purified by immersion.

<!-- @segment an7.41:1.4 -->

Having these seven qualities Sāriputta has mastered his mind and is not mastered by it.”
