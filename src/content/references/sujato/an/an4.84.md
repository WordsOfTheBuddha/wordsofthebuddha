<!-- @segment an4.84:1.1 -->

“Mendicants, someone with four qualities is placed in hell as if delivered there.

<!-- @segment an4.84:1.2 -->

What four?

<!-- @segment an4.84:1.3 -->

They value anger, or denigration, or material things, or honor rather than the true teaching.

<!-- @segment an4.84:1.4 -->

Someone with these four qualities is placed in hell as if delivered there.

<!-- @segment an4.84:2.1 -->

Someone with four qualities is placed in heaven as if delivered there.

<!-- @segment an4.84:2.2 -->

What four?

<!-- @segment an4.84:2.3 -->

They value the true teaching rather than anger, or denigration, or material things, or honor.

<!-- @segment an4.84:2.4 -->

Someone with these four qualities is placed in heaven as if delivered there.”
