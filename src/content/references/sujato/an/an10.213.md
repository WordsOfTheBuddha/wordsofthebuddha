<!-- @segment an10.213:1.1 -->

“A lady with ten qualities is placed in hell as if delivered there.

<!-- @segment an10.213:1.2 -->

What ten?

<!-- @segment an10.213:1.3 -->

She kills living creatures. …

<!-- @segment an10.213:1.4 -->

She steals. …

<!-- @segment an10.213:1.5 -->

She commits sexual misconduct. …

<!-- @segment an10.213:1.6 -->

She lies. …

<!-- @segment an10.213:1.7 -->

She backbites. …

<!-- @segment an10.213:1.8 -->

She speaks harshly. …

<!-- @segment an10.213:1.9 -->

She indulges in talking nonsense. …

<!-- @segment an10.213:1.10 -->

She’s covetous. …

<!-- @segment an10.213:1.11 -->

She has cruel intentions. …

<!-- @segment an10.213:1.12 -->

She has wrong view. …

<!-- @segment an10.213:1.13 -->

A lady with these ten qualities is placed in hell as if delivered there.

<!-- @segment an10.213:2.1 -->

A lady with ten qualities is placed in heaven as if delivered there.

<!-- @segment an10.213:2.2 -->

What ten?

<!-- @segment an10.213:2.3 -->

She doesn’t kill living creatures. …

<!-- @segment an10.213:2.4 -->

She doesn’t steal. …

<!-- @segment an10.213:2.5 -->

She doesn’t commit sexual misconduct. …

<!-- @segment an10.213:2.6 -->

She doesn’t lie. …

<!-- @segment an10.213:2.7 -->

She doesn’t backbite. …

<!-- @segment an10.213:2.8 -->

She doesn’t speak harshly. …

<!-- @segment an10.213:2.9 -->

She doesn’t indulge in talking nonsense. …

<!-- @segment an10.213:2.10 -->

She’s content. …

<!-- @segment an10.213:2.11 -->

She’s kind hearted. …

<!-- @segment an10.213:2.12 -->

She has right view. …

<!-- @segment an10.213:2.13 -->

A lady with these ten qualities is placed in heaven as if delivered there.”
