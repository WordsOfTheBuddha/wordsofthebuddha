title: Untitled Discourse on Greed (1st)
---

“For insight into greed, eight things should be developed.

What eight?

Right view, right purpose, right speech, right action, right livelihood, right effort, right mindfulness, and right immersion.

For insight into greed, these eight things should be developed.”
