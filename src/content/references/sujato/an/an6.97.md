<!-- @segment an6.97:1.1 -->

“Mendicants, these are the six benefits of realizing the fruit of stream-entry.

<!-- @segment an6.97:1.2 -->

What six?

<!-- @segment an6.97:1.3 -->

You’re bound for the true teaching. You’re not liable to decline. You suffer only for a limited period. You have distinct knowledge. You’ve clearly seen causes and the phenomena that arise from causes.

<!-- @segment an6.97:1.4 -->

These are the six benefits of realizing the fruit of stream-entry.”
