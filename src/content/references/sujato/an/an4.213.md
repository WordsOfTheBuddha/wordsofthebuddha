<!-- @segment an4.213:1.1 -->

“Mendicants, someone with four qualities is placed in hell as if delivered there.

<!-- @segment an4.213:1.2 -->

What four?

<!-- @segment an4.213:1.3 -->

Bad conduct by way of body, speech, and mind, and being ungrateful and thankless.

<!-- @segment an4.213:1.4 -->

Someone with these four qualities is placed in hell as if delivered there.

<!-- @segment an4.213:2.1 -->

Someone with four qualities is placed in heaven as if delivered there.

<!-- @segment an4.213:2.2 -->

What four?

<!-- @segment an4.213:2.3 -->

Good conduct by way of body, speech, and mind, and being grateful and thankful.

<!-- @segment an4.213:2.4 -->

Someone with these four qualities is placed in heaven as if delivered there.”
