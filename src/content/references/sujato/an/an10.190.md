<!-- @segment an10.190:1.1 -->

“I will teach you the dark path and the bright path. …

<!-- @segment an10.190:1.2 -->



<!-- @segment an10.190:1.3 -->

And what is the dark path?

<!-- @segment an10.190:1.4 -->

Killing living creatures …

<!-- @segment an10.190:1.5 -->

wrong view.

<!-- @segment an10.190:1.6 -->

This is called the dark path.

<!-- @segment an10.190:2.1 -->

And what is the bright path?

<!-- @segment an10.190:2.2 -->

Not killing living creatures …

<!-- @segment an10.190:2.3 -->

right view.

<!-- @segment an10.190:2.4 -->

This is called the bright path.”
