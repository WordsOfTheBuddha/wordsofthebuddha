<!-- @segment an6.80:1.1 -->

“Mendicants, a mendicant with six qualities soon acquires great and abundant good qualities.

<!-- @segment an6.80:1.2 -->

What six?

<!-- @segment an6.80:1.3 -->

It’s when a mendicant is full of light, full of practice, full of inspiration, and full of eagerness. They don’t slack off when it comes to developing skillful qualities. They reach further.

<!-- @segment an6.80:1.4 -->

A mendicant who possesses these six qualities soon acquires great and abundant good qualities.”
