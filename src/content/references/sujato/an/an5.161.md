title: Getting Rid of Resentment (1st)

<!-- @segment an5.161:1.1 -->

“Mendicants, a mendicant should use these five ways to completely get rid of resentment when it has arisen toward anyone.

<!-- @segment an5.161:1.2 -->

What five?

<!-- @segment an5.161:1.3 -->

You should develop love for an individual you resent.

<!-- @segment an5.161:1.4 -->

That’s how to get rid of resentment for that individual.

<!-- @segment an5.161:1.5 -->

You should develop compassion for an individual you resent. …

<!-- @segment an5.161:1.6 -->



<!-- @segment an5.161:1.7 -->

You should develop equanimity for an individual you resent. …

<!-- @segment an5.161:1.8 -->



<!-- @segment an5.161:1.9 -->

You should disregard an individual you resent, paying no attention to them. …

<!-- @segment an5.161:1.10 -->



<!-- @segment an5.161:1.11 -->

You should focus on the ownership of deeds regarding that individual:

<!-- @segment an5.161:1.12 -->

‘This venerable is the owner of their deeds and heir to their deeds. Deeds are their womb, their relative, and their refuge.

<!-- @segment an5.161:1.13 -->

They shall be the heir of whatever deeds they do, whether good or bad.’

<!-- @segment an5.161:1.14 -->

That’s how to get rid of resentment for that individual.

<!-- @segment an5.161:1.15 -->

A mendicant should use these five ways to completely get rid of resentment when it has arisen toward anyone.”
