<!-- @segment an5.145:1.1 -->

“Mendicants, someone with five qualities is placed in hell as if delivered there.

<!-- @segment an5.145:1.2 -->

What five?

<!-- @segment an5.145:1.3 -->

They kill living creatures, steal, commit sexual misconduct, lie, and consume intoxicants of beer, wine, and liquor.

<!-- @segment an5.145:1.4 -->

Someone with these five qualities is placed in hell as if delivered there.

<!-- @segment an5.145:2.1 -->

Someone with five qualities is placed in heaven as if delivered there

<!-- @segment an5.145:2.2 -->

What five?

<!-- @segment an5.145:2.3 -->

They don’t kill living creatures, steal, commit sexual misconduct, lie, or consume intoxicants of beer, wine, and liquor.

<!-- @segment an5.145:2.4 -->

Someone with these five qualities is placed in heaven as if delivered there.”
