<!-- @segment an7.94:1.1 -->

“Mendicants, there are these seven good qualities.

<!-- @segment an7.94:1.2 -->

What seven?

<!-- @segment an7.94:1.3 -->

Someone is faithful, conscientious, prudent, learned, energetic, mindful, and wise.

<!-- @segment an7.94:1.4 -->

These are the seven good qualities.”
