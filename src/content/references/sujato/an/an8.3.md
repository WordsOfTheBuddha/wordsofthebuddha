<!-- @segment an8.3:1.1 -->

“Mendicants, a mendicant with eight qualities is disliked and disapproved by their spiritual companions, not respected or admired.

<!-- @segment an8.3:1.2 -->

What eight?

<!-- @segment an8.3:1.3 -->

It’s when a mendicant praises the disliked and criticizes the liked. They desire material things and honor. They lack conscience and prudence. They have corrupt wishes and wrong view.

<!-- @segment an8.3:1.4 -->

A mendicant with these eight qualities is disliked and disapproved by their spiritual companions, not respected or admired.

<!-- @segment an8.3:2.1 -->

A mendicant with eight qualities is liked and approved by their spiritual companions, and respected and admired.

<!-- @segment an8.3:2.2 -->

What eight?

<!-- @segment an8.3:2.3 -->

It’s when a mendicant doesn’t praise the disliked and criticize the liked. They don’t desire material things and honor. They have conscience and prudence. They have few desires and right view.

<!-- @segment an8.3:2.4 -->

A mendicant with these eight qualities is liked and approved by their spiritual companions, and respected and admired.”
