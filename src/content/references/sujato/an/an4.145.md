<!-- @segment an4.145:1.1 -->

“Mendicants, there are these four lamps.

<!-- @segment an4.145:1.2 -->

What four?

<!-- @segment an4.145:1.3 -->

The lamps of the moon, sun, fire, and wisdom.

<!-- @segment an4.145:1.4 -->

These are the four lamps.

<!-- @segment an4.145:1.5 -->

The best of these four lamps is the lamp of wisdom.”
