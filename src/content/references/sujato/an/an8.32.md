<!-- @segment an8.32:1.1 -->

“Faith, conscience, and skillful giving

<!-- @segment an8.32:1.2 -->

are qualities true persons follow.

<!-- @segment an8.32:1.3 -->

For this, they say, is the path of the gods,

<!-- @segment an8.32:1.4 -->

which leads to the heavenly realm.”
