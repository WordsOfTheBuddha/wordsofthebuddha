<!-- @segment an4.46:1.1 -->

Then, when the night had passed, the Buddha addressed the mendicants:

<!-- @segment an4.46:1.2 -->

“Tonight, the glorious godling Rohitassa, lighting up the entire Jeta’s Grove, came to me, bowed, stood to one side, and said to me:

<!-- @segment an4.46:1.3 -->

‘Sir, is it possible to know or see or reach the end of the world by traveling to a place where there’s no being born, growing old, dying, passing away, or being reborn?’ …

<!-- @segment an4.46:1.4 -->

<em>(The rest is the same as the previous discourse, AN 4.45.)</em>
