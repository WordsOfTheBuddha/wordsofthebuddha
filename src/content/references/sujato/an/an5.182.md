<!-- @segment an5.182:1.1 -->

“Mendicants, these five wear rag robes.

<!-- @segment an5.182:1.2 -->

What five?

<!-- @segment an5.182:1.3 -->

One may wear rag robes because of stupidity … corrupt wishes … madness … because it is praised by the Buddhas … or for the sake of having few wishes …

<!-- @segment an5.182:1.4 -->

These are the five who wear rag robes.”
