<!-- @segment an7.617:1.1 -->

“Mendicants, for insight into greed, seven things should be developed.

<!-- @segment an7.617:1.2 -->

What seven?

<!-- @segment an7.617:1.3 -->

The perceptions of ugliness, death, repulsiveness of food, dissatisfaction with the whole world, impermanence, suffering in impermanence, and not-self in suffering.

<!-- @segment an7.617:1.4 -->

These seven things should be developed for insight into greed.”
