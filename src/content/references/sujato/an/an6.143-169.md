<!-- @segment an6.143-169:1.1 -->

“For the complete understanding of greed …

<!-- @segment an6.143-169:1.2 -->

complete ending …

<!-- @segment an6.143-169:1.3 -->

giving up …

<!-- @segment an6.143-169:1.4 -->

ending …

<!-- @segment an6.143-169:1.5 -->

vanishing …

<!-- @segment an6.143-169:1.6 -->

fading away …

<!-- @segment an6.143-169:1.7 -->

cessation …

<!-- @segment an6.143-169:1.8 -->

giving away …

<!-- @segment an6.143-169:1.9 -->

letting go of greed these six things should be developed.”
