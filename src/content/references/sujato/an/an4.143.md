<!-- @segment an4.143:1.1 -->

“Mendicants, there are these four lights.

<!-- @segment an4.143:1.2 -->

What four?

<!-- @segment an4.143:1.3 -->

The lights of the moon, sun, fire, and wisdom.

<!-- @segment an4.143:1.4 -->

These are the four lights.

<!-- @segment an4.143:1.5 -->

The best of these four lights is the light of wisdom.”
