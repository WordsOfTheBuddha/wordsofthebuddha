<!-- @segment an1.378:1.1 -->

“Mendicants, this is definitely something worth having, that is, living in the wilderness …

<!-- @segment an1.379:1.1 -->

eating only almsfood …

<!-- @segment an1.380:1.1 -->

wearing rag robes …

<!-- @segment an1.381:1.1 -->

having just three robes …

<!-- @segment an1.382:1.1 -->

teaching Dhamma …

<!-- @segment an1.383:1.1 -->

memorizing the monastic law …

<!-- @segment an1.384:1.1 -->

being very learned …

<!-- @segment an1.385:1.1 -->

being respected …

<!-- @segment an1.386:1.1 -->

being well-presented …

<!-- @segment an1.387:1.1 -->

having a following …

<!-- @segment an1.388:1.1 -->

having a large following …

<!-- @segment an1.389:1.1 -->

coming from a good family …

<!-- @segment an1.390:1.1 -->

being handsome …

<!-- @segment an1.391:1.1 -->

being one who enunciates well …

<!-- @segment an1.392:1.1 -->

having few wishes …

<!-- @segment an1.393:1.1 -->

being healthy.”
