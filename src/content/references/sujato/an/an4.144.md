<!-- @segment an4.144:1.1 -->

“Mendicants, there are four kinds of shining.

<!-- @segment an4.144:1.2 -->

What four?

<!-- @segment an4.144:1.3 -->

The shining of the moon, sun, fire, and wisdom.

<!-- @segment an4.144:1.4 -->

These are the four kinds of shining.

<!-- @segment an4.144:1.5 -->

The best of these four kinds of shining is the shining of wisdom.”
