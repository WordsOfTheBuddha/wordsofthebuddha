<!-- @segment an5.113:1.1 -->

“Mendicants, a mendicant who has five qualities can’t enter and remain in right immersion.

<!-- @segment an5.113:1.2 -->

What five?

<!-- @segment an5.113:1.3 -->

It’s when a mendicant can’t endure sights, sounds, smells, tastes, and touches.

<!-- @segment an5.113:1.4 -->

A mendicant who possesses these five qualities can’t enter and remain in right immersion.

<!-- @segment an5.113:2.1 -->

A mendicant who has five qualities can enter and remain in right immersion.

<!-- @segment an5.113:2.2 -->

What five?

<!-- @segment an5.113:2.3 -->

It’s when a mendicant can endure sights, sounds, smells, tastes, and touches.

<!-- @segment an5.113:2.4 -->

A mendicant who possesses these five qualities can enter and remain in right immersion.”
