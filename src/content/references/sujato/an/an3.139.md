<!-- @segment an3.139:1.1 -->

“Mendicants, there are three kinds of growth.

<!-- @segment an3.139:1.2 -->

What three?

<!-- @segment an3.139:1.3 -->

Growth in faith, ethics, and wisdom.

<!-- @segment an3.139:1.4 -->

These are the three kinds of growth.”
