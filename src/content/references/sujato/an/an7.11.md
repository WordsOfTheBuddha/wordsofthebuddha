title: Underlying Tendencies (1st)
---

“Mendicants, there are these seven underlying tendencies.

What seven?

The underlying tendencies of sensual desire, aversion, views, doubt, conceit, desire for continued existence, and ignorance.

These are the seven underlying tendencies.”
