<!-- @segment an5.177:1.1 -->

“Mendicants, a lay follower should not engage in these five trades.

<!-- @segment an5.177:1.2 -->

What five?

<!-- @segment an5.177:1.3 -->

Trade in weapons, living creatures, meat, intoxicants, and poisons.

<!-- @segment an5.177:1.4 -->

A lay follower should not engage in these five trades.”
