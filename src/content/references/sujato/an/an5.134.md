<!-- @segment an5.134:1.1 -->

“Mendicants, with five factors an anointed aristocratic king lives in his own realm, no matter what region he lives in.

<!-- @segment an5.134:2.1 -->

What five?

<!-- @segment an5.134:2.2 -->

An anointed aristocratic king is well born on both his mother’s and father’s side, stemming from a clean vessel, with irrefutable and impeccable genealogy back to the seventh paternal generation.

<!-- @segment an5.134:2.3 -->

He is rich, affluent, and wealthy, with a full treasury and storehouses.

<!-- @segment an5.134:2.4 -->

He is powerful, having an army of four divisions that is obedient and carries out instructions.

<!-- @segment an5.134:2.5 -->

He has a commander who is astute, competent, and intelligent, able to think issues through as they bear upon the past, future, and present.

<!-- @segment an5.134:2.6 -->

These four things bring his glory to fruition.

<!-- @segment an5.134:2.7 -->

With these five qualities, including glory, an anointed aristocratic king lives in his own realm, no matter what direction he lives in.

<!-- @segment an5.134:2.8 -->

Why is that?

<!-- @segment an5.134:2.9 -->

Because that is how it is for victors.

<!-- @segment an5.134:3.1 -->

In the same way, a mendicant with five qualities lives with mind freed, no matter what region they live in.

<!-- @segment an5.134:3.2 -->

What five?

<!-- @segment an5.134:3.3 -->

It’s when mendicant is ethical, restrained in the monastic code, conducting themselves well and resorting for alms in suitable places. Seeing danger in the slightest fault, they keep the rules they’ve undertaken.

<!-- @segment an5.134:3.4 -->

This is like the anointed aristocratic king’s impeccable birth.

<!-- @segment an5.134:3.5 -->

They’re very learned, remembering and keeping what they’ve learned. These teachings are good in the beginning, good in the middle, and good in the end, meaningful and well-phrased, describing a spiritual practice that’s entirely full and pure. They are very learned in such teachings, remembering them, rehearsing them, mentally scrutinizing them, and penetrating them theoretically.

<!-- @segment an5.134:3.6 -->

This is like the anointed aristocratic king being rich, affluent, and wealthy, with full treasury and storehouses.

<!-- @segment an5.134:3.7 -->

They live with energy roused up for giving up unskillful qualities and embracing skillful qualities. They’re strong, staunchly vigorous, not slacking off when it comes to developing skillful qualities.

<!-- @segment an5.134:3.8 -->

This is like the anointed aristocratic king having power.

<!-- @segment an5.134:3.9 -->

They’re wise. They have the wisdom of arising and passing away which is noble, penetrative, and leads to the complete ending of suffering.

<!-- @segment an5.134:3.10 -->

This is like the anointed aristocratic king having a commander.

<!-- @segment an5.134:3.11 -->

These four qualities bring their freedom to fruition.

<!-- @segment an5.134:3.12 -->

With these five qualities, including freedom, they live in their own realm, no matter what region they live in.

<!-- @segment an5.134:3.13 -->

Why is that?

<!-- @segment an5.134:3.14 -->

Because that is how it is for those whose mind is free.”
