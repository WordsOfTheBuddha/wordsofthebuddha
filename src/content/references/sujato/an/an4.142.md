<!-- @segment an4.142:1.1 -->

“Mendicants, there are these four kinds of radiance.

<!-- @segment an4.142:1.2 -->

What four?

<!-- @segment an4.142:1.3 -->

The radiance of the moon, sun, fire, and wisdom.

<!-- @segment an4.142:1.4 -->

These are the four kinds of radiance.

<!-- @segment an4.142:1.5 -->

The best of these four kinds of radiance is the radiance of wisdom.”
