<!-- @segment an7.10:1.1 -->

“Mendicants, there are these seven fetters.

<!-- @segment an7.10:1.2 -->

What seven?

<!-- @segment an7.10:1.3 -->

The fetters of attraction, aversion, views, doubt, conceit, jealousy, and stinginess.

<!-- @segment an7.10:1.4 -->

These are the seven fetters.”
