<!-- @segment an4.75:1.1 -->

“Mendicants, these four things are the best.

<!-- @segment an4.75:1.2 -->

What four?

<!-- @segment an4.75:1.3 -->

The best form, feeling, perception, and existence.

<!-- @segment an4.75:1.4 -->

These are the four things that are the best.”
