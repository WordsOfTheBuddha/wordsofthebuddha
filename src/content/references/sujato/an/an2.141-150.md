141

<!-- @segment an2.141:1.1 -->

“There are these two gifts.

<!-- @segment an2.141:1.2 -->

What two?

<!-- @segment an2.141:1.3 -->

A gift of things of the flesh and a gift of the teaching.

<!-- @segment an2.141:1.4 -->

These are the two gifts.

<!-- @segment an2.141:1.5 -->

The better of these two gifts is the gift of the teaching.”
