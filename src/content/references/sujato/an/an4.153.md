<!-- @segment an4.153:1.1 -->

“Mendicants, there are these four powers.

<!-- @segment an4.153:1.2 -->

What four?

<!-- @segment an4.153:1.3 -->

The powers of wisdom, energy, blamelessness, and inclusiveness.

<!-- @segment an4.153:1.4 -->

These are the four powers.”
