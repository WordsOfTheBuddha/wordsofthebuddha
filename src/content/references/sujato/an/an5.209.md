<!-- @segment an5.209:1.1 -->

“Mendicants, there are these five drawbacks in reciting with a drawn-out singing voice.

<!-- @segment an5.209:1.2 -->

What five?

<!-- @segment an5.209:1.3 -->

You relish the sound of your own voice. Others relish the sound of your voice. Householders complain:

<!-- @segment an5.209:1.4 -->

‘These ascetics who follow the Sakyan, sing just like us!’ When you’re enjoying the melody, your immersion breaks up. Those who come after follow your example.

<!-- @segment an5.209:1.5 -->

These are the five drawbacks in reciting with a drawn-out singing voice.”
