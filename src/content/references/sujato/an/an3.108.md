<!-- @segment an3.108:1.1 -->

“Mendicants, there are three indulgences that never satisfy.

<!-- @segment an3.108:1.2 -->

What three?

<!-- @segment an3.108:1.3 -->

Sleep,

<!-- @segment an3.108:1.4 -->

drinking beer and wine,

<!-- @segment an3.108:1.5 -->

and sexual intercourse.

<!-- @segment an3.108:1.6 -->

These are the three indulgences that never satisfy.”
