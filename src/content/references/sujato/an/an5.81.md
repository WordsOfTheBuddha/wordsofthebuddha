<!-- @segment an5.81:1.1 -->

“Mendicants, a senior mendicant with five qualities is unlikable and disagreeable to their spiritual companions, not respected or admired.

<!-- @segment an5.81:1.2 -->

What five?

<!-- @segment an5.81:1.3 -->

They desire the desirable, they hate the hateful, they’re deluded by the delusory, they’re annoyed by the annoying, and they’re intoxicated by the intoxicating.

<!-- @segment an5.81:1.4 -->

A senior mendicant with these five qualities is unlikable and disagreeable by their spiritual companions, not respected or admired.

<!-- @segment an5.81:2.1 -->

A senior mendicant with five qualities is dear and beloved to their spiritual companions, respected and admired.

<!-- @segment an5.81:2.2 -->

What five?

<!-- @segment an5.81:2.3 -->

They don’t desire the desirable, they don’t hate the hateful, they’re not deluded by the delusory, they’re not annoyed by the annoying, and they’re not intoxicated by the intoxicating.

<!-- @segment an5.81:2.4 -->

A senior mendicant with these five qualities is dear and beloved to their spiritual companions, respected and admired.”
