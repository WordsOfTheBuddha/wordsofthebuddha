title: Catching (1st)
---

“Mendicants, a woman catches a man using eight features.

What eight?

With weeping,

laughing,

speaking,

appearance,

gifts of wildflowers,

scents,

tastes,

and touches.

A woman catches a man using these eight features.

But those beings who are caught by touch are well and truly caught.”
