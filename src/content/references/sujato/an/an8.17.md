<!-- @segment an8.17:1.1 -->

“Mendicants, a woman catches a man using eight features.

<!-- @segment an8.17:1.2 -->

What eight?

<!-- @segment an8.17:1.3 -->

With weeping,

<!-- @segment an8.17:1.4 -->

laughing,

<!-- @segment an8.17:1.5 -->

speaking,

<!-- @segment an8.17:1.6 -->

appearance,

<!-- @segment an8.17:1.7 -->

gifts of wildflowers,

<!-- @segment an8.17:1.8 -->

scents,

<!-- @segment an8.17:1.9 -->

tastes,

<!-- @segment an8.17:1.10 -->

and touches.

<!-- @segment an8.17:1.11 -->

A woman catches a man using these eight features.

<!-- @segment an8.17:1.12 -->

But those beings who are caught by touch are well and truly caught.”
