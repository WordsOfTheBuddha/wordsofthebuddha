<!-- @segment an5.46:1.1 -->

“Mendicants, there are five accomplishments.

<!-- @segment an5.46:1.2 -->

What five?

<!-- @segment an5.46:1.3 -->

Accomplishment in faith, ethics, learning, generosity, and wisdom.

<!-- @segment an5.46:1.4 -->

These are the five accomplishments.”
