<!-- @segment an4.149:1.1 -->

“Mendicants, there are these four kinds of good conduct by way of speech.

<!-- @segment an4.149:1.2 -->

What four?

<!-- @segment an4.149:1.3 -->

Speech that’s true, harmonious, gentle, and thoughtful.

<!-- @segment an4.149:1.4 -->

These are the four kinds of good conduct by way of speech.”
