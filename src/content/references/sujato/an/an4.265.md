<!-- @segment an4.265:1.1 -->

“Mendicants, someone with four qualities is placed in hell as if delivered there.

<!-- @segment an4.265:1.2 -->

What four?

<!-- @segment an4.265:1.3 -->

They themselves steal …

<!-- @segment an4.265:1.4 -->

Someone with four qualities is placed in heaven as if delivered there. …

<!-- @segment an4.265:2.1 -->

They don’t themselves steal … ”
