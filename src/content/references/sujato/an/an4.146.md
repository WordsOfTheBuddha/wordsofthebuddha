<!-- @segment an4.146:1.1 -->

“Mendicants, there are these four times.

<!-- @segment an4.146:1.2 -->

What four?

<!-- @segment an4.146:1.3 -->

A time for listening to the teaching, a time for discussing the teaching, a time for serenity, and a time for discernment.

<!-- @segment an4.146:1.4 -->

These are the four times.”
