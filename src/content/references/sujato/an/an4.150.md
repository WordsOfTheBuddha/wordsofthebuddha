<!-- @segment an4.150:1.1 -->

“Mendicants, there are these four essentials.

<!-- @segment an4.150:1.2 -->

What four?

<!-- @segment an4.150:1.3 -->

Ethics, immersion, wisdom, and freedom are essentials.

<!-- @segment an4.150:1.4 -->

These are the four essentials.”
