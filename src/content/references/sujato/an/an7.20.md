<!-- @segment an7.20:1.1 -->

“Mendicants, there are these seven qualifications for graduation.

<!-- @segment an7.20:1.2 -->

What seven?

<!-- @segment an7.20:1.3 -->

It’s when a mendicant has a keen enthusiasm to undertake the training …

<!-- @segment an7.20:1.4 -->

to examine the teachings …

<!-- @segment an7.20:1.5 -->

to get rid of desires …

<!-- @segment an7.20:1.6 -->

for retreat …

<!-- @segment an7.20:1.7 -->

to rouse up energy …

<!-- @segment an7.20:1.8 -->

for mindfulness and alertness …

<!-- @segment an7.20:1.9 -->

to penetrate theoretically. And they don’t lose these desires in the future.

<!-- @segment an7.20:1.10 -->

These are the seven qualifications for graduation.”
