<!-- @segment an10.19:1.1 -->

“There are these ten abodes of the noble ones in which the noble ones of the past, present, and future abide.

<!-- @segment an10.19:1.2 -->

What ten?

<!-- @segment an10.19:1.3 -->

A mendicant has given up five factors, is endowed with six factors, has a single guard, has four supports, has cast aside idiosyncratic interpretations of the truth, has totally given up searching, has pure intentions, has stilled the physical process, and is well freed in mind and well freed by wisdom.

<!-- @segment an10.19:1.4 -->

These are the ten abodes of the noble ones in which the noble ones of the past, present, and future abide.”
