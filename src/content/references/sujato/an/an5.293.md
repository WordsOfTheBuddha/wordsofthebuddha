title: An Ājīvaka
---

“Mendicants, an Ājīvaka ascetic with five qualities is placed in hell as if delivered there.

What five?

They kill living creatures, steal, have sex, lie, and consume intoxicants of beer, wine, and liquor.

An Ājīvaka ascetic with these five qualities is placed in hell as if delivered there.”
