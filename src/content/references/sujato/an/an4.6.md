title: A Little Learning
---

<!-- @segment an4.6:1.1 -->

“Mendicants, these four individuals are found in the world.

<!-- @segment an4.6:1.2 -->

What four? They may have:

<!-- @segment an4.6:1.3 -->

Little learning and not get the point of learning.

<!-- @segment an4.6:1.4 -->

Little learning but get the point of learning.

<!-- @segment an4.6:1.5 -->

Much learning but not get the point of learning.

<!-- @segment an4.6:1.6 -->

Much learning and get the point of learning.

<!-- @segment an4.6:1.7 -->

And how has an individual learned little and not got the point of learning?

<!-- @segment an4.6:1.8 -->

It’s when an individual has learned little of the

<!-- @segment an4.6:1.9 -->

statements, mixed prose & verse, discussions, verses, inspired exclamations, legends, stories of past lives, amazing stories, and elaborations.

<!-- @segment an4.6:1.10 -->

And with the little they’ve learned, they understand neither the meaning nor the teaching, nor do they practice in line with the teaching.

<!-- @segment an4.6:1.11 -->

That’s how an individual has learned little and not got the point of learning.

<!-- @segment an4.6:2.1 -->

And how has an individual learned little but has got the point of learning?

<!-- @segment an4.6:2.2 -->

It’s when an individual has learned little of the

<!-- @segment an4.6:2.3 -->

statements, mixed prose & verse, discussions, verses, inspired exclamations, legends, stories of past lives, amazing stories, and elaborations.

<!-- @segment an4.6:2.4 -->

But with the little they’ve learned, they understand the meaning and the teaching, and they practice in line with the teaching.

<!-- @segment an4.6:2.5 -->

That’s how an individual has learned little but has got the point of learning.

<!-- @segment an4.6:3.1 -->

And how has an individual learned much but hasn't got the point of learning?

<!-- @segment an4.6:3.2 -->

It’s when an individual has learned much of the

<!-- @segment an4.6:3.3 -->

statements, mixed prose & verse, discussions, verses, inspired exclamations, legends, stories of past lives, amazing stories, and elaborations.

<!-- @segment an4.6:3.4 -->

But even though they’ve learned much, they understand neither the meaning nor the teaching, nor do they practice in line with the teaching.

<!-- @segment an4.6:3.5 -->

That’s how an individual has learned much but hasn't got the point of learning.

<!-- @segment an4.6:4.1 -->

And how has an individual learned much and has got the point of learning?

<!-- @segment an4.6:4.2 -->

It’s when an individual has learned much of the

<!-- @segment an4.6:4.3 -->

statements, mixed prose & verse, discussions, verses, inspired exclamations, legends, stories of past lives, amazing stories, and elaborations.

<!-- @segment an4.6:4.4 -->

And with the large amount they’ve learned, they understand the meaning and the teaching, and they practice in line with the teaching.

<!-- @segment an4.6:4.5 -->

That’s how an individual has learned much and has got the point of learning.

<!-- @segment an4.6:4.6 -->

These are the four individuals found in the world.

<!-- @segment an4.6:5.1 -->

If you don’t learn much,

<!-- @segment an4.6:5.2 -->

and aren’t steady in ethics,

<!-- @segment an4.6:5.3 -->

they’ll criticize you on both counts,

<!-- @segment an4.6:5.4 -->

for your ethics and your learning.

<!-- @segment an4.6:6.1 -->

If you don’t learn much,

<!-- @segment an4.6:6.2 -->

and you are steady in ethics,

<!-- @segment an4.6:6.3 -->

they’ll praise your ethical conduct,

<!-- @segment an4.6:6.4 -->

since your learning has succeeded.

<!-- @segment an4.6:7.1 -->

If you learn much,

<!-- @segment an4.6:7.2 -->

but aren’t steady in ethics,

<!-- @segment an4.6:7.3 -->

they’ll criticize your ethical conduct,

<!-- @segment an4.6:7.4 -->

for your learning hasn’t succeeded.

<!-- @segment an4.6:8.1 -->

If you learn much,

<!-- @segment an4.6:8.2 -->

and you are steady in ethics,

<!-- @segment an4.6:8.3 -->

they’ll praise you on both counts,

<!-- @segment an4.6:8.4 -->

for your ethics and your learning.

<!-- @segment an4.6:9.1 -->

A wise disciple of the Buddha

<!-- @segment an4.6:9.2 -->

who is learned and has memorized the teachings;

<!-- @segment an4.6:9.3 -->

like a pendant of Black Plum River gold,

<!-- @segment an4.6:9.4 -->

who is worthy to criticize them?

<!-- @segment an4.6:9.5 -->

Even the gods praise them,

<!-- @segment an4.6:9.6 -->

and by the Divinity, too, they’re praised.”
