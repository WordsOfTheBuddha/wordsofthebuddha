<!-- @segment an4.148:1.1 -->

“Mendicants, there are these four kinds of bad conduct by way of speech.

<!-- @segment an4.148:1.2 -->

What four?

<!-- @segment an4.148:1.3 -->

Speech that’s false, backbiting, harsh, or nonsensical.

<!-- @segment an4.148:1.4 -->

These are the four kinds of bad conduct by way of speech.”
