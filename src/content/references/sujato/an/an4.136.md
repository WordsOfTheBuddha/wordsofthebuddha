<!-- @segment an4.136:1.1 -->

“Mendicants, these four individuals are found in the world.

<!-- @segment an4.136:1.2 -->

What four?

<!-- @segment an4.136:1.3 -->

One individual has not fulfilled ethics, immersion, or wisdom.

<!-- @segment an4.136:2.1 -->

One individual has fulfilled ethics, but not immersion or wisdom.

<!-- @segment an4.136:3.1 -->

One individual has fulfilled ethics and immersion, but not wisdom.

<!-- @segment an4.136:4.1 -->

One individual has fulfilled ethics, immersion, and wisdom.

<!-- @segment an4.136:4.2 -->

These are the four individuals found in the world.”
