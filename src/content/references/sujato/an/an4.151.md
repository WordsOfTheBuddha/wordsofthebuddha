<!-- @segment an4.151:1.1 -->

“Mendicants, there are these four faculties.

<!-- @segment an4.151:1.2 -->

What four?

<!-- @segment an4.151:1.3 -->

The faculties of faith, energy, mindfulness, and immersion.

<!-- @segment an4.151:1.4 -->

These are the four faculties.”
