<!-- @segment an5.286:1.1 -->

“Mendicants, a monk with five qualities is placed in hell as if delivered there.

<!-- @segment an5.286:1.2 -->

What five?

<!-- @segment an5.286:1.3 -->

He kills living creatures, steals, has sex, lies, and consumes intoxicants of beer, wine, and liquor.

<!-- @segment an5.286:1.4 -->

A monk with these five qualities is placed in hell as if delivered there.

<!-- @segment an5.286:2.1 -->

A monk with five qualities is placed in heaven as if delivered there.

<!-- @segment an5.286:2.2 -->

What five?

<!-- @segment an5.286:2.3 -->

He doesn’t kill living creatures, steal, have sex, lie, or consume intoxicants of beer, wine, and liquor.

<!-- @segment an5.286:2.4 -->

A monk with these five qualities is placed in heaven as if delivered there.”
