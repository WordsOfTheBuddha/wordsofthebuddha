<!-- @segment an4.261:1.1 -->

“Mendicants, there are these four powers.

<!-- @segment an4.261:1.2 -->

What four?

<!-- @segment an4.261:1.3 -->

The powers of energy, mindfulness, immersion, and wisdom.

<!-- @segment an4.261:1.4 -->

These are the four powers.”
