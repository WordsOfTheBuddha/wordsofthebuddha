<!-- @segment an6.8:1.1 -->

“Mendicants, these six things are unsurpassable.

<!-- @segment an6.8:1.2 -->

What six?

<!-- @segment an6.8:1.3 -->

The unsurpassable seeing, listening, acquisition, training, service, and recollection.

<!-- @segment an6.8:1.4 -->

These are the six unsurpassable things.”
