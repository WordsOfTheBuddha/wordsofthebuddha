<!-- @segment an6.9:1.1 -->

“Mendicants, there are these six topics for recollection.

<!-- @segment an6.9:1.2 -->

What six?

<!-- @segment an6.9:1.3 -->

The recollection of the Buddha, the teaching, the Saṅgha, ethics, generosity, and the deities.

<!-- @segment an6.9:1.4 -->

These are the six topics for recollection.”
