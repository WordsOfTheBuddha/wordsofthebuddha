<!-- @segment an4.137:1.1 -->

“Mendicants, these four individuals are found in the world.

<!-- @segment an4.137:1.2 -->

What four?

<!-- @segment an4.137:1.3 -->

One individual doesn’t value or submit to ethics, immersion, or wisdom.

<!-- @segment an4.137:2.1 -->

One individual values and submits to ethics, but not to immersion or wisdom.

<!-- @segment an4.137:3.1 -->

One individual values and submits to ethics and immersion, but not wisdom.

<!-- @segment an4.137:4.1 -->

One individual values and submits to ethics, immersion, and wisdom.

<!-- @segment an4.137:4.2 -->

These are the four individuals found in the world.”
