<!-- @segment an10.139:1.1 -->

“I will teach you the defiled principle and the undefiled. …

<!-- @segment an10.139:1.2 -->



<!-- @segment an10.139:1.3 -->

And what is the defiled principle?

<!-- @segment an10.139:1.4 -->

Wrong view, wrong purpose, wrong speech, wrong action, wrong livelihood, wrong effort, wrong mindfulness, wrong immersion, wrong knowledge, and wrong freedom.

<!-- @segment an10.139:1.5 -->

This is called the defiled principle.

<!-- @segment an10.139:1.6 -->

And what is the undefiled principle?

<!-- @segment an10.139:1.7 -->

Right view, right purpose, right speech, right action, right livelihood, right effort, right mindfulness, right immersion, right knowledge, and right freedom.

<!-- @segment an10.139:1.8 -->

This is called the undefiled principle.”
