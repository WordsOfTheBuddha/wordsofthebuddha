title: Bases of Psychic Power

<!-- @segment an4.276:1.1 -->

“For insight into greed, four things should be developed.

<!-- @segment an4.276:1.2 -->

What four?

<!-- @segment an4.276:1.3 -->

It’s when a mendicant develops the basis of psychic power that has immersion due to enthusiasm, and active effort.

<!-- @segment an4.276:1.4 -->

They develop the basis of psychic power that has immersion due to energy …

<!-- @segment an4.276:1.5 -->

mental development …

<!-- @segment an4.276:1.6 -->

inquiry, and active effort.

<!-- @segment an4.276:1.7 -->

For insight into greed, these four things should be developed.”
