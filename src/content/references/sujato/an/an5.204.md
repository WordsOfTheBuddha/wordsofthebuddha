<!-- @segment an5.204:1.1 -->

“Mendicants, there are these five powers.

<!-- @segment an5.204:1.2 -->

What five?

<!-- @segment an5.204:1.3 -->

Faith, conscience, prudence, energy, and wisdom.

<!-- @segment an5.204:1.4 -->

These are the five powers.”
