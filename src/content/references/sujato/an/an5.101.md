<!-- @segment an5.101:1.1 -->

“Mendicants, these five qualities make a trainee self-assured.

<!-- @segment an5.101:1.2 -->

What five?

<!-- @segment an5.101:1.3 -->

It’s when a mendicant is faithful, ethical, learned, energetic, and wise.

<!-- @segment an5.101:2.1 -->

A person of faith doesn’t have the insecurities of someone who lacks faith.

<!-- @segment an5.101:2.2 -->

So this quality makes a trainee self-assured.

<!-- @segment an5.101:3.1 -->

An ethical person doesn’t have the insecurities of someone who is unethical.

<!-- @segment an5.101:3.2 -->

So this quality makes a trainee self-assured.

<!-- @segment an5.101:4.1 -->

A learned person doesn’t have the insecurities of a person of little learning.

<!-- @segment an5.101:4.2 -->

So this quality makes a trainee self-assured.

<!-- @segment an5.101:5.1 -->

An energetic person doesn’t have the insecurities of a lazy person.

<!-- @segment an5.101:5.2 -->

So this quality makes a trainee self-assured.

<!-- @segment an5.101:6.1 -->

A wise person doesn’t have the insecurities of someone who is witless.

<!-- @segment an5.101:6.2 -->

So this quality makes a trainee self-assured.

<!-- @segment an5.101:6.3 -->

These are the five qualities that make a trainee self-assured.”
