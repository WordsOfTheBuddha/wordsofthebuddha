<!-- @segment an4.74:1.1 -->

“Mendicants, these four things are the best.

<!-- @segment an4.74:1.2 -->

What four?

<!-- @segment an4.74:1.3 -->

The best ethics, immersion, wisdom, and freedom.

<!-- @segment an4.74:1.4 -->

These are the four things that are the best.”
