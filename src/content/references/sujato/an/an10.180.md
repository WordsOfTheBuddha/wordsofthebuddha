<!-- @segment an10.180:1.1 -->

“I will teach you the skillful and the unskillful …

<!-- @segment an10.180:1.2 -->



<!-- @segment an10.180:1.3 -->

And what is the unskillful?

<!-- @segment an10.180:1.4 -->

Killing living creatures …

<!-- @segment an10.180:1.5 -->

wrong view.

<!-- @segment an10.180:1.6 -->

This is called the unskillful.

<!-- @segment an10.180:2.1 -->

And what is the skillful?

<!-- @segment an10.180:2.2 -->

Not killing living creatures …

<!-- @segment an10.180:2.3 -->

right view.

<!-- @segment an10.180:2.4 -->

This is called the skillful.”
