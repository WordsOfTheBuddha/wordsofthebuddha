<!-- @segment an5.211:1.1 -->

“Mendicants, a mendicant who abuses and insults their spiritual companions, denouncing the noble ones, can expect these five drawbacks.

<!-- @segment an5.211:1.2 -->

What five?

<!-- @segment an5.211:1.3 -->

They’re expelled, cut off, blocked out; or they commit a corrupt offense; or they contract a severe illness. They feel lost when they die. And when their body breaks up, after death, they’re reborn in a place of loss, a bad place, the underworld, hell.

<!-- @segment an5.211:1.4 -->

A mendicant who abuses and insults their spiritual companions, denouncing the noble ones, can expect these five drawbacks.”
