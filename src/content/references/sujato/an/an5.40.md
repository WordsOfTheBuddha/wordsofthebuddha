<!-- @segment an5.40:1.1 -->

“Mendicants, great sal trees grow in five ways supported by the Himalayas, the king of mountains.

<!-- @segment an5.40:1.2 -->

What five?

<!-- @segment an5.40:1.3 -->

The branches, leaves, and foliage;

<!-- @segment an5.40:1.4 -->

the bark;

<!-- @segment an5.40:1.5 -->

the shoots;

<!-- @segment an5.40:1.6 -->

the softwood;

<!-- @segment an5.40:1.7 -->

and the hardwood.

<!-- @segment an5.40:1.8 -->

Great sal trees grow in these five ways supported by the Himalayas, the king of mountains.

<!-- @segment an5.40:1.9 -->

In the same way, a family grows in five ways supported by a family head with faith.

<!-- @segment an5.40:1.10 -->

What five?

<!-- @segment an5.40:1.11 -->

Faith,

<!-- @segment an5.40:1.12 -->

ethics,

<!-- @segment an5.40:1.13 -->

learning,

<!-- @segment an5.40:1.14 -->

generosity,

<!-- @segment an5.40:1.15 -->

and wisdom.

<!-- @segment an5.40:1.16 -->

A family grows in these five ways supported by a family head with faith.

<!-- @segment an5.40:2.1 -->

Supported by the mountain crags

<!-- @segment an5.40:2.2 -->

in the wilds, the formidable forest,

<!-- @segment an5.40:2.3 -->

the tree grows

<!-- @segment an5.40:2.4 -->

to become lord of the forest.

<!-- @segment an5.40:3.1 -->

So too, when the family head

<!-- @segment an5.40:3.2 -->

is ethical and faithful,

<!-- @segment an5.40:3.3 -->

supported by them, they grow:

<!-- @segment an5.40:3.4 -->

children, partners, and kin,

<!-- @segment an5.40:3.5 -->

colleagues, relatives,

<!-- @segment an5.40:3.6 -->

and those dependent for their livelihood.

<!-- @segment an5.40:4.1 -->

Seeing the ethical conduct of the virtuous,

<!-- @segment an5.40:4.2 -->

the generosity and good deeds,

<!-- @segment an5.40:4.3 -->

those who see clearly

<!-- @segment an5.40:4.4 -->

do likewise.

<!-- @segment an5.40:5.1 -->

Having practiced the teaching here,

<!-- @segment an5.40:5.2 -->

the path that goes to a good place,

<!-- @segment an5.40:5.3 -->

they delight in the heavenly realm,

<!-- @segment an5.40:5.4 -->

enjoying all the pleasures they desire.”
