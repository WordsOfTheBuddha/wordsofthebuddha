<!-- @segment an10.155:1.1 -->

“Mendicants, you should not associate with an individual who has ten qualities.

<!-- @segment an10.155:1.2 -->

What ten?

<!-- @segment an10.155:1.3 -->

Wrong view, wrong purpose, wrong speech, wrong action, wrong livelihood, wrong effort, wrong mindfulness, wrong immersion, wrong knowledge, and wrong freedom.

<!-- @segment an10.155:1.4 -->

You should not associate with an individual who has these ten qualities.

<!-- @segment an10.155:2.1 -->

You should associate with an individual who has ten qualities.

<!-- @segment an10.155:2.2 -->

What ten?

<!-- @segment an10.155:2.3 -->

Right view, right purpose, right speech, right action, right livelihood, right effort, right mindfulness, right immersion, right knowledge, and right freedom.

<!-- @segment an10.155:2.4 -->

You should associate with an individual who has these ten qualities.”
