<!-- @segment an6.140:1.1 -->

“For insight into greed, six things should be developed.

<!-- @segment an6.140:1.2 -->

What six?

<!-- @segment an6.140:1.3 -->

The unsurpassable seeing, listening, acquisition, training, service, and recollection.

<!-- @segment an6.140:1.4 -->

For insight into greed, these six things should be developed.”
