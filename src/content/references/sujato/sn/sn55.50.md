<!-- @segment sn55.50:1.1 -->

“Mendicants, there are these four factors for stream-entry.

<!-- @segment sn55.50:1.2 -->

What four?

<!-- @segment sn55.50:1.3 -->

Associating with true persons, listening to the true teaching, rational application of mind, and practicing in line with the teaching.

<!-- @segment sn55.50:1.4 -->

These are the four factors for stream-entry.”
