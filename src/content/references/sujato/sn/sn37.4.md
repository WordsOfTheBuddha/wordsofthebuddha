<!-- @segment sn37.4:1.1 -->

“Mendicants, when ladies have three qualities, when their body breaks up, after death, they are mostly reborn in a place of loss, a bad place, the underworld, hell.

<!-- @segment sn37.4:1.2 -->

What three?

<!-- @segment sn37.4:1.3 -->

A lady lives at home with a heart full of the stain of stinginess in the morning, jealousy in the afternoon, and sexual desire in the evening.

<!-- @segment sn37.4:1.4 -->



<!-- @segment sn37.4:1.5 -->



<!-- @segment sn37.4:1.6 -->

When ladies possess these three qualities, when their body breaks up, after death, they are mostly reborn in a place of loss, a bad place, the underworld, hell.”
