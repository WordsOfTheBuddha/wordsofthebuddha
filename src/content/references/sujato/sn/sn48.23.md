<!-- @segment sn48.23:1.1 -->

“Mendicants, there are these three faculties.

<!-- @segment sn48.23:1.2 -->

What three?

<!-- @segment sn48.23:1.3 -->

The faculty of understanding that enlightenment is imminent. The faculty of enlightenment. The faculty of one who is enlightened.

<!-- @segment sn48.23:1.4 -->

These are the three faculties.”
