<!-- @segment sn14.28:1.1 -->

At Sāvatthī.

<!-- @segment sn14.28:1.2 -->

“Mendicants, sentient beings join together and converge because of an element:

<!-- @segment sn14.28:1.3 -->

those of wrong view with those of wrong view …

<!-- @segment sn14.28:1.4 -->

wrong purpose …

<!-- @segment sn14.28:1.5 -->

wrong speech …

<!-- @segment sn14.28:1.6 -->

wrong action …

<!-- @segment sn14.28:1.7 -->

wrong livelihood …

<!-- @segment sn14.28:1.8 -->

wrong effort …

<!-- @segment sn14.28:1.9 -->

wrong mindfulness …

<!-- @segment sn14.28:1.10 -->

wrong immersion …

<!-- @segment sn14.28:2.1 -->

Those who have right view …

<!-- @segment sn14.28:2.2 -->

right purpose …

<!-- @segment sn14.28:2.3 -->

right speech …

<!-- @segment sn14.28:2.4 -->

right action …

<!-- @segment sn14.28:2.5 -->

right livelihood …

<!-- @segment sn14.28:2.6 -->

right effort …

<!-- @segment sn14.28:2.7 -->

right mindfulness …

<!-- @segment sn14.28:2.8 -->

right immersion with those who have right immersion.”
