<!-- @segment sn27.9:1.1 -->

At Sāvatthī.

<!-- @segment sn27.9:1.2 -->

“Mendicants, desire and greed for the earth element,

<!-- @segment sn27.9:1.3 -->

the water element,

<!-- @segment sn27.9:1.4 -->

the fire element,

<!-- @segment sn27.9:1.5 -->

the air element,

<!-- @segment sn27.9:1.6 -->

the space element,

<!-- @segment sn27.9:1.7 -->

or the consciousness element is a corruption of the mind. …”
