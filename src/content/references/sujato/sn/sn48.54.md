<!-- @segment sn48.54:1.1 -->

“The footprints of all creatures that walk can fit inside an elephant’s footprint, so an elephant’s footprint is said to be the biggest of them all.

<!-- @segment sn48.54:1.2 -->

In the same way, the faculty of wisdom is said to be the best of the steps that lead to awakening; that is, for becoming awakened.

<!-- @segment sn48.54:1.3 -->

And what are the steps that lead to awakening?

<!-- @segment sn48.54:1.4 -->

The faculties of faith,

<!-- @segment sn48.54:1.5 -->

energy,

<!-- @segment sn48.54:1.6 -->

mindfulness,

<!-- @segment sn48.54:1.7 -->

immersion,

<!-- @segment sn48.54:1.8 -->

and wisdom are steps that lead to awakening, in that they lead to becoming awakened.

<!-- @segment sn48.54:1.9 -->

The footprints of all creatures that walk can fit inside an elephant’s footprint, so an elephant’s footprint is said to be the biggest of them all.

<!-- @segment sn48.54:1.10 -->

In the same way, the faculty of wisdom is said to be the best of the steps that lead to awakening; that is, for becoming awakened. ”
