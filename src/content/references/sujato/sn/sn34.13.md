At Sāvatthī.

<!-- @segment sn34.13:1.2 -->

“Mendicants, there are these four meditators.

<!-- @segment sn34.13:1.3 -->

What four?

<!-- @segment sn34.13:1.4 -->

One meditator is skilled in entering immersion but not in positivity for immersion. …”
