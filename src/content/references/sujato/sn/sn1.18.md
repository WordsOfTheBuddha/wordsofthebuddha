<!-- @segment sn1.18:1.1 -->

“Can a person constrained by conscience

<!-- @segment sn1.18:1.2 -->

be found in the world,

<!-- @segment sn1.18:1.3 -->

who is cognizant of blame,

<!-- @segment sn1.18:1.4 -->

like a fine horse of the whip?”

<!-- @segment sn1.18:2.1 -->

“Few are those constrained by conscience,

<!-- @segment sn1.18:2.2 -->

who live always mindful.

<!-- @segment sn1.18:2.3 -->

Having reached the end of suffering,

<!-- @segment sn1.18:2.4 -->

smoothly they walk in the rough.”
