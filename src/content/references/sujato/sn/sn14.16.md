<!-- @segment sn14.16:1.1 -->

At Sāvatthī.

<!-- @segment sn14.16:1.2 -->

“Mendicants, sentient beings join together and converge because of an element.

<!-- @segment sn14.16:1.3 -->

Those who have inferior convictions join together and converge with those who have inferior convictions.

<!-- @segment sn14.16:1.4 -->

In the past …

<!-- @segment sn14.16:1.5 -->



<!-- @segment sn14.16:2.1 -->

In the future …

<!-- @segment sn14.16:2.2 -->



<!-- @segment sn14.16:3.1 -->

At present, too, sentient beings join together and converge because of an element.

<!-- @segment sn14.16:3.2 -->

Those who have inferior convictions join together and converge with those who have inferior convictions.

<!-- @segment sn14.16:4.1 -->

It’s like how dung joins together with dung,

<!-- @segment sn14.16:4.2 -->

urine with urine,

<!-- @segment sn14.16:4.3 -->

spit with spit,

<!-- @segment sn14.16:4.4 -->

pus with pus,

<!-- @segment sn14.16:4.5 -->

and blood with blood.

<!-- @segment sn14.16:4.6 -->

In the same way, sentient beings join together and converge because of an element.

<!-- @segment sn14.16:4.7 -->

Those who have inferior convictions join together and converge with those who have inferior convictions.

<!-- @segment sn14.16:4.8 -->

In the past …

<!-- @segment sn14.16:4.9 -->

In the future …

<!-- @segment sn14.16:4.10 -->

At present, too, sentient beings join together and converge because of an element.

<!-- @segment sn14.16:4.11 -->

Those who have inferior convictions join together and converge with those who have inferior convictions.

<!-- @segment sn14.16:5.1 -->

Sentient beings join together and converge because of an element.

<!-- @segment sn14.16:5.2 -->

Those who have good convictions join together and converge with those who have good convictions.

<!-- @segment sn14.16:5.3 -->

In the past …

<!-- @segment sn14.16:5.4 -->



<!-- @segment sn14.16:6.1 -->

In the future …

<!-- @segment sn14.16:6.2 -->

At present, too, sentient beings join together and converge because of an element.

<!-- @segment sn14.16:6.3 -->

Those who have good convictions join together and converge with those who have good convictions.

<!-- @segment sn14.16:7.1 -->

It’s like how milk joins together with milk,

<!-- @segment sn14.16:7.2 -->

oil with oil,

<!-- @segment sn14.16:7.3 -->

ghee with ghee,

<!-- @segment sn14.16:7.4 -->

honey with honey,

<!-- @segment sn14.16:7.5 -->

and molasses with molasses.

<!-- @segment sn14.16:7.6 -->

In the same way, sentient beings join together and converge because of an element.

<!-- @segment sn14.16:7.7 -->

Those who have good convictions join together and converge with those who have good convictions.

<!-- @segment sn14.16:7.8 -->

In the past …

<!-- @segment sn14.16:7.9 -->

In the future …

<!-- @segment sn14.16:7.10 -->

At present, too, sentient beings join together and converge because of an element.

<!-- @segment sn14.16:7.11 -->

Those who have good convictions join together and converge with those who have good convictions.”

<!-- @segment sn14.16:8.1 -->

That is what the Buddha said.

<!-- @segment sn14.16:8.2 -->

Then the Holy One, the Teacher, went on to say:

<!-- @segment sn14.16:9.1 -->

“Socializing creates a snarl;

<!-- @segment sn14.16:9.2 -->

they’re cut off by being aloof.

<!-- @segment sn14.16:9.3 -->

If you’re lost in the middle of a great sea,

<!-- @segment sn14.16:9.4 -->

and you clamber up on a little log, you’ll sink.

<!-- @segment sn14.16:10.1 -->

So too, a person who lives well

<!-- @segment sn14.16:10.2 -->

sinks by relying on a lazy person.

<!-- @segment sn14.16:10.3 -->

Hence you should avoid such

<!-- @segment sn14.16:10.4 -->

a lazy person who lacks energy.

<!-- @segment sn14.16:11.1 -->

Dwell with the noble ones

<!-- @segment sn14.16:11.2 -->

who are secluded and determined

<!-- @segment sn14.16:11.3 -->

and always energetic;

<!-- @segment sn14.16:11.4 -->

the astute who practice absorption.”
