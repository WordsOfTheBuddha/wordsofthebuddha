<!-- @segment sn2.21:1.1 -->

So I have heard.

<!-- @segment sn2.21:1.2 -->

At one time the Buddha was staying near Sāvatthī in Jeta’s Grove, Anāthapiṇḍika’s monastery.

<!-- @segment sn2.21:1.3 -->

Then, late at night, the glorious godling Shiva, lighting up the entire Jeta’s Grove, went up to the Buddha, bowed, stood to one side,

<!-- @segment sn2.21:1.4 -->

and recited these verses in the Buddha’s presence:

<!-- @segment sn2.21:2.1 -->

“Associate only with the virtuous!

<!-- @segment sn2.21:2.2 -->

Try to get close to the virtuous!

<!-- @segment sn2.21:2.3 -->

Understanding the true teaching of the good,

<!-- @segment sn2.21:2.4 -->

things get better, not worse.

<!-- @segment sn2.21:3.1 -->

Associate only with the virtuous!

<!-- @segment sn2.21:3.2 -->

Try to get close to the virtuous!

<!-- @segment sn2.21:3.3 -->

Understanding the true teaching of the good,

<!-- @segment sn2.21:3.4 -->

wisdom is gained—but not from anyone else.

<!-- @segment sn2.21:4.1 -->

Associate only with the virtuous!

<!-- @segment sn2.21:4.2 -->

Try to get close to the virtuous!

<!-- @segment sn2.21:4.3 -->

Understanding the true teaching of the good,

<!-- @segment sn2.21:4.4 -->

you grieve not among the grieving.

<!-- @segment sn2.21:5.1 -->

Associate only with the virtuous!

<!-- @segment sn2.21:5.2 -->

Try to get close to the virtuous!

<!-- @segment sn2.21:5.3 -->

Understanding the true teaching of the good,

<!-- @segment sn2.21:5.4 -->

you shine among your relatives.

<!-- @segment sn2.21:6.1 -->

Associate only with the virtuous!

<!-- @segment sn2.21:6.2 -->

Try to get close to the virtuous!

<!-- @segment sn2.21:6.3 -->

Understanding the true teaching of the good,

<!-- @segment sn2.21:6.4 -->

sentient beings go to a good place.

<!-- @segment sn2.21:7.1 -->

Associate only with the virtuous!

<!-- @segment sn2.21:7.2 -->

Try to get close to the virtuous!

<!-- @segment sn2.21:7.3 -->

Understanding the true teaching of the good,

<!-- @segment sn2.21:7.4 -->

sentient beings live happily.”

<!-- @segment sn2.21:8.1 -->

Then the Buddha replied to Shiva in verse:

<!-- @segment sn2.21:9.1 -->

“Associate only with the virtuous!

<!-- @segment sn2.21:9.2 -->

Try to get close to the virtuous!

<!-- @segment sn2.21:9.3 -->

Understanding the true teaching of the good,

<!-- @segment sn2.21:9.4 -->

you’re released from all suffering.”
