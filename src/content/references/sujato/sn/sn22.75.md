<!-- @segment sn22.75:1.1 -->

At Sāvatthī.

<!-- @segment sn22.75:1.2 -->

“Mendicants, a learned noble disciple truly understands the origin, the disappearance, the gratification, the drawback, and the escape when it comes to form,

<!-- @segment sn22.75:1.3 -->

feeling,

<!-- @segment sn22.75:1.4 -->

perception,

<!-- @segment sn22.75:1.5 -->

choices,

<!-- @segment sn22.75:1.6 -->

and consciousness.”
