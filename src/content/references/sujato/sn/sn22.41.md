title: In Line With the Teaching (3rd)
---

At Sāvatthī.

“Mendicants, when a mendicant is practicing in line with the teaching, this is what’s in line with the teaching. They should meditate observing suffering in form, feeling, perception, choices, and consciousness. …

They’re freed from suffering, I say.”
