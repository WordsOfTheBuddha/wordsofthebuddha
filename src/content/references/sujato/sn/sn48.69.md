<!-- @segment sn48.69:1.1 -->

“Mendicants, of all the trees belonging to the titans, the Vivid Patala is said to be the best.

<!-- @segment sn48.69:1.2 -->

In the same way, the faculty of wisdom is said to be the best of the qualities on the side of awakening; that is, for becoming awakened.

<!-- @segment sn48.69:1.3 -->

And what are the qualities on the side of awakening?

<!-- @segment sn48.69:1.4 -->

The faculties of faith, energy, mindfulness, immersion, and wisdom are qualities on the side of awakening, in that they lead to awakening.

<!-- @segment sn48.69:1.5 -->
