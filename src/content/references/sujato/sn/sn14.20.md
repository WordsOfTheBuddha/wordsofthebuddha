title: Beginning With Imprudence

<!-- @segment sn14.20:1.1 -->

At Sāvatthī.

<!-- @segment sn14.20:1.2 -->

“Mendicants, sentient beings join together and converge because of an element:

<!-- @segment sn14.20:1.3 -->

the imprudent with the imprudent …

<!-- @segment sn14.20:1.4 -->

unlearned …

<!-- @segment sn14.20:1.5 -->

witless.

<!-- @segment sn14.20:1.6 -->

The prudent with the prudent …

<!-- @segment sn14.20:1.7 -->

learned … wise.

<!-- @segment sn14.20:2.1 -->

… the imprudent with the imprudent …

<!-- @segment sn14.20:2.2 -->

lazy …

<!-- @segment sn14.20:2.3 -->

witless.

<!-- @segment sn14.20:2.4 -->

The prudent with the prudent …

<!-- @segment sn14.20:2.5 -->

energetic … wise.

<!-- @segment sn14.20:3.1 -->

… the imprudent with the imprudent …

<!-- @segment sn14.20:3.2 -->

unmindful …

<!-- @segment sn14.20:3.3 -->

witless.

<!-- @segment sn14.20:3.4 -->

The prudent with the prudent …

<!-- @segment sn14.20:3.5 -->

mindful … wise.”
