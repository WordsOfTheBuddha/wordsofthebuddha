<!-- @segment sn35.235:1.1 -->

“Mendicants, I will teach you an exposition of the teaching on burning.

<!-- @segment sn35.235:1.2 -->

Listen …

<!-- @segment sn35.235:1.3 -->

And what is the exposition of the teaching on burning?

<!-- @segment sn35.235:1.4 -->

You’d be better off mutilating your eye faculty with a red-hot iron nail, burning, blazing and glowing, than getting caught up in the features by way of the details in sights known by the eye.

<!-- @segment sn35.235:1.5 -->

For if you die at a time when your consciousness is still tied to gratification in the features or details, it’s possible you’ll go to one of two destinations:

<!-- @segment sn35.235:1.6 -->

hell or the animal realm.

<!-- @segment sn35.235:1.7 -->

I speak having seen this drawback.

<!-- @segment sn35.235:2.1 -->

You’d be better off mutilating your ear faculty with a sharp iron spike …

<!-- @segment sn35.235:2.2 -->



<!-- @segment sn35.235:2.3 -->



<!-- @segment sn35.235:2.4 -->



<!-- @segment sn35.235:3.1 -->

You’d be better off mutilating your nose faculty with a sharp nail cutter …

<!-- @segment sn35.235:3.2 -->



<!-- @segment sn35.235:3.3 -->



<!-- @segment sn35.235:3.4 -->



<!-- @segment sn35.235:3.5 -->



<!-- @segment sn35.235:4.1 -->

You’d be better off mutilating your tongue faculty with a sharp razor …

<!-- @segment sn35.235:4.2 -->



<!-- @segment sn35.235:4.3 -->



<!-- @segment sn35.235:4.4 -->



<!-- @segment sn35.235:4.5 -->



<!-- @segment sn35.235:5.1 -->

You’d be better off mutilating your body faculty with a sharp spear, burning, blazing and glowing, than getting caught up in the features by way of the details in touches known by the body.

<!-- @segment sn35.235:5.2 -->

For if you die at a time when your consciousness is still tied to gratification in the features or details, it’s possible you’ll go to one of two destinations:

<!-- @segment sn35.235:5.3 -->



<!-- @segment sn35.235:5.4 -->

hell or the animal realm.

<!-- @segment sn35.235:5.5 -->

I speak having seen this drawback.

<!-- @segment sn35.235:6.1 -->

You’d be better off sleeping.

<!-- @segment sn35.235:6.2 -->

For I say that sleep is useless, fruitless, and folly for the living. But while you’re asleep you won’t fall under the sway of such thoughts that would make you create a schism in the Saṅgha.

<!-- @segment sn35.235:6.3 -->

I speak having seen this drawback.

<!-- @segment sn35.235:7.1 -->

A learned noble disciple reflects on this:

<!-- @segment sn35.235:7.2 -->

‘Forget mutilating the eye faculty with a red-hot iron nail, burning, blazing and glowing!

<!-- @segment sn35.235:7.3 -->

I’d better focus on the fact that

<!-- @segment sn35.235:7.4 -->

the eye, sights, eye consciousness, and eye contact are impermanent. And the painful, pleasant, or neutral feeling that arises dependent on eye contact is also impermanent.

<!-- @segment sn35.235:8.1 -->

Forget mutilating the ear faculty with a sharp iron spike, burning, blazing and glowing!

<!-- @segment sn35.235:8.2 -->

I’d better focus on the fact that

<!-- @segment sn35.235:8.3 -->

the ear, sounds, ear consciousness, and ear contact are impermanent. And the painful, pleasant, or neutral feeling that arises dependent on ear contact is also impermanent.

<!-- @segment sn35.235:9.1 -->

Forget mutilating the nose faculty with a sharp nail cutter, burning, blazing and glowing!

<!-- @segment sn35.235:9.2 -->

I’d better focus on the fact that

<!-- @segment sn35.235:9.3 -->

the nose, smells, nose consciousness, and nose contact are impermanent. And the painful, pleasant, or neutral feeling that arises dependent on nose contact is also impermanent.

<!-- @segment sn35.235:10.1 -->

Forget mutilating the tongue faculty with a sharp razor, burning, blazing and glowing!

<!-- @segment sn35.235:10.2 -->

I’d better focus on the fact that

<!-- @segment sn35.235:10.3 -->

the tongue, tastes, tongue consciousness, and tongue contact are impermanent. And the painful, pleasant, or neutral feeling that arises dependent on tongue contact is also impermanent.

<!-- @segment sn35.235:11.1 -->

Forget mutilating the body faculty with a sharp spear, burning, blazing and glowing!

<!-- @segment sn35.235:11.2 -->

I’d better focus on the fact that

<!-- @segment sn35.235:11.3 -->

the body, touches, body consciousness, and body contact are impermanent. And the painful, pleasant, or neutral feeling that arises dependent on body contact is also impermanent.

<!-- @segment sn35.235:12.1 -->

Forget sleeping!

<!-- @segment sn35.235:12.2 -->

I’d better focus on the fact that

<!-- @segment sn35.235:12.3 -->

the mind, ideas, mind consciousness, and mind contact are impermanent. And the painful, pleasant, or neutral feeling that arises dependent on mind contact is also impermanent.’

<!-- @segment sn35.235:13.1 -->

Seeing this, a learned noble disciple grows disillusioned with the eye, sights, eye consciousness, and eye contact. And they become disillusioned with the painful, pleasant, or neutral feeling that arises dependent on eye contact.

<!-- @segment sn35.235:13.2 -->

They grow disillusioned with the ear … nose … tongue … body … mind … painful, pleasant, or neutral feeling that arises dependent on mind contact.

<!-- @segment sn35.235:13.3 -->

Being disillusioned, desire fades away. When desire fades away they’re freed. When they’re freed, they know they’re freed.

<!-- @segment sn35.235:13.4 -->

They understand: ‘Rebirth is ended, the spiritual journey has been completed, what had to be done has been done, there is nothing further for this place.’

<!-- @segment sn35.235:13.5 -->

This is the exposition of the teaching on burning.”
