<!-- @segment sn48.22:1.1 -->

“Mendicants, there are these three faculties.

<!-- @segment sn48.22:1.2 -->

What three?

<!-- @segment sn48.22:1.3 -->

The faculties of femininity, masculinity, and life.

<!-- @segment sn48.22:1.4 -->

These are the three faculties.”
