<!-- @segment sn46.47:1.1 -->

“Sir, they speak of someone who is ‘prosperous’.

<!-- @segment sn46.47:1.2 -->

How is a prosperous person defined?”

<!-- @segment sn46.47:1.3 -->

“Mendicant, they’re called prosperous because they’ve developed and cultivated the seven awakening factors.

<!-- @segment sn46.47:1.4 -->

What seven?

<!-- @segment sn46.47:1.5 -->

The awakening factors of mindfulness, investigation of principles, energy, rapture, tranquility, immersion, and equanimity.

<!-- @segment sn46.47:1.6 -->

They’re called prosperous because they’ve developed and cultivated these seven awakening factors.”
