<!-- @segment sn35.224:1.1 -->

“Mendicants, the eye, ear, nose, tongue, body, and mind are not-self.

<!-- @segment sn35.224:1.2 -->

Seeing this …

<!-- @segment sn35.224:1.3 -->

They understand: ‘… there is nothing further for this place.’”
