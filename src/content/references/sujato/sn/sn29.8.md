<!-- @segment sn29.8:1.1 -->

At Sāvatthī.

<!-- @segment sn29.8:1.2 -->

Seated to one side, that mendicant said to the Buddha:

<!-- @segment sn29.8:1.3 -->

“Sir, what is the cause, what is the reason why someone, when their body breaks up, after death, is reborn in the company of the womb-born dragons?”

<!-- @segment sn29.8:1.4 -->

(Tell all in full.)
