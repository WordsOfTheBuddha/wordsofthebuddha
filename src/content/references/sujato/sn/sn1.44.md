<!-- @segment sn1.44:1.1 -->

“One the root, two the loops,

<!-- @segment sn1.44:1.2 -->

three the stains, five the spreads,

<!-- @segment sn1.44:1.3 -->

and the ocean, the twelfth whirlpool:

<!-- @segment sn1.44:1.4 -->

such is the abyss crossed by the seer.”
