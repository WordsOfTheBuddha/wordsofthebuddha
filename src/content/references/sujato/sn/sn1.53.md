<!-- @segment sn1.53:1.1 -->

“Who’s your friend while journeying?

<!-- @segment sn1.53:1.2 -->

Who’s your friend at home?

<!-- @segment sn1.53:1.3 -->

Who’s your friend in need?

<!-- @segment sn1.53:1.4 -->

Who’s your friend in the next life?”

<!-- @segment sn1.53:2.1 -->

“A caravan is your friend while journeying.

<!-- @segment sn1.53:2.2 -->

Mother is your friend at home.

<!-- @segment sn1.53:2.3 -->

An ally in a time of need

<!-- @segment sn1.53:2.4 -->

is a friend time and again.

<!-- @segment sn1.53:2.5 -->

But the good deeds you’ve done yourself—

<!-- @segment sn1.53:2.6 -->

that’s your friend in the next life.”
