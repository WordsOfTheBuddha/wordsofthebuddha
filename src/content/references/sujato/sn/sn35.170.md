“Mendicants, you should give up desire and greed for what is impermanent.

<!-- @segment sn35.170:1.2 -->

And what is impermanent?

<!-- @segment sn35.170:1.3 -->

The eye, ear, nose, tongue, body, and mind are impermanent …”
