<!-- @segment sn48.63:1.1 -->

“Mendicants, when these five faculties are developed and cultivated they lead to the complete understanding of the course of time.

<!-- @segment sn48.63:1.2 -->

What five?

<!-- @segment sn48.63:1.3 -->

The faculties of faith, energy, mindfulness, immersion, and wisdom.

<!-- @segment sn48.63:1.4 -->

When these five faculties are developed and cultivated they lead to the complete understanding of the course of time.”
