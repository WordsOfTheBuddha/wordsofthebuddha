<!-- @segment sn1.68:1.1 -->

“By what is the world fastened shut?

<!-- @segment sn1.68:1.2 -->

On what is the world grounded?

<!-- @segment sn1.68:1.3 -->

By what is the world ensnared?

<!-- @segment sn1.68:1.4 -->

By what is it surrounded?”

<!-- @segment sn1.68:2.1 -->

“The world is fastened shut by mortality.

<!-- @segment sn1.68:2.2 -->

The world is grounded on suffering.

<!-- @segment sn1.68:2.3 -->

Craving has ensnared the world.

<!-- @segment sn1.68:2.4 -->

It’s surrounded by old age.”
