title: Perception
description: The Buddha teaches that desire and greed for perceptions of sights, sounds, smells, tastes, touches, and ideas is a corruption of the mind that must be abandoned so the mind inclines to renunciation and becomes fit for realizing anything attainable by insight.
qualities: greed, desire, craving
theme: training guideline, principle

<!-- @segment sn27.6:1.1 -->

At Sāvatthī.

<!-- @segment sn27.6:1.2 -->

“Mendicants, desire and greed for perception of sights,

<!-- @segment sn27.6:1.3 -->

perception of sounds,

<!-- @segment sn27.6:1.4 -->

perception of smells,

<!-- @segment sn27.6:1.5 -->

perception of tastes,

<!-- @segment sn27.6:1.6 -->

perception of touches,

<!-- @segment sn27.6:1.7 -->

or perception of ideas is a corruption of the mind. …”
