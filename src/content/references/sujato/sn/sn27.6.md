<!-- @segment sn27.6:1.1 -->

At Sāvatthī.

<!-- @segment sn27.6:1.2 -->

“Mendicants, desire and greed for perception of sights,

<!-- @segment sn27.6:1.3 -->

perception of sounds,

<!-- @segment sn27.6:1.4 -->

perception of smells,

<!-- @segment sn27.6:1.5 -->

perception of tastes,

<!-- @segment sn27.6:1.6 -->

perception of touches,

<!-- @segment sn27.6:1.7 -->

or perception of ideas is a corruption of the mind. …”

<!-- @segment sn27.6:1.8 -->
