<!-- @segment sn35.126:1.1 -->

At one time the Buddha was staying near Nāḷandā in Pāvārika’s mango grove.

<!-- @segment sn35.126:1.2 -->

Then the householder Upāli went up to the Buddha … and asked him,

<!-- @segment sn35.126:1.3 -->

“What is the cause, sir, what is the reason why some sentient beings are not fully extinguished in this very life?

<!-- @segment sn35.126:1.4 -->

What is the cause, sir, what is the reason why some sentient beings are fully extinguished in this very life?” …

<!-- @segment sn35.126:1.5 -->

(Tell in full as in SN 35.124.)
