<!-- @segment sn17.31:1.1 -->

At Sāvatthī.

<!-- @segment sn17.31:1.2 -->

“Possessions, honor, and popularity are grim …

<!-- @segment sn17.31:1.3 -->

Devadatta split the Saṅgha because his mind was overcome and overwhelmed by possessions, honor, and popularity.

<!-- @segment sn17.31:1.4 -->

So grim are possessions, honor, and popularity. …”
