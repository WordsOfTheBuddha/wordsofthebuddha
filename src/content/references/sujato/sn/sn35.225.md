<!-- @segment sn35.225:1.1 -->

“Mendicants, sights, sounds, smells, tastes, touches, and ideas are impermanent.

<!-- @segment sn35.225:1.2 -->

Seeing this …

<!-- @segment sn35.225:1.3 -->

They understand: ‘… there is nothing further for this place.’”
