<!-- @segment sn36.30:1.1 -->

“Mendicants, there are these three feelings.

<!-- @segment sn36.30:1.2 -->

What three?

<!-- @segment sn36.30:1.3 -->

Pleasant, painful, and neutral feelings.

<!-- @segment sn36.30:1.4 -->

These are the three feelings.”
