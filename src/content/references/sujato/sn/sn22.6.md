<!-- @segment sn22.6:1.1 -->

At Sāvatthī.

<!-- @segment sn22.6:1.2 -->

“Mendicants, meditate in retreat.

<!-- @segment sn22.6:1.3 -->

A mendicant in retreat truly understands.

<!-- @segment sn22.6:1.4 -->

What do they truly understand?

<!-- @segment sn22.6:1.5 -->

The origin and ending of form, feeling, perception, choices, and consciousness. …”
