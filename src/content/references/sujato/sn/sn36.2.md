<!-- @segment sn36.2:1.1 -->

“Mendicants, there are these three feelings.

<!-- @segment sn36.2:1.2 -->

What three?

<!-- @segment sn36.2:1.3 -->

Pleasant, painful, and neutral feeling.

<!-- @segment sn36.2:1.4 -->

These are the three feelings.

<!-- @segment sn36.2:2.1 -->

Whether it’s pleasant or painful,

<!-- @segment sn36.2:2.2 -->

as well as what’s neutral,

<!-- @segment sn36.2:2.3 -->

whatever there is that is felt

<!-- @segment sn36.2:2.4 -->

internally and externally:

<!-- @segment sn36.2:3.1 -->

having known, ‘this is suffering,

<!-- @segment sn36.2:3.2 -->

deceptive, breaking apart’,

<!-- @segment sn36.2:3.3 -->

seeing feelings vanish with every contact,

<!-- @segment sn36.2:3.4 -->

that’s how they become free of desire for them.”
