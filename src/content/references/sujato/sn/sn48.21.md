<!-- @segment sn48.21:1.1 -->

“Mendicants, there are these five faculties.

<!-- @segment sn48.21:1.2 -->

What five?

<!-- @segment sn48.21:1.3 -->

The faculties of faith, energy, mindfulness, immersion, and wisdom.

<!-- @segment sn48.21:1.4 -->

As long as I didn’t truly understand these five faculties’ origin, disappearance, gratification, drawback, and escape, I didn’t announce my supreme perfect awakening in this world with its gods, Māras, and Divinities, this population with its ascetics and brahmins, its gods and humans.

<!-- @segment sn48.21:1.5 -->

But when I did truly understand these five faculties’ origin, disappearance, gratification, drawback, and escape, I announced my supreme perfect awakening in this world with its gods, Māras, and Divinities, this population with its ascetics and brahmins, its gods and humans.

<!-- @segment sn48.21:1.6 -->

Knowledge and vision arose in me:

<!-- @segment sn48.21:1.7 -->

‘My freedom is unshakable; this is my last rebirth; now there’ll be no more future lives.’”
