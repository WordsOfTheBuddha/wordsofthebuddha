<!-- @segment sn26.8:1.1 -->

At Sāvatthī.

<!-- @segment sn26.8:1.2 -->

“Mendicants, the arising of craving for sights …

<!-- @segment sn26.8:1.3 -->



<!-- @segment sn26.8:1.4 -->

craving for ideas … is the manifestation of old age and death.

<!-- @segment sn26.8:1.5 -->

The cessation of craving for sights …

<!-- @segment sn26.8:1.6 -->



<!-- @segment sn26.8:1.7 -->

craving for ideas … is the disappearance of old age and death.”
