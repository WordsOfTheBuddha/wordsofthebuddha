<!-- @segment sn15.18:1.1 -->

At Sāvatthī.

<!-- @segment sn15.18:1.2 -->

“It’s not easy to find a sentient being who in all this long time has not previously been your son. …

<!-- @segment sn15.18:1.3 -->

This is quite enough for you to become disillusioned, dispassionate, and freed regarding all conditions.”
