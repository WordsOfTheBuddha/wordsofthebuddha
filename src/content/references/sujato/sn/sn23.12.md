<!-- @segment sn23.12:1.1 -->

At Sāvatthī.

<!-- @segment sn23.12:1.2 -->

Seated to one side, Venerable Rādha said to the Buddha:

<!-- @segment sn23.12:1.3 -->

“Sir, they speak of ‘Māra nature’.

<!-- @segment sn23.12:1.4 -->

What is of Māra nature?”

<!-- @segment sn23.12:1.5 -->

“Rādha, form is of Māra nature. Feeling, perception, choices, and consciousness are of Māra nature.

<!-- @segment sn23.12:1.6 -->

Seeing this …

<!-- @segment sn23.12:1.7 -->

They understand: ‘… there is nothing further for this place.’”
