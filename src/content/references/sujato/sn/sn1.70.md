<!-- @segment sn1.70:1.1 -->

“In what has the world arisen?

<!-- @segment sn1.70:1.2 -->

With what does it get intimate?

<!-- @segment sn1.70:1.3 -->

By grasping what

<!-- @segment sn1.70:1.4 -->

is the world troubled in what?”

<!-- @segment sn1.70:2.1 -->

“The world has arisen in six.

<!-- @segment sn1.70:2.2 -->

It gets intimate with six.

<!-- @segment sn1.70:2.3 -->

Derived from the six,

<!-- @segment sn1.70:2.4 -->

the world’s troubled in six.”
