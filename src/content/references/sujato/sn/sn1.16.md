<!-- @segment sn1.16:1.1 -->

“Sleepiness, sloth, and yawning,

<!-- @segment sn1.16:1.2 -->

discontent, and grogginess after eating:

<!-- @segment sn1.16:1.3 -->

because of this the noble path

<!-- @segment sn1.16:1.4 -->

doesn’t shine for creatures here.”

<!-- @segment sn1.16:2.1 -->

“Sleepiness, sloth, and yawning,

<!-- @segment sn1.16:2.2 -->

discontent, and grogginess after eating:

<!-- @segment sn1.16:2.3 -->

when this is energetically fended off,

<!-- @segment sn1.16:2.4 -->

the noble path is purified.”
