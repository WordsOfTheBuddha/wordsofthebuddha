title: Craving For Sights
---

<!-- @segment sn25.8:1.1 -->

At Sāvatthī.

<!-- @segment sn25.8:1.2 -->

“Mendicants, craving for sights is impermanent, decaying, and perishing.

<!-- @segment sn25.8:1.3 -->

Craving for sounds,

<!-- @segment sn25.8:1.4 -->

craving for smells,

<!-- @segment sn25.8:1.5 -->

craving for tastes,

<!-- @segment sn25.8:1.6 -->

craving for touches,

<!-- @segment sn25.8:1.7 -->

and craving for ideas are impermanent, decaying, and perishing.

<!-- @segment sn25.8:1.8 -->

Someone who has faith and confidence in these teachings is called a follower by faith. …”

<!-- @segment sn25.8:1.9 -->
