At Sāvatthī.

“Mendicants, an unlearned ordinary person doesn’t truly understand the origin, the disappearance, the gratification, the drawback, and the escape when it comes to form,

feeling,

perception,

choices,

and consciousness.

A learned noble disciple does truly understand the origin, the disappearance, the gratification, the drawback, and the escape when it comes to form,

feeling,

perception,

choices,

and consciousness.”
