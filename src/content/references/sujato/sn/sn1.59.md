<!-- @segment sn1.59:1.1 -->

“What is a person’s partner?

<!-- @segment sn1.59:1.2 -->

What instructs them?

<!-- @segment sn1.59:1.3 -->

Enjoying what is a mortal

<!-- @segment sn1.59:1.4 -->

released from all suffering?”

<!-- @segment sn1.59:2.1 -->

“Faith is a person’s partner.

<!-- @segment sn1.59:2.2 -->

Wisdom instructs them.

<!-- @segment sn1.59:2.3 -->

Delighting in extinguishment a mortal

<!-- @segment sn1.59:2.4 -->

is released from all suffering.”
