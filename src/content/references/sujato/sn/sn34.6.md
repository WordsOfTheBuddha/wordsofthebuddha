<!-- @segment sn34.6:1.1 -->

At Sāvatthī.

<!-- @segment sn34.6:1.2 -->

“Mendicants, there are these four meditators.

<!-- @segment sn34.6:1.3 -->

What four?

<!-- @segment sn34.6:1.4 -->

One meditator is skilled in immersion but not in the territory of immersion. …”
