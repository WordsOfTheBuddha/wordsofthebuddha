title: Naturally Not-Self
---

At Sāvatthī.

Seated to one side, Venerable Rādha said to the Buddha:

“Sir, they speak of having ‘not-self nature’.

What has a nature of not-self?”

“Rādha, form, feeling, perception, choices, and consciousness have a nature of not-self.

Seeing this …

They understand: ‘… there is nothing further for this place.’”
