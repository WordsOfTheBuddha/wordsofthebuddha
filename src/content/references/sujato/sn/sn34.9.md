<!-- @segment sn34.9:1.1 -->

At Sāvatthī.

<!-- @segment sn34.9:1.2 -->

“Mendicants, there are these four meditators.

<!-- @segment sn34.9:1.3 -->

What four?

<!-- @segment sn34.9:1.4 -->

One meditator is skilled in immersion but not in practicing persistently for it. …”
