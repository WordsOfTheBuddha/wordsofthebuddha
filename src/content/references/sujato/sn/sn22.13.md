<!-- @segment sn22.13:1.1 -->

At Sāvatthī.

<!-- @segment sn22.13:1.2 -->

“Mendicants, form, feeling, perception, choices, and consciousness are suffering.

<!-- @segment sn22.13:1.3 -->

Seeing this …

<!-- @segment sn22.13:1.4 -->

They understand: ‘… there is nothing further for this place.’”
