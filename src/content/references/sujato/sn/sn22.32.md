<!-- @segment sn22.32:1.1 -->

At Sāvatthī.

<!-- @segment sn22.32:1.2 -->

“Mendicants, I will teach you what is brittle and what is not brittle.

<!-- @segment sn22.32:1.3 -->

Listen …

<!-- @segment sn22.32:1.4 -->

And what is brittle? What is not brittle?

<!-- @segment sn22.32:1.5 -->

Form is brittle,

<!-- @segment sn22.32:1.6 -->

but its cessation, settling, and ending is not brittle.

<!-- @segment sn22.32:1.7 -->

Feeling …

<!-- @segment sn22.32:1.8 -->



<!-- @segment sn22.32:1.9 -->

perception …

<!-- @segment sn22.32:1.10 -->

choices …

<!-- @segment sn22.32:1.11 -->



<!-- @segment sn22.32:1.12 -->

consciousness is brittle,

<!-- @segment sn22.32:1.13 -->

but its cessation, settling, and ending is not brittle.”
