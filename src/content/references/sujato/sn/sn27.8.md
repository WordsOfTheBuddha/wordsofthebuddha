title: Craving
description: The Buddha teaches that desire and greed for craving for sights, sounds, smells, tastes, touches, and ideas is a corruption of the mind that must be abandoned so the mind inclines to renunciation and becomes fit for realizing anything attainable by insight.
qualities: craving, greed, desire
theme: training guideline, principle
---

<!-- @segment sn27.8:1.1 -->

At Sāvatthī.

<!-- @segment sn27.8:1.2 -->

“Mendicants, desire and greed for craving for sights,

<!-- @segment sn27.8:1.3 -->

craving for sounds,

<!-- @segment sn27.8:1.4 -->

craving for smells,

<!-- @segment sn27.8:1.5 -->

craving for tastes,

<!-- @segment sn27.8:1.6 -->

craving for touches,

<!-- @segment sn27.8:1.7 -->

or craving for ideas is a corruption of the mind. …”
