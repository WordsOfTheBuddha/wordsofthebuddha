<!-- @segment sn48.31:1.1 -->

“Mendicants, there are these five faculties.

<!-- @segment sn48.31:1.2 -->

What five?

<!-- @segment sn48.31:1.3 -->

The faculties of pleasure, pain, happiness, sadness, and equanimity.

<!-- @segment sn48.31:1.4 -->

These are the five faculties.”
