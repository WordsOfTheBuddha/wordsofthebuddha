title: Beginning With the Unlearned

<!-- @segment sn14.21:1.1 -->

At Sāvatthī.

<!-- @segment sn14.21:1.2 -->

“Mendicants, sentient beings join together and converge because of an element:

<!-- @segment sn14.21:1.3 -->

the unlearned with the unlearned …

<!-- @segment sn14.21:1.4 -->

lazy …

<!-- @segment sn14.21:1.5 -->

witless.

<!-- @segment sn14.21:1.6 -->

The learned with the learned …

<!-- @segment sn14.21:1.7 -->

energetic … wise.

<!-- @segment sn14.21:2.1 -->

… the unlearned with the unlearned …

<!-- @segment sn14.21:2.2 -->

unmindful …

<!-- @segment sn14.21:2.3 -->

witless.

<!-- @segment sn14.21:2.4 -->

The learned with the learned …

<!-- @segment sn14.21:2.5 -->

mindful … wise.”
