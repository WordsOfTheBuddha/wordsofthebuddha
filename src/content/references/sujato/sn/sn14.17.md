At Sāvatthī.

<!-- @segment sn14.17:1.2 -->

“Mendicants, sentient beings join together and converge because of an element:

<!-- @segment sn14.17:1.3 -->

the faithless with the faithless,

<!-- @segment sn14.17:1.4 -->

the unconscientious with the unconscientious,

<!-- @segment sn14.17:1.5 -->

the imprudent with the imprudent,

<!-- @segment sn14.17:1.6 -->

the unlearned with the unlearned,

<!-- @segment sn14.17:1.7 -->

the lazy with the lazy,

<!-- @segment sn14.17:1.8 -->

the unmindful with the unmindful,

<!-- @segment sn14.17:1.9 -->

and the witless with the witless.

<!-- @segment sn14.17:2.1 -->
