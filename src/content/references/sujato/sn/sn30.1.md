<!-- @segment sn30.1:1.1 -->

At Sāvatthī.

<!-- @segment sn30.1:1.2 -->

“Mendicants, phoenixes reproduce in these four ways.

<!-- @segment sn30.1:1.3 -->

What four?

<!-- @segment sn30.1:1.4 -->

Phoenixes are born from eggs, from a womb, from moisture, or spontaneously.

<!-- @segment sn30.1:1.5 -->

These are the four ways that phoenixes reproduce.”
