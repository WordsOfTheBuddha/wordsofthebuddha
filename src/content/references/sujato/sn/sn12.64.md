<!-- @segment sn12.64:1.1 -->

At Sāvatthī.

<!-- @segment sn12.64:1.2 -->

“Mendicants, these four fuels maintain sentient beings that have been born and help those about to be born.

<!-- @segment sn12.64:1.3 -->

What four?

<!-- @segment sn12.64:1.4 -->

Edible food, whether solid or subtle; contact is the second, mental intention the third, and consciousness the fourth.

<!-- @segment sn12.64:1.5 -->

These are the four fuels that maintain sentient beings that have been born and help those about to be born.

<!-- @segment sn12.64:2.1 -->

If there is desire, relishing, and craving for edible food, consciousness becomes established there and grows.

<!-- @segment sn12.64:2.2 -->

Where consciousness is established and grows, name and form are conceived.

<!-- @segment sn12.64:2.3 -->

Where name and form are conceived, there is the growth of choices.

<!-- @segment sn12.64:2.4 -->

Where choices grow, there is regeneration into a new state of existence in the future.

<!-- @segment sn12.64:2.5 -->

Where there is regeneration into a new state of existence in the future, there is rebirth, old age, and death in the future.

<!-- @segment sn12.64:2.6 -->

Where there is rebirth, old age, and death in the future, I say this is full of sorrow, anguish, and distress.

<!-- @segment sn12.64:3.1 -->

If there is desire, relishing, and craving for contact as fuel …

<!-- @segment sn12.64:3.2 -->

If there is desire, relishing, and craving for mental intention as fuel …

<!-- @segment sn12.64:3.3 -->

If there is desire, relishing, and craving for consciousness as fuel, consciousness becomes established there and grows.

<!-- @segment sn12.64:3.4 -->

Where consciousness is established and grows, name and form are conceived.

<!-- @segment sn12.64:3.5 -->

Where name and form are conceived, there is the growth of choices.

<!-- @segment sn12.64:3.6 -->

Where choices grow, there is regeneration into a new state of existence in the future.

<!-- @segment sn12.64:3.7 -->

Where there is regeneration into a new state of existence in the future, there is rebirth, old age, and death in the future.

<!-- @segment sn12.64:3.8 -->

Where there is rebirth, old age, and death in the future, I say this is full of sorrow, anguish, and distress.

<!-- @segment sn12.64:4.1 -->

Suppose an artist or painter had some dye, red lac, turmeric, indigo, or rose madder. And on a polished plank or a wall or a canvas they’d create the form of a woman or a man, whole in its major and minor limbs.

<!-- @segment sn12.64:4.2 -->

In the same way, if there is desire, relishing, and craving for edible food, consciousness becomes established there and grows.

<!-- @segment sn12.64:4.3 -->

Where consciousness is established and grows, name and form are conceived.

<!-- @segment sn12.64:4.4 -->

Where name and form are conceived, there is the growth of choices.

<!-- @segment sn12.64:4.5 -->

Where choices grow, there is regeneration into a new state of existence in the future.

<!-- @segment sn12.64:4.6 -->

Where there is regeneration into a new state of existence in the future, there is rebirth, old age, and death in the future.

<!-- @segment sn12.64:4.7 -->

Where there is rebirth, old age, and death in the future, I say this is full of sorrow, anguish, and distress.

If there is desire, relishing, and craving for mental intention as fuel …

<!-- @segment sn12.64:5.3 -->

If there is desire, relishing, and craving for consciousness as fuel, consciousness becomes established there and grows.

<!-- @segment sn12.64:6.1 -->

If there is no desire, relishing, and craving for edible food, consciousness does not become established there and doesn’t grow.

<!-- @segment sn12.64:6.2 -->

Where consciousness is not established and doesn’t grow, name and form are not conceived.

<!-- @segment sn12.64:6.3 -->

Where name and form are not conceived, there is no growth of choices.

<!-- @segment sn12.64:6.4 -->

Where choices don’t grow, there is no regeneration into a new state of existence in the future.

<!-- @segment sn12.64:6.5 -->

Where there is no regeneration into a new state of existence in the future, there is no rebirth, old age, and death in the future.

<!-- @segment sn12.64:6.6 -->

Where there is no rebirth, old age, and death in the future, I say there’s no sorrow, anguish, and distress.

If there is no desire, relishing, and craving for mental intention as fuel …

<!-- @segment sn12.64:7.3 -->

<!-- @segment sn12.64:8.1 -->

Suppose there was a bungalow or a hall with a peaked roof, with windows on the northern, southern, or eastern side. When the sun rises and a ray of light enters through a window, where would it land?”

<!-- @segment sn12.64:8.2 -->

“On the western wall, sir.”

<!-- @segment sn12.64:8.3 -->

“If there was no western wall, where would it land?”

<!-- @segment sn12.64:8.4 -->

“On the ground, sir.”

<!-- @segment sn12.64:8.5 -->

“If there was no ground, where would it land?”

<!-- @segment sn12.64:8.6 -->

“In water, sir.”

<!-- @segment sn12.64:8.7 -->

“If there was no water, where would it land?”

<!-- @segment sn12.64:8.8 -->

“It wouldn’t land, sir.”

<!-- @segment sn12.64:8.9 -->

If there is no desire, relishing, and craving for contact as fuel …

<!-- @segment sn12.64:9.2 -->

If there is no desire, relishing, and craving for mental intention as fuel …
