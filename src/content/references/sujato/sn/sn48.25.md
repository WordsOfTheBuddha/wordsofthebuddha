<!-- @segment sn48.25:1.1 -->

“Mendicants, there are these six faculties.

<!-- @segment sn48.25:1.2 -->

What six?

<!-- @segment sn48.25:1.3 -->

The faculties of the eye, ear, nose, tongue, body, and mind.

<!-- @segment sn48.25:1.4 -->

These are the six faculties.”
