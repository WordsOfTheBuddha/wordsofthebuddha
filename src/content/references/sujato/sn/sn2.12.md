<!-- @segment sn2.12:1.1 -->

Standing to one side, the godling Vishnu spoke this verse in the Buddha’s presence:

<!-- @segment sn2.12:2.1 -->

“Happy are the men

<!-- @segment sn2.12:2.2 -->

who pay homage to the Holy One!

<!-- @segment sn2.12:2.3 -->

They apply themselves to Gotama’s instructions,

<!-- @segment sn2.12:2.4 -->

diligently training.”

<!-- @segment sn2.12:3.1 -->

“Those who practice absorption <j>in accord with the training”,

<!-- @segment sn2.12:3.2 -->

said the Buddha to Vishnu,

<!-- @segment sn2.12:3.3 -->

“in the way of teaching I’ve proclaimed,

<!-- @segment sn2.12:3.4 -->

diligent while there is still time;

<!-- @segment sn2.12:3.5 -->

they won’t fall under the sway of Death.”
