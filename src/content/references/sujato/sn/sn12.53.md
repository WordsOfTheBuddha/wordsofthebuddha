<!-- @segment sn12.53:1.1 -->

At Sāvatthī.

<!-- @segment sn12.53:1.2 -->

“There are things that tighten the fetters. When you concentrate on the gratification provided by these things, your craving grows.

<!-- @segment sn12.53:1.3 -->

Craving is a requirement for grasping.

<!-- @segment sn12.53:1.4 -->

Grasping is a requirement for continued existence.

<!-- @segment sn12.53:1.5 -->

Continued existence is a requirement for rebirth.

<!-- @segment sn12.53:1.6 -->

Rebirth is a requirement for old age and death, sorrow, lamentation, pain, sadness, and distress to come to be.

<!-- @segment sn12.53:1.7 -->

That is how this entire mass of suffering originates.

<!-- @segment sn12.53:2.1 -->

Suppose an oil lamp depended on oil and a wick to burn.

<!-- @segment sn12.53:2.2 -->

And from time to time someone would pour oil in and adjust the wick.

<!-- @segment sn12.53:2.3 -->

Fed and fuelled by that, the oil lamp would burn for a long time.

<!-- @segment sn12.53:2.4 -->

In the same way, there are things that tighten the fetters. When you concentrate on the gratification provided by these things, your craving grows.

<!-- @segment sn12.53:2.5 -->

Craving is a requirement for grasping.

<!-- @segment sn12.53:2.6 -->

Grasping is a requirement for continued existence.

<!-- @segment sn12.53:2.7 -->

Continued existence is a requirement for rebirth.

<!-- @segment sn12.53:2.8 -->

Rebirth is a requirement for old age and death, sorrow, lamentation, pain, sadness, and distress to come to be.

<!-- @segment sn12.53:2.9 -->

That is how this entire mass of suffering originates.

<!-- @segment sn12.53:3.1 -->

There are things that tighten the fetters. When you concentrate on the drawbacks of these things, your craving ceases.

<!-- @segment sn12.53:3.2 -->

When craving ceases, grasping ceases.

<!-- @segment sn12.53:3.3 -->

When grasping ceases, continued existence ceases.

<!-- @segment sn12.53:3.4 -->

When continued existence ceases, rebirth ceases.

<!-- @segment sn12.53:3.5 -->

When rebirth ceases, old age and death, sorrow, lamentation, pain, sadness, and distress cease.

<!-- @segment sn12.53:3.6 -->

That is how this entire mass of suffering ceases.

<!-- @segment sn12.53:4.1 -->

Suppose an oil lamp depended on oil and a wick to burn.

<!-- @segment sn12.53:4.2 -->

And no-one would pour oil in and adjust the wick from time to time.

<!-- @segment sn12.53:4.3 -->

As the original fuel is used up and no more is added, the oil lamp would be extinguished due to not being fed.

<!-- @segment sn12.53:4.4 -->

In the same way, there are things that tighten the fetters. When you concentrate on the drawbacks of these things, your craving ceases.

<!-- @segment sn12.53:4.5 -->

When craving ceases, grasping ceases. …

<!-- @segment sn12.53:4.6 -->

That is how this entire mass of suffering ceases.”
