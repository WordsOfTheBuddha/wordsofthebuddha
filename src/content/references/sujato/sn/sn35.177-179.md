<!-- @segment sn35.177-179:1.1 -->

“Mendicants, you should give up desire … greed … desire and greed for what is impermanent.

<!-- @segment sn35.177-179:1.2 -->

And what is impermanent?

<!-- @segment sn35.177-179:1.3 -->

Sights,

<!-- @segment sn35.177-179:1.4 -->

sounds,

<!-- @segment sn35.177-179:1.5 -->

smells,

<!-- @segment sn35.177-179:1.6 -->

tastes,

<!-- @segment sn35.177-179:1.7 -->

touches,

<!-- @segment sn35.177-179:1.8 -->

and ideas are impermanent …”
