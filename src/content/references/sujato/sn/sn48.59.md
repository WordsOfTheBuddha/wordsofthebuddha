<!-- @segment sn48.59:1.1 -->

At Sāvatthī.

<!-- @segment sn48.59:1.2 -->

“Mendicants, these five faculties don’t arise to be developed and cultivated except when a Realized One, a perfected one, a fully awakened Buddha has appeared.

<!-- @segment sn48.59:1.3 -->

What five?

<!-- @segment sn48.59:1.4 -->

The faculties of faith, energy, mindfulness, immersion, and wisdom.

<!-- @segment sn48.59:1.5 -->

These five faculties don’t arise to be developed and cultivated except when a Realized One, a perfected one, a fully awakened Buddha has appeared.”
