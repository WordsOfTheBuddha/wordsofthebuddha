<!-- @segment sn1.61:1.1 -->

“What overcomes everything?

<!-- @segment sn1.61:1.2 -->

What is it that nothing excels?

<!-- @segment sn1.61:1.3 -->

What is the one thing

<!-- @segment sn1.61:1.4 -->

that has everything under its sway?”

<!-- @segment sn1.61:2.1 -->

“Name overcomes everything.

<!-- @segment sn1.61:2.2 -->

Nothing excels name.

<!-- @segment sn1.61:2.3 -->

Name is the one thing

<!-- @segment sn1.61:2.4 -->

that has everything under its sway.”
