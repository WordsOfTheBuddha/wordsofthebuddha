At Sāvatthī.

“Mendicants, the earth element is impermanent, decaying, and perishing.

The water element,

the fire element,

the air element,

the space element,

and the consciousness element are impermanent, decaying, and perishing.

Someone who has faith and confidence in these teachings is called a follower by faith. …”
