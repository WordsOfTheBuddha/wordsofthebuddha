<!-- @segment sn25.9:1.1 -->

At Sāvatthī.

<!-- @segment sn25.9:1.2 -->

“Mendicants, the earth element is impermanent, decaying, and perishing.

<!-- @segment sn25.9:1.3 -->

The water element,

<!-- @segment sn25.9:1.4 -->

the fire element,

<!-- @segment sn25.9:1.5 -->

the air element,

<!-- @segment sn25.9:1.6 -->

the space element,

<!-- @segment sn25.9:1.7 -->

and the consciousness element are impermanent, decaying, and perishing.

<!-- @segment sn25.9:1.8 -->

Someone who has faith and confidence in these teachings is called a follower by faith. …”
