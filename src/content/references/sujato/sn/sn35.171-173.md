<!-- @segment sn35.171-173:1.1 -->

“Mendicants, you should give up desire … greed … desire and greed for what is suffering.

<!-- @segment sn35.171-173:1.2 -->

And what is suffering?

<!-- @segment sn35.171-173:1.3 -->

The eye, ear, nose,

<!-- @segment sn35.171-173:1.4 -->

tongue, body,

<!-- @segment sn35.171-173:1.5 -->

and mind are suffering …”
