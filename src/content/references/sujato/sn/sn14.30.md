<!-- @segment sn14.30:1.1 -->

At one time the Buddha was staying near Sāvatthī in Jeta’s Grove, Anāthapiṇḍika’s monastery. …

<!-- @segment sn14.30:1.2 -->

“Mendicants, there are these four elements.

<!-- @segment sn14.30:1.3 -->

What four?

<!-- @segment sn14.30:1.4 -->

The elements of earth, water, fire, and air.

<!-- @segment sn14.30:1.5 -->

These are the four elements.”
