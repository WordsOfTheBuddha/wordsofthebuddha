<!-- @segment sn14.25:1.1 -->

At Sāvatthī.

<!-- @segment sn14.25:1.2 -->

“Mendicants, sentient beings join together and converge because of an element:

<!-- @segment sn14.25:1.3 -->

those who kill living creatures with those who kill living creatures,

<!-- @segment sn14.25:1.4 -->

those who steal …

<!-- @segment sn14.25:1.5 -->

commit sexual misconduct …

<!-- @segment sn14.25:1.6 -->

lie …

<!-- @segment sn14.25:1.7 -->

consume intoxicants of beer, wine, and liquor …

<!-- @segment sn14.25:2.1 -->

Those who refrain from killing living creatures …

<!-- @segment sn14.25:2.2 -->

who refrain from stealing …

<!-- @segment sn14.25:2.3 -->

who refrain from sexual misconduct …

<!-- @segment sn14.25:2.4 -->

who refrain from lying …

<!-- @segment sn14.25:2.5 -->

those who refrain from intoxicants of beer, wine, and liquor with those who refrain from intoxicants of beer, wine, and liquor.”
