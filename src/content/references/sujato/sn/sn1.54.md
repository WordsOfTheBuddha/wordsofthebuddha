<!-- @segment sn1.54:1.1 -->

“What is the site for human beings?

<!-- @segment sn1.54:1.2 -->

What is the best comrade here?

<!-- @segment sn1.54:1.3 -->

By what do the creatures who live off the earth

<!-- @segment sn1.54:1.4 -->

sustain their life?”

<!-- @segment sn1.54:2.1 -->

“Children are the site for human beings.

<!-- @segment sn1.54:2.2 -->

A wife is the best comrade.

<!-- @segment sn1.54:2.3 -->

The creatures who live off the earth

<!-- @segment sn1.54:2.4 -->

sustain their life by rain.”
