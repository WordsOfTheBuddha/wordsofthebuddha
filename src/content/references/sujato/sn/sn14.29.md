<!-- @segment sn14.29:1.1 -->

At Sāvatthī.

<!-- @segment sn14.29:1.2 -->

“Mendicants, sentient beings come together and converge because of an element:

<!-- @segment sn14.29:1.3 -->

those of wrong view with those of wrong view …

<!-- @segment sn14.29:1.4 -->

wrong purpose …

<!-- @segment sn14.29:1.5 -->

wrong speech …

<!-- @segment sn14.29:1.6 -->

wrong action …

<!-- @segment sn14.29:1.7 -->

wrong livelihood …

<!-- @segment sn14.29:1.8 -->

wrong effort …

<!-- @segment sn14.29:1.9 -->

wrong mindfulness …

<!-- @segment sn14.29:1.10 -->

wrong immersion …

<!-- @segment sn14.29:1.11 -->

wrong knowledge …

<!-- @segment sn14.29:1.12 -->

wrong freedom …

<!-- @segment sn14.29:2.1 -->

Those who have right view …

<!-- @segment sn14.29:2.2 -->

right purpose …

<!-- @segment sn14.29:2.3 -->

right speech …

<!-- @segment sn14.29:2.4 -->

right action …

<!-- @segment sn14.29:2.5 -->

right livelihood …

<!-- @segment sn14.29:2.6 -->

right effort …

<!-- @segment sn14.29:2.7 -->

right mindfulness …

<!-- @segment sn14.29:2.8 -->

right immersion …

<!-- @segment sn14.29:2.9 -->

right knowledge …

<!-- @segment sn14.29:2.10 -->

right freedom with those who have right freedom.”

<!-- @segment sn14.29:2.11 -->



<!-- @segment sn14.29:2.12 -->

(Tell all in full for the past, future, and present.)
