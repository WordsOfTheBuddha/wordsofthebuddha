<!-- @segment sn31.1:1.1 -->

At one time the Buddha was staying near Sāvatthī in Jeta’s Grove, Anāthapiṇḍika’s monastery. …

<!-- @segment sn31.1:1.2 -->

The Buddha said this:

<!-- @segment sn31.1:1.3 -->

“Mendicants, I will teach you about the gods of the centaur realm.

<!-- @segment sn31.1:1.4 -->

Listen …

<!-- @segment sn31.1:1.5 -->

And what are the gods of the centaur realm?

<!-- @segment sn31.1:1.6 -->

There are gods who live in fragrant roots,

<!-- @segment sn31.1:1.7 -->

fragrant heartwood,

<!-- @segment sn31.1:1.8 -->

fragrant softwood,

<!-- @segment sn31.1:1.9 -->

fragrant bark,

<!-- @segment sn31.1:1.10 -->

fragrant shoots,

<!-- @segment sn31.1:1.11 -->

fragrant leaves,

<!-- @segment sn31.1:1.12 -->

fragrant flowers,

<!-- @segment sn31.1:1.13 -->

fragrant fruit,

<!-- @segment sn31.1:1.14 -->

fragrant sap,

<!-- @segment sn31.1:1.15 -->

and fragrant scents.

<!-- @segment sn31.1:1.16 -->

These are called the gods of the centaur realm.”
