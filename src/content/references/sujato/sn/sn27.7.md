title: Intention
description: The Buddha teaches that desire and greed for intentions regarding sights, sounds, smells, tastes, touches, and ideas is a corruption of the mind that must be abandoned so the mind inclines to renunciation and becomes fit for realizing anything attainable by insight.
qualities: desire, greed, craving
theme: training guideline, principle

<!-- @segment sn27.7:1.1 -->

At Sāvatthī.

<!-- @segment sn27.7:1.2 -->

“Mendicants, desire and greed for intention regarding sights,

<!-- @segment sn27.7:1.3 -->

intention regarding sounds,

<!-- @segment sn27.7:1.4 -->

intention regarding smells,

<!-- @segment sn27.7:1.5 -->

intention regarding tastes,

<!-- @segment sn27.7:1.6 -->

intention regarding touches,

<!-- @segment sn27.7:1.7 -->

or intention regarding ideas is a corruption of the mind. …”
