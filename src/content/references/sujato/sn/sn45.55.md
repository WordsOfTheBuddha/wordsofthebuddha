<!-- @segment sn45.55:1.1 -->

“Mendicants, the dawn is the forerunner and harbinger of the sunrise.

<!-- @segment sn45.55:1.2 -->

In the same way accomplishment in rational application of mind is the forerunner and harbinger of the noble eightfold path for a mendicant.

<!-- @segment sn45.55:1.3 -->

A mendicant accomplished in rational application of mind can expect to develop and cultivate the noble eightfold path.

<!-- @segment sn45.55:1.4 -->

And how does a mendicant accomplished in rational application of mind develop and cultivate the noble eightfold path?

<!-- @segment sn45.55:1.5 -->

It’s when a mendicant develops right view, right purpose, right speech, right action, right livelihood, right effort, right mindfulness, and right immersion, which rely on seclusion, fading away, and cessation, and ripen as letting go.

<!-- @segment sn45.55:1.6 -->

That’s how a mendicant accomplished in rational application of mind develops and cultivates the noble eightfold path.”
