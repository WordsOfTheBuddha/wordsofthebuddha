<!-- @segment sn12.44:1.1 -->

At Sāvatthī.

<!-- @segment sn12.44:1.2 -->

“Mendicants, I will teach you the origin and disappearance of the world.

<!-- @segment sn12.44:1.3 -->

Listen and apply your mind well, I will speak.”

<!-- @segment sn12.44:1.4 -->

“Yes, sir,” they replied.

<!-- @segment sn12.44:1.5 -->

The Buddha said this:

<!-- @segment sn12.44:2.1 -->

“And what, mendicants, is the origin of the world?

<!-- @segment sn12.44:2.2 -->

Eye consciousness arises dependent on the eye and sights. The meeting of the three is contact.

<!-- @segment sn12.44:2.3 -->

Contact is a requirement for feeling.

<!-- @segment sn12.44:2.4 -->

Feeling is a requirement for craving.

<!-- @segment sn12.44:2.5 -->

Craving is a requirement for grasping.

<!-- @segment sn12.44:2.6 -->

Grasping is a requirement for continued existence.

<!-- @segment sn12.44:2.7 -->

Continued existence is a requirement for rebirth.

<!-- @segment sn12.44:2.8 -->

Rebirth is a requirement for old age and death, sorrow, lamentation, pain, sadness, and distress to come to be.

<!-- @segment sn12.44:2.9 -->

This is the origin of the world.

<!-- @segment sn12.44:3.1 -->

Ear consciousness arises dependent on the ear and sounds. …

<!-- @segment sn12.44:3.2 -->

Nose consciousness arises dependent on the nose and smells. …

<!-- @segment sn12.44:3.3 -->

Tongue consciousness arises dependent on the tongue and tastes. …

<!-- @segment sn12.44:3.4 -->

Body consciousness arises dependent on the body and touches. …

<!-- @segment sn12.44:3.5 -->

Mind consciousness arises dependent on the mind and ideas. The meeting of the three is contact.

<!-- @segment sn12.44:3.6 -->

Contact is a requirement for feeling. …

<!-- @segment sn12.44:3.7 -->

Rebirth is a requirement for old age and death, sorrow, lamentation, pain, sadness, and distress to come to be.

<!-- @segment sn12.44:3.8 -->

This is the origin of the world.

<!-- @segment sn12.44:4.1 -->

And what is the ending of the world?

<!-- @segment sn12.44:4.2 -->

Eye consciousness arises dependent on the eye and sights. The meeting of the three is contact.

<!-- @segment sn12.44:4.3 -->

Contact is a requirement for feeling.

<!-- @segment sn12.44:4.4 -->

Feeling is a requirement for craving.

<!-- @segment sn12.44:4.5 -->

When that craving fades away and ceases with no residue left behind, grasping ceases.

<!-- @segment sn12.44:4.6 -->

When grasping ceases, continued existence ceases. …

<!-- @segment sn12.44:4.7 -->

That is how this entire mass of suffering ceases.

<!-- @segment sn12.44:4.8 -->

This is the ending of the world.

<!-- @segment sn12.44:5.1 -->

Ear consciousness arises dependent on the ear and sounds. …

<!-- @segment sn12.44:5.2 -->

Nose consciousness arises dependent on the nose and smells. …

<!-- @segment sn12.44:5.3 -->

Tongue consciousness arises dependent on the tongue and tastes. …

<!-- @segment sn12.44:5.4 -->

Body consciousness arises dependent on the body and touches. …

<!-- @segment sn12.44:5.5 -->

Mind consciousness arises dependent on the mind and ideas. The meeting of the three is contact.

<!-- @segment sn12.44:5.6 -->

Contact is a requirement for feeling.

<!-- @segment sn12.44:5.7 -->

Feeling is a requirement for craving.

<!-- @segment sn12.44:5.8 -->

When that craving fades away and ceases with no residue left behind, grasping ceases.

<!-- @segment sn12.44:5.9 -->

When grasping ceases, continued existence ceases. …

<!-- @segment sn12.44:5.10 -->

That is how this entire mass of suffering ceases.

<!-- @segment sn12.44:5.11 -->

This is the ending of the world.”
