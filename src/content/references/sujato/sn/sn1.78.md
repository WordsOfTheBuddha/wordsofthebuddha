<!-- @segment sn1.78:1.1 -->

“What should one who wants what’s best <j>not give away?

<!-- @segment sn1.78:1.2 -->

What should a mortal not forsake?

<!-- @segment sn1.78:1.3 -->

What should be released when it’s good,

<!-- @segment sn1.78:1.4 -->

but not when it’s bad?”

<!-- @segment sn1.78:2.1 -->

“A man shouldn’t give away himself.

<!-- @segment sn1.78:2.2 -->

He shouldn’t forsake himself.

<!-- @segment sn1.78:2.3 -->

Speech should be released when it’s good,

<!-- @segment sn1.78:2.4 -->

but not when it’s bad.”
