<!-- @segment sn48.67:1.1 -->

“Mendicants, of all the trees in the Black Plum Tree Land, the black plum is said to be the best.

<!-- @segment sn48.67:1.2 -->

In the same way, the faculty of wisdom is said to be the best of the qualities on the side of awakening; that is, for becoming awakened.

<!-- @segment sn48.67:1.3 -->

And what are the qualities on the side of awakening?

<!-- @segment sn48.67:1.4 -->

The faculties of faith,

<!-- @segment sn48.67:1.5 -->

energy,

<!-- @segment sn48.67:1.6 -->

mindfulness,

<!-- @segment sn48.67:1.7 -->

immersion,

<!-- @segment sn48.67:1.8 -->

and wisdom are qualities on the side of awakening, in that they lead to becoming awakened.

<!-- @segment sn48.67:1.9 -->

Of all the trees in the Black Plum Tree Land, the black plum is said to be the best.

<!-- @segment sn48.67:1.10 -->

In the same way, the faculty of wisdom is said to be the best of the qualities on the side of awakening; that is, for becoming awakened.”
