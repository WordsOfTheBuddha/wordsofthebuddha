<!-- @segment sn31.4-12:1.1 -->

At Sāvatthī.

<!-- @segment sn31.4-12:1.2 -->

Seated to one side, that mendicant said to the Buddha:

<!-- @segment sn31.4-12:1.3 -->

“Sir, what is the cause, what is the reason why someone, when their body breaks up, after death, is reborn in the company of the gods who live in fragrant heartwood …

<!-- @segment sn31.4-12:1.4 -->

softwood …

<!-- @segment sn31.4-12:1.5 -->

bark …

<!-- @segment sn31.4-12:1.6 -->

sprouts …

<!-- @segment sn31.4-12:1.7 -->

leaves …

<!-- @segment sn31.4-12:1.8 -->

flowers …

<!-- @segment sn31.4-12:1.9 -->

fruit …

<!-- @segment sn31.4-12:1.10 -->

sap …

<!-- @segment sn31.4-12:1.11 -->

fragrant scents?”

<!-- @segment sn31.4-12:1.12 -->

“Mendicant, it’s when someone does good things by way of body, speech, and mind.

<!-- @segment sn31.4-12:1.13 -->

And they’ve heard:

<!-- @segment sn31.4-12:1.14 -->

‘The gods who live in fragrant heartwood … fragrant scents are long-lived, beautiful, and very happy.’

<!-- @segment sn31.4-12:1.15 -->

They think:

<!-- @segment sn31.4-12:1.16 -->

‘If only, when my body breaks up, after death, I would be reborn in the company of the gods who live in fragrant heartwood …

<!-- @segment sn31.4-12:1.17 -->



<!-- @segment sn31.4-12:1.18 -->



<!-- @segment sn31.4-12:1.19 -->



<!-- @segment sn31.4-12:1.20 -->



<!-- @segment sn31.4-12:1.21 -->



<!-- @segment sn31.4-12:1.22 -->



<!-- @segment sn31.4-12:1.23 -->



<!-- @segment sn31.4-12:1.24 -->

fragrant scents!’

<!-- @segment sn31.4-12:1.25 -->

They give gifts of fragrant heartwood …

<!-- @segment sn31.4-12:1.26 -->



<!-- @segment sn31.4-12:1.27 -->



<!-- @segment sn31.4-12:1.28 -->



<!-- @segment sn31.4-12:1.29 -->



<!-- @segment sn31.4-12:1.30 -->



<!-- @segment sn31.4-12:1.31 -->



<!-- @segment sn31.4-12:1.32 -->



<!-- @segment sn31.4-12:1.33 -->

fragrant scents.

<!-- @segment sn31.4-12:1.34 -->

When their body breaks up, after death, they’re reborn in the company of the gods who live in fragrant scents.

<!-- @segment sn31.4-12:1.35 -->

This is the cause, this is the reason why someone, when their body breaks up, after death, is reborn in the company of the gods who live on fragrant scents.”
