<!-- @segment sn22.73:1.1 -->

At Sāvatthī.

<!-- @segment sn22.73:1.2 -->

“Mendicants, an unlearned ordinary person doesn’t truly understand the gratification, the drawback, and the escape when it comes to form,

<!-- @segment sn22.73:1.3 -->

feeling,

<!-- @segment sn22.73:1.4 -->

perception,

<!-- @segment sn22.73:1.5 -->

choices,

<!-- @segment sn22.73:1.6 -->

and consciousness.

<!-- @segment sn22.73:1.7 -->

A learned noble disciple does truly understand the gratification, the drawback, and the escape when it comes to form,

<!-- @segment sn22.73:1.8 -->

feeling,

<!-- @segment sn22.73:1.9 -->

perception,

<!-- @segment sn22.73:1.10 -->

choices,

<!-- @segment sn22.73:1.11 -->

and consciousness.”
