title: Unethical

At Sāvatthī.

“Mendicants, sentient beings join together and converge because of an element:

the faithless with the faithless …

shameless …

imprudent …

unethical …

witless …

The faithful with the faithful …

conscientious …

prudent …

ethical …

and the wise with the wise.”
