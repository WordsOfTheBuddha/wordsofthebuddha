<!-- @segment sn14.24:1.1 -->

At Sāvatthī.

<!-- @segment sn14.24:1.2 -->

“Mendicants, sentient beings join together and converge because of an element:

<!-- @segment sn14.24:1.3 -->

the faithless with the faithless …

<!-- @segment sn14.24:1.4 -->

shameless …

<!-- @segment sn14.24:1.5 -->

imprudent …

<!-- @segment sn14.24:1.6 -->

unethical …

<!-- @segment sn14.24:1.7 -->

witless …

<!-- @segment sn14.24:2.1 -->

The faithful with the faithful …

<!-- @segment sn14.24:2.2 -->

conscientious …

<!-- @segment sn14.24:2.3 -->

prudent …

<!-- @segment sn14.24:2.4 -->

ethical …

<!-- @segment sn14.24:2.5 -->

and the wise with the wise.”
