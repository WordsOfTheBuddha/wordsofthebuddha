<!-- @segment sn1.63:1.1 -->

“What leads the world on?

<!-- @segment sn1.63:1.2 -->

What drags it around?

<!-- @segment sn1.63:1.3 -->

What is the one thing

<!-- @segment sn1.63:1.4 -->

that has everything under its sway?”

<!-- @segment sn1.63:2.1 -->

“Craving leads the world on.

<!-- @segment sn1.63:2.2 -->

Craving drags it around.

<!-- @segment sn1.63:2.3 -->

Craving is the one thing

<!-- @segment sn1.63:2.4 -->

that has everything under its sway.”
