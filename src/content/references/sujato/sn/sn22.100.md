<!-- @segment sn22.100:1.1 -->

At Sāvatthī.

<!-- @segment sn22.100:1.2 -->

“Mendicants, this transmigration has no known beginning.

<!-- @segment sn22.100:1.3 -->

No first point is found of sentient beings roaming and transmigrating, shrouded by ignorance and fettered by craving.

<!-- @segment sn22.100:1.4 -->

Suppose a hound on a leash was tethered to a strong post or pillar. Whether walking, standing, sitting, or lying down, it stays right beside that post or pillar.

<!-- @segment sn22.100:1.5 -->

In the same way, an unlearned ordinary person regards form like this: ‘This is mine, I am this, this is my self.’

<!-- @segment sn22.100:1.6 -->

They regard feeling …

<!-- @segment sn22.100:1.7 -->

perception …

<!-- @segment sn22.100:1.8 -->

choices …

<!-- @segment sn22.100:1.9 -->

consciousness like this: ‘This is mine, I am this, this is my self.’

<!-- @segment sn22.100:1.10 -->

When walking, they walk right beside the five grasping aggregates.

<!-- @segment sn22.100:1.11 -->

When standing …

<!-- @segment sn22.100:1.12 -->

sitting …

<!-- @segment sn22.100:1.13 -->

lying down, they lie down right beside the five grasping aggregates.

<!-- @segment sn22.100:1.14 -->

So you should regularly check your own mind:

<!-- @segment sn22.100:1.15 -->

‘For a long time this mind has been corrupted by greed, hate, and delusion.’

<!-- @segment sn22.100:1.16 -->

Sentient beings are corrupted because the mind is corrupted.

<!-- @segment sn22.100:1.17 -->

Sentient beings are purified because the mind is purified.

<!-- @segment sn22.100:2.1 -->

Mendicants, have you seen the painting named ‘Conduct’?”

<!-- @segment sn22.100:2.2 -->

“Yes, sir.”

<!-- @segment sn22.100:2.3 -->

“That painting was devised by the mind, but the mind is even more diverse than that.

<!-- @segment sn22.100:2.4 -->

So you should regularly check your own mind:

<!-- @segment sn22.100:2.5 -->

‘For a long time this mind has been corrupted by greed, hate, and delusion.’

<!-- @segment sn22.100:2.6 -->

Sentient beings are corrupted because the mind is corrupted.

<!-- @segment sn22.100:2.7 -->

Sentient beings are purified because the mind is purified.

<!-- @segment sn22.100:3.1 -->

I don’t see any other order of beings as elaborate as the animal realm.

<!-- @segment sn22.100:3.2 -->

The creatures in the animal realm were devised by the mind, but the mind is even more diverse than that.

<!-- @segment sn22.100:3.3 -->

So you should regularly check your own mind:

<!-- @segment sn22.100:3.4 -->

‘For a long time this mind has been corrupted by greed, hate, and delusion.’

<!-- @segment sn22.100:3.5 -->

Sentient beings are corrupted because the mind is corrupted.

<!-- @segment sn22.100:3.6 -->

Sentient beings are purified because the mind is purified.

<!-- @segment sn22.100:4.1 -->

Suppose an artist or painter had some dye, red lac, turmeric, indigo, or rose madder. And on a polished plank or a wall or a canvas they’d create the form of a woman or a man, whole in its major and minor limbs.

<!-- @segment sn22.100:4.2 -->

In the same way, when an unlearned ordinary person generates a future life, all they generate is form, feeling, perception, choices, and consciousness.

<!-- @segment sn22.100:4.3 -->

What do you think, mendicants?

<!-- @segment sn22.100:4.4 -->

Is form permanent or impermanent?”

<!-- @segment sn22.100:4.5 -->

“Impermanent, sir.”

<!-- @segment sn22.100:4.6 -->

“Is feeling …

<!-- @segment sn22.100:4.7 -->

perception …

<!-- @segment sn22.100:4.8 -->

choices …

<!-- @segment sn22.100:4.9 -->

consciousness permanent or impermanent?” …

<!-- @segment sn22.100:4.10 -->

“So you should truly see …

<!-- @segment sn22.100:4.11 -->

Seeing this …

<!-- @segment sn22.100:4.12 -->

They understand: ‘… there is nothing further for this place.’”
