<!-- @segment sn2.27:1.1 -->

Standing to one side, the godling Nanda spoke this verse in the Buddha’s presence:

<!-- @segment sn2.27:2.1 -->

“Time flies, nights pass by,

<!-- @segment sn2.27:2.2 -->

the stages of life leave us one by one.

<!-- @segment sn2.27:2.3 -->

Seeing this peril in death,

<!-- @segment sn2.27:2.4 -->

do good deeds that bring you joy.”

<!-- @segment sn2.27:3.1 -->

“Time flies, nights pass by,

<!-- @segment sn2.27:3.2 -->

the stages of life leave us one by one.

<!-- @segment sn2.27:3.3 -->

Seeing this peril in death,

<!-- @segment sn2.27:3.4 -->

a seeker of peace would drop the world’s bait.”
