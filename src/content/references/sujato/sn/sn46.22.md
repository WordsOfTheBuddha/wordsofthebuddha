<!-- @segment sn46.22:1.1 -->

“Mendicants, I will teach you the seven awakening factors.

<!-- @segment sn46.22:1.2 -->

Listen …

<!-- @segment sn46.22:1.3 -->

And what are the seven awakening factors?

<!-- @segment sn46.22:1.4 -->

The awakening factors of mindfulness, investigation of principles, energy, rapture, tranquility, immersion, and equanimity.

<!-- @segment sn46.22:1.5 -->

These are the seven awakening factors.”
