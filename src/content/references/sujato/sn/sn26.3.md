<!-- @segment sn26.3:1.1 -->

At Sāvatthī.

<!-- @segment sn26.3:1.2 -->

“Mendicants, the arising of eye consciousness …

<!-- @segment sn26.3:1.3 -->



<!-- @segment sn26.3:1.4 -->

mind consciousness …

<!-- @segment sn26.3:1.5 -->

is the manifestation of old age and death.

<!-- @segment sn26.3:1.6 -->

The cessation of eye consciousness …

<!-- @segment sn26.3:1.7 -->



<!-- @segment sn26.3:1.8 -->

mind consciousness …

<!-- @segment sn26.3:1.9 -->

is the disappearance of old age and death.”
