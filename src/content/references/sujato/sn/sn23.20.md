title: Liable to Fall
---

At Sāvatthī.

Seated to one side, Venerable Rādha said to the Buddha:

“Sir, they speak of a ‘liability to fall’.

What has a liability to fall?”

“Rādha, form, feeling, perception, choices, and consciousness have a liability to fall.

Seeing this …

They understand: ‘… there is nothing further for this place.’”
