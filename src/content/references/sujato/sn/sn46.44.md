<!-- @segment sn46.44:1.1 -->

Then a mendicant went up to the Buddha … and asked him,

<!-- @segment sn46.44:1.2 -->

“Sir, they speak of ‘a witless idiot’.

<!-- @segment sn46.44:1.3 -->

How is a witless idiot defined?”

<!-- @segment sn46.44:1.4 -->

“Mendicant, they’re called a witless idiot because they haven’t developed and cultivated the seven awakening factors.

<!-- @segment sn46.44:1.5 -->

What seven?

<!-- @segment sn46.44:1.6 -->

The awakening factors of mindfulness, investigation of principles, energy, rapture, tranquility, immersion, and equanimity.

<!-- @segment sn46.44:1.7 -->

They’re called a witless idiot because they haven’t developed and cultivated these seven awakening factors.”
