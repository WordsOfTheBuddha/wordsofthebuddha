title: Cut How Many?

<!-- @segment sn1.5:1.1 -->

At Sāvatthī.

<!-- @segment sn1.5:1.2 -->

Standing to one side, that deity spoke this verse in the Buddha’s presence:

<!-- @segment sn1.5:2.1 -->

“Cut how many? Drop how many?

<!-- @segment sn1.5:2.2 -->

Develop how many more?

<!-- @segment sn1.5:2.3 -->

When a mendicant slips how many chains

<!-- @segment sn1.5:2.4 -->

are they said to have crossed the flood?”

<!-- @segment sn1.5:3.1 -->

“Five to cut, five to drop,

<!-- @segment sn1.5:3.2 -->

and five more to develop.

<!-- @segment sn1.5:3.3 -->

When a mendicant slips five chains

<!-- @segment sn1.5:3.4 -->

they’re said to have crossed the flood.”
