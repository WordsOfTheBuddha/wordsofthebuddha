<!-- @segment sn48.1:1.1 -->

At Sāvatthī.

<!-- @segment sn48.1:1.2 -->

There the Buddha said:

<!-- @segment sn48.1:1.3 -->

“Mendicants, there are these five faculties.

<!-- @segment sn48.1:1.4 -->

What five?

<!-- @segment sn48.1:1.5 -->

The faculties of faith, energy, mindfulness, immersion, and wisdom.

<!-- @segment sn48.1:1.6 -->

These are the five faculties.”
