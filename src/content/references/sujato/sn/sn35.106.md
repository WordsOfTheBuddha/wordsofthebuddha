<!-- @segment sn35.106:1.1 -->

“Mendicants, I will teach you the origin and disappearance of suffering.

<!-- @segment sn35.106:1.2 -->

Listen …

<!-- @segment sn35.106:1.3 -->

And what, mendicants, is the origin of suffering?

<!-- @segment sn35.106:1.4 -->

Eye consciousness arises dependent on the eye and sights. The meeting of the three is contact.

<!-- @segment sn35.106:1.5 -->

Contact is a requirement for feeling.

<!-- @segment sn35.106:1.6 -->

Feeling is a requirement for craving.

<!-- @segment sn35.106:1.7 -->

This is the origin of suffering …

<!-- @segment sn35.106:1.8 -->



<!-- @segment sn35.106:1.9 -->



<!-- @segment sn35.106:1.10 -->



<!-- @segment sn35.106:1.11 -->

Mind consciousness arises dependent on the mind and ideas.

<!-- @segment sn35.106:1.12 -->

The meeting of the three is contact.

<!-- @segment sn35.106:1.13 -->

Contact is a requirement for feeling.

<!-- @segment sn35.106:1.14 -->

Feeling is a requirement for craving.

<!-- @segment sn35.106:1.15 -->

This is the origin of suffering.

<!-- @segment sn35.106:2.1 -->

And what is the disappearance of suffering?

<!-- @segment sn35.106:2.2 -->

Eye consciousness arises dependent on the eye and sights. The meeting of the three is contact.

<!-- @segment sn35.106:2.3 -->

Contact is a requirement for feeling.

<!-- @segment sn35.106:2.4 -->

Feeling is a requirement for craving.

<!-- @segment sn35.106:2.5 -->

When that craving fades away and ceases with no residue left behind, grasping ceases.

<!-- @segment sn35.106:2.6 -->

When grasping ceases, continued existence ceases.

<!-- @segment sn35.106:2.7 -->

When continued existence ceases, rebirth ceases.

<!-- @segment sn35.106:2.8 -->

When rebirth ceases, old age and death, sorrow, lamentation, pain, sadness, and distress cease.

<!-- @segment sn35.106:2.9 -->

That is how this entire mass of suffering ceases.

<!-- @segment sn35.106:2.10 -->

This is the disappearance of suffering. …

<!-- @segment sn35.106:2.11 -->



<!-- @segment sn35.106:2.12 -->

Mind consciousness arises dependent on the mind and ideas. The meeting of the three is contact.

<!-- @segment sn35.106:2.13 -->

Contact is a requirement for feeling.

<!-- @segment sn35.106:2.14 -->

Feeling is a requirement for craving.

<!-- @segment sn35.106:2.15 -->

When that craving fades away and ceases with no residue left behind, grasping ceases.

<!-- @segment sn35.106:2.16 -->

When grasping ceases, continued existence ceases.

<!-- @segment sn35.106:2.17 -->

When continued existence ceases, rebirth ceases.

<!-- @segment sn35.106:2.18 -->

When rebirth ceases, old age and death, sorrow, lamentation, pain, sadness, and distress cease.

<!-- @segment sn35.106:2.19 -->

That is how this entire mass of suffering ceases.

<!-- @segment sn35.106:2.20 -->

This is the disappearance of suffering.”
