<!-- @segment sn12.45:1.1 -->

So I have heard.

<!-- @segment sn12.45:1.2 -->

At one time the Buddha was staying at Ñātika in the brick house.

<!-- @segment sn12.45:1.3 -->

Then while the Buddha was in private retreat he spoke this exposition of the teaching:

<!-- @segment sn12.45:2.1 -->

“Eye consciousness arises dependent on the eye and sights. The meeting of the three is contact.

<!-- @segment sn12.45:2.2 -->

Contact is a requirement for feeling.

<!-- @segment sn12.45:2.3 -->

Feeling is a requirement for craving.

<!-- @segment sn12.45:2.4 -->

Craving is a requirement for grasping. …

<!-- @segment sn12.45:2.5 -->

That is how this entire mass of suffering originates.

<!-- @segment sn12.45:3.1 -->

Ear consciousness arises dependent on the ear and sounds. …

<!-- @segment sn12.45:3.2 -->

Nose consciousness arises dependent on the nose and smells. …

<!-- @segment sn12.45:3.3 -->

Tongue consciousness arises dependent on the tongue and tastes. …

<!-- @segment sn12.45:3.4 -->

Body consciousness arises dependent on the body and touches. …

<!-- @segment sn12.45:3.5 -->

Mind consciousness arises dependent on the mind and ideas. The meeting of the three is contact.

<!-- @segment sn12.45:3.6 -->

Contact is a requirement for feeling.

<!-- @segment sn12.45:3.7 -->

Feeling is a requirement for craving.

<!-- @segment sn12.45:3.8 -->

Craving is a requirement for grasping. …

<!-- @segment sn12.45:3.9 -->

That is how this entire mass of suffering originates.

<!-- @segment sn12.45:4.1 -->

Eye consciousness arises dependent on the eye and sights. The meeting of the three is contact.

<!-- @segment sn12.45:4.2 -->

Contact is a requirement for feeling.

<!-- @segment sn12.45:4.3 -->

Feeling is a requirement for craving.

<!-- @segment sn12.45:4.4 -->

When that craving fades away and ceases with no residue left behind, grasping ceases.

<!-- @segment sn12.45:4.5 -->

When grasping ceases, continued existence ceases. …

<!-- @segment sn12.45:4.6 -->

That is how this entire mass of suffering ceases.

<!-- @segment sn12.45:5.1 -->

Ear consciousness arises dependent on the ear and sounds. …

<!-- @segment sn12.45:5.2 -->

Mind consciousness arises dependent on the mind and ideas. The meeting of the three is contact.

<!-- @segment sn12.45:5.3 -->

Contact is a requirement for feeling.

<!-- @segment sn12.45:5.4 -->

Feeling is a requirement for craving.

<!-- @segment sn12.45:5.5 -->

When that craving fades away and ceases with no residue left behind, grasping ceases.

<!-- @segment sn12.45:5.6 -->

When grasping ceases, continued existence ceases. …

<!-- @segment sn12.45:5.7 -->

That is how this entire mass of suffering ceases.”

<!-- @segment sn12.45:6.1 -->

Now at that time a certain monk was standing listening in on the Buddha.

<!-- @segment sn12.45:6.2 -->

The Buddha saw him

<!-- @segment sn12.45:6.3 -->

and said,

<!-- @segment sn12.45:6.4 -->

“Monk, did you hear that exposition of the teaching?”

<!-- @segment sn12.45:6.5 -->

“Yes, sir.”

<!-- @segment sn12.45:6.6 -->

“Learn that exposition of the teaching,

<!-- @segment sn12.45:6.7 -->

memorize it,

<!-- @segment sn12.45:6.8 -->

and remember it.

<!-- @segment sn12.45:6.9 -->

That exposition of the teaching is beneficial and relevant to the fundamentals of the spiritual life.”
