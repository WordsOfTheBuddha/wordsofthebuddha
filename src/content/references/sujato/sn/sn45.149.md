<!-- @segment sn45.149:1.1 -->

At Sāvatthī.

<!-- @segment sn45.149:1.2 -->

“Mendicants, all the hard work that gets done depends on the earth and is grounded on the earth.

<!-- @segment sn45.149:1.3 -->

In the same way, a mendicant develops and cultivates the noble eightfold path depending on and grounded on ethics.

<!-- @segment sn45.149:1.4 -->

And how does a mendicant grounded on ethics develop and cultivate the noble eightfold path?

<!-- @segment sn45.149:1.5 -->

It’s when a mendicant develops right view, right purpose, right speech, right action, right livelihood, right effort, right mindfulness, and right immersion, which rely on seclusion, fading away, and cessation, and ripen as letting go.

<!-- @segment sn45.149:1.6 -->

That’s how a mendicant grounded on ethics develops and cultivates the noble eightfold path.”
