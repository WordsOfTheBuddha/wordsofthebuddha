title: Contact

<!-- @segment sn27.4:1.1 -->

At Sāvatthī.

<!-- @segment sn27.4:1.2 -->

“Mendicants, desire and greed for eye contact,

<!-- @segment sn27.4:1.3 -->

ear contact,

<!-- @segment sn27.4:1.4 -->

nose contact,

<!-- @segment sn27.4:1.5 -->

tongue contact,

<!-- @segment sn27.4:1.6 -->

body contact,

<!-- @segment sn27.4:1.7 -->

or mind contact is a corruption of the mind. …”
