<!-- @segment sn22.34:1.1 -->

At Sāvatthī.

<!-- @segment sn22.34:1.2 -->

“Mendicants, give up what’s not yours.

<!-- @segment sn22.34:1.3 -->

Giving it up will be for your welfare and happiness.

<!-- @segment sn22.34:1.4 -->

And what isn’t yours?

<!-- @segment sn22.34:1.5 -->

Form isn’t yours: give it up.

<!-- @segment sn22.34:1.6 -->

Giving it up will be for your welfare and happiness.

<!-- @segment sn22.34:1.7 -->

Feeling …

<!-- @segment sn22.34:1.8 -->

Perception …

<!-- @segment sn22.34:1.9 -->

Choices …

<!-- @segment sn22.34:1.10 -->

Consciousness isn’t yours: give it up.

<!-- @segment sn22.34:1.11 -->

Giving it up will be for your welfare and happiness.

<!-- @segment sn22.34:1.12 -->

Give up what’s not yours.

<!-- @segment sn22.34:1.13 -->

Giving it up will be for your welfare and happiness.”
