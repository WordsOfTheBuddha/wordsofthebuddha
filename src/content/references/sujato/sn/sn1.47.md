<!-- @segment sn1.47:1.1 -->

“Whose merit always grows

<!-- @segment sn1.47:1.2 -->

by day and by night?

<!-- @segment sn1.47:1.3 -->

What people—firm in principle, <j>accomplished in conduct—

<!-- @segment sn1.47:1.4 -->

are going to heaven?”

<!-- @segment sn1.47:2.1 -->

“Planters of parks or groves,

<!-- @segment sn1.47:2.2 -->

those who build a bridge,

<!-- @segment sn1.47:2.3 -->

a drinking place and well,

<!-- @segment sn1.47:2.4 -->

and those who give monastic quarters.

<!-- @segment sn1.47:3.1 -->

Their merit always grows

<!-- @segment sn1.47:3.2 -->

by day and by night.

<!-- @segment sn1.47:3.3 -->

Those people—firm in principle, <j>accomplished in conduct—

<!-- @segment sn1.47:3.4 -->

are going to heaven.”
