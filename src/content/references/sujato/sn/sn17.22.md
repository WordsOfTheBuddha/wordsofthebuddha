<!-- @segment sn17.22:1.1 -->

At Sāvatthī.

<!-- @segment sn17.22:1.2 -->

“Possessions, honor, and popularity are grim …

<!-- @segment sn17.22:1.3 -->

Even if you’re alone with the finest lady in the land she might not occupy your mind, but possessions, honor and popularity would still occupy your mind.

<!-- @segment sn17.22:1.4 -->

So grim are possessions, honor, and popularity. …”
