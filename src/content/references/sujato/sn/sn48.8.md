<!-- @segment sn48.8:1.1 -->

“Mendicants, there are these five faculties.

<!-- @segment sn48.8:1.2 -->

What five?

<!-- @segment sn48.8:1.3 -->

The faculties of faith, energy, mindfulness, immersion, and wisdom.

<!-- @segment sn48.8:1.4 -->

And where should the faculty of faith be seen?

<!-- @segment sn48.8:1.5 -->

In the four factors of stream-entry.

<!-- @segment sn48.8:1.6 -->



<!-- @segment sn48.8:1.7 -->

And where should the faculty of energy be seen?

<!-- @segment sn48.8:1.8 -->

In the four right efforts.

<!-- @segment sn48.8:1.9 -->



<!-- @segment sn48.8:1.10 -->

And where should the faculty of mindfulness be seen?

<!-- @segment sn48.8:1.11 -->

In the four kinds of mindfulness meditation.

<!-- @segment sn48.8:1.12 -->



<!-- @segment sn48.8:1.13 -->

And where should the faculty of immersion be seen?

<!-- @segment sn48.8:1.14 -->

In the four absorptions.

<!-- @segment sn48.8:1.15 -->



<!-- @segment sn48.8:1.16 -->

And where should the faculty of wisdom be seen?

<!-- @segment sn48.8:1.17 -->

In the four noble truths.

<!-- @segment sn48.8:1.18 -->



<!-- @segment sn48.8:1.19 -->

These are the five faculties.”
