<!-- @segment sn1.15:1.1 -->

“In the still of high noon,

<!-- @segment sn1.15:1.2 -->

when the birds have settled down,

<!-- @segment sn1.15:1.3 -->

the mighty forest itself resounds—

<!-- @segment sn1.15:1.4 -->

that strikes me as so scary!”

<!-- @segment sn1.15:2.1 -->

“In the still of high noon,

<!-- @segment sn1.15:2.2 -->

when the birds have settled down,

<!-- @segment sn1.15:2.3 -->

the mighty forest itself resounds—

<!-- @segment sn1.15:2.4 -->

that strikes me as so delightful!”
