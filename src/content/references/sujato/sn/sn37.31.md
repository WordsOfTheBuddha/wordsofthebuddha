<!-- @segment sn37.31:1.1 -->

“Mendicants, there are these five powers of a lady.

<!-- @segment sn37.31:1.2 -->

What five?

<!-- @segment sn37.31:1.3 -->

Attractiveness, wealth, relatives, children, and ethical behavior.

<!-- @segment sn37.31:1.4 -->

It is not because of the powers of attractiveness, wealth, relatives, or children that ladies, when their body breaks up, after death, are reborn in a good place, a heavenly realm.

<!-- @segment sn37.31:1.5 -->

It is because of the power of ethical behavior that ladies, when their body breaks up, after death, are reborn in a good place, a heavenly realm.

<!-- @segment sn37.31:1.6 -->

These are the five powers of a lady.”
