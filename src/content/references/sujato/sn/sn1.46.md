<!-- @segment sn1.46:1.1 -->

“It’s resounding with a group of nymphs,

<!-- @segment sn1.46:1.2 -->

but haunted by a gang of goblins!

<!-- @segment sn1.46:1.3 -->

This grove is called ‘Deluding’.

<!-- @segment sn1.46:1.4 -->

How will I keep going?”

<!-- @segment sn1.46:2.1 -->

“That path is called ‘the direct way’,

<!-- @segment sn1.46:2.2 -->

and it’s headed for the place called ‘fearless’.

<!-- @segment sn1.46:2.3 -->

The chariot is called ‘unswerving’,

<!-- @segment sn1.46:2.4 -->

fitted with wheels of the teaching.

<!-- @segment sn1.46:3.1 -->

Conscience is its bench-back,

<!-- @segment sn1.46:3.2 -->

mindfulness its upholstery.

<!-- @segment sn1.46:3.3 -->

I say the teaching is the driver,

<!-- @segment sn1.46:3.4 -->

with right view running out in front.

<!-- @segment sn1.46:4.1 -->

Any woman or man

<!-- @segment sn1.46:4.2 -->

who has such a vehicle,

<!-- @segment sn1.46:4.3 -->

by means of this vehicle

<!-- @segment sn1.46:4.4 -->

has drawn near to extinguishment.”
