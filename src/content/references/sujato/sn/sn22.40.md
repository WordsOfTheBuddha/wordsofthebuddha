<!-- @segment sn22.40:1.1 -->

At Sāvatthī.

<!-- @segment sn22.40:1.2 -->

“Mendicants, when a mendicant is practicing in line with the teaching, this is what’s in line with the teaching. They should meditate observing impermanence in form, feeling, perception, choices, and consciousness. …

<!-- @segment sn22.40:1.3 -->

They’re freed from suffering, I say.”
