<!-- @segment sn45.15:1.1 -->

At Sāvatthī.

<!-- @segment sn45.15:1.2 -->

“Mendicants, these eight things don’t arise to be developed and cultivated apart from the Holy One’s training.

<!-- @segment sn45.15:1.3 -->

What eight?

<!-- @segment sn45.15:1.4 -->

They are: right view, right purpose, right speech, right action, right livelihood, right effort, right mindfulness, and right immersion.

<!-- @segment sn45.15:1.5 -->

These are the eight things that don’t arise to be developed and cultivated apart from the Holy One’s training.”
