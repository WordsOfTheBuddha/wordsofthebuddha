<!-- @segment sn35.102:1.1 -->

“Mendicants, give up what’s not yours.

<!-- @segment sn35.102:1.2 -->

Giving it up will be for your welfare and happiness.

<!-- @segment sn35.102:1.3 -->

And what isn’t yours?

<!-- @segment sn35.102:1.4 -->

The eye isn’t yours: give it up.

<!-- @segment sn35.102:1.5 -->



<!-- @segment sn35.102:1.6 -->

Giving it up will be for your welfare and happiness.

<!-- @segment sn35.102:1.7 -->

Sights …

<!-- @segment sn35.102:1.8 -->



<!-- @segment sn35.102:1.9 -->



<!-- @segment sn35.102:1.10 -->

Eye consciousness …

<!-- @segment sn35.102:1.11 -->



<!-- @segment sn35.102:1.12 -->



<!-- @segment sn35.102:1.13 -->

Eye contact …

<!-- @segment sn35.102:1.14 -->



<!-- @segment sn35.102:1.15 -->



<!-- @segment sn35.102:1.16 -->

The pleasant, painful, or neutral feeling that arises dependent on mind contact isn’t yours: give it up.

<!-- @segment sn35.102:1.17 -->



<!-- @segment sn35.102:1.18 -->

Giving it up will be for your welfare and happiness.

<!-- @segment sn35.102:1.19 -->

Give up what’s not yours.

<!-- @segment sn35.102:1.20 -->

Giving it up will be for your welfare and happiness.”
