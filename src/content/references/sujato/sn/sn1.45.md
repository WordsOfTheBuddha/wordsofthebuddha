<!-- @segment sn1.45:1.1 -->

“Behold him of peerless name <j>who sees the subtle meaning,

<!-- @segment sn1.45:1.2 -->

giver of wisdom, <j>unattached to the realm of sensuality:

<!-- @segment sn1.45:1.3 -->

the all-knower, so very intelligent,

<!-- @segment sn1.45:1.4 -->

the great seer treading the noble road.”
