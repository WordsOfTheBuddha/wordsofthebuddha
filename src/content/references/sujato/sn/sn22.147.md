<!-- @segment sn22.147:1.1 -->

At Sāvatthī.

<!-- @segment sn22.147:1.2 -->

“Mendicants, when a gentleman has gone forth out of faith, this is what’s in line with the teaching.

<!-- @segment sn22.147:1.3 -->

They should meditate observing impermanence in form,

<!-- @segment sn22.147:1.4 -->

feeling,

<!-- @segment sn22.147:1.5 -->

perception,

<!-- @segment sn22.147:1.6 -->

choices,

<!-- @segment sn22.147:1.7 -->

and consciousness. …

<!-- @segment sn22.147:1.8 -->

They’re freed from suffering, I say.”
