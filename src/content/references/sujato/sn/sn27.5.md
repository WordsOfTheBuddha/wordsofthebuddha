title: Feeling
description: The Buddha teaches that desire and greed for feelings born of eye, ear, nose, tongue, body, and mind contact is a corruption of the mind that must be abandoned so the mind inclines to renunciation and becomes fit for realizing anything attainable by insight.
qualities: greed, desire, craving

<!-- @segment sn27.5:1.1 -->

At Sāvatthī.

<!-- @segment sn27.5:1.2 -->

“Mendicants, desire and greed for feeling born of eye contact,

<!-- @segment sn27.5:1.3 -->

feeling born of ear contact,

<!-- @segment sn27.5:1.4 -->

feeling born of nose contact,

<!-- @segment sn27.5:1.5 -->

feeling born of tongue contact,

<!-- @segment sn27.5:1.6 -->

feeling born of body contact,

<!-- @segment sn27.5:1.7 -->

or feeling born of mind contact is a defilement of the mind. …”
