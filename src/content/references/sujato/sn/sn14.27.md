<!-- @segment sn14.27:1.1 -->

At Sāvatthī.

<!-- @segment sn14.27:1.2 -->

“Mendicants, sentient beings join together and converge because of an element:

<!-- @segment sn14.27:1.3 -->

those who kill living creatures with those who kill living creatures,

<!-- @segment sn14.27:1.4 -->

those who steal …

<!-- @segment sn14.27:1.5 -->

commit sexual misconduct …

<!-- @segment sn14.27:1.6 -->

lie …

<!-- @segment sn14.27:1.7 -->

backbite …

<!-- @segment sn14.27:1.8 -->

speak harshly …

<!-- @segment sn14.27:1.9 -->

talk nonsense …

<!-- @segment sn14.27:1.10 -->

are covetous …

<!-- @segment sn14.27:1.11 -->

are malicious …

<!-- @segment sn14.27:1.12 -->

have wrong view …

<!-- @segment sn14.27:2.1 -->

Those who refrain from killing living creatures …

<!-- @segment sn14.27:2.2 -->

who refrain from stealing …

<!-- @segment sn14.27:2.3 -->

who refrain from sexual misconduct …

<!-- @segment sn14.27:2.4 -->

who refrain from lying …

<!-- @segment sn14.27:2.5 -->

who refrain from backbiting …

<!-- @segment sn14.27:2.6 -->

who refrain from harsh speech …

<!-- @segment sn14.27:2.7 -->

who refrain from talking nonsense …

<!-- @segment sn14.27:2.8 -->

are not covetous …

<!-- @segment sn14.27:2.9 -->

are not malicious …

<!-- @segment sn14.27:2.10 -->

have right view with those who have right view.”
