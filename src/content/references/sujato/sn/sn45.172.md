<!-- @segment sn45.172:1.1 -->

“Mendicants, there are these four yokes.

<!-- @segment sn45.172:1.2 -->

What four?

<!-- @segment sn45.172:1.3 -->

The yokes of sensual pleasures, future lives, views, and ignorance.

<!-- @segment sn45.172:1.4 -->

These are the four yokes.

<!-- @segment sn45.172:1.5 -->

The noble eightfold path should be developed for the direct knowledge, complete understanding, finishing, and giving up of these four yokes.”
