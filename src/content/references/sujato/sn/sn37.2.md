<!-- @segment sn37.2:1.1 -->

“Mendicants, when a man has five factors he is extremely undesirable to a lady.

<!-- @segment sn37.2:1.2 -->

What five?

<!-- @segment sn37.2:1.3 -->

He’s not attractive, wealthy, or ethical; he’s idle, and he doesn’t beget children.

<!-- @segment sn37.2:1.4 -->

When a man has these five factors he is extremely undesirable to a lady.

<!-- @segment sn37.2:1.5 -->

When a man has five factors he is extremely desirable to a lady.

<!-- @segment sn37.2:1.6 -->

What five?

<!-- @segment sn37.2:1.7 -->

He’s attractive, wealthy, and ethical; he’s deft and tireless, and he begets children.

<!-- @segment sn37.2:1.8 -->

When a man has these five factors he is extremely desirable to a lady.”
