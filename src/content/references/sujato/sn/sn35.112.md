<!-- @segment sn35.112:1.1 -->

“Mendicants, without directly knowing and completely understanding sights …

<!-- @segment sn35.112:1.2 -->

sounds …

<!-- @segment sn35.112:1.3 -->

smells …

<!-- @segment sn35.112:1.4 -->

tastes …

<!-- @segment sn35.112:1.5 -->

touches …

<!-- @segment sn35.112:1.6 -->

ideas, without dispassion for them and giving them up, you can’t end suffering.

<!-- @segment sn35.112:1.7 -->

By directly knowing and completely understanding sights …

<!-- @segment sn35.112:1.8 -->

sounds …

<!-- @segment sn35.112:1.9 -->

smells …

<!-- @segment sn35.112:1.10 -->

tastes …

<!-- @segment sn35.112:1.11 -->

touches …

<!-- @segment sn35.112:1.12 -->

ideas, having dispassion for them and giving them up, you can end suffering.”
