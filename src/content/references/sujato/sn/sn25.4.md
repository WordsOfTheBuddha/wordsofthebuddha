<!-- @segment sn25.4:1.1 -->

At Sāvatthī.

<!-- @segment sn25.4:1.2 -->

“Mendicants, eye contact is impermanent, decaying, and perishing.

<!-- @segment sn25.4:1.3 -->

Ear contact,

<!-- @segment sn25.4:1.4 -->

nose contact,

<!-- @segment sn25.4:1.5 -->

tongue contact,

<!-- @segment sn25.4:1.6 -->

body contact,

<!-- @segment sn25.4:1.7 -->

and mind contact are impermanent, decaying, and perishing.

<!-- @segment sn25.4:1.8 -->

Someone who has faith and confidence in these teachings is called a follower by faith. …”

<!-- @segment sn25.4:1.9 -->
