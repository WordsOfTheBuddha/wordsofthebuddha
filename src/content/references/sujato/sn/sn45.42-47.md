<!-- @segment sn45.42-47:1.1 -->

“Mendicants, if wanderers who follow another religion were to ask you:

<!-- @segment sn45.42-47:1.2 -->

‘Reverends, what’s the goal of leading the spiritual life under the ascetic Gotama?’ You should answer them like this:

<!-- @segment sn45.42-47:1.3 -->

‘The goal of leading the spiritual life under the Buddha is to give up the fetters.’ …

<!-- @segment sn45.42-47:1.4 -->

‘… to uproot the underlying tendencies.’ …

<!-- @segment sn45.42-47:1.5 -->

‘… to completely understand the course of time.’ …

<!-- @segment sn45.42-47:1.6 -->

‘… to end the defilements.’ …

<!-- @segment sn45.42-47:1.7 -->

‘… to realize the fruit of knowledge and freedom.’ …

<!-- @segment sn45.42-47:1.8 -->

‘… for knowledge and vision.’ …”
