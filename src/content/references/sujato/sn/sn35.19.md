<!-- @segment sn35.19:1.1 -->

“Mendicants, if you take pleasure in the eye, you take pleasure in suffering.

<!-- @segment sn35.19:1.2 -->

If you take pleasure in suffering, you’re not free from suffering, I say.

<!-- @segment sn35.19:1.3 -->

If you take pleasure in the ear …

<!-- @segment sn35.19:1.4 -->

nose …

<!-- @segment sn35.19:1.5 -->

tongue …

<!-- @segment sn35.19:1.6 -->



<!-- @segment sn35.19:1.7 -->

body …

<!-- @segment sn35.19:1.8 -->

mind, you take pleasure in suffering.

<!-- @segment sn35.19:1.9 -->

If you take pleasure in suffering, you’re not free from suffering, I say.

<!-- @segment sn35.19:2.1 -->

If you don’t take pleasure in the eye, you don’t take pleasure in suffering.

<!-- @segment sn35.19:2.2 -->

If you don’t take pleasure in suffering, you’re free from suffering, I say.

<!-- @segment sn35.19:2.3 -->

If you don’t take pleasure in the ear …

<!-- @segment sn35.19:2.4 -->

nose …

<!-- @segment sn35.19:2.5 -->

tongue …

<!-- @segment sn35.19:2.6 -->



<!-- @segment sn35.19:2.7 -->

body …

<!-- @segment sn35.19:2.8 -->

mind, you don’t take pleasure in suffering.

<!-- @segment sn35.19:2.9 -->

If you don’t take pleasure in suffering, you’re free from suffering, I say.”
