title: Things That Tighten Fetters

<!-- @segment sn35.109:1.1 -->

“Mendicants, I will teach you the things that tighten the fetters, and the fetter.

<!-- @segment sn35.109:1.2 -->

Listen …

<!-- @segment sn35.109:1.3 -->

What are the things that tighten the fetters? And what is the fetter?

<!-- @segment sn35.109:1.4 -->

The eye is something that tightens the fetters.

<!-- @segment sn35.109:1.5 -->

The desire and greed for it is the fetter.

<!-- @segment sn35.109:1.6 -->

The ear … nose … tongue … body …

<!-- @segment sn35.109:1.7 -->

mind is something that tightens the fetters.

<!-- @segment sn35.109:1.8 -->

The desire and greed for it is the fetter.

<!-- @segment sn35.109:1.9 -->

These are called the things that tighten the fetters, and this is the fetter.”
