<!-- @segment sn1.27:1.1 -->

“From where do streams recoil?

<!-- @segment sn1.27:1.2 -->

Where does the cycle spin no more?

<!-- @segment sn1.27:1.3 -->

Where does name and form

<!-- @segment sn1.27:1.4 -->

cease with no residue left behind?”

<!-- @segment sn1.27:2.1 -->

“Where water and earth,

<!-- @segment sn1.27:2.2 -->

fire and air have no footing—

<!-- @segment sn1.27:2.3 -->

from here the streams recoil;

<!-- @segment sn1.27:2.4 -->

in reference to this, the cycle spins no more;

<!-- @segment sn1.27:2.5 -->

in reference to this, name and form

<!-- @segment sn1.27:2.6 -->

ceases with no residue left behind.”
