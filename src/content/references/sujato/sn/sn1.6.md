<!-- @segment sn1.6:1.1 -->

At Sāvatthī.

<!-- @segment sn1.6:1.2 -->

Standing to one side, that deity spoke this verse in the Buddha’s presence:

<!-- @segment sn1.6:2.1 -->

“How many sleep among the waking?

<!-- @segment sn1.6:2.2 -->

How many wake among the sleeping?

<!-- @segment sn1.6:2.3 -->

By how many do you gather dust?

<!-- @segment sn1.6:2.4 -->

By how many are you cleansed?”

<!-- @segment sn1.6:3.1 -->

“Five sleep among the waking.

<!-- @segment sn1.6:3.2 -->

Five wake among the sleeping.

<!-- @segment sn1.6:3.3 -->

By five you gather dust.

<!-- @segment sn1.6:3.4 -->

By five you’re cleansed.”
