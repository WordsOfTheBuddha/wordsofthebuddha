<!-- @segment sn22.23:1.1 -->

At Sāvatthī.

<!-- @segment sn22.23:1.2 -->

“Mendicants, I will teach you the things that should be completely understood, and complete understanding.

<!-- @segment sn22.23:1.3 -->

Listen …

<!-- @segment sn22.23:1.4 -->

And what things should be completely understood?

<!-- @segment sn22.23:1.5 -->

Form, feeling, perception, choices, and consciousness.

<!-- @segment sn22.23:1.6 -->

These are called the things that should be completely understood.

<!-- @segment sn22.23:1.7 -->

And what is complete understanding?

<!-- @segment sn22.23:1.8 -->

The ending of greed, hate, and delusion.

<!-- @segment sn22.23:1.9 -->

This is called complete understanding.”
