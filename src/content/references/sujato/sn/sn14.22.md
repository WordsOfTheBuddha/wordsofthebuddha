<!-- @segment sn14.22:1.1 -->

At Sāvatthī.

<!-- @segment sn14.22:1.2 -->

“Mendicants, sentient beings join together and converge because of an element:

<!-- @segment sn14.22:1.3 -->

the lazy with the lazy …

<!-- @segment sn14.22:1.4 -->

unmindful …

<!-- @segment sn14.22:1.5 -->

witless …

<!-- @segment sn14.22:1.6 -->

energetic …

<!-- @segment sn14.22:1.7 -->

mindful … wise …”

<!-- @segment sn14.22:1.8 -->



<!-- @segment sn14.22:1.9 -->

(Tell all in full for the past, future, and present.)
