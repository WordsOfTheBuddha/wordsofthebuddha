<!-- @segment sn45.167:1.1 -->

“Mendicants, there are these three stains.

<!-- @segment sn45.167:1.2 -->

What three?

<!-- @segment sn45.167:1.3 -->

Greed, hate, and delusion.

<!-- @segment sn45.167:1.4 -->

These are the three stains.

<!-- @segment sn45.167:1.5 -->

The noble eightfold path should be developed for the direct knowledge, complete understanding, finishing, and giving up of these three stains.”
