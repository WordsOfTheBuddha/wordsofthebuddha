title: Exterior and Cause Are Not-Self

<!-- @segment sn35.145:1.1 -->

“Mendicants, sights are not-self.

<!-- @segment sn35.145:1.2 -->

The cause and reason that gives rise to sights is also not-self.

<!-- @segment sn35.145:1.3 -->

Since sights are produced by what is not-self, how could they be self?

<!-- @segment sn35.145:1.4 -->

Sounds …

<!-- @segment sn35.145:1.5 -->

Smells …

<!-- @segment sn35.145:1.6 -->

Tastes …

<!-- @segment sn35.145:1.7 -->

Touches …

<!-- @segment sn35.145:1.8 -->

Ideas are not-self.

<!-- @segment sn35.145:1.9 -->

The cause and reason that gives rise to ideas is also not-self.

<!-- @segment sn35.145:1.10 -->

Since ideas are produced by what is not-self, how could they be self?

<!-- @segment sn35.145:1.11 -->

Seeing this …

<!-- @segment sn35.145:1.12 -->

Being disillusioned, desire fades away. When desire fades away they’re freed. When they’re freed, they know they’re freed.

<!-- @segment sn35.145:1.13 -->

They understand: ‘Rebirth is ended, the spiritual journey has been completed, what had to be done has been done, there is nothing further for this place.’”
