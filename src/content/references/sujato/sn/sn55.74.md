<!-- @segment sn55.74:1.1 -->

“Mendicants, when four things are developed and cultivated they lead to penetrating wisdom.

<!-- @segment sn55.74:1.2 -->

What four?

<!-- @segment sn55.74:1.3 -->

Associating with true persons, listening to the true teaching, rational application of mind, and practicing in line with the teaching.

<!-- @segment sn55.74:1.4 -->

When these four things are developed and cultivated they lead to penetrating wisdom.”
