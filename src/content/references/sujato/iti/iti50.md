<!-- @segment iti50:1.1 -->

This was said by the Buddha, the Perfected One: that is what I heard.

<!-- @segment iti50:2.1 -->

“Mendicants, there are these three unskillful roots.

<!-- @segment iti50:2.2 -->

What three?

<!-- @segment iti50:2.3 -->

Greed, hate, and delusion.

<!-- @segment iti50:2.4 -->

These are the three unskillful roots.”

<!-- @segment iti50:2.5 -->

The Buddha spoke this matter.

<!-- @segment iti50:2.6 -->

On this it is said:

<!-- @segment iti50:3.1 -->

“When greed, hate, and delusion,

<!-- @segment iti50:3.2 -->

have arisen inside oneself,

<!-- @segment iti50:3.3 -->

they harm a person of wicked heart,

<!-- @segment iti50:3.4 -->

as a bamboo is destroyed by its own fruit.”

<!-- @segment iti50:4.1 -->

This too is a matter that was spoken by the Blessed One: that is what I heard.
