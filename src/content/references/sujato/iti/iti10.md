<!-- @segment iti10:1.1 -->

This was said by the Buddha, the Perfected One: that is what I heard.

<!-- @segment iti10:2.1 -->

“Mendicants, without directly knowing and completely understanding hate, without dispassion for it and giving it up, you can’t end suffering.

<!-- @segment iti10:2.2 -->

By directly knowing and completely understanding hate, having dispassion for it and giving it up, you can end suffering.”

<!-- @segment iti10:2.3 -->

The Buddha spoke this matter.

<!-- @segment iti10:2.4 -->

On this it is said:

<!-- @segment iti10:3.1 -->

“When overcome by hate

<!-- @segment iti10:3.2 -->

beings go to a bad place.

<!-- @segment iti10:3.3 -->

Having rightly understood that hate,

<!-- @segment iti10:3.4 -->

the discerning give it up.

<!-- @segment iti10:3.5 -->

Once they’ve given it up,

<!-- @segment iti10:3.6 -->

they never return to this world.”

<!-- @segment iti10:4.1 -->

This too is a matter that was spoken by the Blessed One: that is what I heard.
