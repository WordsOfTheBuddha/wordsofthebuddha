<!-- @segment iti61:1.1 -->

This was said by the Buddha, the Perfected One: that is what I heard.

<!-- @segment iti61:2.1 -->

“Mendicants, there are these three eyes.

<!-- @segment iti61:2.2 -->

What three?

<!-- @segment iti61:2.3 -->

The eye of the flesh, the eye of clairvoyance, and the eye of wisdom.

<!-- @segment iti61:2.4 -->

These are the three eyes.”

<!-- @segment iti61:2.5 -->

The Buddha spoke this matter.

<!-- @segment iti61:2.6 -->

On this it is said:

<!-- @segment iti61:3.1 -->

“The eye of flesh, the clairvoyant eye,

<!-- @segment iti61:3.2 -->

the supreme eye of wisdom:

<!-- @segment iti61:3.3 -->

these three eyes

<!-- @segment iti61:3.4 -->

were taught by the supreme person.

<!-- @segment iti61:4.1 -->

The arising of the eye of flesh

<!-- @segment iti61:4.2 -->

is the path to the clairvoyant eye.

<!-- @segment iti61:4.3 -->

When knowledge arises—

<!-- @segment iti61:4.4 -->

the supreme eye of wisdom—

<!-- @segment iti61:4.5 -->

one who gains that eye

<!-- @segment iti61:4.6 -->

is released from all suffering.”

<!-- @segment iti61:5.1 -->

This too is a matter that was spoken by the Blessed One: that is what I heard.
