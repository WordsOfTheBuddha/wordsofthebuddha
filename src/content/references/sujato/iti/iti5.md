<!-- @segment iti5:1.1 -->

This was said by the Buddha, the Perfected One: that is what I heard.

<!-- @segment iti5:2.1 -->

“Mendicants, give up one thing.

<!-- @segment iti5:2.2 -->

and I guarantee you non-return.

<!-- @segment iti5:2.3 -->

What one thing?

<!-- @segment iti5:2.4 -->

Disdain is the one thing. Give it up,

<!-- @segment iti5:2.5 -->

and I guarantee you non-return.”

<!-- @segment iti5:2.6 -->

The Buddha spoke this matter.

<!-- @segment iti5:2.7 -->

On this it is said:

<!-- @segment iti5:3.1 -->

“When overcome by disdain

<!-- @segment iti5:3.2 -->

beings go to a bad place.

<!-- @segment iti5:3.3 -->

Having rightly understood that disdain,

<!-- @segment iti5:3.4 -->

the discerning give it up.

<!-- @segment iti5:3.5 -->

Once they’ve given it up,

<!-- @segment iti5:3.6 -->

they never return to this world.”

<!-- @segment iti5:4.1 -->

This too is a matter that was spoken by the Blessed One: that is what I heard.
