<!-- @segment iti51:1.1 -->

This was said by the Buddha, the Perfected One: that is what I heard.

<!-- @segment iti51:2.1 -->

“Mendicants, there are these three elements.

<!-- @segment iti51:2.2 -->

What three?

<!-- @segment iti51:2.3 -->

The elements of form, formlessness, and cessation.

<!-- @segment iti51:2.4 -->

These are the three elements.”

<!-- @segment iti51:2.5 -->

The Buddha spoke this matter.

<!-- @segment iti51:2.6 -->

On this it is said:

<!-- @segment iti51:3.1 -->

“Comprehending the element of form,

<!-- @segment iti51:3.2 -->

not stuck in the formless,

<!-- @segment iti51:3.3 -->

those who are released in cessation,

<!-- @segment iti51:3.4 -->

they are conquerors of death.

<!-- @segment iti51:4.1 -->

Having directly experienced <j>the element free of death,

<!-- @segment iti51:4.2 -->

free of attachments;

<!-- @segment iti51:4.3 -->

having realized relinquishment

<!-- @segment iti51:4.4 -->

of attachments, the undefiled

<!-- @segment iti51:4.5 -->

fully awakened Buddha teaches

<!-- @segment iti51:4.6 -->

the sorrowless, stainless state.”

<!-- @segment iti51:5.1 -->

This too is a matter that was spoken by the Blessed One: that is what I heard.
