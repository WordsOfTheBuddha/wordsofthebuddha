<!-- @segment iti64:1.1 -->

This was said by the Buddha, the Perfected One: that is what I heard.

<!-- @segment iti64:2.1 -->

“Mendicants, there are these three kinds of bad conduct.

<!-- @segment iti64:2.2 -->

What three?

<!-- @segment iti64:2.3 -->

Bad conduct by way of body, speech, and mind.

<!-- @segment iti64:2.4 -->

These are the three kinds of bad conduct.”

<!-- @segment iti64:2.5 -->

The Buddha spoke this matter.

<!-- @segment iti64:2.6 -->

On this it is said:

<!-- @segment iti64:3.1 -->

“Having done bad things

<!-- @segment iti64:3.2 -->

by way of body,

<!-- @segment iti64:3.3 -->

speech, and mind,

<!-- @segment iti64:3.4 -->

and whatever else is corrupt;

<!-- @segment iti64:4.1 -->

not having done good deeds,

<!-- @segment iti64:4.2 -->

and having done many bad,

<!-- @segment iti64:4.3 -->

when their body breaks up, that witless person

<!-- @segment iti64:4.4 -->

is reborn in hell.”

<!-- @segment iti64:5.1 -->

This too is a matter that was spoken by the Blessed One: that is what I heard.
