<!-- @segment iti33:1.1 -->

This was said by the Buddha, the Perfected One: that is what I heard.

<!-- @segment iti33:2.1 -->

“Mendicants, an individual with two qualities is placed in heaven as if delivered there.

<!-- @segment iti33:2.2 -->

What two?

<!-- @segment iti33:2.3 -->

Excellent conduct and excellent view.

<!-- @segment iti33:2.4 -->

A individual with these two qualities is placed in heaven as if delivered there.”

<!-- @segment iti33:2.5 -->

The Buddha spoke this matter.

<!-- @segment iti33:2.6 -->

On this it is said:

<!-- @segment iti33:3.1 -->

“If a person possesses

<!-- @segment iti33:3.2 -->

these two qualities—

<!-- @segment iti33:3.3 -->

excellent conduct

<!-- @segment iti33:3.4 -->

and excellent views—

<!-- @segment iti33:3.5 -->

when their body breaks up, that wise person

<!-- @segment iti33:3.6 -->

is reborn in heaven.”

<!-- @segment iti33:4.1 -->

This too is a matter that was spoken by the Blessed One: that is what I heard.
