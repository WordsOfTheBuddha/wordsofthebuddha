<!-- @segment iti32:1.1 -->

This was said by the Buddha, the Perfected One: that is what I heard.

<!-- @segment iti32:2.1 -->

“Mendicants, an individual with two qualities is placed in hell as if delivered there.

<!-- @segment iti32:2.2 -->

What two?

<!-- @segment iti32:2.3 -->

Bad conduct and bad view.

<!-- @segment iti32:2.4 -->

A individual with these two qualities is placed in hell as if delivered there.”

<!-- @segment iti32:2.5 -->

The Buddha spoke this matter.

<!-- @segment iti32:2.6 -->

On this it is said:

<!-- @segment iti32:3.1 -->

“If a person possesses

<!-- @segment iti32:3.2 -->

these two qualities—

<!-- @segment iti32:3.3 -->

bad conduct

<!-- @segment iti32:3.4 -->

and bad views—

<!-- @segment iti32:3.5 -->

when their body breaks up, that witless person

<!-- @segment iti32:3.6 -->

is reborn in hell.”

<!-- @segment iti32:4.1 -->

This too is a matter that was spoken by the Blessed One: that is what I heard.
