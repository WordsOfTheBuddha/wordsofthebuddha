title: The Boy’s Questions

<!-- @segment kp4:1.1 -->

What is the one?

<!-- @segment kp4:1.2 -->

All sentient beings are sustained by food.

<!-- @segment kp4:2.1 -->

What is the two?

<!-- @segment kp4:2.2 -->

Name and form.

<!-- @segment kp4:3.1 -->

What is the three?

<!-- @segment kp4:3.2 -->

Three feelings.

<!-- @segment kp4:4.1 -->

What is the four?

<!-- @segment kp4:4.2 -->

Four noble truths.

<!-- @segment kp4:5.1 -->

What is the five?

<!-- @segment kp4:5.2 -->

Five grasping aggregates.

<!-- @segment kp4:6.1 -->

What is the six?

<!-- @segment kp4:6.2 -->

Six interior sense fields.

<!-- @segment kp4:7.1 -->

What is the seven?

<!-- @segment kp4:7.2 -->

Seven awakening factors.

<!-- @segment kp4:8.1 -->

What is the eight?

<!-- @segment kp4:8.2 -->

The noble eightfold path.

<!-- @segment kp4:9.1 -->

What is the nine?

<!-- @segment kp4:9.2 -->

Nine abodes of sentient beings.

<!-- @segment kp4:10.1 -->

What is the ten?

<!-- @segment kp4:10.2 -->

One endowed with ten factors is called “perfected”.
