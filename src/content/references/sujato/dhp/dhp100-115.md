Better than a thousand

<!-- @segment dhp100:2 -->

meaningless sayings

<!-- @segment dhp100:3 -->

is a single meaningful saying,

<!-- @segment dhp100:4 -->

hearing which brings you peace.

<!-- @segment dhp101:0 -->



<!-- @segment dhp101:1 -->

Better than a thousand

<!-- @segment dhp101:2 -->

meaningless verses

<!-- @segment dhp101:3 -->

is a single meaningful verse,

<!-- @segment dhp101:4 -->

hearing which brings you peace.

<!-- @segment dhp102:0 -->



<!-- @segment dhp102:1 -->

Better than reciting

<!-- @segment dhp102:2 -->

a hundred meaningless verses

<!-- @segment dhp102:3 -->

is a single saying of Dhamma,

<!-- @segment dhp102:4 -->

hearing which brings you peace.

<!-- @segment dhp103:1 -->

The supreme conqueror is

<!-- @segment dhp103:2 -->

not he who conquers a million men in battle,

<!-- @segment dhp103:3 -->

but he who conquers a single man:

<!-- @segment dhp103:4 -->

himself.

<!-- @segment dhp104:0 -->



<!-- @segment dhp104:1 -->

It is surely better to conquer oneself

<!-- @segment dhp104:2 -->

than all those other folk.

<!-- @segment dhp104:3 -->

When a person has tamed themselves,

<!-- @segment dhp104:4 -->

always living restrained,

<!-- @segment dhp105:1 -->

no god nor centaur,

<!-- @segment dhp105:2 -->

nor Māra nor divinity,

<!-- @segment dhp105:3 -->

can undo the victory

<!-- @segment dhp105:4 -->

of such a personage.

<!-- @segment dhp106:0 -->



<!-- @segment dhp106:1 -->

Rather than a thousandfold sacrifice,

<!-- @segment dhp106:2 -->

every month for a full century,

<!-- @segment dhp106:3 -->

it’s better to honor for a single hour

<!-- @segment dhp106:4 -->

one who has developed themselves.

<!-- @segment dhp106:5 -->

That offering is better

<!-- @segment dhp106:6 -->

than the hundred year sacrifice.

<!-- @segment dhp107:0 -->



<!-- @segment dhp107:1 -->

Rather than serve the sacred flame

<!-- @segment dhp107:2 -->

in the forest for a hundred years,

<!-- @segment dhp107:3 -->

it’s better to honor for a single hour

<!-- @segment dhp107:4 -->

a personage who has developed themselves.

<!-- @segment dhp107:5 -->

That offering is better

<!-- @segment dhp107:6 -->

than the hundred year sacrifice.

<!-- @segment dhp108:0 -->



<!-- @segment dhp108:1 -->

Whatever sacrifice or offering in the world

<!-- @segment dhp108:2 -->

a seeker of merit may make for a year,

<!-- @segment dhp108:3 -->

none of it is worth a quarter

<!-- @segment dhp108:4 -->

of bowing to the sincere.

<!-- @segment dhp109:0 -->



<!-- @segment dhp109:1 -->

For one in the habit of bowing,

<!-- @segment dhp109:2 -->

always honoring the elders,

<!-- @segment dhp109:3 -->

four blessings grow:

<!-- @segment dhp109:4 -->

lifespan, beauty, happiness, and strength.

<!-- @segment dhp110:0 -->



<!-- @segment dhp110:1 -->

Better to live a single day

<!-- @segment dhp110:2 -->

ethical and absorbed in meditation

<!-- @segment dhp110:3 -->

than to live a hundred years

<!-- @segment dhp110:4 -->

unethical and lacking immersion.

<!-- @segment dhp111:0 -->



<!-- @segment dhp111:1 -->

Better to live a single day

<!-- @segment dhp111:2 -->

wise and absorbed in meditation

<!-- @segment dhp111:3 -->

than to live a hundred years

<!-- @segment dhp111:4 -->

witless and lacking immersion.

<!-- @segment dhp112:0 -->



<!-- @segment dhp112:1 -->

Better to live a single day

<!-- @segment dhp112:2 -->

energetic and strong,

<!-- @segment dhp112:3 -->

than to live a hundred years

<!-- @segment dhp112:4 -->

lazy and lacking energy.

<!-- @segment dhp113:0 -->



<!-- @segment dhp113:1 -->

Better to live a single day

<!-- @segment dhp113:2 -->

seeing rise and fall

<!-- @segment dhp113:3 -->

than to live a hundred years

<!-- @segment dhp113:4 -->

blind to rise and fall.

<!-- @segment dhp114:0 -->



<!-- @segment dhp114:1 -->

Better to live a single day

<!-- @segment dhp114:2 -->

seeing the state free of death

<!-- @segment dhp114:3 -->

than to live a hundred years

<!-- @segment dhp114:4 -->

blind to the state free of death.

<!-- @segment dhp115:0 -->



<!-- @segment dhp115:1 -->

Better to live a single day

<!-- @segment dhp115:2 -->

seeing the supreme teaching

<!-- @segment dhp115:3 -->

than to live a hundred years

<!-- @segment dhp115:4 -->

blind to the supreme teaching.
