title: 13. The World

<!-- @segment dhp167:1 -->

Don’t resort to lowly things,

<!-- @segment dhp167:2 -->

don’t abide in negligence,

<!-- @segment dhp167:3 -->

don’t resort to wrong views,

<!-- @segment dhp167:4 -->

don’t perpetuate the world.

<!-- @segment dhp168:0 -->



<!-- @segment dhp168:1 -->

Get up, don’t be heedless,

<!-- @segment dhp168:2 -->

live by principle, with good conduct.

<!-- @segment dhp168:3 -->

For one of good conduct sleeps at ease,

<!-- @segment dhp168:4 -->

in this world and the next.

<!-- @segment dhp169:1 -->

Live by principle, with good conduct,

<!-- @segment dhp169:2 -->

don’t conduct yourself badly.

<!-- @segment dhp169:3 -->

For one of good conduct sleeps at ease,

<!-- @segment dhp169:4 -->

in this world and the next.

<!-- @segment dhp170:0 -->



<!-- @segment dhp170:1 -->

Look upon the world

<!-- @segment dhp170:2 -->

as a bubble

<!-- @segment dhp170:3 -->

or a mirage,

<!-- @segment dhp170:4 -->

then the King of Death won’t see you.

<!-- @segment dhp171:0 -->



<!-- @segment dhp171:1 -->

Come, see this world decked out

<!-- @segment dhp171:2 -->

like a fancy royal chariot.

<!-- @segment dhp171:3 -->

Here fools founder,

<!-- @segment dhp171:4 -->

but the discerning are not chained.

<!-- @segment dhp172:0 -->



<!-- @segment dhp172:1 -->

He who once was heedless,

<!-- @segment dhp172:2 -->

but turned to heedfulness,

<!-- @segment dhp172:3 -->

lights up the world

<!-- @segment dhp172:4 -->

like the moon freed from clouds.

<!-- @segment dhp173:0 -->



<!-- @segment dhp173:1 -->

Someone whose bad deed

<!-- @segment dhp173:2 -->

is supplanted by the good,

<!-- @segment dhp173:3 -->

lights up the world,

<!-- @segment dhp173:4 -->

like the moon freed from clouds.

<!-- @segment dhp174:0 -->



<!-- @segment dhp174:1 -->

Blind is the world,

<!-- @segment dhp174:2 -->

few are those who clearly see.

<!-- @segment dhp174:3 -->

Only a handful go to heaven,

<!-- @segment dhp174:4 -->

like a bird freed from a net.

<!-- @segment dhp175:0 -->



<!-- @segment dhp175:1 -->

Swans fly by the sun’s path,

<!-- @segment dhp175:2 -->

psychic sages fly through space.

<!-- @segment dhp175:3 -->

The attentive leave the world,

<!-- @segment dhp175:4 -->

having vanquished Māra with his legions.

<!-- @segment dhp176:0 -->



<!-- @segment dhp176:1 -->

When a personage, spurning the hereafter,

<!-- @segment dhp176:2 -->

transgresses in just one thing—

<!-- @segment dhp176:3 -->

lying—

<!-- @segment dhp176:4 -->

there is no evil they would not do.

<!-- @segment dhp177:0 -->



<!-- @segment dhp177:1 -->

The miserly don’t ascend to heaven,

<!-- @segment dhp177:2 -->

it takes a fool to not praise giving.

<!-- @segment dhp177:3 -->

The attentive celebrate giving,

<!-- @segment dhp177:4 -->

and so find happiness in the hereafter.

<!-- @segment dhp178:0 -->



<!-- @segment dhp178:1 -->

The fruit of stream-entry is better

<!-- @segment dhp178:2 -->

than being the one king of the earth,

<!-- @segment dhp178:3 -->

than going to heaven,

<!-- @segment dhp178:4 -->

than lordship over all the world.

<!-- @segment dhp178:5 -->
