“Pañcime, bhikkhave, ānisaṁsā dhammassavane. Katame pañca? Assutaṁ suṇāti, sutaṁ pariyodāpeti, kaṅkhaṁ vitarati, diṭṭhiṁ ujuṁ karoti, cittamassa pasīdati. Ime kho, bhikkhave, pañca ānisaṁsā dhammassavane”ti.

Dutiyaṁ.
