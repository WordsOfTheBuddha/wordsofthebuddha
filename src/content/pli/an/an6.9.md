---
title: Anussatiṭṭhāna sutta - Subjects for Recollection
description: "The Buddha outlines the six subjects for recollection: the Buddha, the Dhamma, the Saṅgha, virtue, generosity, and the deities."
qualities: mindfulness
theme: principle
slug: an6.9
---

“Chayimāni, bhikkhave, anussatiṭṭhānāni. Katamāni cha?

Buddhānussati, dhammānussati, saṅghānussati, sīlānussati, cāgānussati, devatānussati.

Imāni kho, bhikkhave, cha anussatiṭṭhānānī”ti.
