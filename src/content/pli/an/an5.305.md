“Rāgassa, bhikkhave, abhiññāya pañca dhammā bhāvetabbā. Katame pañca? Aniccasaññā, anicce dukkhasaññā, dukkhe anattasaññā, pahānasaññā, virāgasaññā— rāgassa, bhikkhave, abhiññāya ime pañca dhammā bhāvetabbā”ti.
