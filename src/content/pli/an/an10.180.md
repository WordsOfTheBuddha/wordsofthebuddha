title: Kusalasutta
---

“Kusalañca vo, bhikkhave, desessāmi akusalañca.

Taṁ suṇātha …pe…

katamañca, bhikkhave, akusalaṁ?

Pāṇātipāto …pe…

micchādiṭṭhi—

idaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, akusalaṁ.

Katamañca, bhikkhave, kusalaṁ?

Pāṇātipātā veramaṇī …pe…

sammādiṭṭhi—

idaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, kusalan”ti.

Tatiyaṁ.
