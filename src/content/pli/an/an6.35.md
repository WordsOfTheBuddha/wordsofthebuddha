“Chayime, bhikkhave, dhammā vijjābhāgiyā. Katame cha? Aniccasaññā, anicce dukkhasaññā, dukkhe anattasaññā, pahānasaññā, virāgasaññā, nirodhasaññā— ime kho, bhikkhave, cha dhammā vijjābhāgiyā”ti.

Pañcamaṁ.
