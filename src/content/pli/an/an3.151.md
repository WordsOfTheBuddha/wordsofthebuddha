“Tīhi, bhikkhave …pe… sāvajjena kāyakammena, sāvajjena vacīkammena, sāvajjena manokammena …pe….

Tīhi, bhikkhave …pe… anavajjena kāyakammena, anavajjena vacīkammena, anavajjena manokammena …pe….

Chaṭṭhaṁ.
