title: Āloka sutta - Light
description: "The Buddha outlines the four lights: the light of the moon, the light of the sun, the light of fire, and the light of wisdom."
qualities: wisdom
theme: inspiration
slug: an4.143
simile: light
---

“Cattārome, bhikkhave, ālokā. Katame cattāro? Candāloko, sūriyāloko, aggāloko, paññāloko— Ime kho, bhikkhave, cattāro ālokā. Etadaggaṁ, bhikkhave, imesaṁ catunnaṁ ālokānaṁ yadidaṁ paññāloko”ti.
