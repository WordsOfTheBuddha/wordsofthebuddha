Sāvatthinidānaṁ.

“Kismiṁ nu kho, bhikkhave, sati, kiṁ upādāya, kiṁ abhinivissa evaṁ diṭṭhi uppajjati: ‘hoti ca na ca hoti tathāgato paraṁ maraṇā’”ti?

“Bhagavaṁmūlakā no, bhante, dhammā …pe… niyato sambodhiparāyano”ti.

Sattarasamaṁ.
