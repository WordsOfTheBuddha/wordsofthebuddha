kiṁsu bhaṇḍānamuttamaṁ;

Kiṁsu satthamalaṁ loke,

kiṁsu lokasmimabbudaṁ.

haranto pana ko piyo;

Kiṁsu punappunāyantaṁ,

abhinandanti paṇḍitā”ti.

itthī bhaṇḍānamuttamaṁ;

Kodho satthamalaṁ loke,

corā lokasmimabbudā.

haranto samaṇo piyo;

Samaṇaṁ punappunāyantaṁ,

abhinandanti paṇḍitā”ti.
