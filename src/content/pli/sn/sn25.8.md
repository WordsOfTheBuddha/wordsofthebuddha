---
title: Rūpataṇhā sutta - Craving for Form
description: Craving for forms, sounds, odors, tastes, tangible objects, and mental objects is impermanent, changing, and becoming otherwise. Accepting this through faith or wisdom guarantees stream-entry.
qualities: craving, recognition of impermanence, faith
theme: principle, wisdom
slug: sn25.8
---

Sāvatthinidānaṁ.

“Rūpataṇhā, bhikkhave, aniccā vipariṇāmī aññathābhāvī; saddataṇhā … gandhataṇhā … rasataṇhā … phoṭṭhabbataṇhā … dhammataṇhā aniccā vipariṇāmī aññathābhāvī.

Yo, bhikkhave, ime dhamme evaṁ saddahati adhimuccati, ayaṁ vuccati ‘saddhānusārī …pe… sambodhiparāyano’”ti.
