kiṁsu rattindivakkhayo;

Kiṁ malaṁ brahmacariyassa,

kiṁ sinānamanodakan”ti.

Vayo rattindivakkhayo;

Itthī malaṁ brahmacariyassa,

Etthāyaṁ sajjate pajā;

Tapo ca brahmacariyañca,

Taṁ sinānamanodakan”ti.
