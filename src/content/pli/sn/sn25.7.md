---
title: Rūpasañcetanā sutta - Intention regarding Form
description: Intentions regarding forms, sounds, odors, tastes, tangible objects, and mental objects are impermanent, changing, and becoming otherwise. Accepting this through faith or wisdom guarantees stream-entry.
qualities: desire, recognition of impermanence, faith
theme: principle, wisdom
slug: sn25.7
---

Sāvatthinidānaṁ.

“Rūpasañcetanā, bhikkhave, aniccā vipariṇāmī aññathābhāvī; saddasañcetanā … gandhasañcetanā … rasasañcetanā … phoṭṭhabbasañcetanā … dhammasañcetanā aniccā vipariṇāmī aññathābhāvī.

Yo, bhikkhave, ime dhamme evaṁ saddahati adhimuccati, ayaṁ vuccati ‘saddhānusārī …pe… sambodhiparāyano’”ti.
