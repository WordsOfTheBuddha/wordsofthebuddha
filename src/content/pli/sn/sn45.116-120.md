“Seyyathāpi, bhikkhave, yā kācimā mahānadiyo, seyyathidaṁ— evameva kho, bhikkhave, bhikkhu …pe… evameva kho, bhikkhave, bhikkhu …pe… evameva kho, bhikkhave, bhikkhu …pe… evameva kho, bhikkhave, bhikkhu …pe… gaṅgā, yamunā, aciravatī, sarabhū, mahī, sabbā tā pācīnaninnā pācīnapoṇā pācīnapabbhārā; dutiyaṁ.

tatiyaṁ.

catutthaṁ.

pañcamaṁ.

evameva kho, bhikkhave, bhikkhu …pe… chaṭṭhaṁ.
