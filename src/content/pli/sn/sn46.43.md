“Mārasenappamaddanaṁ vo, bhikkhave, maggaṁ desessāmi;

taṁ suṇātha.

Katamo ca, bhikkhave, mārasenappamaddano maggo?

Yadidaṁ—

satta bojjhaṅgā.

Katame satta?

Satisambojjhaṅgo …pe… upekkhāsambojjhaṅgo—

ayaṁ kho, bhikkhave, mārasenappamaddano maggo”ti.

Tatiyaṁ.
