_How "Heir to Kamma" works (AN 5.57). Why does the photon go where it goes?_

- **Support:** This field supports the new consciousness, guiding it into a specific trajectory (realm).

- **Experience:** You formulate a will to _do_ something about the perception.

- **Experience:** The "Fever" of wanting. The system is now hot and unstable.

- **Experience:** You "acquire" the object or a new existence () to feed the craving. This _Acquisition_ resets the cycle, creating a new Excited Atom (Rebirth).

### **4. The Spectrum of Results: Ethics & Realms**

_How specific behaviors tune the frequency of the "Field," leading to specific Rebirths._

The "Frequency" (Wisdom) and "Coherence" (Integrity) of your wave determine which "receiver" (Realm) can absorb you.

#### **A. Unethical Conduct The Inferior Realm (Hell/Ghost)**

- **The Physics:** **Incoherent Scattering & Noise.**
- **The Action:** Lying, killing, stealing. These are "out of phase" actions. They create destructive interference and jagged, chaotic waveforms.
- **The Result:** A chaotic wave cannot be absorbed by a delicate, refined structure. It can only be absorbed by **Dense Matter** (heavy, dark worlds).
- **The Experience:** High resistance. The wave hits the "wall" of the realm and turns instantly into **Heat** (Suffering/Burn).

#### **B. Ethical Conduct The Human/Heavenly Realm**

- **The Physics:** **Harmonic Oscillation.**
- **The Action:** Generosity () and Ethics (). These smooth out the waveform. You remove the "spikes" of conflict.
- **The Result:** The wave is regular and smooth. It resonates with **Structured Matter** (Human/Deva worlds).
- **The Experience:** Low resistance. The flow is sustainable and pleasant, though still subject to decay (entropy).

#### **C. Brahma Viharas (Metta) & Jhanas The Brahma Realm**

- **The Physics:** **Resonance & Superconductivity.**
- **The Action:** _Metta_ (Loving-kindness) is a non-destructive, perfectly pervasive field. _Jhana_ is a **Standing Wave** (a laser).
- **The Result:**
- **Jhana 1-3:** The wave finds a **Resonant Cavity**. It enters a state of amplification without friction. It is a "High Energy" state but with "Zero Resistance" (Sukha).
- **Jhana 4 (Equanimity):** The wave becomes a perfect **Soliton**—a self-reinforcing packet that moves without losing energy. It is "pure."

- **The Rebirth:** A laser beam cannot be "caught" by a brick wall (it burns through) or a muddy swamp (it scatters). It can only be "caught" by a **Crystal** (Brahma World) that matches its purity.

- **The Result:**
